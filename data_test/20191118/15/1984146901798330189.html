Chris Ramsey's bulge has its own Twitter account as Strictly fans get distracted by tight trousers

Strictly Come Dancing fans were drawn to Chris Ramsey's tight trousers during Saturday night's Blackpool show

18 Nov 2019, 15:50 by Simon Duke

While he let his dancing do the talking to great aplomb on Saturday night, Chris Ramsey got Strictly Come Dancing viewers talking for a whole different reason!

And attention on the South Shields' comedian's bulge has escalated so much that it even has its own Twitter account.

Chris and professional partner Karen Hauer opened the live show at the Tower Ballroom in Blackpool in serious style at the weekend with a high octane Salsa to Mark Ronson & Bruno Mars' worldwide smash Uptown Funk.

Their routine had the audience and some of the judges on their feet and earned Chris & Karen a score of 33, their second highest to date, just behind the score for THAT Let's Get Ready to Rhumble dance.

But, after the dust had settled on what was a spectacular night of fancy footwork, Strictly fans were talking about something else...namely Chris' bulge, with one tweeting "Completely distracted by Chris's crotch."

His wife Rosie even got on the act. Uploading a zoomed in screen grab of Chris in some VERY tight pink trousers, she joked "

"You guys didn’t think I married him for his personality did you?!?! . Oh you did?? .. That’s embarrassing."

Rosie was actually referenced in another fan tweet, with someone posting "Rosie is a lucky woman."

And now the bulge has it's own Twitter account. Called Chris Ramsey's Bulge, with the handle @BulgeChris, the account's bio reads "I am the monster which lurks inside the undergarments of Chris ‘The Package’ Ramsey and I am a grower."

So far Chris hasn't followed the account but we reckon it's only a matter of time!
