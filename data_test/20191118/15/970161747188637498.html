Jessie J strips down to Calvins for topless shoot as she opens up about anxiety

18 Nov 2019, 15:32

Jessie J just posted a *fire emoji, fire emoji, fire emoji* pic to her Instagram story wearing nothing but Calvin Klein underwear and she looks incredible.

The 31-year-old singer took to Instagram to share the photo to her 8.7 million followers with the caption: ‘Not an ad! But I am #InMyCalvins’.

It shows the Bang Bang singer topless but wearing high-waisted Calvin Klein underwear, as she took a selfie in the mirror.

The strategic placement of the camera in front of the mirror shows off both the front and back… and she looks all kinds of fire.

‘My anxiety is so bad today,’ she wrote. ‘Like the worst it’s been in ages.’

She added: ‘Not judging myself. Not trying to over react. Just being as calm as I can. Being gentle with myself. Protecting my energy and space.

‘Wanted to share for anyone else feeling the same way. You are not alone.

‘Give yourself some time. Try and find some quiet space and breathe.’

She also shared a simple graphic with the message: ‘Check up on your people. Seeing them on social media is not enough.’

Earlier this year, Jessie J blasted critics who judged her for getting a tattoo with a spelling mistake in it.

She posted a photo of her in bikini with the caption: ‘Yes my tattoo says… “don’t loose who you are in the blur of the stars”. Yes they are MY lyrics from my song who you are. Yes that I wrote. Yes I spelt the tattoo wrong. Yes I repeat it’s spelt wrong.

‘Yes I got it done in Essex. Yes the tattoo artist didn’t mention it. Yes I was 18. Yes I still don’t know the difference between lose and loose. Yes It’s the reason I wear everything high waisted [sic].’
