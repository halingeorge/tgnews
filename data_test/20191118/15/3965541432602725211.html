Husband, wife found dead after murder-suicide on Fox Island

18 Nov 2019, 15:07 by KOMO News Staff

FOX ISLAND, Wash. - A husband and wife are dead after a murder-suicide Sunday at a Fox Island home, Pierce County sheriff's officials said.

Deputies and medics responded to the scene, a residence on Queets Place on Fox Island, just before 5 p.m. after dispatchers received a 911 call from a woman reporting a domestic violence altercation with her husband.

The caller reported that her husband had a gun, then seconds later the dispatcher heard multiple shots being fired.

When deputies arrived at the home, they found the body of the 48-year-old woman in front of the home. She had been shot to death.

Next to the victim, deputies also located the 57-year-old husband dead from a self-inflicted gunshot wound.

Detectives and forensics experts processed the scene and interviewed witnesses throughout the night.
