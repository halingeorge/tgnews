How to watch the impeachment hearings

18 Nov 2019, 15:26 by KOMO News Staff

You can watch the impeachment hearings live on KOMO and on KOMO's Facebook page starting at 6 a.m. Tuesday.

After Tuesday, the hearings will resume at 6 a.m. Wednesday and then again at 6 a.m. Thursday.

You can watch all three days on television and on our Facebook page.
