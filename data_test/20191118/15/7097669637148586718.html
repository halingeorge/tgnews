White House attempts to quell rumors about Trump’s health

18 Nov 2019, 15:04 by Yahoo News Video

The White House has attempted to quell rumors about Donald Trump’s health after an unscheduled visit to a doctor over the weekend.

Trump has claimed his visit to the Walter Reed medical center on Saturday was “phase one” of his yearly physical, despite him having no public events on his schedule for that day and his last physical being only nine months ago.
