Dy. Chief Law Officer Vacancy At Paradip Port Trust

18 Nov 2019, 15:27 by Manav Malhotra

Paradip Port Trust invites application for the post of Dy. Chief Law Officer.

Name of the Post: Dy. Chief Law Officer

No. of post: 01 (One)

Age Limit: 40 Years

Pay Scale: Rs.24900-50500/-

Essential Qualification and Experience

  • Degree in Law from a recognized University.
  • 09 years' executive experience in a Legal Establishment of an Industrial/ Commercial/ Govt. Undertaking.

How to apply?

  • Applications, complete in all respects in the prescribed format and addressed to the Secretary, Paradip Port Trust, Dist - Jagatsinghpur, Odisha- 754142 should reach the office of The Secretary, Paradip Port Trust by REGISTERED POST WITH AD on or before 09.12.2019.
  • The envelope containing the application should be clearly super scribed in BOLD capital letters with the post applied for as: - "APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF DY. CHIEF LAW OFFICER (CLASS-I).
