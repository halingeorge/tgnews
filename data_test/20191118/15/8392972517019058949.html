McDonald's is giving away 2,000 free Christmas jumpers – here's how you can get one

Are you #ReindeerReady?

18 Nov 2019, 15:33 by Nancy Brown

Hot on the heels of the news that McDonald's is extending its breakfast hours comes yet another reason to celebrate.

The fast food giant has announced that it's giving away 2,000 Christmas jumpers in a bid to get its customers #ReindeerReady this festive season.

The dark green jumper takes its inspiration from the new McDonald's Christmas ad, which features a cute reindeer called Archie.

The jumper – available in medium, large and extra large – features a reindeer with a McDonald's Christmas bauble hanging from its antlers.

To get your hands on one of the jumpers, just fill out your details here and keep your fingers crossed that you're a winner when the draw closes on December 5.

Entry is free and you don’t need to have purchased something from McDonald’s to enter. However, you can't put your name in more than once.

Back in 2017, the restaurant used Love Island stars Chris Hughes and Kem Cetinay to launch their #ReindeerReady Christmas jumpers.

This year, former Gogglebox star Scarlett Moffatt – and her pooch Bonnie – are on board.

She posted a picture of herself enjoying a McDelivery while wearing her festive McDonald's jumper.

She said: "Me & Bonnie have got our matching #ReindeerReady Christmas jumpers on after falling in love with Archie the reindeer in the new @mcdonaldsuk Xmas advert."

Scarlett's boyfriend Scott Dobinson commented: "Aww Bonnie and the oversized jumper."

She replied: "She’s like me loves to be comfy in an oversized."

If you're not one of the lucky winners, fear not – you can still feel festive with one of McDonald's Matchmakers Mint McFlurry, available from November 20.
