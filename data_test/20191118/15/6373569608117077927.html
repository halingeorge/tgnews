KFC's festive menu includes Christmas dinner box with festive fries and stuffing burger

The Christmas burger has fried chicken, hash browns, stuffing mayo, and cranberry sauce

18 Nov 2019, 15:23 by Jess Morcom

If you love both Christmas and fried chicken, then your prayers may have just been answered.

KFC's Christmas menu has just launched in restaurants today, and contains three new seasonal items.

The KFC Festive Burger, Festive Fries and a new Mint Choc Krushem Soft Serve combine the brand's love of Original Recipe fried chicken with traditional Christmas ingredients.

The Festive Burger includes a classic Original Recipe fillet with a crispy hash brown, sage and onion stuffing mayo, tangy cranberry dressing and a layer of cheese.

It costs £4.59 by itself or you can opt for a meal for £1 extra.

Alternatively, you can also get a Festive Burger Box Meal for £6.89, which comes with a burger, drink, fries and some festive sides of beans and a chicken drumstick.

There's also a new flavour of fries for the Christmas period. KFC's Festive Fries are sprinkled with a smoky seasoning of pepper, toasted onions and garlic and herbs.

The Mint Choc Krushem Soft Serve is another new limited edition item, piled high with soft vanilla ice cream, drizzled with chocolate and mint sauce, sprinkled with Cookie Crunch and finished with a glittery chocolate bow tie.

Everything will be available from now until January 1, and you can also order it on Deliveroo.

Jo Tivers, Top Festive Elf at KFC UK & Ireland, commented on the new menu, saying: “A round of santa-plause for this epic festive feast please.

"We'll let turkey have its moment on the 25th but until then, we’ve got your back.".
