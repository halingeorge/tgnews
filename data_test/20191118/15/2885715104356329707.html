Massachusetts man arrested after son allegedly takes ‘Spiderman heroin’ to school

The child said told his teacher that he got it from his living room and that he becomes spiderman when he put it in his mouth.

18 Nov 2019, 15:30 by Trends Desk

A Massachusetts man was arrested without bail on Thursday after his five-year-old son allegedly brought a packet of heroin to school. Benny Garcia of Holyoke pleaded not guilty to the drug charges and is awaiting a hearing on Novermber 20.

According to police, the five-year-old allegedly brought to school a bag of heroin which had a spider man stamp on the packet. The child told his teacher that he got it from his living room and that he becomes spiderman when he put it in his mouth. The teacher soon informed the school principal about the “suspicious substance”, who immediately called up the police.
