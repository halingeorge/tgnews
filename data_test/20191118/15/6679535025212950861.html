T20I ticket sales to begin on Nov. 25

18 Nov 2019, 15:18 by Tvmrp

The ticket sales for the India-West Indies T20 cricket match, to be held at the Sports Hub on December 8, will begin on Nov. 25.

The tickets will be available only on KCA’s ticketing partner site Paytm. The link will be soon made available on KCA’s website. The prices of the tickets are ₹1000 for upper tier, ₹2000 for lower tier, ₹3000 for special chair and ₹5000 for executive pavilion.

A person can buy a maximum of six tickets from an email ID or from a mobile number.

The upper tier tickets will be available for ₹500 for students but they should upload their ID card while buying the tickets. A green protocol will be maintained at the stadium.

Kadakampally Surendran, the Minister for Tourism and Devasom, inaugurated the organising committee office at a function held at the Sports Hub here on Monday.
