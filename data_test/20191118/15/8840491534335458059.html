Journalist challenging Mayo GAA's ruling on his board nomination

18 Nov 2019, 15:25

MAYO GAA County Board has informed Mayo sports journalist Michael Gallagher that his nomination for the role as public relations officer has not been accepted due to a technicality.

Mr. Gallagher was told he was not a member of Ballycroy GAA Club, the club which nominated him, on March 31 in line with the organisation's guidelines.

In a statement issued to The Connaught Telegraph, Mr. Gallagher said his challenge for the position is not over yet.

He said: “They (county board) rang me last night (Sunday) saying I was disqualified because I wasn't a member of Ballycroy GAA on March 31.

“I thanked them for getting in touch.

"They asked me to withdraw. I refused and told them to disqualify me. They did so.

“We're taking it to another forum in the coming week.”
