Prachi sets meet record in 200m freestyle

18 Nov 2019, 15:32 by Special Correspondent

Prachi Tokas of Delhi bagged her second gold with a new meet record, as she won the women’s 200m freestyle with a time of 2 minute 10.57 seconds, in the 65th National School Games swimming championship at the Shyama Prasad Mukherjee Swimming Pool here on Monday.

Prachi had won the 800m freestyle gold with a new meet record on Sunday.

The others to set new meet marks were Harsh Saroha, Kareena Shankta, Nina Venkatesh, Kiara Bangera, Anushka Patil and Jasnoor Kaur.
