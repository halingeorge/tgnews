Patriots’ Stephon Gilmore Calls Out Zach Ertz For Crying Too Much During Game (VIDEO)

18 Nov 2019, 15:47 by Darrelle Lincoln

The New England Patriots took a trip to Philly fresh off their first loss of the season and a bye week ready to get back on the winning track.

That’s exactly what they did with a 17-10 victory that moved the Patriots to 9-1 on the season with Philadelphia dropping to 5-5.

In the midst of that game was a battle between Eagles tight end Zach Ertz and one of the best cover guys in the NFL, New England cornerback Stephon Gilmore.

Following the matchup, Gilmore had something to say about Ertz and he did not mince words.
