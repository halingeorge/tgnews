Good Morning Britain's Charlotte Hawkins sobs after watching Piers Morgan advert

GMB's team were tasked to create a tear-jerking advert to rival John Lewis' Christmas trailer Excitable Edgar

18 Nov 2019, 15:56 by Irishmirror.ie

Good Morning Britain's Charlotte Hawkins cried after a Piers Morgan advert.

The breakfast presenter, 44, broke down into tears when on the set of Good Morning Britain on Monday.

The Good Morning Britain team were tasked to create a tear-jerking advert to rival John Lewis' Christmas trailer Excitable Edgar which has caused a stir in the lead up to Xmas.

They come up with a mini Christmas advert capturing the childhood friendship of Piers and Susanna Reid.

Emotional Charlotte dried her free-flowing tears after watching the cheerful clip.

So if Charlotte's reaction was anything to go on, they weren't far off rivalling the advert.

Clearly amused, Piers drew attention to his co-star's tears when he asked her if she was crying. "Are you crying?" He said.

Charlotte shrugged off his prying comments with a smile. She said: "Don't make a fuss."

Piers tried to antagonise her: "You watched that load of cheese and you are crying?"

She responded: "I'm sorry, it's just the friendship. Just sometimes these things get me."

Charlotte may have been left feeling warm and fuzzy inside after watching the GMB advert but she confessed she didn't cry over Excitable Edgar.

John Lewis set Excitable Edgar in a land far, far, far away as they told the heart-warming Christmas story about little girl Ava and her friend Edgar the dragon.

In a sad twist of events, the hapless dragon finds himself shunned from the villagers after he accidentally burns down the Christmas tree and decorations.

But the little redheaded girl hatches an ingenious plan to help her fire-breathing friend spread happiness in time for Xmas.

Ava gives her dragon pal the job of cooking the Christmas pudding, much to everyone's delight.
