Colin Kaepernick Reportedly Has Not Gotten Any Offers After Workout

18 Nov 2019, 15:03 by VladTV

Update 11/18/2019 2:44pm:

Colin Kaepernick recently held a workout to show off his skill to various NFL teams for consideration. Since then, the quarterback has reportedly not gotten any calls from any of the 32 organizations in the league.

This comes after Kaepernick delivered a message at the end of his workout telling the NFL and team owners to “stop running from the truth.” Stay tuned for more details in this situation.

source: NBC Sports

Original 11/17/2019 4:31pm:

A workout was recently held by Colin Kaepernick for NFL teams to see that he is more than capable of playing the league. After the workout was finished, Kaepernick had some words for the NFL and Roger Goodell.

Kaepernick said “I've been ready for three years; I've been denied for three years. We all know why I came out here and showed it today in front of everybody. We have nothing to hide. So we're waiting for the 32 owners, the 32 teams, Roger Goodell, all them to stop running. Stop running from the truth, stop running from the people." Watch the full statement above.
