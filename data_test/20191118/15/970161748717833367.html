Who are the special guests in Rick and Morty season 4?

18 Nov 2019, 15:00

After a two year wait, Rick and Morty fans are finally being rewarded for their patience this week with the return of the much-anticipated fourth season.

Sociopathic scientist Rick and his adventure-loving grandson Morty return with the first episode of season four on 20 November on E4 and All 4.

Episode one, a take on 2014 action film Edge Of Tomorrow with the name Edge of ToMorty: Rick Die Rickpeat is the first cinematic-inspired installment of the new series to hit our screens and the reviews are already promising a triumphant return for the adult cartoon.

The main voice actors in the series including Justin Roiland (who voices both Rick Sanchez and Morty) Chris Parnell, Spencer Grammer and Sarah Chalke are all returning this season, but which special guests have joined them?

Last season saw the likes of actress Susan Sarandon, singer Logic and Dirty John star Christian Slater guest star in the series alongside a host of other famous names.

Rick and Morty season 4 promises a similarly strong line-up of special guests, including acclaimed director Taika Waititi who will voice a new character – a pink alien – called Glootie.

Other guest stars confirmed for season four include Sam Neill, Paul Giamatti and Kathleen Turner, as well as Ferris Bueller’s Day Off star Matthew Broderick and Game of Thrones actor Liam Cunningham.

Other guest stars are yet to be announced, but will be revealed as each new episode airs.
