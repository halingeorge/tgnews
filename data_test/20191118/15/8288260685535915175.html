Ed Woodward drops Manchester United transfer hint amid John McGinn rumour

Latest Aston Villa transfer news | Man Utd continue to be linked with McGinn ahead of January

18 Nov 2019, 15:30 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Ed Woodward says Manchester United have a ‘clear vision’ for recruitment – and hinted the club are ready to spend amid reports linking them with Aston Villa midfielder John McGinn.

United were first credited with an interest in McGinn in the summer transfer window after the Scotland international played a key role in Villa’s promotion to the Premier League.

The 25-year-old signed a new five-year deal at Villa Park ahead of the first game of the season but it is understood United have continued to monitor his performances.

Old Trafford executive vice-chairman Woodward addressed the New York Stock Exchange to inform shareholders of results from the first quarter of the financial year.

And recruitment to Ole Gunnar Solskjaer’s squad was high on the agenda after a difficult start to the season.

Woodward said: “We have a clear vision in terms of football philosophy and recruitment.

"The significant investments that we have made in recent years in areas such as transfers, recruitment infrastructure, analytics and academy are already beginning to bear fruit.

"We are very proud to be approaching our 4,000th game featuring an academy player and we are particularly optimistic regarding considerable young talent coming through.

"The ultimate goal is win trophies playing exciting football with a team that fuses academy graduates and world-class acquisitions.”

 

McGinn has proved to be one of Villa's best bargain signings in recent years having excelled since joining from Scottish Premiership side Hibernian for under £3m last year.

According to ESPN, McGinn and team-mate Jack Grealish are on a transfer hit-list alongside six other players United are looking at in a bid to beef up their squad in January.
