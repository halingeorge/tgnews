Aldi is selling GIANT Kevin the Carrot toys just in time for Christmas

The lovable character Kevin the Carrot will be back in stores with new friends too

18 Nov 2019, 15:11 by Brenda Dennehy

Budget supermarket Aldi has confirmed its Kevin the Carrot and friends toys will be back in stores next week.

Aldi's 2019 Christmas advert featuring the beloved vegetable was a huge hit and now we know when the much-anticipated range will be available to buy in store.

Whoever thought that a soft toy posing as one of our five a day would be such a runaway success?

So much so, folks queued in the freezing cold last year to buy cute little toy.

For those who wish to go above and beyond, there's also the option to buy a one metre tall Kevin toy, as well as a Giant Katie.

A few of the famous carrots friends are tagging a long with him as well.

The supermarket giant has introduced Tiny Tom and wait for it....Russell Sprout - you guessed it, a sprout! (We know - it's the best name yet, so good you may even eat your sprouts at your Christmas dinner)!

This year, the range is bigger and better than ever, with 23 Kevin related products including Family Pyjamas, a Children’s Christmas Jumper and a range of stationery.

 

If your desire for vegetable-themed stuff still hasn't been satisfied, there are also a full range of products available of the beloved root vegetable.

Notebooks, pyjamas, wrapping paper, crackers, mugs, seriously, you name it, it is available to buy.

Not to fear Ringmaster Kevin and Katie will also be back.

The budget retailer has confirmed that the toys will be available to buy in stores on  Thursday November 28  and available to pre-order online from November 24.

 

You might remember that last year the demand for Kevin the Carrot toys was so intense that they completely sold out after just one day on the shelves - so make sure you don't miss out!

As for the advert itself, we see that Kevin has managed to get himself into something of a pickle after tangling with Russell and his street gang, The Leafy Blinders.
