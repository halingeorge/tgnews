Star Wars: Poe, Finn & Chewie Go on Wild Ride in New Rise of Skywalker Photo

18 Nov 2019, 15:11 by Jason Guisao

In a newly released photo of Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker, Poe Dameron, Finn and Chewbacca sit in the cockpit of the legendary Millennium Falcon.

The photo, released by Entertainment Weekly, shows Poe and Chewbacca with their mouths agape as gaze at something off screen. Poe, in particular, looks pretty surprised by whatever they're seeing. Meanwhile, ex-Stormtrooper Finn looks on with intensity.

RELATED: Daisy Ridley Describes Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker in Four Words

Bringing the Skywalker Saga to an end, The Rise of Skywalker chronicles the final battle between the First Order and the Resistance. Rey, Finn and Poe, joined by an extensive cast of rebel heroes, will stand against swarming armies of stormtroopers led by Kylo Ren and the Emperor.

Directed and co-written by J.J. Abrams, Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker stars Daisy Ridley, Adam Driver, John Boyega, Oscar Isaac, Lupita Nyong’o, Domhnall Gleeson, Kelly Marie Tran, Joonas Suotamo, Billie Lourd, Keri Russell, Anthony Daniels, Mark Hamill, Billy Dee Williams and Carrie Fisher, with Naomi Ackie and Richard E. Grant. The film arrives December 20.
