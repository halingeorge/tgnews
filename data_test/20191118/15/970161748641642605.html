Seventeen’s S.Coups takes hiatus amid anxiety treatment

18 Nov 2019, 15:30

Today it was announced Seventeen’s S.Coups has stepped back from the band and taken a hiatus to receive treatment for anxiety.

His management, Pledis Entertainment, made the announcement on Monday as they posted a statement to Twitter.

In it, the company revealed the decision for S.Coups to take some time out was made by him and the other band members.

When so many stars battle mental health issues, the performer should be applauded for seeking the help he needs, with many fans praising the star.

‘We would like to make an official announcement in regard to the health condition of S.Coups,’ the statement, translated to English read, according to All K Pop.

‘S.Coups has recently been undergoing a detailed examination and diagnosis of his health, due to symptoms of anxiety. And after thorough discussion, all of the Seventeen members, including S.Coups himself, have decided that it would be best for him to take the time to rest and temporarily abstain from participating in Seventeen’s activities.

‘We sincerely extend our apologies to all fans who have continued showing their love and support for Seventeen and hope for your understanding, as we have made this decision to prioritize our member’s health and well-being.

‘We will make sure to continue trying our best to protect our artists.’

The statement continued: ‘In addition, we sincerely hope that there will be no excessive assumptions or misunderstandings regarding the health of S.Coups, and will make further announcements accordingly in regard to his recovery and future activities.’

After the news was announced fans began using #GetWellSoonSCOUPS to send their wishes to the star and hopes of his swift return in good health.

‘We’ll miss you but we’ll be waiting patiently for you as your mental and physical health come first,’ one wrote on Twitter. ‘The caring eldest brother to seventeen n the most amazing leader, we love and appreciate you so so much.’

Another simply wrote of S.Coups: ‘We love you so much, leader scoups get lots of rest and come back soon when you’re ready oki [sic].’

‘Our precious leader, please rest well so you can get better soon we are here to support you and send all our love to you, you did an amazing work, now it’s time to prioritize yourself and heal well,’ one Seventeen fan sweetly wrote.
