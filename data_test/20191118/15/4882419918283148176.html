Jaime Latre, who rowed with Messi, to ref Leganes v Barca

The Catalans have been handed the list of officials for the coming weekend

18 Nov 2019, 15:26 by German Bona

The RFEF have published their refereeing appointments for the weekend - week 14 of La Liga - the first after the international break.

Santiago Jaime Latre is taking charge of Barcelona's trip to face Leganes at Butarque on Saturday, 1pm. The VAR ref will be Mario Melero López.

Latre has reffed Barca 10 times, they have won eight and drawn two. However he fell out with Lionel Messi last time out, in a 1-1 draw against Athletic at Camp Nou. The cameras captured Messi saying "you're threatening me, you're threatening me, always the same with you, always the same," to the official.

Messi came on off the bench in that game and criticised the officials for their leniency towards Athletic players and for a corner not given.

That wasn't the first time Messi had problems with Latre. In 16-17, a 2-1 win at Sevilla, Messi was booked for tying his shoelace on the pitch, in a surreal incident that the referee deemed timewasting. Barca appealed the card.
