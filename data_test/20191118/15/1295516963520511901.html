Arrow Star Describes Series Finale in Three Words

18 Nov 2019, 15:11 by Sam Stone

As Arrow hurtles towards its series finale, one star has summed up the final episode in three words.

In Season 8, Oliver Queen and Felicity Smoak's adult daughter Mia (Katherine McNamara) was sent back in time to join the present-day cast with her half-brother William Clayton-Queen and Connor Hawke. In an interview about her surprise reunion with the father she never knew, McNamara described the series finale, set to air on January 28, in three words.
