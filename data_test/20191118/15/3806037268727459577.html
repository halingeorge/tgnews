Colmar Reinterprets the Four Elements For Latest Outerwear Collection

Prepare for the colder weather ahead.

18 Nov 2019, 15:27 by Kevin Arulrajah

LDF_COLMAR_FW19_MAIN VIDEO from HYPEBEAST on Vimeo.

Heritage Italian luxury outerwear label, Colmar, unveils its latest range of jackets inspired by the four elements. Water, Earth, Air and Fire symbolise the starting point of the creative process which leads to the experimentation of colors, prints and patterns.

Blending material innovation and experimental designs, The Originals collection consists of ripstop and flocked fabrics, with camouflage prints which evoke the patterns of bark and moss. The jackets are filled with technical wools and covered with opaque nylon and industrial fabrics to provide an iridescent effect. The jackets are finished off with curved shapes in continuous motion intersecting with the geometrical shapes that rise to form new patterns in the material. Heavily celebrating the four elements, the winter staples are available in Forest Green, Abyss Blue, navy blue, white, black and metallic colorways.

Priced between $469 and $819 USD, The Orginal collection is now available at select retailers and online.
