Magnetic Mix 065: Wez Saunders

Defected Records managing director Wez Saunders celebrates 20 years of the label with this Magnetic Mix.

18 Nov 2019, 15:00 by Ryan Middleton

Defected Records managing director Wez Saunders is here to celebrate 20 years of the game-changing label. With a huge celebration all year long, Defected has stamped itself as one of the UK’s best labels, period, and one of the best in house music around the world. We asked Saunders to make us a mix in honor of the label’s anniversary. In this Magnetic Mix he shines the light on some classics, in addition to big tunes from Defected and the DFTD imprint. If you need an intro to the label or just want to hear great house music, this is for you.

You will find out more about him tomorrow with a special Industry Insider feature.

Defected is on tour in the US now with LA and Chicago on deck, with a Glitterbox show in NYC on December 6.
