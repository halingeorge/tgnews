HC closes plea challenging recruitment notification

18 Nov 2019, 15:57 by Staff ReporterMADURAI

The Madurai Bench of the Madras High Court on Monday closed a public interest litigation petition that challenged a notification issued by Nuclear Power Corporation of India Limited for recruitment under ‘C’ and ‘D’ categories.

A Division Bench of Justices T.S. Sivagnanam and R. Tharani observed that a service matter was filed in the name of a public interest litigation petition. Therefore, the petition could not be entertained, the court said and closed the petition.

The petitioner, M. Appavu, a former MLA, had sought a direction to recruit local residents.
