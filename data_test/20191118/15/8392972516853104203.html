I'm A Celebrity viewers brand new series' line-up 'worst ever'

Hard to top last year's

18 Nov 2019, 15:02 by Richard Bell

ITV viewers have branded the I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! line-up "disappointing" and accused the show of "scraping the barrel" for the camp this year.

After watching the opening episode of the new series, which aired last night (17.11.19), some viewers on Twitter even said they thought it was the "worst line-up ever".

Other admitted they thought ITV would have trouble topping the 2018 line-up, which included The Chase's Anne Hegerty, football manager Harry Redknapp and new Extra Camp host Emily Atack.

One wrote on Twitter: "This has to be the worst #ImACeleb line-up, #scrapingthebarell, apart from #ianwright."

Another said: "Has to be said, this is a pretty poor line-up #ImACeleb."

A third tweeted: "Apart from Haskell, Wright and possibly Kate Garraway, it's the worst line up ever."

Seriously the worst line-up of so-called celebs I've ever seen.

Someone else wrote: "Best line-up ever? No! You will never beat last year's!

"The best line-up is still last year's #ImACeleb," echoed a fifth.

"Seriously the worst line-up of so-called celebs I've ever seen," tweeted another, who added: "Truly disappointed! #ImACeleb."
