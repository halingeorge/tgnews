Stree actor joins Kartik Aaryan and Janhvi Kapoor in Dostana 2

18 Nov 2019, 15:03 by Filmfare

Kartik Aaryan and Janhvi Kapoor have already started shooting for their next film, Dostana 2, in Chandigarh. The actors are super excited about the sequel and have been prepping up for the film for a while now. While Kartik followed the Dharma ritual and sought Karan Johar’s blessings before leaving for the film, Janhvi visited the Golden Temple in Amritsar before heading for the shoot. Now we hear that another actor is joining them in the film and it's Abhishek Banerjee of Stree fame.

Abhishek played Rajkummar Rao’s friend in Stree and won over viewer's with his comic timing. Now he's stepping into this rom-com and he seems to be  excited about the same. The actor spoke to an entertainment portal and said that he’s glad to be a part of this Dharma film. He’s initially worked with Collin D’Cunha (director of Dostana 2) as casting director for Secret Superstar. He couldn’t believe that life has come full circle. 

Abhishek was last seen in Bala as Ayushmann’s friend cum barber and he sure tickcled our bones in the superhit film. Here’s look forward to more from this budding actor.
