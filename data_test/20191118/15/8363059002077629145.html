John Lewis Black Friday Bundle Includes Free £69 Wireless Earbuds

18 Nov 2019, 15:20 by Shabana Arif

John Lewis is giving away a free pair of OnePlus Bullets Wireless In-Ear Headphones with the OnePlus 7 Pro right now.

The headphones are being bundled up with the OnePlus 7 Pro, available from the retailer in Almond, Mirror Grey, and Nebula Blue, and usually retail at an hilarious £69 on their own. So you'll only be forking out £599 for just the handset, which isn't too bad if you're after a decent pair of wireless in-ear headphones but don't want to spend any actual money on them.

The earbuds are the original Bullets from last year, as opposed to this year's refresh, but as we mentioned in our comparison, there aren't too many differences, and if you already own the first generation of earbud, an upgrade would be pretty pointless, so as a freebie with a new smartphone, it's not too shabby.

John Lewis doesn't say how long the deal is on for, but considering we're in the throes of Black Friday sales, we imagine it'll be good for a week or so at least.
