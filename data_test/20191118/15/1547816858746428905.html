Motichoor Chaknachoor Director Debamitra AGHAST, Cries Out, 'Please Don't See My Film, Producer RUINED It' - EXCLUSIVE

18 Nov 2019, 15:34 by Vickey Lalwani

Motichoor Chaknachoor director Debamitra is shocked that her film has been brutally tampered with. In an EXCLUSIVE INTERVIEW to SpotboyE.com, Debamitra says it all

Motichoor Chaknachoor director Debamitra Bansal is shell-shocked by the end product. After her huge fallout with the producer Rajesh Bhatia that led to her ouster, she claims that the movie’s story-line has been totally changed and disowns the film at this point.

Here are some excerpts from our chat after the movie hit theatres on Friday, Nov 15

Did you see Motichoor Chaknachoor?
I was shown the film when the court had issued an order to Rajesh Bhatia (producer) stating that you will have to show the film to the director. And after seeing the movie, I thought to myself: OMG! What have they made?

But, only you directed the film...
I had directed the film no doubt, but they chucked out portions and introduced unnecessary shots. There is nothing left in the film. It looks like a C-grade Bhojpuri film.
