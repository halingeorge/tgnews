Jeffree Star celebrated his recent birthday with some of his favorite things: friends, family, and lots of marijuana.

The beauty mogul turned 34 on Friday, and attended a birthday party held in his home state of California. Fellow YouTubers like Shane Dawson and Ryland Adams were in attendance, as was Star’s longtime boyfriend, Nathan Schwandt.

Star’s party also had a cannabis theme, and was complete with a giant birthday cake that looked like a marijuana leaf.
