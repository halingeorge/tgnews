Jodie Chesney's teenage killers jailed for life for knifing Girl Scout in park

18 Nov 2019, 15:56

A drug dealer and a 17-year-old boy have been jailed for life for the murder of Girl Scout Jodie Chesney.

Svenson Ong-a-Kwie, 19, was sentenced to a minimum term of 26 years at the Old Bailey today.

A 17-year-old youth was locked up for at least 18 years for his part in the murder.
