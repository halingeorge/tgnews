6 Players Who Could End The Year Without A Club

18 Nov 2019, 15:59 by Soccer Laduma

With the domestic season quickly approaching the halfway mark, Soccer Laduma takes a look at six of the many players who could end 2019 without a club. 

George Lebese who parted ways with Mamelodi Sundowns during the final hours of the July/August transfer window this year, is still a free agent and according to Siya sources, there is yet to be a club to show official interest in the player.

As previously revealed by the Siya crew, Lebese’s camp was also looking for options overseas, although it could be possible that he will still be a free agent by the end of the year.  

Elsewhere, Bhongolethu Jayiya, who was previously on the books of Kaizer Chiefs, received his clearance from Amakhosi in May this year; however, the player is still unattached and could possibly end the year as a free agent.

Siphiwe Tshabalala is another player who is still unattached after returning home from a season-long stint in Turkey. While the player has been linked with a return abroad, nothing has materialized as yet. 

Former Orlando Pirates defender, Patrick Phungwayo is still a free agent after parting ways with Free State Stars in July this year. 

Former Mamelodi Sundowns centre-back, Thabo Nthethe is also on the list of free agents, with the 35-year-old unattached to a club since leaving Chippa United in January this year. 

Another defender, who is still a free agent since July 01, is Brian Onyango, despite the player having recently undergone a training stint with Pirates.
