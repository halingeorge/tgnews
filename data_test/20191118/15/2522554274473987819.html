50 Cent Claims He Turned Off His Own Instagram Account

18 Nov 2019, 15:07 by Tony M. Centeno

50 Cent has cleared the air about why his Instagram account was deactivated.

On Sunday afternoon (Nov. 17), 50 jumped on his Twitter timeline to address the rumor that his Instagram account was shut down. According to 50, the rapper, who dropped his last studio album Animal Ambition: An Untamed Desire to Win back in 2014, shut down his Instagram page himself.

"LOL Im cool with all these false report, if you don’t know why something happens you can’t just make some shit up smh I turned my own IG page off SUCKERS. #LeCheminDuRoi #bransoncognac," 50 said in a tweet that includes a screenshot of a news story suggesting that 50's IG account was deactivated because he made some jokes at the expense of Power actress Naturi Naughton.
