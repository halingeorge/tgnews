Trump support booms among GOP base in Iowa

18 Nov 2019, 15:04 by Dominick Mastrangelo

President Trump is backed by a record number of registered Republicans in the state of Iowa, according to a new poll.

The poll, conducted by the Des Moines Register and released Monday, shows Trump's approval rating among Republicans at a lofty 85%, up four points since March.

“I think he’s doing a tremendous job, really, as far as I’m concerned,” Trump supporter Wayne Sparker told the largest newspaper in a key early-voting state for the 2020 Democratic presidential primary.

The poll was conducted Nov. 8-13, before the first public congressional testimony in the impeachment investigation into Trump's July 25 phone call with Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky.
