Ouachita Parish investigators searching for missing woman

18 Nov 2019, 15:52 by KNOE

OUACHITA PARISH, La. (KNOE) - The Ouachita Parish Sheriff's Office is looking for a woman reported missing.

They are trying to find 43-year-old Sherry Maum.

Maum was last seen in Ouachita Parish on November 9, 2019.

She is described as a White Female, 5’5” tall and weighing 105 lbs.

OPSO says Maum has no permanent address but frequents the Calhoun and Bawcomville areas.

If you know where to find Sherry Maum, you are asked to call the Ouachita Parish Sheriff’s Office at 318-329-1200.
