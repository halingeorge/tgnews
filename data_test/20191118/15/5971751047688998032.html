Elisa’s 5G network powered by Nokia is super fast

18 Nov 2019, 15:21 by Marin

Nokia is empowering the 5G network of Finland’s operator Elisa. Harri Holma from Nokia just demonstrated how great the download speeds over Elisa’s 5G network will be in the city of Espoo, Finland. The maximum speed achieved was greater than 1.14 Gbps, which is crazy when compared to the speeds we currently have with 4G networks. One gigabyte can be downloaded in just 10 seconds, and that sounds great. Nokia’s engineers did a good job.
