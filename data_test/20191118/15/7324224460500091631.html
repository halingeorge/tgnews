Paramedics called as car crashes into post in West Bromwich

A man and a woman were taken to hospital after the car they were in crashed into a post.

18 Nov 2019, 15:29 by Jordan Reynolds

The crash happened just before 3pm today near Tesco Extra and New Square Shopping Centre in West Bromwich.

The hatchback the man and woman were in was left with severe front-end damage and oil could be seen leaking from the vehicle.

National Express buses are being diverted while emergency services deal with the crash.
