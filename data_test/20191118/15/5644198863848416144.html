China to launch inspection on use of illegal African swine fever vaccines

18 Nov 2019, 15:15

BEIJING: China's agriculture ministry said on Monday (Nov 18) it will launch an investigation into the illegal production, sale and use of African swine fever vaccines in the country, citing online reports of their use by some farmers.

No country has yet approved a vaccine for the usually deadly hog disease, although several institutes around the world are trying to develop one.
