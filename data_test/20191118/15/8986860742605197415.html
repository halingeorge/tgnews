Photo of a young guy enjoying Gari soakings with sliced tomatoes goes viral

18 Nov 2019, 15:51 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Gari soakings aka Agyenkwa is one food that comes in handy when most people are broke especially in the boarding houses and students in the hostel.

Even though it’s easy to be made, the way you enjoy eating your gari is up to you.

While some prefer to add milk to the ingredients others, on the other hand, like to change the taste by adding yogurt and biscuit.

Well, a young man has taken gari a notch higher by adding tomatoes.

On social media, the twitter user with the handle @lemerstachu shared a photo of a gari soaking he is about to take which has gotten people talking.
