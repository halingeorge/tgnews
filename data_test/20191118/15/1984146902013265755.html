Friends' Central Perk set is coming to the Metrocentre but only for a few days

The set for classic American comedy series Friends will take up residence at intu Metrocentre in Gateshead

18 Nov 2019, 15:00 by Simon Duke

Feel like you're always stuck in second gear? Well, we've got some news that should have you hurtling full throttle towards a certain shopping centre this week.

If you are a fan of Friends, you will most definitely want to head to intu Metrocentre this week as a set from the classic American comedy is taking up residence in Gateshead to celebrate the screening of some of the show's most iconic episodes at Odeon cinema.

The news comes just as Aldi prepares to launch its bargain Friends range for Christmas.

Odeon Metrocentre will be home to a replica of the famous Central Perk set in their foyer for the perfect photo opp for lovers of all thinks Rachel, Ross et al.

Pop along to the cinema from Thursday, November 21 to Monday, November 25 for some social worthy snaps. Tag yourself in at intu Metrocentre using the #friendsatINTU so we everyone can see your snaps.

The Central Perk sofa's arrival at the Metrocentre ties in with the screenings of a whole host of classic Friends episodes at Odeons across the country as the series turns a staggering 25 years old!

Friends 25th: The One With The Anniversary is a celebration of all things Friends.

With 12 episodes in total showing across 3 dates in December (8, 11 and 15 - 4 episodes each date) you can watch some of your favourite episodes of the hit TV show with your very own friends, alongside other ‘Frienatics’ or even take your ‘lobster’.
