SPA Vs ROM Dream11 : Table toppers Spain look to wrap up their thriving qualification process with another win when they take on a lacklustre Romania side.

Romania needed a win in their last outing against Sweden to make today’s contest against Spain meaningful, but unfortunately for the side, a 2-0 defeat in that clash meant today’s encounter is an inconsequential one for both sides. Where Spain have assured themselves of not only qualification to Euro 2020 but of first place in Group F as well, Romania’s aspirations of making it into the showpiece have come to an end.

The only team to have enjoyed an unbeaten campaign in Group F of the Euro 2020 qualifying process, for Spain this journey could prove to be a defining moment in their bid of revitalising their sojourn in the footballing fraternity. Followed up by a dismal outing in the world cup, the side went for a complete overhaul in a bid to stop being underachievers, roping in a slew of new players as the side looked to embark on a successful sortie on the global stage.

They come into today’s encounter having tasted consummate success against Malta the last time around, a 7-0 routing which was a testament to the massive strides the team has had over the course of this qualification round. And when they take on Romania today in their culminating group stage match, the Spanish will be looking to revel their rapturous supporters in a performance reminiscent of the last match.

For Romania on the other hand, although a win won’t change the shape of the group, a favourable result for them will see them set a statement and lay down the marker for their future travails. Things will be extremely jittery for them today but against a Spain side which is bound to shuffle its side around and give a slew of their lesser played players chances in this one, Romania will be well aware that reslliance and resolve could end up going a long way in asserting a win for them today.
