Young PE man reported missing at Cape Town beach

18 Nov 2019, 15:29 by Algoa FM

A young Port Elizabeth man is one of two people who were reported missing in the ocean at Strandfontein in Cape Town.

NSRI spokesperson, Craig Lambinon, said their Strandfontein duty crew was activated on Monday morning following reports of two people in difficulty in the water at a place known as 'Garbage Beach'.

He said various other role-players also responded to the scene, including the Skymed helicopter, to search for two males, "a 17-year-old male from Delft in Cape Town and a 22-year-old male believed to be from Port Elizabeth and visiting friends in Delft".

"A police dive unit has been activated to continue in an ongoing search operation," he said.
