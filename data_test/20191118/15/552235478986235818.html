Springholm Primary kids enjoy Hallowe'en party as well as football coaching and curling lessons

Queen of the South coaches have been helping with their football while P7 headed to Dumfries for some curling

18 Nov 2019, 15:38 by Stuart Gillespie, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/stuart-gillespie/

P7 children at Springholm enjoyed a Hallowe’en party at the school.

They played corners, musical statues, dooking for apples, decorating biscuits, pin the nose on the pumpkin, pin the wart on the witch and music.

 

They are also currently enjoying an eight week block of coaching from Queen of the South FC.

And the primary seven class took part in curling lessons at Dumfries Ice Bowl at that start of the month.
