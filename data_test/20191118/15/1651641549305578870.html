Microsoft To Do for iOS updated with smart due dates feature

18 Nov 2019, 15:26 by Pradeep

Microsoft today released an updated To Do app for iPhone and iPad. This v2.5 update comes with new rich entry task bar that allows you to set due dates, reminders and add a task to My Day all while creating a task.

Also, when you type something like “remind me today at 8:00” into the task name, To Do app will take advantage of natural language processing and offer you to set reminder for 8:00. In addition to these new features, this update comes with following improvements.

  • We made some color improvements to our sidebar when you’re in dark mode.
  • We updated some of our illustrations.
  • We fixed some crashes and squashed some bugs.
