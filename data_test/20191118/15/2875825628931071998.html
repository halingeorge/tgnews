Video shows group of crows repeatedly 'ice skating' down frosty roof of Cork on Ice

'We'll have to charge them next time'

18 Nov 2019, 15:42 by Andrew Byrne

A group of crows were filmed repeatedly "ice skating" down an icy roof on Monday morning.

The video was filmed in the early hours of the morning in Cork city as the birds used the ice covered roof of Cork on Ice to slide down.

Originally, the video shows just one bird performing the unusual act but as the camera spans across the roof a number of other crows are seen doing the same thing.

The footage was filmed by Paddy O'Shea on the Centre Park Road before posting the video on Twitter.

The freelance lighting technician told the Irish Mirror: "I was working on the lighting set up for Cork on Ice so I was on site early.

"There was a bunch of crows messing about on the ice covered roof of the marquee.

"I thought it was very funny that the crows were ice skating on the roof of the Cork ice rink."
