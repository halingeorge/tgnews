‘Rent a Vet’

18 Nov 2019, 15:42 by BRIEF

SAN DIEGO — Action Telecom, based in the San Diego suburb Santee, pleaded guilty to defrauding the government of $11 million in federal contracts in a “rent-a-vet” scheme, to get contracts reserved for service-disabled, veteran-owned businesses.
