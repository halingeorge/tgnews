Marvel Select Spider-Man Far From Home Figure Hitting Disney Stores Now

18 Nov 2019, 15:38 by Luke Y. Thompson

Relax. Spider-Man hasn’t switched his citizenship. He’s just visiting and respecting that gigantic Union Jack, not wielding it. Moreover, one way to recognize a Spider-Man: Far From Home figure is any symbol of the European countries he visits. Like the United Kingdom for the big climax. Additionally one might notice that his suit bears the distinct hallmarks of Peter Parker’s custom design. This one courtesy of Stark Industries’ portable costume-maker. And Marvel Select, now.

Appearing in Disney Stores as of now, this Spider-Man figure features all the black webbing detail that the Hasbro Marvel Legends version lacked. Multiple hands and the giant flag come packed in.

Furthermore, here’s the official description:

Earlier this year, Marvel’s Spider-Man: Far From Home gave fans another pulse-pounding chapter in the Spider-Man saga, sending Peter Parker around the globe in service of SHIELD and showing him (and all of Earth) adjusting to a world without Tony Stark, a.k.a. Iron Man. Now, Diamond Select Toys is proud to deliver the newest Marvel Select action figure, depicting Spidey in his new duds from the end of Spider-Man: Far From Home!

Measuring approximately 7 inches tall and featuring 16 points of articulation, for all your wall-crawling and web-swinging poses, this action figure includes four interchangeable pairs of hands and a British flag and flagpole. It comes packaged in the display-ready Select action figure packaging, with side-panel artwork for shelf reference.
