Schatrax returns with new EP, SCHATRAX2501

The 12-inch, out January 17th, is the first release on his label since 2016.

18 Nov 2019, 15:50 by Steph Lee

Schatrax is back with a four-track EP of unreleased material, titled SCHATRAX2501.

It's the first release on the Schatrax label since 2016, when the English artist briefly revived it to continue his Vintage Vinyl reissue series.

SCHATRAX2501, in contrast, contains never before heard house tracks. Limited vinyl copies are available for pre-order on Schatrax's website, before the official release on January 17th.

Hear clips for the upcoming record on Schatrax's website.

Tracklist
A1 Folkdance
A2 Coral
B1 Sisphunk
B2 Into The Stars

Schatrax will release SCHATRAX2501 on January 17th, 2020.

Label: Schatrax
