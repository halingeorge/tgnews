Hollyoaks' Stephanie Davis reveals she has autism in emotional video

Star bravely spoke out about her struggles

18 Nov 2019, 15:54 by Carena Crawford

Stephanie Davis, who plays Sinead Shelby in Hollyoaks has posted a video to her new YouTube channel to tell fans she has "high-functioning autism".

The actress, who is currently taking a break from the soap, admitted she used alcohol to calm her anxiety and now she is learning about herself, she understands why she was doing it.

She revealed she had posted the video in a series of tweets, explaining she wants to help others as well as come to terms with the diagnosis herself.

The star also said she has been diagnosed with other mental health issues but she doesn't think she's ready to talk about them yet.
