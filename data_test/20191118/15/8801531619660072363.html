Senior Consultant Vacancy At Ministry Of Law And Justice, Department of Legal Affairs, New Delhi

18 Nov 2019, 15:36 by Manav Malhotra

Ministry of Law and Justice, Department of Legal Affairs, New Delhi invites application for the post of Senior Consultant.

Name of the Post: Senior Consultant

Age Limit: 40 Years

Remuneration: Rs. 50,000/-

Essential Qualification and Experience

  • Must have at least a bachelor's degree in law with minimum post-qualification experience of ten years.
  • Knowledge and experience in the fields of Finance, Administration, and management will be preferred (for both fresh as well as retired employee).

How to apply?

  • The interested candidates are required to submit in their application in the prescribed format.
  • The envelope containing the applicant's details as mentioned above should be clearly labelled "Application for the post of Consultant in the Department of Legal Affairs, and addressed to Shri A. K. Joshi, Deputy Secretary, Room No. 418-A-Wing, Ministry of Law & Justice, Department of Legal Affairs, Shastri Bhavan, New Delhi-110001 and should reach on or before 23.11.2019.
