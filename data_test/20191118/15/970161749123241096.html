Harry Styles 'has no regrets about making a dig at Zayn Malik'

18 Nov 2019, 15:54

Harry Styles doesn’t regret making a dig at Zayn Malik during his Saturday Night Live opening monologue.

The Watermelon Sugar singer hosted (and sang) on the NBC sketch show for the first time and immediately joked about Zayn when talking about his One Direction pals.

A source told Hollywood Life: ‘Harry knew what he was getting into when he was joking about Zayn.

‘There has been issues with Zayn with all of the guys for a long time and this joke was simply innocent fun tinged with some reality.

‘Zayn has always been difficult, so to do that joke was on purpose and Harry doesn’t regret it.’

During his opening monologue, the 25-year-old joked: ‘I love those guys. They’re my brothers. Niall, Liam, Louis… and, uh, Ringo. Yeah, that’s it.’

Considering Zayn hasn’t kept in contact with his former band mates since being the first to leave four years ago, the joke was a relief to hear.
