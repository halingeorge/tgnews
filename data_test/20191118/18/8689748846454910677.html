Travis Greene Declares Ghana As The Country With The Best Women And Best Jollof (+Video)

18 Nov 2019, 18:51 by vanessa nordzi

American gospel singer and pastor, Travis Montorius Greene  has declared Ghana as the country with the most beautiful women and best jollof.

According to Travis Greene  Ghana has the best women. Since Travis is married to a Ghanaian, he must be speaking from experience.

Travis Greene was in Ghana for the annual “Heaven on Earth Conference” organized by the Empowerment Worship Centre, under the leadership of Prophet Gideon Danso.

This year’s event, which is themed “Thy Will be Done” saw live ministrations from Tim Godfrey of “Nara Ekele Mo” fame and EWC Live.

During his ministration Travis Greene stated that he is always excited to be in Ghana as they serve the best jollof and best women.

“There is no place in the world like Ghana, Ghana has the best Jollof rice and the best women because I married one”  he stated.

Travis Greene began his music career in 2007 with the release of ‘The More’ via Greenelight Records. He has since released two studio albums, ‘Stretching Out’ in 2010, ‘The Hill’ in 2015, and an extended play, ‘Intentional’ in 2015.
