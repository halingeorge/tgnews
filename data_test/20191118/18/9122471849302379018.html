Adam Schefter Has Injury Update For Colts RB Marlon Mack

18 Nov 2019, 18:35 by Alek Arend

When Colts RB Marlon Mack gets going, it’s tough to slow down the Indianapolis offense. Mack was a key piece in the Colts’ 33-13 victory over Jacksonville on Sunday. But the win came with a cost.

The running back suffered a right hand fracture during the game. According to the latest report from ESPN’s Adam Schefter, Mack will undergo surgery today and “is out indefinitely.”
