Report: Eddie Van Halen Released From Hospital Following Complications From Throat Cancer Treatment

18 Nov 2019, 18:37 by Shakiel Mahjouri

Eddie Van Halen is on the mend.

The rockstar was reportedly admitted to the hospital last week, according to TMZ. Van Halen, 64, was suffering from intestinal issues and abdominal pains. The reaction is believed to have been caused by drugs Van Halen is taking for his throat cancer.

Van Halen was reportedly released from the hospital on Sunday. Not the time to take bed rest, Van Halen instead spent time rehearsing with his 28-year-old son Wolfgang. The rocker’s son has been his bassist since 2006.

Van Halen was initially diagnosed with throat cancer in 2000. He underwent surgery and doctors removed a third of his tongue. He has continued getting cancer treatment over the last five years.
