Here Are All The Winners Of The 2019 BET Soul Train Awards

18 Nov 2019, 18:48 by Chris Mench

The 2019 BET Soul Train Awards went down on Sunday night, honoring the best of soul, R&B, and hip-hop music. This year’s big winners include 2019 breakout stars like Summer Walker and Lizzo as well as more established acts like Beyoncé, Chris Brown, Cardi B, Khalid, and Kirk Franklin. The evening included performances from Wale, Pink Sweat$, Queen Naija, SiR, and K. Michelle.

Check out all the winners below (listed in bold at the top of the category):

  • Lucky Daye
  • Mahalia
  • Nicole Bus
  • Pink Sweat$
  • YK Osiris
