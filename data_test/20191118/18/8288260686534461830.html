Police probe as pregnant mum and two young daughters vanish from Newcastle-under-Lyme Travelodge

Hajar Mohammed was heavily pregnant when she was last seen

18 Nov 2019, 18:42 by Jordan Coussins, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/jordan-coussins/

A desperate plea to find a heavily pregnant mum who went missing with her two daughters more than two months ago has been launched by detectives.

Staffordshire Police issued an urgent appeal to find Hajar Mohammed, aged 35, and her daughters, eight-year-old Reem and six-year-old Marjam.

They were last seen leaving a Travelodge in Lower Street, Newcastle-under-Lyme, at around 5.30pm on Tuesday, September 10.

 

Despite enquiries being made by the force's Missing Person Enquiry Team, the family has not been located.

Detectives from Force CID are now investigating and want help to locate the family to ensure they are safe and well.

Hajar is 5ft 3ins, of Middle Eastern appearance and was heavily pregnant when she was last seen. She was wearing a beige head scarf and a black floor-length dress.

 

Marjam and Reem were last seen wearing matching clothing including a pink and light-grey rain coat, a grey t-shirt with unicorn details, light-blue jeans with a unicorn at the top of left leg and trainers that matched their coats.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 304 of 12 September, call 101 or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
