VIDEO: “Never in my pastoral job will I work for God to take the glory” – Rev. Obofour vows

18 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Adu Sarpeah

Founder of Anointed Palace Chapel (APC), Kwaku Agyei Antwi, popularly known as Rev. Obofour has stated categorically that never in his pastoral job will he work for God to take the glory.

In a viral footage sighted by ghbase.com, the “Iron Boy” Prophet noted that the Ultimate Reality otherwise known as God does not share His glory with anyone, therefore, he’s also following same footprints in his pastoral job.

Nonetheless, it’s not clear what resulted in his outburst against God but taking into consideration his demeanour in the viral footage, Rev. Obofour seems serious with his weird or better still blasphemous proclamation.
