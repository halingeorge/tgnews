Q&A amounted to a TV version of the meme 'OK Boomer'

18 Nov 2019, 18:53 by Neil McMahon

If you tuned into the ABC on Monday night hoping to watch Australia Shuts Up For A Minute you would have been disappointed.

Instead, Australia was talking – and talking, and talking some more – in a program imaginatively titled Australia Talks, followed up by a special edition of Q&A inspired by the program, which was in turn inspired by the survey.
