TMC MP Nusrat Jahan, Admitted to Hospital For Breathing Problems, Returns Home Safe

TMC MP from Basirhat Nusrat Jahan was admitted to Apollo Hospital in Kolkata on Sunday due to respiratory problems.

18 Nov 2019, 18:54

New Delhi: Trinamool Congress (TMC) MP from Basirhat Nusrat Jahan, who was admitted to a hospital in Kolkata after she complained of breathing problems, on Monday got discharged from the hospital after her health condition improved, news agency ANI reported.
