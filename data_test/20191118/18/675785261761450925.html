Football Manager 2020 wonderkids: FM20 best young players in the game

There's nothing better than unearthing the next Lionel Messi, and with this extensive list you might just be able to sign him before anyone else gets wind - and for a bargain price

18 Nov 2019, 18:30 by Jake Polden, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/jake-polden/

Football Manager has returned with its latest offering for 2020.

The extensive database has been updated and there are new tactics, and ways of deploying them, for you to get your head around.

But more important than formations, tactics, team talks and press conferences is transfers.

Well, is there anything better than signing the next Lionel Messi for just £25,000 rather than the current one on £400,000 per week? Nope. No there isn't.

We've been getting to grips with the new game and have compiled a list of must-buy wonderkids.

Some are affordable, some not so much, while some will be willing to sign for your team, and... some not so much.

Either way all the players listed below are future world-beaters and if you purchase a handful of these you'll be tasting success in no time at all.
