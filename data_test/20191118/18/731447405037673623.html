Flørist debuts on The Trilogy Tapes with 4 Letter Word EP

The four-track 12-inch is out this week.

18 Nov 2019, 18:12 by Andrew Ryce

The Trilogy Tapes is releasing a new EP from Flørist.

4 Letter Word is Canadian artist Logan Sturrock's first EP for the label. It was put together in the first few months after his move to Berlin, according to an Instagram post.

"I had just moved my entire life to a new continent," he said. "Lots of things were up in the air ,and I felt like I was losing my mind at times (I still do), but the support and love from friends makes this all possible."

The new EP follows the launch of Sturrock's Pavilion label, with another Flørist EP, last month.
