Apple just released iOS 13.2.3 and iPadOS 13.2.3 to the public

18 Nov 2019, 18:06 by Zach Epstein

Apple fans, we have good news and bad news. The good news is that Apple just released new updates for it mobile devices. The bad news is that unfortunately, iOS 13.2.3 and iPadOS 13.2.3 don’t include the major RAM management fix that speeds up iPhones and iPads by fixing the big multitasking bug everyone has been complaining about. You’ll have to wait for iOS 13.3 and iPadOS 13.3 for those fixes (or just install the beta). In the meantime, iOS 13.2.3 and iPadOS 13.2.3 have plenty of other bug fixes so definitely install these updates as soon as possible.

As we’re sure you know by now, installing a new iOS 13.2.3 or iPadOS beta on your iPhone, iPad or iPod touch couldn’t be easier. Just navigate to Settings > General > Software Update and then tap “Download and Install” at the bottom of that page. If you want, you can also install the update through iTunes by connecting your iOS device to a computer. Whichever method you choose, just make sure to back up your device before installing the update.
