ROAD SMASH

Woman rushed to hospital after being knocked down by car outside Bearsden primary school

18 Nov 2019, 18:13 by Justin Bowie

A WOMAN was rushed to hospital after being knocked down by a car outside a Bearsden Primary School.

Emergency services raced to the scene at around 4.10pm after the grim road smash on Duntocher Road in the town.

It happened near to St Nicholas' Primary school.

The road was locked down by cops as the pedestrian injured in the crash was taken to hospital.

Her condition is currently unknown.

And Duntocher Road remains closed around the scene of the incident.

A Police Scotland spokesperson said: "We were called at around 4.10pm to a crash on Duntocher Road involving a car and female pedestrian.

"The pedestrian was taken to hospital.

"Her condition is currently unknown."
