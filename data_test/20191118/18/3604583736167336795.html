New homes for tragedy-hit Mount Pleasant road

18 Nov 2019, 18:30 by Jolyon Attwooll

Six new social housing residences are planned at Mount Pleasant sites where a man died at work and a woman was killed in unexplained circumstances.

The DHHS has recently lodged a planning application for numbers 1 and 2 Kenworthy Place in the suburb in the south-east of Ballarat.

Number 1 Kenworthy Place was where a Ballarat woman, Dannyll Goodsell, died. Her body was found in her partly burnt home on October 5 last year.

On October 23, the address was the scene of another fire, which was described as "suspicious".

Scott Cameron, the former partner of Ms Goodsell, pleaded not guilty to her murder last month.

The other property listed in planning application was 2 Kenworthy Place, where a construction worker died on September 11 this year.

The 56-year-old Horsham man was working alongside one other person to demolish the house when the wall collapsed.
