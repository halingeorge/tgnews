'Fits like a dream' - The praise Nottingham Forest fans have ahead of possible Michael Dawson return

Nottingham Forest latest | Dawson came through the Forest academy and is into the second season of his second spell at the City Ground as he looks to help the Reds achieve promotion

18 Nov 2019, 18:06 by Josh Murray

Nottingham Forest captain Michael Dawson could be set for a return from injury against Bristol City this weekend.

The centre-back - who celebrates his 36th birthday today (Monday) and began his career at the City Ground- has not featured for the Reds since sustaining a calf injury in the 3-2 victory over Stoke City in September.

Before that, he had played every minute for Forest in the Championship this season during an eye-catching start for Sabri Lamouchi's side, losing only one of their opening nine fixtures.

Despite losing two of the last six in which Dawson has been missing, only Leeds United have conceded fewer than Forest's 13 goals in the League.
