Adult Redskins Fan Snatches Ball Away From Kid After Jets Touchdown (VIDEO)

18 Nov 2019, 18:16 by Darrelle Lincoln

Grown people behaving terribly at sporting events continues to be a yearly thing, and every single incident just makes you shake your head.

The New York Jets played the Washington Redskins at FedEx Field on Sunday, and it turned into a complete blowout as the road team enjoyed more success in the end zone than the home team.

At one point during the team, Jets wide receiver Robby Anderson took a Lambo Leap into the crowd after scoring, but when he gave the ball to a young fan, it would be snatched out of that kid’s hands by a grown ass man.
