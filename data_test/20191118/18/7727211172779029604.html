Michelle Keegan looks 'unrecognisable' in new photo which stuns fans

The Our Girl star posed with her cute dog Pip

18 Nov 2019, 18:33 by Sophie McCoid

Michelle Keegan stunned fans with her latest photo - with some saying she looks 'unrecognisable'.

Our Girl actress Michelle shared a photo of her glammed up in a black leather corset, with long diamond earrings and her make-up done to perfection.

And she cradled her adorable pet Chihuahua Pip in her arms.

Michelle, who also played Tina McIntyre in Coronation Street, captioned the photo: "Make up & hair did ✅ Beauty light ✅ Dog upstaging me."

And her 4.1m followers were quick to comment on her new look - reports the Manchester Evening News.

Tamara_arabella_x said: Llook how different she looks? Xxx"

m18knk said: "I didn’t even recognise this as you."

And sophiarose10213 said: "you ever seen someone so perfect or na."

Michelle, 32, now plays Erin in the Sky One comedy drama Brassic and army medic Lance Corporal Georgie Lane in the popular BBC One drama Our Girl.

Her soap character Tina McIntyre was brutally murdered by neighbour Rob Donovan in Corrie in 2014.

Barmaid Tina plunged from the balcony of the builders' yard before being beaten to death with a lead pipe by twisted Rob.

Last week she won hearts when she shared a photo of herself in a car clutching a bag of McDonald's goodies with the words: "Keep what you love close, and your filet-o-fish even closer."
