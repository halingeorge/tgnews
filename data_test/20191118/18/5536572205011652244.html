Max Kellerman Destroyed Stephen A. Smith’s Comments About Colin Kaepernick On ‘First Take’ (VIDEO)

18 Nov 2019, 18:57 by Darrelle Lincoln

Max Kellerman set social media on fire with his comments about Colin Kaepernick.

During Monday’s version of ‘First Take,’ the co-host first responded to Stephen A. Smith criticizing Colin Kaepernick wearing a Kunta Kinte t-shirt to his workout.

“Who cares if he’s wearing a Kunta Kinte T-shirt, isn’t the original sin of this country slavery? Is that the reason that 31 of the 32 owners are white? Yes, of course.”
