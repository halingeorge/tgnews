‘I’ll never forget’ - Director reveals John McGinn's reaction to failed transfer

Latest Aston Villa transfer news includes what McGinn said after a move fell through prior to AVFC switch

18 Nov 2019, 18:31 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Hibernian’s sporting director has revealed John McGinn’s brilliant reaction to a failed transfer prior to signing for Aston Villa.

McGinn joined Villa in the summer of 2018 and ranks as one of the club's best bargains in recent years having been snapped up for under £3m.

The midfielder played a starring role in the Championship promotion campaign and has continued to impress in the Premier League for Dean Smith’s side.

But it might have been very different had Hibs not shown such steely resolve in the face of interest from Nottingham Forest beforehand.

The Championship side tried to prise McGinn away from Easter Road two years ago but failed to agree a fee with Hibs.

Now, the Scottish Premiership club’s sporting director Graeme Mathie has spoken about what happened in more detail.

Mathie revealed Forest were offering ‘four or five times’ McGinn’s wages at Hibs – and also opened up on how he reacted when the proposed deal fell through.

 

"I'll never forget the text he (McGinn) sent back," Mathie told the Daily Record.

"He came back and said: 'Look, I get it, this is football, it's no problem at all. I'm loving being at Hibs and I want to have the best season of my career and hopefully get a move to a good club'.

"Let's be honest, while John was committed to Hibs, somebody was potentially offering to give him four or five times his money to take him to the English Championship and us, as a club, had said no to that.

"I thought that was an unbelievable thing for a young man to say."
