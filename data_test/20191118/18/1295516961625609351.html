Justice League: BossLogic Shares an Epic Snyder Cut Poster

18 Nov 2019, 18:11 by Renaldo Matadeen

Following petitions and vocal protests by Justice League fans to #ReleaseTheSnyderCut, Warner Bros. also has to contend with several of the movie's actors currently voicing similar opinions. Now, courtesy of graphic artist BossLogic, we have an idea of how a theatrical poster could look for the release.

The image, posted on BossLogic's social media, is very intimidating, with the Lord of Apokolips, Darkseid, front and center. We can see the silhouette of his general, Steppenwolf, just below, and the Justice League's shadows even further beneath, making it clear Darkseid's the focal point of the piece, which even has an HBO Max tag on it.
