Stephen King Finally Gets His Own POP! Vinyl Toys from Funko

18 Nov 2019, 18:37 by John Squires

Countless Stephen King characters have been turned into POP! vinyl toys by Funko over the years, and now King himself has finally been immortalized with his own Funko toys!

Two different Stephen King POP! toys are on the way:

  • Black shirt & grey pants – holding book
  • Barnes & Noble Exclusive – blood-covered, holding axe & book

Both of these are listed as “Coming Soon.”
