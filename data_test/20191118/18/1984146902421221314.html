How old is Caitlyn Jenner? I'm A Celebrity appearance for American star

The lineup for the new series of I'm A Celebrity Get Me Out of Here includes American star Caitlyn Jenner

18 Nov 2019, 18:30 by Simon Duke

Let's get ready to juuuungle! Yes, it's time for the return of I'm A Celebrity and on the lineup for the new series is none other than Caitlyn Jenner.

It's hard to believe that it's been 17 years since I'm A Celebrity first hit our screens, with Tony Blackburn the ITV show's first ever winner.

Since then the likes of Kerry Katona, Dougie Poynter, Christopher Biggins, Vicky Pattison and Scarlett Moffatt have all triumphed in the jungle and we know who'll be following in their footsteps this year.

The I'm A Celebrity lineup for this year includes Ian Wright, Nadine Coyle, Kate Garraway and Caitlyn.

Born Bruce Jenner, in April 2015 Caitlyn announced that she was a trans woman, having experienced gender dysphoria since childhood.

The news was made public with a now famous Vanity Fair cover shoot and in a candid TV interview with US host Diane Sawyer, Caitlyn said “I’m so happy after such a long struggle to be living my true self. Welcome to the world Caitlyn. Can’t wait for you to get to know her/me.”

How old is Caitlyn Jenner?

Born on October 28 1949, Caitlyn is now 70 years old.

She has six children Kylie Jenner, Kendall Jenner, Brody Jenner, Brandon Jenner, Cassandra Marino and Burt Jenner.

Caitlyn has previous I'm A Celeb experience, having starred on the American version well over a decade ago.
