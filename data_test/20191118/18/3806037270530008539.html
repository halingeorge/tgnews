Sony Reveals PlayStation 4 Black Friday & Cyber Monday 2019 Deals

Including a deal for the PlayStation 4 Pro.

18 Nov 2019, 18:01 by Isaac Rouse

Although Nintendo won’t be announcing any this year, Sony has announced all the PlayStation 4 deals being offered for this 2019’s Black Friday and Cyber Monday, which will extend from November 24 through December 2 (December 6 in Canada). One of the deals announced includes a $100 USD off sale on Sony’s PlayStation 4 Pro, and another offers a 1TB PlayStation PS4 Bundle offer that comes with The Last of Us: Remastered, God of War, and Horizon Zero Dawn: Complete Edition for $199 USD.

There’s also a PlayStation VR multi-game deal for $199.99 USD that comes with a PS VR Headset, a PS Camera, and vouchers to download Astro Bot Rescue Mission, Skyrim VR, Resident Evil 7: Biohazard, Everybody’s Golf VR, and PlayStation VR Worlds. Check out the full deals below, then watch the announcement trailer for Gill in Street Fighter V: Champion Edition.
