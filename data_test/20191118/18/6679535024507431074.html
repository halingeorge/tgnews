‘TS a role model for its people-centric policies’

Vice-Chairman of TSPB pats officials for effective implementation of the policies

18 Nov 2019, 18:09 by Special Correspondent

Telangana has become a role model for other States in the country for the people-centric policies of the government, and the success of the policies lies in effective implementation by the officials, Vice Chairman of Telangana State Planning Board B. Vinod noted on Monday.

Felicitation function

Mr. Vinod was speaking at an event organised to felicitate the candidates who were selected to various wings of the government through the Group II examinations conducted by the Telangana State Public Service Commission.

The candidates had undergone training at the city-based Prashasan Study Circle founded by former TSPSC member T. Vivek.

Additional Director General of Police B. Shivadhar Reddy urged the new recruits to discharge their duties with honesty.

109 qualified

As many as 109 candidates who attended the mock interviews organised by the study circle qualified, Mr. Vivek explained.

Senior officers including Additional Commissioner-GST S. Jayakameshwari; Deputy Commissioner-Excise S. Y. Qureshi; Joint Inspector General Registration and Stamps V. Srinivasulu apart from a number of retired bureaucrats spoke on the occasion.
