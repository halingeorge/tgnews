2019 MTN 4Syte Music Video Awards: Watch Medikal’s performance with Ghana Jesus, Mmebusem

18 Nov 2019, 18:10 by Nana Yaw

This year’s MTN 4Syte Music Video Awards saw performances from A-list artists such as Sarkodie, Stonebwoy, R2Bees, Shatta Wale and others but one of them that took our breath away was Medikal’s performance.

He did it in a way I think many will copy in their subsequent performances. He brought sensational Kumawood actor, Mmebusem who now parades himself as ‘Ghana Jesus’ on stage.

The comic actor who was on stage with the “Omo Ada” hitmaker and their performance on the night left the audience screaming with laughter.

They had an interaction midway in the performance where Mmebusem pronounced that Medikal used the talent he gave him to save Ghana’s rap music.
