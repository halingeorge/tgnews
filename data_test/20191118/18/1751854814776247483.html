It looks like Xiaomi fans are in for a treat. The Chinese electronics giant has established a partnership with one of the country’s most popular online retailers, Gearbest to launch the Xiaomi Black Friday 2019 Sale.

As part of the recently kicked-off promo, Gearbest users will be able to avail of noteworthy discounts on a wide range of Xiaomi-branded products. To expedite and simplify the search process, all products have carefully been added to specific categories including Hot Picks, Cool Coupon, Cool Electronics, Smart Life and Xiaomi Life.

First off, the promotion page features Xiaomi’s recently unveiled Mi Note 10 (CC9 Pro) smartphone with 6GB RAM and 128GB of internal storage space. The aforesaid model would normally set you back a pretty penny, but you can now buy it for just $549.99 under the New Arrival category.

This is a 9 percent reduction in the world’s first 108MP Xiaomi phone, which usually retails for $606.62. The black, white, as well as green color options of the phone,  will be available for a lowered price for nineteen days.

The following section dubbed Hot Picks features a slew of Xiaomi’s well-received mobile devices. More importantly, you can get your hands on these phones without burning a hole in your pocket as they’d be bearing lowered prices during the activity period.

Aside from the mobile phone, the category features some of Xiaomi’s highly popular devices including the Mi Ruby Notebook, the Mi AirDots, the Xiaomi Wear Smart Bluetooth Watch and lots more. It is worth mentioning here that Gearbest users will be able to use specific coupon codes with each item to get great discounts.

For instance, the Xiaomi Mi Ruby Notebook usually carries a steep $969.87 price tag, but you can bring its original asking price down to only $529.99 simply by applying coupon code GBXMRUBY01 during the checkout process.

If you are a coupon chaser, you might want to check out the following section called Cool Coupon, wherein each item has been assigned a dedicated coupon code. To get discounts, all you need to do is to apply these coupons before you click the ‘Place Your Order’ button.

Electronics lovers can head straight to the Cool Electronics section, which features everything from a smart bracelet, to earphones, smart cameras, dash cams and lots more. Each item included under this section will be up for grabs for lowered prices during the promotion sale.

Featuring a slew of smart appliances that come in handy in day to day life, the Smart Life, as well as the Xiaomi Life categories include heavily-discounted devices that usually cost a bomb but are now available for considerably lowered prices. For instance, the Xiaomi Mi Smart Robot Vacuum Cleaner usually retails for $399, but you can purchase it for only $245.99.

This is a noteworthy 38 percent discount and it will be valid for only eleven days. To ensure you do not end missing this opportunity, simply visit this link and start placing orders for your favorite products before the activity period ends.
