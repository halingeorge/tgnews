Nokia 7 Plus got the November security patch update

18 Nov 2019, 18:37 by Marin

Nokia 7 Plus started receiving the November security patch update from Google. The update is 97.1 MB large and just brings the security patch. I just updated our 7 Plus, and the installation was nice and quick. The phone is working well, so pick up your 7 Plus and make it secure and up to date.

By the way, good folks from HMD shared an info that Nokia 7.2 with the WW_1_200 Build number should be receiving the combined October and November security patch.
