Ant McPartlin rocks a gold crown to celebrate 44th birthday in Australia

18 Nov 2019, 18:49

Ant McPartlin was the centre of attention as he rocked a gold crown for his birthday dinner with bestie Declan Donnelly and their other halves.

The TV host celebrated his birthday – which is today – the best way anyone could, by wearing an inflatable crown and eating endless food with his loved ones.

Both Ant and Dec took a break from hosting I’m A Celebrity Get Me Out Of Here! to ring in Ant’s special day at The Crafty Cows Restaurant.

His girlfriend Anne-Marie Corbett, Dec’s wife Ali Astall and baby Isla were there to sing the star an embarrassing ‘Happy Birthday’.

Ant started the celebrations with a non-alcoholic drink while chatting away to his girlfriend and friends.

The TV host is back on I’m A Celeb after taking a year off.

He pulled out of hosting in 2018 after he stepped away following a drink driving conviction and battles with alcohol and prescription drugs.

Ant and Dec’s Instagram page shared a picture of a chocolate cake that was cut into Ant’s name.

The cake was sprinkled with M&M’s as well as M&M chocolate bars because why not.

Holly Willoughby, who replaced the host last year, wished him a ‘Happy birthday’ underneath the post and added several love hearts.

Dragon’s Den investor Peter Jones and Irish actress Laura Whitmore also wished the host well on his big day.

Meanwhile, fans have been absolutely loving Ant rejoining Dec as host of the new series of the series but they’re already missing Holly.

Fans were happy Ant was back by Dec’s side in the jungle, but social media was divided as Holly proved to be popular the year before.

One fan wrote on Twitter: ‘So happy to see @antanddec back together again for @imacelebrity but I do miss @hollywills.’

And another tweeted: ‘I miss Holly Willoughby already, not gonna lie.’

While one mother wrote: ‘My little boy has just said the jungle is strange without Holly, he loved you last year.’

Not even one ad break in, however, and people began rushing to Ant’s defence.

One fan tweeted: ‘So glad Ant is reunited with Dec on I’m A Celebrity there (sic) a great pair together. [I] mean @hollywills done a great job last year but it’s always great when them two are together.’
