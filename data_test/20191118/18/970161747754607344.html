Who plays Lydia's mum Agatha in Emmerdale?

18 Nov 2019, 18:05

Lydia Hart (Karen Blick) has had a number of shocks over the last few weeks in Emmerdale, none more so than Mandy Dingle’s (Lisa Riley) discovery that her mum Agatha, who they thought was dead, is actually alive.

In tonight’s episode, Lydia will finally come face-to-face with the mother she previously thought of as having died in a shock twist that will also hold some further life-changing news for Lydia.

But as Agatha tries to explain herself and what has come to pass to a dumbfounded Lydia, who is the actress that has been cast to play the role?

Who plays Lydia’s mum Agatha in Emmerdale?

Lydia’s mum Agatha is played by actress Judith Barker, a British actress who is best known for playing Janet Reid in Coronation Street.

Judith appeared in Corrie between 1969 and 1977 and her character was Ken Barlow’s second wife.
