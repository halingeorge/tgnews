The best Christmas Eve box fillers and pre-filled gift sets for 2019

18 Nov 2019, 18:16 by Precious Adesina

With Christmas around the corner, the young ones of the household will be finding it hard not to rip open all their perfectly wrapped presents. While you've probably become a pro at stopping them, a new tradition has taken the UK by storm that may just rid you of this problem entirely: The Christmas Eve box. 

Like many of the festive traditions we have adopted today, the giving of Christmas Eve boxes is said to have come from a German custom of opening presents on Christmas Eve. Rather than opening all the gifts a day early, the boxes in Britain include snacks, toys, memorabilia and lounge wear; things to get the recipient in the Christmas spirit. You can buy them pre-made or you can create one yourself.

If you've decided to go down the route of making one and are struggling with filler ideas, we've rounded up a list of the best Christmas Eve box fillers below. But, we also understand that, while many people enjoy making their own, sometimes there just isn't enough time do to that with all the other Christmas-related tasks that are on the agenda. That's why we've added in the best pre-made Christmas Eve boxes for both adults and children to make the search a little easier for you.
