Temasek-backed start-up seeks to cool Singapore in a 'greener' way

18 Nov 2019, 18:15 by Amala Balakrishner

SINGAPORE (Nov 18) : A US start-up backed by Temasek Foundation, the non-profit arm of Singapore’s state-owned investment firm, is set to beat Singapore’s humidity with a new bladeless fan.

Temasek Foundation is investing US$1 million (S$1.4 million) in Phononic to help develop the pilot project, called Outdoor Active Cooling in Singapore (OACIS). Eight OACIS fans are currently on trial in Singapore.
