Visa-on-arrival facility sought for UAE citizens

18 Nov 2019, 18:21 by Special Correspondent

M.K. Raghavan, MP, has urged Minister for External Affairs S. Jaishankar to extend the facility of visa-on-arrival to the citizens of UAE at the Calicut airport.

The Embassy of India, UAE, introduced the facility to all citizens of UAE from November 16 aimed at boosting trade, commerce and tourism. As of now the facility is available at six designated international airports at Bengaluru, Chennai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Kolkata and Mumbai.

Mr. Raghavan said a large number of UAE citizens frequent Kozhikode. More than a million Indians in the UAE are from Kerala. Of them, 35% are from central and south Malabar, he said.
