Violence erupts in Bigg Boss house as Asim Riaz and Siddharth Shukla indulge in ugly fight

18 Nov 2019, 18:33 by JKR Staff

In an extraordinary development, two known friends namely Siddharth Shukla and Asim Riaz will indulge in a big fight on Tuesday. A promo released by Colors TV has shown Siddharth and Asim violently pushing each other in anger.

The reason for the fight is not known, but the video released by Colors TV shows Siddharth asking Asim for oranges. Asim refuses to oblige, making Siddharth lose his cool. When asked by Siddharth why he had refused to give him oranges, Asim told him that he did not appreciate his angry approach.

An incensed Siddharth then pushes Asim violently. But unlike the last time, this time Asim refuses to take it lying down and pushes Siddharth back violently too. Soon, housemates intervene pushing both of them away.

The violence involving the two erstwhile friends came as a shock since both had made amends after the last week’s brewing tension. On Monday night, Siddharth had nominated Asim to be saved from this week’s nomination. While explaining his reason, Siddharth had spoken highly of his friendship with Asim.

The fight between Asim and Siddharth has evoked strong reactions from social media users.
