What happened to Kiosk Keith on I'm A Celebrity and why has he been replaced?

The Dingo Dollar Challenges on I'm A Celebrity normally result in a visit to Kiosk Keith but not anymore

18 Nov 2019, 18:21 by Simon Duke

On Monday night viewers saw the first Dingo Dollar Challenge of I'm A Celebrity's new series but there was something, or rather someone, missing again - Kiosk Keith.

In years gone by we've seen straight-faced and tough-talking Keith take no prisoners as campmates have got plenty of questions wrong, resulting in him pulling the shutters down on his kiosk rather than handing them a treat.

But this time round, just like last year, he has been replaced by, and while his replacement is just as ruthless, fans of I'm A Celebrity have once again been left wondering where Kiosk Keith was.

"Remember in Neighbours when Lucy Robinson was sent to boarding school and she came home from the holidays and she was a completely different actress?" explained Declan Donnelly to co-host Holly Willoughby last year.

"Well it's a little bit like that."

But there seems to be more to it.

Twelve months ago The Sun alleged the 53-year-old was dropped from the show after bad behaviour at work.

A source told the paper he turned up drunk and behaved inappropriately towards a female colleague.

The source alleged: “It wasn’t unusual for Keith to turn up to work drunk as he’s done it before but this incident has shocked everyone.”
