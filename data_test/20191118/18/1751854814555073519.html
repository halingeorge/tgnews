While online shoppers prepare the upcoming Black Friday sale, leading e-tailers are coming up with big promotion sales to give them something to cheer about.

Keeping in line with that, Banggood has launched a new promotion sale, wherein loyal Banggood users can get great discounts on a wide range of products across home appliances, outdoor and multiple other categories. Dubbed as the Banggood Black Friday 2019 – Home Appliances & Outdoor Sale, the recently kicked-off promo offers free gifts, free orders, products under $9.99 and lots more.

The 1/2 Price Daily activity will be valid between November 12th to November 27th. The section is loaded with highly useful products that have been assigned dedicated coupons that can be used for a discount. For example, 2 pieces of Replacement Toothbrush Heads Compatible for Soocas X1/X3/X5/V1 Soocare Electric Toothbrush usually retails for $10.23, but you can use coupon code BGBFE29 to reduce this retail price.

Products that have received 99% positive feedbacks have been placed under the Top Sellers section, and you can get noteworthy discounts on some of them. Coupon chasers might want to check out the following category called Coupon Deals which promises ‘More Goods, Less Cost.’

Each product added under the above-mentioned section has a specific coupon code assigned to it. You can apply these coupons to reduce the original asking price of the product.

For instance, the Roborock S50 Smart Robot Vacuum Cleaner would normally set you back a pretty penny, but you can use coupon code BG50MY to reduce its original price to just $462.08. This is a noteworthy reduction in the device’s selling price of $614.39.

Next off, we have the New Products section. As the name suggests, this category features the latest arrivals on Banggood. More importantly, you can avail of big discounts most of them. All products featured under the aforesaid section come in handy in day to day life.

The following category is called Home & Garden Must, and it is further divided into Hot Pick, Kitchen, Dining & Bar, Bathroom, and Gardening & Home Decor sub-categories. Likewise, the Home Appliances Must section features products that have been split into Hot Pick, Cleaning, Smart Home and Home Appliances sub-categories.

A must check-out for adventure junkies who love adventurous outdoor activities, the Outdoor Must category includes products that have been divided into Hot Pick, Bike & Bicycle, Camping, and Fishing & Hunting sub-categories to simplify and expedite the search process.

Right lightning can enhance the overall ambiance of a room, and that’s no secret. Regrettably, these devices aren’t easy on the pocketbook. To make top-end lighting accessories available to cost-conscious buyers, Banggood has also included a Light & Lighting Must category on the activity page.

If you are a brand-conscious buyer, we highly recommend you to check out the Top Brands category which features products from popular manufacturers such as DIGOO, BlitzWolf, XMUND, and Xiaomi. The concluding section is called More to Love and you can grab up to 60 percent off on all products included under this section for a limited period.

There will a $0.01 Snap Up starting from November 20th and it will be available until November 22nd. The 373 Free Gits & Free Orders activity will kick off on November 22nd and will wrap up on November 30th.

Top five spenders between November 12th to November 30th will qualify for the Free Gifts & Free Orders activity.  If your order value is over $0.01, you still stand a chance of winning free orders.
