Student injured after fall from bus

18 Nov 2019, 18:24 by Special Correspondent

A Plus Two student at a school in Thrikkakara suffered hip injuries after she was allegedly pushed out of a private bus by the conductor.

Girl’s statement

The Thrikkakara police said that they would record the statement of the girl, who was admitted at a private hospital in the city. “We will initiate action against those involved in the incident,” they said.

The incident occurred at the Judgemukku stop, Thrikkakara.

The school authorities alleged that the private bus employees were often not permitting the school students to enter the vehicles.

Police presence

Police personnel were usually present near the bus stop at the time when high school students leave the campus. However, their services were not available when the classes for Plus Two batches got over, they said.
