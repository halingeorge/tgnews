Georgia Issues Monday Update On Injured Photographer

18 Nov 2019, 18:17 by Tzvi Machlin

One of the scariest injuries to occur in Week 12 of college football befell Georgia photographer Chamberlain Smith, who was accidentally knocked out while on the sidelines.

Fortunately, it looks like Smith is going to make a full recovery from the concussion and bruises she received. Smith made that much clear in a tweet on Sunday.

On Monday, the University of Georgia provided those concerned with an update on Smith’s recovery.

Prior to head coach Kirby Smart’s press conference, a Georgia spokesperson said that Smith has returned home to recuperate. Smith is reportedly “anxious to get back to work” as well.
