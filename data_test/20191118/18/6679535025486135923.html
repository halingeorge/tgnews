Interceptor boats arrive

18 Nov 2019, 18:18 by Staff Reporter

As part of security arrangements during the visit of President Ram Nath Kovind to the Ezhimala Naval Academy on Tuesday, two high-speed interceptor boats of the Indian Navy from Kochi Naval Base arrived at the Azhikal port on Monday.

A team led by Lieutenant Navaneet and Lieutenant Ishan came by the interceptor boats T-410 and T-315 here. U. Babu Gopinath, representative of the Minister of Ports and Highways, Senior Port Conservator M. Sudheer Kumar, Officer-in-Charge Captain Pratheesh, and Port Supervisor E.T. Namith received the naval staff.
