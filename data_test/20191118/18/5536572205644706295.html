Troy Aikman Says Cowboys Need to Pay Dak Prescott After Win Over Lions (TWEET)

18 Nov 2019, 18:42 by Darrelle Lincoln

It’s about that time that people stop questioning Dak Prescott.

The Dallas Cowboys quarterback put up monster numbers once again on Sunday, throwing for 444 yards and three touchdowns on 29 of 46 attempts in Sunday’s 35-27 road win in Detroit.

This game marked his third 400-plus pass yard game of the season. Through 10 games, Prescott has four tilts of 375-plus yards and 2-plus TDs. The only other player in NFL history to accomplish that feat in 10 games is Joe Montana in 1990.

The Cowboys are averaging 444.6 total yards per game, most in franchise history.

That is a lot of history that Dak is setting, and everyone sees it, including Troy Aikman.

The hall of famer took to Twitter on Sunday and told the franchise to pay him.
