Speech and hearing therapy centre

18 Nov 2019, 19:43 by Staff Reporter

An outreach centre for speech therapy and acoustic training was established in collaboration with All India Institute of Speech and Hearing (AIISH) at Gulbarga Institute of Medical Sciences (GIMS) in Kalaburagi on Monday.

The centre will provide assessment and rehabilitative service for communication disorders, audiology, speech therapy and counselling.

Screening for Otoacoustic emission (OAE), auditory brain stem response (ABR), audiometry, auditory training and counselling and diagnostic evaluations will be carried out to evaluate newborns to identify communication disorders at an early stage.

The centre is also trying to get Assistance to Disabled Persons (ADIP) scheme for purchasing fitting aids or appliances that are sophisticated and scientifically manufactured using modern techniques.
