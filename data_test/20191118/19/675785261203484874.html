Manny Pacquiao's two-man shortlist for boxing return next spring

Filipino legend Pacquiao, who was last seen beating Keith Thurman and also defeated Adrien Broner in 2019, will be out of action until April or May next year

18 Nov 2019, 19:58 by Martin Domin, https://www.facebook.com/mirrorfighting

Manny Pacquiao will fight either Mikey or Danny Garcia when he returns to the ring next year.

The eight-weight world champion recorded impressive victories over Adrien Broner and Keith Thurman in 2019.

And despite being 40, he has no plans to retire with his next fight pencilled in for April or May.

Pacquiao will be free from his political obligations in the Philippines Senate next spring as he looks to continue his legendary career.

Mikey Garcia has not fought since he lost to Errol Spence on his welterweight debut earlier this year but plans to continue at 147lb.

His namesake Danny has alternated victories and defeats in his last four fights, including losses to Thurman and Shawn Porter.

Mikey Garcia has long campaigned to fight Pacquiao with his uncle Peter telling Fight Hype recently: “Whenever he doesn’t have a fight, he spars two times a week…but since two weeks ago, he’s been coming five times a week.

“My grandpa said, ‘He’s going to get a date soon. He doesn’t come on Tuesdays when he doesn’t have a fight coming up.’ It’s still in negotiations. It’s a fight that me, my dad and my grandpa like at welterweight.

“He decided last year after he fought Robert Easter, when he started calling out Errol Spence. All of us thought, ‘yeah, it’s a fight [against Pacquiao] he could take, if we could get ready for, it was a fight that he could possibly win.

"There was a fight that was not easier. I’m not saying Pacquiao is an easier fight, but it’s more easier in terms of size. He’s a smaller guy.

"He’s moved up a lot of weight. Errol’s a guy that has to cut weight. He’s a big guy at welterweight. The style is a lot more difficult."

Pacquiao's decision to focus on either Garcia means Britain's Amir Khan will once again miss out on a shot at his former sparring partner.
