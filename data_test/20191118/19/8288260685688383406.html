'When is Frank signing you?' - Chelsea fans deliver Aston Villa transfer demand

Latest Aston Villa news | Chelsea fans are calling on John Terry to return to Stamford Bridge after the AVFC assistant's sublime goal in training

18 Nov 2019, 19:34 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Chelsea fans want to see John Terry return to the club after the Aston Villa assistant scored a sublime volley in training.

Terry still enjoys legendary status at Stamford Bridge having left Chelsea at the end of his contract to initially join Villa as a player in 2017.

He made over 700 appearances for the club in a 22-year stint which began as a 14-year-old, winning the Premier League and FA Cup on five occasions and three League Cup as well as captaining them to Champions League and Europa League glory.

Terry hung up his boots last year and returned to Villa last October as assistant head coach to Dean Smith, who delivered promotion back to the top flight in May.

Terry - known for his deadly poaching instinct at set-pieces - showed he still has it in training.

The 38-year-old volleyed in from distance when the ball sat up just right for him with the goalkeeper standing no chance.

Predictably, Bodymoor Heath cameras caught the strike from all angles and Chelsea fans were quick to spot the clip and asked Terry to return to Stamford Bridge, reports the Daily Star.  

One supporter said: “You’ve still got it, JT. When is [Frank] Lampard signing you?”

Another commented: “Sign him up again.”

“The comeback is on,” a third said.

And another added: “Frank, sign him.”

 

Under Terry's pal Lampard, Chelsea are third in the Premier League with 26 points from 12 games having won their last six on the bounce.
