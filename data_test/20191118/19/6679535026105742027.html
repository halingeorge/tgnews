Assault: Aged woman succumbs to injuries

Hit by her son with a stone

18 Nov 2019, 19:21 by Staff Reporter

An aged woman succumbed to injuries that she had sustained after being assaulted by her son in their house near Attukal two weeks ago.

The deceased was identified as Gomathy of Kalluvila. She had sustained serious head injuries after being allegedly attacked by her son Rejikumar alias Rajeev using a stone on November 4. Ever since the attack, she was being treated at the Government Medical College Hospital. Rejikumar had been arrested..

Meanwhile, the Fort police arrested their maid, Beena, 42, of Attukal, for allegedly stealing a necklace weighing two sovereigns from Gomathy, while she had been lying in a pool of blood following the attack.
