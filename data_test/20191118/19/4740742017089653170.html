Top tip for making mouldy shower tiles look good as new - without scrubbing

It's incredibly simple - and effective

18 Nov 2019, 19:49 by Courtney Pochin, Emma Grimshaw

It's no secret that mould thrives in bathrooms as the warm, damp conditions offer the perfect breeding conditions for the slime.

Trying to get rid of it from tiles can be incredibly tricky, but one woman claims to have found a solution, reports the Mirror.

In a post on a cleaning tips and tricks Facebook group, an unnamed woman revealed she'd found a simple hack for removing mould without doing intense scrubbing - and it only cost around £2.

The woman initially posted a photo of her mouldy grout on the group, asking fellow members for suggestions on how to get them looking new.

Many people responded, sharing ideas including using toilet bowl cleaner and just re-sealing the tiles.

After trying a few of the hacks, she finally found one that really did the trick, and all she needed was cotton wool and bleach.

The woman claims she soaked pieces of cotton wool in the cleaning product and packed them all around the edges of the tiles.

She left it on the tiles overnight and by the morning, the difference was incredible.

She re-shared her original photo as well as a new one showing the results of the cleaning trick.

"Thank you to everyone who has commented," she said.

"I used cotton wool and bleach and have attached photos of the results. Wow!"
