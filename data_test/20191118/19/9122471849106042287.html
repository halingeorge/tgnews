Injury Update For Pittsburgh Steelers RB James Conner

18 Nov 2019, 19:38 by Matt Hladik

Pittsburgh Steelers running back James Conner left Thursday’s loss to the Cleveland Browns after re-aggravating a shoulder injury. Conner was one of four Steelers to go down in the first half alone.

The third-year pro had already missed two games this season due to his ailing shoulder. Fortunately, it looks like Conner dodged a major bullet this time.

According to NFL Network’s Tom Pelissero, Conner’s injury is not worse than it had been before he played on Thursday. He may be able to play this Sunday, but it is too early to tell.
