Bill Belichick Takes a Shot at Lane Johnson During Postgame Press Conference (VIDEO)

18 Nov 2019, 19:24 by Darrelle Lincoln

It was sometime in 2018 when Eagles right tackle Lane Johnson suggested that it was more fun to play for the Philadelphia Eagles than it is to play for the New England Patriots.

As you know, the Patriots don’t forget anything, but who knew it would be Bill Belichick to respond.

Following Sunday’s victory over the Eagles — Belichick used a word he almost never uses to describe an NFL game.

He said it was fun.

“They just did a good job,” Belichick said of his players. “It looked like everyone had fun out there today and we played a lot of good energy and it was good to come down here and get a win.”

“Everyone had fun out there today.”
