Bafana Hopeful Bags Euro Brace

18 Nov 2019, 19:45 by Soccer Laduma

He’s already scored 15 goals in Belgium by the age of 22, and young Kurt Abrahams is playing a vital role for Westerlo as they aim for top spot in the Belgian Proximus League.

Read: Tyson Relieved - Bafana ‘Put Ourselves Under Pressure’

The young South African attacker scored his sixth and seventh goals of the season over the weekend as he helped his side to a 5-3 victory over Roeselare, but also got his second assist of the campaign and was also involved with their other two goals as well.

Abrahams has set tongues wagging this season with performances as Westerlo look to secure promotion into the top flight come the end of the 2019/20 campaign, and his showing on Sunday was probably his best yet.

Westerlo are making moves this season and have lost just four of their opening 15 games as they push to climb up the table, though they will be facing competition from, among others, Darren Keet’s OH Leuven, whom they face on Friday night.
