Fed's Rosengren: Pretty close to 2% inflation target

18 Nov 2019, 19:44 by Ross J Burland

Eric Rosengren is president and CEO of the Federal Reserve Bank of Boston, one of 12 regional Federal Reserve banks, is crossing the wires on Bloomberg TV and has said that the Federal Reserve is pretty close to the target;

Additional comments: 

  • Pretty close to the 2% inflation target.
  • Less policy room compared with before.
  • We should think about more capital buffers for banks. 
  • Slightly worried we have less room if suffer shock.
  • Be aware of distorting financial markets to hit inflation. 
  • Prefers adopting inflation range.
  • Doesn’t like negative rates, says neg. rates would be ‘pretty disruptive’ in US.
