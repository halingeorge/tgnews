Phil Schiller Took This Amazing Photo on His iPhone 11 Pro

18 Nov 2019, 19:31 by Charlotte Henry

Top Apple exec Phil Schiller seemed to have a good time seeing Bruce Springsteen Sunday night at the Stone Pony club in Asbury Park, New Jersey, a gig that was a benefit for Boston College. Using an iPhone 11 Pro, he took a fantastic, up-close photograph of The Boss and shared it on Twitter. The level of detail, for instance on Mr. Springstreeen’s face and picking out the instruments behind, is really is quite stunning. [Edit: we updated the story to reflect where the gig was held. Thanks to Paul Kent for the heads up!]
