Luis Enrique to Arsenal: Spanish boss set for dramatic return to management

Former Barcelona manager Luis Enrique has previously been linked with a move to Arsenal

18 Nov 2019, 19:14 by Andy Ha, Lee Wilmot, Joe Doyle

Arsenal are said to have given Unai Emery a month to save his job.

The Gunners boss has overseen a downturn in form of late, with a 2-0 loss against Leicester before the international break pouring on the pressure.

Emery's side are without a win in four Premier League games, and are now eight points off the top four with just 12 games played.

There has been lots of speculation about whether the Gunners will part ways with the Spanish coach - and who could replace him if he does leave the Emirates Stadium.

One of those frequently named has been former Barcelona and Spain head coach Luis Enrique.

Here's what we know so far about the talk surrounding the former midfielder and a possible move to north London.

Raul Sanllehi contact

Arsenal have been eager to deny links with Jose Mourinho already this month.

And they may have to start talking about whether or not they are in talks with Luis Enrique soon, too.

According to El Confidencial , Enrique is a candidate to take over at the Emirates if Emery is sacked.

Furthermore, Spanish journalist Kike Marin has indicated on his official Twitter page that there has been contact between enrique and Arsenal's head of football Raul Sanllehi.

Marin said that Sanllehi was "looking for a substitute" and that there had been talks with Enrique over being that replacement.
