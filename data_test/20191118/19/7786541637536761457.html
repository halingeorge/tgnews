Video shows Arizona deputy tackling 15-year-old quadruple amputee

18 Nov 2019, 19:17 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

A disturbing eight-minute cellphone video of an Arizona sheriff's department deputy tackling and cursing at a 15-year-old boy with no limbs at a group home has prompted an internal affairs investigation.

>> Read more trending news

The incident was recorded by another teen at a group home in the Tucson area in September, KOLD reported. It was provided to the television station by the Pima County Public Defender's Office.

The incident sparked an internal affairs investigation by the Pima County Sheriff's Department, The Washington Post reported. Deputy James Allerton, a spokesman for Pima County Sheriff's Department, told the Arizona Republic on Friday that the deputy, Manuel Van Santen, was placed on administrative leave.
