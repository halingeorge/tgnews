A room that offers policemen comfort

18 Nov 2019, 19:58 by Special Correspondent

The city police have opened a modern resting room for police personnel with a host of amenities. The room at Tondiarpet police station was inaugurated by Additional Commissioner North, R. Dhinakaran.

“Unlike in the past, police personnel here will be provided a bunk bed, air-conditioner facility, dining table and a hygienic toilet,” said G. Subbu Lakshmi, Deputy Commissioner of Police, Washermanpet. “We have provided all comforts to our personnel who do their duty without any break,” she said. Similar rooms will be set up in other police stations as well, said the officers.
