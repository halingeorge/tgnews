Urban Meyer Calls On Ohio State Fans Ahead Of Penn State Game

18 Nov 2019, 19:10 by Andrew Holleran

Columbus, Ohio will play host to two big-time college football pregame shows on Saturday morning. ESPN’s College GameDay and FOX’s Big Noon Kickoff will be in town for the Ohio State-Penn State matchup.

ESPN’s pregame show has been around for much longer, but FOX’s has a special leg up when it comes to the Buckeyes: Urban Meyer.

The former Ohio State head coach has thrived in his TV role this season. He’s made FOX’s pregame show a really intriguing watch.

Meyer is calling on Ohio State fans to show up Saturday morning for his network’s show.

“BUCKEYE NATION!” Meyer tweeted. “See you Saturday outside the RPAC starting at 10 a.m. for the Big Noon Kickoff Show.”
