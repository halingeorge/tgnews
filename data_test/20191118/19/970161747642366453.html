Love Island's Molly-Mae Hague she strips off for topless photoshoot

18 Nov 2019, 19:18

Molly-Mae Hague is giving us serious envy as posed in her underwear on a boat in Barbados.

The Love Island star, who is dating fellow contestant Tommy Fury, took part in a solo trip to the Caribbean island with fast fashion giant Pretty Little Thing, where she was treated to a day of luxury aboard a catamaran off the coast.

Joined by a host of photographers, the 20-year-old wore her trademark long blonde hair in a lengthy fishtail braid (not a high bun in sight!) and later removed her bikini top in order to get a nice, even tan as she lounged around in the sun.

Clutching a towel to her front to prevent things from getting a bit nippy, Molly-Mae showed off her incredible figure in white, thong style bikini bottoms.

We’re not sure what we’re more jealous of: the bikini, the boat or the body.

Changing out of her swimwear to get a second shot, the influencer donned an eeny weeny gold bikini with a triangle style top, wearing her hair in a high ponytail.

We know it’s nearly Christmas, but can it please be summer already? Because these pictures are making us far too jealous.

Having recently moved in with boyfriend Tommy to a flat in Manchester, the Love Island runners up couldn’t be happier together.

In fact, Molly-Mae recently revealed that she suspected the relationship could move to the next step in the coming months, as she thinks Tommy will propose to her before the year is out.

Yep, you heard that right.

‘I wouldn’t be surprised if he did pop the question when we’re in the Maldives over New Year,’ she speculated.

‘I don’t waste my time, I wouldn’t want to be in a relationship with someone thinking, “This is only short term.”

‘I wasn’t thinking, “When’s the sell-by date on this one?” We’re going to be together forever.’
