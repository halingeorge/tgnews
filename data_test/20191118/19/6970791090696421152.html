Roc Nation Claims JAY-Z Has Not Reacted To Colin Kaepernick/NFL Workout

18 Nov 2019, 19:07 by Dwayne Taylor

Music mogul JAY-Z has reportedly kept his lips sealed on the publicized Colin Kaepernick and NFL weekend workout. Young Hov’s Roc Nation has responded to reports claiming JAY had frustrations toward the free agent quarterback.

On Monday, Roc Nation’s Twitter page updated followers on the JAY rumor situation.
