Cong. says CM should resign as DJB chief

18 Nov 2019, 19:57 by Staff Reporter

The Delhi Congress on Monday protested outside a Delhi Jal Board (DJB) pumping station near Ramlila ground following a study which stated that contaminated water was being supplied by the DJB. Delhi Pradesh Congress Committee president Subhash Chopra said, “The Aam Aadmi Party government has failed to supply clean drinking water and clean air to the people of Delhi.”

Demanding Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal’s resignation as DJB Chairman, Mr. Chopra said: “A criminal case should also be registered against him for... distributing contaminated water which is not fit for drinking.”
