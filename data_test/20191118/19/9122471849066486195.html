Steelers WR JuJu Smith-Schuster Might Be Out vs. Cincinnati

18 Nov 2019, 19:40 by Alek Arend

Myles Garrett’s helmet swinging hit on Mason Rudolph and subsequent brawl has dominated the NFL discussion since Thursday night. Lost in the shuffle is JuJu Smith-Schuster and the crushing helmet-to-helmet hit he absorbed, leading to his early exit from the game.

The Steelers receiver found himself in between three Browns defenders, two of which made contact with Smith-Schuster’s helmet with their own helmet. Pittsburgh’s star receiver suffered a concussion as a result.

According to the latest report from ESPN’s Adam Schefter, it appears Smith-Schuster is dealing with more than just a concussion. The receiver also suffered an undisclosed knee injury which may sideline him for Sunday’s game against the Bengals.
