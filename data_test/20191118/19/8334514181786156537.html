Yemen's Houthis seize three ships in Red Sea: Al Masirah TV

18 Nov 2019, 19:38

CAIRO (Reuters) - Yemen’s Houthis seized three ships, including one belonging to Saudi Arabia, three miles off Uqban island at the south end of the Red Sea, the group’s Al-Masirah TV said on Monday.

It did not identify the other ships but said all of them were taken to Salif port.

The Saudi-led military coalition fighting the Iran-aligned Houthis said earlier that the group had seized a vessel towing a South Korean drilling rig.
