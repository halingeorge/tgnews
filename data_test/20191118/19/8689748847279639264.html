UK Based Ghanaian Musician, Reggie Zippy Shares Cute Photos Of His Family

18 Nov 2019, 19:55 by Ghbase New Writer

UK-based Ghanaian singer Reggie Zippy and a member of the music duo, Reggie N Bollie has flaunted his family on social media in a recent post.

The artist, who participated in the X-Factor with his partner Bollie in 2015, was in awe of his own family as he gushed over them with cute words.

Apparently, one of his daughters celebrated her birthday recently and that was when he decided to shown them to the world.

He captioned one of the photos:

I look at them and I say, damn I’m so blessed it’s not even funny 😄😍. Last night at Dior’s pre-birthday party 😁🥳❤️❤️❤️❤️. #myfamily#prouddad #happybirthday 🎉🎈🎁🎂🎈lizzydadzieYou’re than blessed bro God bless your family ❤️

In a different post, he wrote:

THanking God for adding another year to our daughter’s age today. Happy birthday to you Dior😄, mummy n daddy wish you God’s blessings, happiness and protection as we celebrate your life today. We love you so much 😘❤️💙🎂🎂🎉🎉 #family #birthdaygirl #love #daddysgirl
