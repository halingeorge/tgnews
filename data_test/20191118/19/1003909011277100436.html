Hobbit reunion set for Calgary Expo 2020

18 Nov 2019, 19:03 by Eric Volmers

Four hobbits will be helping Calgary Expo celebrate its 15th anniversary.

A special “Middle-earth reunion” featuring Lord of the Rings alumni Frodo, Samwise Gamgee, Pippin and Merry — actors Elijah Wood, Sean Astin, Billy Boyd and Dominic Monaghan, respectively — is the first announcement for Calgary Expo 2020, which will run April 23 to 26 at Stampede Park.

Astin and Boyd participated in a Rings reunion in 2014. Astin was also on hand last year for a Goonies reunion. Wood was a guest in 2018.

Tickets for Calgary Expo will be available online only at calgaryexpo.com beginning Dec. 4.
