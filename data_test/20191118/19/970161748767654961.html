How did Mel Owen die in EastEnders?

18 Nov 2019, 19:25

Fans have been heaping praise on actress Tamzin Outhwaite after her final scenes in EastEnders as Mel Owen were aired last week.

Tamzin has now departed the soap after over two decades of playing Mel, with viewers sad to see the door closed on the Walford legend for good.

Taking to Twitter to thank her fans for all of their compliments and messages of support Tamzin wrote: ‘Thank you so much for all the love…I could not have relished playing Mel any more and I could not feel more proud. Here’s to the next 21 years of life, the next job, the next chapter.’

So, if you missed Tamzin’s dramatic final scenes, here’s how Mel was killed off in EastEnders last week.

How did Mel die in EastEnders?

Mel Owen was killed in a tragic high speed collision on the motorway after her storyline with Sharon came to a dramatic head.
