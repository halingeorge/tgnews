Where is I’m a Celebrity filmed?

18 Nov 2019, 19:00

I’m a Celebrity 2019 has kicked off and it’s as compulsive as ever.

For many viewers at home, it’s as much a sign of it being Christmas as the John Lewis advert or the Coca-Cola truck.

But as we settle down for wintry nights watching celebrities including Caitlyn Jenner, Roman Kemp and Nadine Coyle ‘enjoying’ the warmer weather down under, where exactly is the camp?

Here’s what you need to know about that I’m A Celebrity location.

Where is I’m a Celeb filmed?

The show is filmed in Murwillumbah, New South Wales.

More specifically it’s filmed inside the Springbrook National Park.

The show has been filmed here since its second series back in 2003 – having previously been filmed in Cairns, Queensland.

So while some things will be different this year – like no more live critters used for Bush Tucker trials – it’ll still look like the camp we’ve come to know and love for such iconic moments as Peter Andre and Katie Price falling in love before our eyes and Gillian McKeith being, well, Gillian McKeith.
