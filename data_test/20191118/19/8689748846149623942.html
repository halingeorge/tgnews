I Teach Women How To Walk, Talk, Cook And Lay Bed For Their Husbands, Only Hypocrites Are Against It – Mama Zimbi Fumes

18 Nov 2019, 19:33 by vanessa nordzi

Ghanaian television and radio broadcast journalist, women’s rights activist, blogger, marriage counselor, and an actress Dr. Joyce Akumaa Dongotey-Padi popularly known as Akumaa Mama Zimbi known for her fascinating, yet sexually stimulating, ways of expressing herself.

The sensational sex coach has described a section of the public as hypocrites for referring to her sex tips show as inappropriate and vulgar.

According to Mama Zimbi, no matter how many times she is criticised she is never going to change her style of hosting her midnight “adult” show.

“A spade must be called a spade; the hypocrisy is too much. If I describe the genitals in English, there won’t be a problem but when I call it natively, there is trouble,” she lamented on Hitz FM’s Daybreak Hitz.

Contrary to public perception, Akumaa Mama Zimbi who hosts “Odo Ahomaso” explained that the intention behind her show is to give sexual education that will help married couple maintain a healthy relationship rather than promote profanity.

“The sex bit is just the tip of the iceberg but because people don’t listen to the show, they attribute it to profanity. They lack education on the essence of the show. I educate women on how to walk, talk, cook, lay bed and not just preach about intimacy,” she stated.

Mama Zimbi further bemoaned that she is unperturbed by the backlash she constantly receives from the public.
