The Hindu in School launched in 50 govt. schools

Copies sponsored by HPCL to be distributed daily

18 Nov 2019, 19:55 by Special Correspondent

Aimed at inculcating reading habits and instilling the quest for knowledge among students, The Hindu In School was launched in 50 government schools in the city on Monday.

The copies sponsored by Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited (HPCL), under their corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiative, will be distributed in 50 government schools in the city daily, throughout the academic year.

Sarabjit Singh, Chief Regional Manager, Cochin Retail Regional Office of HPCL, Ernakulam, inaugurated the initiative by handing over the copies to the Principal and students of Model Technical Higher Secondary School, Kaloor.

The initiative also focuses on ensuring that information is accessible to students of all classes in society.
