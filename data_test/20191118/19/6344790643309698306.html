Original Song by Taylor Swift and Andrew Lloyd Webber 'Beautiful Ghosts' for Feature Film Adaptation of CATS

18 Nov 2019, 19:00 by Jessica Fisher

We have a new song for you to check out from the upcoming film adaptation of the famed Broadway musical, Cats. The musical was written by Andrew Lloyd Webber, and he has contributed the music for a new song – with lyrics by cast member Taylor Swift – for the film as well. This will be the only brand new song included in the film, with the other music from the original returning.

The song seems to fit in with the other songs in the musical, and Swift performs it beautifully. I have always liked the songs from Cats, but I don’t know how well it’s all going to translate on film. But for now, listen to the new song, and let us know what you think.
