First Trailer For THE DEATH AND LIFE OF JOHN F. DONOVAN with Kit Harington and Natalie Portman

18 Nov 2019, 19:00 by Jessica Fisher

We’ve got an interesting new trailer and poster for you to check out for a film called The Death and Life of John F. Donovan, starring Jacob Tremblay (Room), Natalie Portman (Black Swan), Kit Harington (Game of Thrones), Thandie Newton (Westworld), Sarah Gadon (True Detective), Michael Gambon (Harry Potter films), Susan Sarandon (Thelma and Louise, The Client), and Kathy Bates (Fried Green Tomatoes, American Horror Story).

The film follows a young man who recounts his five-year pen pal relationship during his childhood with a famed actor who passed away unexpectedly. The child grows up to be an actor himself and comes to terms with the man he knew and the reveal of their secret friendship.

The cast is so good, and the story looks heartfelt and intriguing. Check out the trailer and poster below, and see The Death and Life of John F. Donovan in limited release in theaters, as well as on digital and VOD on December 13th.
