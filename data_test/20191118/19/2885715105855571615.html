Gujarat: CM Rupani to attend Gandhi Sankalp Yatra concluding programme

The Yatra has been passing through Gandhinagar Lok Sabha constituency for the past two days.

18 Nov 2019, 19:11 by Express News Service

Chief Minister Vijay Rupani and Union minister Narendra Singh Tomar will attend the concluding ceremony of the Gandhi Sankalp Yatra at Sabarmati Ashram in Ahmedabad on Tuesday. The Yatra has been passing through Gandhinagar Lok Sabha constituency for the past two days.

An official release from BJP’s Ahmedabad city unit said that as part of celebrations of Mahatma Gandhi’s 150th birth anniversary, Prime Minister Narendra Modi has appealed to all the Lok Sabha members to hold rallies in their respective constituencies to spread Gandhian ideology. Accordingly, such a rally is passing through the Gandhinagar Lok Sabha constituency areas for the past two days. And it will be concluded at Sabarmati Ashram in Ahmedabad, on Tuesday.
