Kim Kardashian Creates A ’90s Infomercial For SKIMS Starring Kris Jenner, Kyle Richards And Lisa Rinna

18 Nov 2019, 19:43 by Jamie Samhan

“You don’t want sweet chariots to swing low!“

A line that sounds like it could come straight out of the ’90s and that is exactly the feeling Kim Kardashian was going for when she created a series of infomercials for SKIMS.

The three videos posted to the solution wear’s Instagram page feature Kris Jenner hosting the throwback alongside “Real Housewives” Kyle Richards and Lisa Rinna.

After a rocky start to the line, the newly branded SKIMS have been a massive success so much so they are promoting the restock which is expected to sell out just as fast, if not faster than the launch.

 

In the last of the three clips, the ladies get intimate about why they love the line with Rinna taking it all off to show her SKIMS to the world.

Infomercials should have never gone out of style but now they are here to stay.
