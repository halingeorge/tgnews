Green Lantern Concept Art Reveals Alternate Designs for Infected Rats

18 Nov 2019, 19:11 by Samuel Gelman

New concept art from the infamous Green Lantern movies shows an alternate design for the film's infected rats.

Character designer Jerad S. Marantz sheared the art on his Instagram page. "An infected rat design for the #greenlanternmovie I did a while back," he wrote. "Had a lot of fun on the project."

Despite the movie's failure, Warner Media has not given up on the character. HBO Max is currently developing a series based on the Green Lantern Corps. Greg Berlanti, who directed and wrote the Ryan Reynolds-led film, will produce the series. No cast has been announced and no release date has been confirmed.

Marantz credits go beyond Green Lantern. He has worked on various comic films, including Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame, Spider-Man: Far from Home, Justice League, Deadpool 2, Guardians of the Galaxy, Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2, X-Men: Age of Apocalypse and Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice. 

Written and directed by Greg Berlanti, Green Lantern stars Ryan Reynolds, Blake Lively, Peter Sarsgaard, Mark Strong, Angela Bassett, Tim Robbins and Martin Campbell. The film is now available on Blue-ray and digital.
