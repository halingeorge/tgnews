Uddhav Thackeray postpones visit to Ayodhya

18 Nov 2019, 19:25 by Tanvi Deshpande

Shiv Sena chief Uddhav Thackeray has postponed his proposed visit to Ayodhya on November 24 in view of security reasons and the delay in government formation in Maharashtra.

After the Supreme Court verdict on Ayodhya last week. Mr. Thackeray had announced that he would visit Ayodhya.

“However, security in Ayodhya is still heightened and political parties are not being allowed to visit. Besides, there is no breakthrough on talks over government formation in the State,” said a Sena leader.
