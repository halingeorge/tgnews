Join Glenn Danzig for a Screening of ‘Verotika’ in Philadelphia on Dec. 13th

18 Nov 2019, 19:34 by Brad Miska

As we wait for Glenn Danzig‘s hotly-anticipated Verotika to arrive on VOD platforms sometime next year, the film continues to quietly tour across the country. Those in Philadelphia will get the change to experience the next great so-bad-it’s-good movie when it screens at the Philadelphia Film Society on December 13th. Glenn will be in attendance as will producer James Cullen Bressack. Tickets are now available.

Verotika is a direct adaptation of Danzig’s comic books that became the buzz of the film world when reviews dubbed it an instant cult classic that joins the ranks of best-worst-movies The Room and Birdemic. Our very own Patrick Bromley called it the “horror equivalent of The Room” in his review.

The press release describes the anthology as “a creepy, surreal & bloody trilogy of erotic horror stories.”

Verotika stars Alice Haig, Ashley Wisdom, Kayden Kross, Natalia Borowsky, Rachel Alig, Scotch Hopkins, and Sean Kanan.
