Michelle Visage tears up on Strictly: It Takes Two as she defends voguing

18 Nov 2019, 19:40

Michelle Visage let out her inner fierce as she appeared on Strictly: It Takes Two with dance partner Giovanni Pernice to discuss her elimination.

The RuPaul’s Drag Race star couldn’t help but return to her usual straight forward comments as she defended her last dance – which was to Madonna’s Vogue – on Strictly Come Dancing.

Speaking to host Rylan Clark-Neal, she explained: ‘It was a love letter to my LGBTQIA+ community, which is why I did it.

‘I wanted to shed light on a community that doesn’t get enough attention. Especially this dance, in particular, that is a street dance.

‘There’s a lot of controversy online saying it’s not dancing it’s posing and walking and arm-ography. No. Just because you don’t understand it doesn’t mean it’s not a dance.’
