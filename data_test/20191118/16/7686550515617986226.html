Police investigate 'unexplained' death of woman, 46, in Brecon

Lorraine Price, 46, was found dead at a property on Sunday, November 17

18 Nov 2019, 16:37 by Katie-Ann Gupwell

A 46-year-old woman has been found dead at a property in Brecon .

Dyfed-Powys Police described the death of Lorraine Price, 46 as 'unexplained' and said officers are investigating the circumstances.

Officers were called to an address in Talbot Terrace on Sunday, November 17, where the 46-year-old was found deceased.

Detective Chief Inspector Paul Jones said: “We are not looking for anyone in connection with Lorraine’s death, but we are nevertheless trying to piece together what has happened over the past few days.

“We are asking anyone who saw Lorraine between the morning of Thursday, November 14 and the evening of Sunday, November 17 to please contact us, as any information you have could help the investigation.

“We would like to pass our sincere condolences to Lorraine’s family for their tragic loss.”

People living in the area have been advised there may be a higher than usual police presence while enquiries are taking place.

Anyone with information that could help police with the investigation is urged to contact CID at Brecon Police Station.

You can report information online at: http://bit.ly/dppReportOnline , by email at contactcentre@dyfed-powys.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101.

When making a report, please use reference number DP-20191117-278.
