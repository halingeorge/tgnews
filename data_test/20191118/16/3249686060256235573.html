Check out the first lineup for the 2019 Melon Music Awards!

18 Nov 2019, 16:29 by Yanderedan

Earlier this week music company Melon has announced the first lineup for their 2019 Melon Music Awards. After confirming BTS will be performing, Melon has also revealed that MAMAMOO, ChungHa, Heize, and Jannabi will be performing.
