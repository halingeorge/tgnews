Limited-edition Jonas Brothers Coors Light six-packs now available in Tampa via goPuff

18 Nov 2019, 16:49 by WFTS Digital Staff

TAMPA, Fla. — The Limited-edition bottles of Coors Light featuring a customized label from the Jonas Brothers are now available in Tampa.

In October, Joe, Kevin, and Nick announced they were brewing a special batch after being invited by Coors to its brewery in Golden while in Colorado on their Happiness Begins tour.

The brothers’ have been fans of the beer for years. From Joe Jonas having custom-made Coors Light bottles at his wedding in France to Kevin Jonas making a cameo with Coors Light in the band's "Only Human" video.

"The limited edition Coors Light brewed with the Jonas Brothers is still the same refreshing Coors Light but what makes it truly special is that Nick, Kevin, and Joe played a part in brewing it and creating a custom bottle to celebrate the partnership," Coors Light said in a release.

You can buy the limited-edition Jonas Brothers Coors Light six-packs exclusively via goPuff’s site or iOS / Android app.
