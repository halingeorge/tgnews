Navinci and Clovd (Topp Dogg) to visit Israel on Christmas

18 Nov 2019, 16:22 by itaih9

Navinci and Clovd (Topp Dogg) have recently announced through Instagram posts that they will visit Israel on December 25th-26th. 

It was revealed locally that Navinci (Hansol from Topp Dogg) will visit Israel. His visit was planned for October but was postponed due to South Korean military issues. 

Recently, former Topp Dogg member Clovd (Gohn) decided to join Navinci. 

They will visit on the Jewish holiday Hanukkah. They will host fan meetings, a show, and dance classes.
