Rutgers Cheerleader Addresses Saturday’s Funny Mistake vs. Ohio State

18 Nov 2019, 16:09 by Andrew Holleran

Ohio State did so much scoring against Rutgers on Saturday, even the Scarlet Knights cheerleaders were celebrating. It wasn’t on purpose, though.

A Rutgers cheerleader mistakenly did some clapping following a J.K. Dobbins touchdown during Saturday’s blowout.

The cheerleader was seen clapping following the touchdown, before realizing the mistake.
