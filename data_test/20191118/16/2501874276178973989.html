Sheriff's Office Investigates Armed Robbery in Isla Vista

18 Nov 2019, 16:30

Update by Santa Barbara Sheriff's Office
 4:30 p.m., November 18, 2019

Deputies Investigate Home Invasion Robbery

At around 10:22 this morning, Sheriff’s Deputies responded to the 6500-block of Sabado Tarde after a victim called 911 to report that he had just been robbed. When they arrived, deputies contacted two victims who said they had been robbed at gun point inside their residence by three suspects. The suspects were described as a white male adult wearing all black clothing with a ski mask, armed with a handgun; a white male adult wearing a camouflage jacket, armed with a handgun; and a black male adult wearing all black that was unarmed. The suspects are associated with a newer model BMW 3 Series that has a 6 in the license plate sequence. 

One of the victims had minor injuries that did not require immediate medical attention. The other victim was uninjured. The suspects stole two laptops, a cell phone, a wallet and unknown amount of marijuana.  It is believed that victims were specifically targeted. This investigation is ongoing and anyone with information about the robbery is encouraged to call the Sheriff’s Office Anonymous Tip Line at (805)681-4171. 

Update by UC Santa Barbara Alerts
 12:00 p.m., November 18, 2019

Regarding the initial robbery incident, UCPD and Santa Barbara Sheriffs Department are NOT REQUESTING A LOCK DOWN of the area.  Information was provided to keep the community informed of an active ongoing investigation.  Santa Barbara Sheriffs Department and UCPD personnel are in the area and are monitoring the situation.  If any new information regarding the whereabouts of the suspects is learned, UCPD will provide the community with an update.  Please do not contact UCPD Dispatch or Santa Barbara Sheriffs Department dispatch unless you have information regarding this incident.
