Samsung announces special edition of Galaxy Note10+ to celebrate Star Wars

18 Nov 2019, 16:15 by Pradeep

Samsung today announced a special edition Galaxy Note10+ to celebrate the release of Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker movie. This special edition Galaxy Note10+ features Star Wars-inspired design elements and exclusive Star Wars-themed digital content including wallpapers, shutdown animations, icons and sounds. And it will also come with a specially designed case, a metal badge as a collector’s item, an S Pen, and Galaxy Buds.

Discover the dark side of Galaxy with a black device featuring red accents and red S Pen. Accessorize with an embossed Leather Cover, coordinated Galaxy Buds, and an exclusive metal badge as a collector’s item. And you’ll find it all in a Sith-inspired package so you get the full Star Wars experience as you unbox.

The Galaxy Note10+ Star Wars Special Edition will be available in Australia, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Hong Kong, Korea, Norway, Russia, Spain, Sweden, Turkey, UAE, UK and USA from December 10, 2019.
