Caroline Flack gives first glimpse at Winter Love Island rocking dazzling yellow

The presenter showed off her tanned arms and sun-kissed hair as she shoots promos in South Africa for the first series of Winter Love Island

18 Nov 2019, 16:30 by Irishmirror.ie

Love Island'sCaroline Flack has been teasing fans ahead of the upcoming Winter Love Island.

The show, which is expected for a January release date, will be the first of its kind and means that fans will be getting double helpings of Love Island in 2020.

Now Caroline's out in Cape Town filming promos for the show, and stunning in a bright yellow summer dress. The star added that she got her hair and makeup done by celeb hairstylist Gemma Wheatcroft.

"Day 1 shoot," the blond beauty wrote, "Hair and makeup by @gemflossi."

Fans rushed to the comments section to gush over the 40 year old.

"Naturally beautiful. Wow," one wrote.

Another penned: "No way in hell do you look 40! Aging like fine wine haha x."

A third poured their heart out to Caroline, writing: "Unreal Miss Flack, looking on fire again babe."

Others just wanted to know about Love Island.

"When does Love Island start again?" one demanded.

"Love Island????" another needed to know.

She also shared some glimpses of South Africa on her story, including one of her looking over her bare shoulder in a sultry pose.

"So we've just landed in Cape Town," she told fans as she was getting her makeup done, "We're here to film the advert for Love Island season six. We're not really sure what to expect, because we're not used to filming for Love Island during the winter."

Later she added: "Just finished the first shot of the day.

"To begin with a had this yellow dress on, it looked so weird. I looked by I'd been run over by a roller and someone tried to plump me back up again.

"I'm never wearing that dress again."

While an official date is yet to be confirmed, it's being widely reported that the show will be hitting our screens as soon as 8th January.

MailOnline have reported that staff for the show will be jetting out on New Year's Day, with the show set to kick off a week later.

The sources also told the publication that fans can expect a two week shorter edition of the show, and that producers will be using "test Islanders" ahead of the main cast's arrival.

They said: "It has been decided that the show will run six weeks instead of the usual eight. The new cast of singletons fly out just days before the launch but bosses work with test Islanders to uncover the best spots in the villa to create drama."

As well as Caroline, fans will be happy to know that comedian Iain Stirling will again be lending his voice to the show as the drama unfolds.

"Do you know what?" he told Good Morning Britain, "Winter Love Island is gonna come back sooner that you thought and it's gonna be enjoyable."
