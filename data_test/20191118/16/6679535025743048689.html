T20 | India women make it 4-0

18 Nov 2019, 16:43 by PTI

The series already won, India continued its domination over the West Indies by notching up a five-run win in the rain-truncated fourth women’s T20 International here on Sunday.

India now leads the five-match series 4-0.

In the rain-marred fourth T20I, reduced to nine overs a side, India made 50 for seven wickets after being put in.

The Indians then bowled and fielded with discipline to restrict the West Indies to 45 for five in their nine overs.

The scores: India Women 50/9 in nine overs (Hayley Matthews 3/13, Afy Fletcher 2/2, Sheneta Grimmond 2/10) bt West Indies Women 45/5 in nine overs (Anuja Patil 2/8).
