Eagle-eyed I'm A Celebrity fans spot 'hidden meaning' of Ant's two new tattoos

Ant is back on I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! having taken a year off last year

18 Nov 2019, 16:26 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

I'm a Celebrity viewers think they've spotted the hidden meaning behind Ant McPartlin's new tattoos.

The Geordie TV personality has two new tats - and he flaunted them on Sunday evening.

Ant is back on I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! having taken a year off last year.

Holly Willoughby stepped in in 2018, with Ant now returning to host alongside Declan Donnelly.

He is now sporting a change in appearance, though, with two new tattoos: an arrow on his right forearm, and a rose design on the left.

And viewers reckon they know the reason behind them with one writing: “Ant on I'm a Celeb has a mental health awareness tattoo on his arm.”

Elsewhere the rose is believed to mean 'promise, hope and new beginnings' according to website undergroundinkcny.com.

The 43-year-old is hosting the show alongside Dec on ITV1.

The pair were back on our screens yesterday (Sunday, November 17) as ten celebrities headed into the jungle.

 

Among this year's batch of stars entering the Australian bush include Caitlyn Jenner, Kate Garraway, James Haskell and Ian Wright.

Adele Roberts, Nadine Coyle, Roman Kemp, Jacqueline Jossa and Andrew Maxwell are also on the show.

 

Myles Stephenson is a member of X Factor winning band Rak-Su and completes the line-up.
