Wiley challenges Dizzee Rascal to a rap battle in London’s streets

18 Nov 2019, 16:00 by https://www.facebook.com/charlotte.krol1

"Let’s go I’m on 💨💨💨💨💨💨"

Wiley has dared Dizzee Rascal to a rap “clash” on London’s streets.

The ‘Godfather of Grime’ today (November 18) tweeted the ‘Boy In Da Corner’ artist to spit bars in one of two east London streets. “Dizzee let’s have a clash Roman road or Devon’s road ? You choose,” he wrote, citing areas of Bow where the two rappers grew up and were part of the grime collective Roll Deep.
