Search for missing sailboat enters 3rd day

18 Nov 2019, 16:16 by EndPlay

BAR HARBOR, MaineÂ (AP) - A U.S. Coast Guard airplane is in the air as the search for a missing sailboat enters the third day.

The Coast Guard searched 2,700 nautical miles over the weekend after someone called 911 early Saturday to say that a boat was in distress. The Coast Guard determined that the call came from a location 20 miles south of Mount Desert Island.

The missing sailboat is a 40-footer named the Dove, with three crew members.

Petty Officer Nicole Groll, a Coast Guard spokeswoman, said the search has covered an area from Maine to Nantucket Island off the Massachusetts coast.

So far, searchers have found no evidence of the boat. Groll said the Coast Guard is investigating all options about where the boat could've gone.
