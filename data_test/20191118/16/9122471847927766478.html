Minnesota Twins Prospect Ryan Costello Reportedly Found Dead

18 Nov 2019, 16:19 by Matt Lombardi

Minnesota Twins prospect Ryan Costello was reportedly found dead in his hotel room in Auckland, New Zealand, his team announced Monday morning. Costello had just arrived in the country about a week ago to play for the Auckland Tuatara.

The Tuatara report that preliminary indications suggest he died of “natural causes.” The team is set to hold a press conference about the situation on Tuesday.

Costello was just 23 years old. He was set to play third base for the Tuatara this season.

“The Tuatara would like to express their deepest condolences to Ryan’s family back home in the United States and to the whole Minnesota Twins organisation.”
