Charcoal Street Festival Live Concert to be staged on December 21

18 Nov 2019, 16:35 by Simon Asare, GNA

     

Accra, Nov. 18, GNA - The 2019 edition of the Charcoal Street Festival Live Concert will kick start on Saturday, December 21, at the Omanjor Washing Bay on the Dweneho Road, Accra.

This year's festival, under the theme 'Development Through Unity', seeks to project the Omanjor Community to the rest of the World and also be a platform to seek social interventions for the community.

The festival portrays the Ghanaian arts industry through cultural display, traditional way of dressing, rap, modelling, dancing, painting, among others, to showcase and ignite Ghanaian culture.

The festival organised by Cr8tive Empire in collaboration with Deebee Gh. Ltd would be a platform for Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) in the community to exhibit their products and promote their brands.

There are spaces available for arts exhibitors who would want to show their creative works during the festival.
