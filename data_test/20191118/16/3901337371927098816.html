New Role Abroad For Ex-PSL Coach!

18 Nov 2019, 16:36 by Soccer Laduma

A former PSL coach has been appointed in a new role by a club abroad.

Following his time coaching in South Africa at Black Leopards and Richards Bay FC respectively, Belgian mentor Jean-Francois Losciuto went on to assist Luc Eymael at Egyptian club Gaish.

With Eymael having since returned to Mzansi for a coaching role at Lidoda Duvha, Losciuto has surfaced at Futuro Kings FC in Equatorial Guinea.

Losciuto holds a UEFA A and UEFA B Licences, among other qualifications and has previously worked in various African countries, including Togo, Rwanda, Burkina Faso and Nigeria.

He has also previously been an assistant coach of Nigeria's senior national team under Sunday Oliseh at some point in his career.
