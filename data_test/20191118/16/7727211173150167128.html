New Year's Eve celebrations in Liverpool and Merseyside to welcome 2020 in style

Book a party experience to remember and wave goodbye to the decade

18 Nov 2019, 16:39 by Leena Sidat

‘Tis the season to be jolly…

Celebrate Christmas and wave goodbye to 2019 - and the decade - with a whole host of fantastic events and parties taking place in and around Liverpool this December.

Here’s a list of places to visit for a fantastic festive experience; there’s something for everyone to enjoy!

1. Sapporo Teppanyaki

For something a bit different, Sapporo Teppanyaki is offering Christmas and New Year's menus with a twist, keeping the festive feeling, but mixing things up with delicious Japanese treats, all cooked in front of you by its entertaining chefs.

Perfect for groups who are bored of the same old New Year's dinner, choose the lunch menu or banquet menu for a unique festive experience.

Where to go: 134 Duke St, East Village, Liverpool, L1 5AG

Get in touch: Visit www.sapporo.co.uk or call 0151 705 3005

2. The Bold Hotel

The Bold Hotel is putting on a show like no other this New Year’s Eve, with music from the renowned Frankie Valli and The Four Seasons tribute act, ‘Who Loves You’.

In the hotel’s impressive Red Rum Suite, you’re guaranteed a night to remember. Start the celebrations with a glass of fizz before being seated for a delicious three course festive feast, created by the in-house catering team.

Be entertained with the sounds and showmanship of one of rock and roll’s most prestigious bands and then dance the night away with top classics from the hotel’s resident DJ.

Famed for its legendary Christmas party nights, The Bold Hotel is the place to be this festive season. Be quick though, dates are selling out fast!

Where to go: 583 Lord Street, Southport, Merseyside, PR9 0BE

Get in touch: Visit www.boldhotel.com or call 01704 532 578

3. Lord Street Hotel and Champagne Bar

Celebrate the New Year in style at the lavish Lord Street Hotel and Champagne Bar.

Named after the famous, tree-lined boulevard at the heart of Southport, the hotel is the epitome of luxury and is the perfect place to see in the New Year with your friends and family.

With an evening filled with live performances from the best Soul and Motown acts and a buffet full of festive favourites, including turkey with all the trimmings, Lord Street Hotel invites you to dance and dine this season.

Guests are sure to love the hotel’s New Year’s Eve Soul and Motown party, and to make the most of it and not have to cut your festive fun short, you can book a room and stay the night. The hotel has 23 fabulous en-suite bedrooms, with sumptuous beds for a great night’s sleep, a wonderful way to see in 2020.

Where to go: 178 - 180 Lord Street, Southport, PR9 0QG

Get in touch: Visit www.lordstreethotel.com or call 01704 532 578

4. Christakis

Liverpool’s new Christakis restaurant is the ultimate venue for your New Year's Eve celebrations.

From food to entertainment, the Duke Street restaurant has everything, and its combination of Mediterranean meat, fish and vegetarian dishes along with traditional Greek entertainment is a winner for any celebration.

Enjoy a lively atmosphere alongside fine food and drinks, as traditional Greek dancing and belly dancing are added to the mix. Bouzouki nights and evenings of traditional dance will continue to run during the Christmas and New Year party season.

Where to go: 136-138 Duke Street, Liverpool City Centre, Merseyside, L1 5BB

Get in touch: Visit www.sugarvine.com/restaurant/christakis-liverpool-city-centre or call 0151 708 9791

5. Suites Hotel & Spa

Suites Hotel & Spa is hosting a New Year's Eve event for all of the family.

The hotel is offering a wonderful four course dinner with fun, games and its resident DJ, plus a fantastic fireworks display and traditional piper at midnight to ring in the New Year - all for £75 per adult and £37.50 per child.

You can also upgrade and add a night in a superior suite, available from £150, including a full English breakfast and use of the hotel’s leisure facilities.

Where to go: Ribblers Lane, Prescot, Merseyside, L34 9HA

Get in touch: Call 0151 549 2222 or visit www.suiteshotelgroup.com

6. Thornton Manor

Step back in time for a night of rip-roaring revelry at the 1920’s themed New Year’s Eve party at Thornton Manor, by order of the Peaky Blinders.

Welcome the new decade in style with exhilarating entertainment, delicious food and spectacular performers as you immerse yourself in this shadowy world of grit and glamour.

Enjoy a glass of champagne upon arrival, five course dinner, a breathtaking live show, leading DJ and state of the art disco, a spin on the dodgems in Charlie’s Yard, backroom gambling den with fun casino in aid of Clatterbridge Cancer Charity, and midnight breakfast - all for £75.

Where to go: Manor Road, Thornton Hough, Cheshire, CH63 1JB

Get in touch: Visit www.jdparties.co.uk or follow on Twitter and Instagram

7. La Parrilla Mexican Bar & Grill Bold Street

Celebrate Christmas and New Year’s Eve at La Parrilla Mexican Bar & Grill on Bold Street.

Rated number one on Trip Advisor as Liverpool's best Mexican restaurant, the tapas bar is offering a two course Christmas party menu for £17.95 per person and three courses for £19.95.

For New Year's Eve, you can choose from an early bird menu, available from 4pm to 6pm, priced at £29.95 per person for two courses and £44.65 for four courses from 7.30pm onwards.

Where to go: 57 Bold Street, Liverpool, L1 4EU

Get in touch: Visit www.laparrillarestaurantboldstreet.co.uk or call 0151 708 7575

8. La Parrilla Mexican Bar & Grill Waterloo

Head down to La Parrilla Mexican Bar & Grill on South Road, Waterloo, for a unique Christmas and New Year’s experience.

Rated Liverpool's best Mexican restaurant on Trip Advisor, the tapas bar is offering a two course Christmas party menu for £17.95 per person and three courses for £19.95.

On New Year’s Eve, diners can choose from an early bird menu, available from 4pm to 6pm, priced at £29.95 per person for two courses and £44.65 for four courses from 7.30pm onwards.

Where to go: 36 South Road, Waterloo, Liverpool, L22 5PQ

Get in touch: Visit www.laparrillarestaurant.co.uk or call 0151 949 0310
