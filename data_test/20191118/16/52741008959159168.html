Police looking for man who approached two kids

It happened in the Pioneer and Bechtel Drive area last Tuesday

18 Nov 2019, 16:04 by KitchenerToday Staff

Waterloo Regional Police continue their search for a man who allegedly approached two children in Kitchener last week.

The incident happened Tuesday, November 12 at around 5:30 p.m.

A man was observed loitering in the area of Bechtel Drive and Pioneer Drive.
