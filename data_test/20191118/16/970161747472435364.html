Lib Dems and SNP won't be included in election TV debates

18 Nov 2019, 16:39

The Liberal Democrats and SNP have lost a High Court challenge against ITV over its decision to exclude their party leaders from a televised election debate.

ITV is hosting a head-to-head debate between Boris Johnson and Jeremy Corbyn on Tuesday, which will now not include any other party leaders.

The judges concluded: ‘No arguable breach of the broadcast code is shown.’
