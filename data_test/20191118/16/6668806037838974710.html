5 big stories from the week gone by

18 Nov 2019, 16:38 by Kajol Mathur

To beat the beginning-of-the-week blues, we have picked for you the hottest and most trending stories, which broke the internet last week. From superstar Shah Rukh Khan to the lovely Deepika Padukone – here we bring to you our top newsmakers.

Check’em out!

1.     Shah Rukh Khan shoots for Ranbir Kapoor – Alia Bhatt starrer Brahmastra

Leaving all his fans super thrilled last week was King Shah Rukh Khan, who will be seen in a special role in Ayan Mukerji directorial Brahmastra. The superstar started shooting for his part last week. His vanity van was spotted in Mumbai’s Film City, where Alia and Ranbir had been shooting for the leftover portions of Brahmastra.

2.     Trailer release of Deepika Padukone’s Chhapaak revealed

This is exciting news for all Deepika Padukone fans. The makers of Chhapaak have finally announced the trailer release date of the film. Helmed by Meghna Gulzar, the film stars Deepika essaying the role of real-life acid attack survivor Laxmi Aggarwal. Speaking of the same, the makers have announced that the trailer will go live in the first week of December this year. This sure has got DP fans beaming.

3.     Parineeti Chopra won’t be seen in Ajay Devgn’s Bhuj: The Pride of India

Earlier reports suggested that Parineeti Chopra would be seen in the Ajay Devgn-Sanjay Dutt-Sonakshi Sinha starrer Bhuj: The Pride of India. However, it looks like the same won’t be happening anymore. If the latest reports are anything to go by, the actress has opted out of the film citing date issues. She is currently busy working on the Saina Nehwal biopic.

4.     Tabu to feature in the Kartik Aaryan- Kiara Advani's Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2

As per the latest reports, the makers of Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2 have roped in Tabu for a prominent role in the film. The details of her character have been kept under wraps. According to a leading daily, the actress loved the role she was offered in and is expected to join the cast for filming from January 2020.  The film stars Kartik Aaryan and Kiara Advani in lead roles.

5.      Ayushmann Khurrana's Shubh Mangal Zyada Saavdhan gets a new release date

Just when the world is talking about Ayushmann Khurrana’s latest release Bala doing wonders at the box-office, we have an update for you on his next -  Shubh Mangal Zyada Saavdhan. The makers have decided to change the release date. It was initially slated to hit the screens on March 13, 2020 but will now release on February 21, 2020.
