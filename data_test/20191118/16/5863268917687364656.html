Firdous Ashiq refutes news about PM expressing anger over PTI media team

18 Nov 2019, 16:40 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (APP) - Special Assistant to the Prime Minister on Information and Broadcasting Dr Firdous Ashiq Awan on Saturday rejected a news story carried by a section of press claiming that the Prime Minister was annoyed at the PTI media team for failing to convey the government performance to people.

In twitter message, Dr. Firdous Ashiq Awan said that this is totally fake news and a completely concocted story published by a private local news channel. Nothing of this sort was discussed in the meeting, she categorically said.

“And I would hope after this official denial by me, channel will retract this false story,” she said.

It was stated earlier that Prime Minister Imran Khan was reportedly annoyed at his social media team over its failure to convey the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) government’s performance to the people, private local news channel said after the party held a meeting of its core committee.
