SEC Announces Game Times, Channels For Rivalry Weekend

18 Nov 2019, 16:43 by Matt Lombardi

Amazingly, we’ve got just a few weeks left in the 2019 college football regular season. Monday, the SEC announced the game times and channels for all of its rivalry weekend games.

The main event, the Iron Bowl, is slated to kick off at 3:30 PM ET on CBS. Auburn will be looking to end any chance of Alabama sneaking into the College Football Playoff.

The Crimson Tide will be quarterbacked by Mac Jones after starter Tua Tagovailoa suffered a serious hip injury against Mississippi State. The Tigers will be looking to take advantage.

The Egg Bowl, played annually between Ole Miss and Mississippi State, it set for Thanksgiving night at 7:30 PM ET.

Missouri vs. Arkansas, in Little Rock, will be on Friday night at 2:30 PM ET.
