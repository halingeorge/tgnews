The Official Title Sequence For Marvel's HAWKEYE Series Has Been Released

18 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Joey Paur

Earlier this year during the big Marvel panel at Comic-Con, the studio showed off the opening title sequence for their Disney+ series Hawkeye. That animated graphic art sequence has now been released for the rest of you to see.

This is said to be the official title sequence for the series, but I imagine when the series launches in the Fall of 2021, there will be a different title sequence.
