Party time

Watch as Hearts-daft Lee McGregor is serenaded in Edinburgh pub after win over Kash Farooq

18 Nov 2019, 16:25 by David Fowler

LEE McGREGOR was serenaded in an Edinburgh boozer as he celebrated his win over Kash Farooq.

The Scot won an explosive fight by split decision in front of a packed Emirates Arena on Saturday night.

In just his eighth bout as a pro McGregor triumphed to add the British bantamweight belt to his Commonwealth title.

And he partied in style with his Gorgie Hearts AFC team-mates.

The amateur football side uploaded a video of the new champ in the pub with his belt with chants of "Saturday night and I like the way you move Lee McGregor" sang throughout.
