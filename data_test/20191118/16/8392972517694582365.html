Cadbury hits back at claims £45 Christmas selection box is a 'rip off'

It does contain 26 chocolate bars…

18 Nov 2019, 16:17 by Nancy Brown

Cadbury has released a giant Christmas selection box featuring 26 chocolate bars and weighing in at 2.1kg.

The limited-edition Ultimate Selection Box is on sale now and costs £45.

Inside you'll find treats including Dairy Milk, Dairy Milk Crunchie, Dairy Milk Tiffin, Crunchie, Double Decker, Fruit & Nut, Whole Nut, Wispa, Curly Wurly, Freddo Caramel and a Chomp.

Cadbury said: "Probably the world's biggest selection box, the Cadbury Ultimate Selection Box is ideal for sharing, containing 26 chocolate bars and weighing over 2 kilos."

It added: "Best of luck fitting this under the tree."
