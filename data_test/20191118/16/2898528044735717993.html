UFC Sao Paulo medical suspensions: Jan Blachowicz potentially out six months

18 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Guilherme Cruz, https://facebook.com/guicruzmma/

It might take a while for Jan Blachowicz to get back inside the Octagon.

The Polish fighter defeated Ronaldo Souza via decision in the main event of UFC Sao Paulo on Saturday night, but could be forced out of action for 180 days due to injuries to both feet, the Brazilian MMA Athletic Commission (CABMMA) announced Monday.

Blachowicz assured “everything’s ok” on his social media the day after the five-round contest.
