12 fantastic Christmas events to enjoy at Bluebells with the whole family

From a festive adventure through the woodlands to breakfast with Santa, there is lots to enjoy

18 Nov 2019, 16:32 by Leena Sidat

This festive season, why not take the kids down to Bluebell Dairy Farm for lots of fantastic Christmassy fun!

The farm has a wonderful array of Christmas related activities and events lined up over the winter, for children and adults alike.

Here's a list of 12 fabulous festive events to get involved in and enjoy.

1. Santa’s Woodland Adventure

A truly magical experience for the whole family, the adventure takes place in groups for a fun and relaxed session with Santa!

The adventure starts at The Candycane Express Departure Lounge, where children can visit the Sugar Plum Sweetie Shop, adults can enjoy a complimentary glass of mulled wine, and together, you can play festive board games and write Santa a letter, before boarding the Express and journey across to the woods.

After meeting Mrs Claus, embark on an excursion to search for Santa's cabin, and enjoy a 10 minute session with the man in red telling him all your Christmas wishes.

Then, visit Bluebell Frostcrackers’ Toyshop where kids can choose whichever toy they like, followed by an elf hunt!

Finally, head over to the stables to make some nutritious reindeer food to take home and sprinkle around your garden to lead Rudolph to your house.

This experience requires pre-booking, and prices range from £5.95 to £17.95. To find out more, click here.

2. Santa’s Starlit Grotto

Meet Santa under the stars this Christmas for a magical and unforgettable festive evening.

In a group format to ensure a great time for all, the experience includes the Candycane Express and Departure Lounge, Santa's Woodland Cabin, Bluebell Frostcrackers' Toyshop, Elf Hunt and Reindeer Food Making activities, similarly to Santa's Woodland Adventure, but on selected days in December.

Available with pre-booking only, and prices range from £7.95 to £18.95. Visit this page for more information.

3. Breakfast with Santa

Enjoy a delicious, home cooked breakfast in the company of Santa and his elves.

Using fresh, locally sourced ingredients, this full English breakfast is truly warming and filling on a cold winter's day - served with unlimited tea or coffee for adults.

Each child will also receive a hand-wrapped present from Santa himself, and there will be gingerbread biscuits to decorate and enjoy with the elves as well.

Admission is £15.50 per child, and £10.50 per adult.

The experience is available on November 30 and selected dates in December. Booking is essential. Click here for more details.

4. Fresh Christmas trees

Providing high quality Christmas trees since 2008, all of Bluebells' trees are locally sourced from within 30 miles of Derby and are cut throughout the festive season to ensure freshness and to help them last as long as possible.

As each individual has their own idea of the perfect tree, the farm has three different varieties on offer.

This year, trees will be available from Saturday, November 25, 2019. Click here to find out more.

5. Festive afternoon tea

Enjoy a wonderfully festive afternoon tea with an ice cream twist!

Available weekdays throughout December, booking is required at least 48 hours before.

6. Gift ideas

Bluebells offers a range of great gift ideas for your family and friends this Christmas.

Ranging from gift vouchers and annual memberships to animal adoptions, there are lots of possible present options.

7. Christmas ice cream

Specialists in luxury artisan ice cream, Bluebells has over 26 delicious flavours of ice cream and sorbet, including panettone ice cream, Irish cream, chocolate orange, prosecco and strawberry, and gin and tonic sorbet.

New for this year, the farm has panettone and cherry cheesecake flavoured ice cream, as well as the return of the mouth-watering Christmas pudding ice cream, specially for the season!

8. Christmas school trips

Why not treat your class or nursery to a magical Christmas experience this year? Bluebells offers discounted rates for school visits.

Available on December 4, 11 and 18 from 10am to 2.30pm, get in touch on 01332 673 924 or email info@bluebelldairy.co.uk if you're interested in booking a fabulous festive day out for the class.

9. Nativity animals

Bluebells has a wide variety of animals on-site for visitors to see and meet in its Animal Patch.

Head down and visit all of the farm's friendly animals, including Bill the post bellied pig, Twinkle the donkey, Merlin the Shetland pony, Arthur the giant rabbit and Fred, Nina and Bella the cheeky goats.

You can also join in some fun animal activities in the play area, and have the opportunity to feed and pet some of the animals.

10. Winter weekday offer

This winter, kids can enjoy entrance to the farm, a lovely meal and an ice cream for just £5.95 per child.

The offer is available from November 4 to 29, 2019.

11. Festive menu

Really get into the Christmas spirit with Bluebells' festive food offerings!

The farm has a range of lovingly homemade festive cakes from its in-house baker Sarah, and a variety of hot food and drink to warm you up this winter.

12. Support a farmer

Treat yourself to some fresh milk from Bluebells' special milk vending machine.

Whether it's to leave out for Santa on Christmas Eve or to make some lovely warm custard to accompany your desserts this Christmas, you're guaranteed only the finest milk.

So, this festive season, head down to Bluebells with the whole family for a truly unique Christmas experience.

Bluebells is located at Brunswood Farm, Locko Road, Derby, DE21 7AR.

For more information on any of Bluebells' events, visit the website.
