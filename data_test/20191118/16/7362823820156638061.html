Impeachment hearing witness Marie Yovanovitch receives standing ovation at DC jazz club

18 Nov 2019, 16:15 by Nick Givas

Marie Yovanovitch, former ambassador to Ukraine, received a standing ovation at a Washington, D.C. jazz club on Sunday evening, following her testimony in the Trump impeachment inquiry just two days prior.

The praise for the former bureaucrat was caught on video and posted to Twitter by an onlooker at the Blues Alley Club in Georgetown.

The audience clapped and cheered for the 60-year-old, as patrons stopped to thank her for her testimony against President Trump.
