Kerry Katona compares Prince Andrew 'car crash' interview to her slurred speech This Morning appearance

Kerry made her infamous This Morning interview where she slurred her words back in 2008

18 Nov 2019, 16:10 by Olivia Williams

One person who is pretty happy with Prince Andrew's 'disastrous' interview is no other than Kerry Katona.

The Warrington-born star posted on Instagram about her glee that her This Morning interview back in 2008 was no longer the "car crash of the century", reports Mirror Online.

The Duke of York answered questions for the first time on Saturday, November 16 for the first time about his links to paedophile billionaire Jeffrey Epstein.

The royal appeared to stumble and stutter as he was grilled by Newsnight host Emily Maitlis about allegations he had slept with a then 17-year-old, Virginia Roberts - who claims she was trafficked by Epstein to be a sex slave.

Prince Andrew made a string of bizarre claims during the excruciating interview, including his insistence that he couldn't sweat because of a medical condition meaning Virginia's testimony that he was "profusely sweating" while dancing at a club on March, 10 2001 couldn't be true.

He also said he didn't meet her at the club because he was at a Pizza Express in Woking at the time.

After the interview left the nation both infuriated and in stitches, Kerry took to Instagram today to express her gratitude to Prince Andrew.

She compared it to the time she slurred her words during a disastrous live TV appearance on This Morning more than 10 years ago.

Kerry was accused of being drunk but she has since insisted medication for her bi-polar disorder had affected her speech.

And today she took to Instagram to say Prince Andrew had stolen her crown for the worst TV interview ever.
