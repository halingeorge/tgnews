Berlin party Radiant Love launches label with new Fio Fa EP

You Think, out now, also features D. Tiffany on remix duty.

18 Nov 2019, 16:15 by Steph Lee

Berlin-based party Radiant Love has started a label under the same name.

The inaugural release, titled You Think, comes with two original house cuts from Irish producer Fio Fa, plus a remix from Canada's D. Tiffany. The 12-inch is out now.

Radiant Love, a "rave, label, art and performance collective," began in 2018, quickly becoming one of the most popular parties in Berlin's queer clubbing community.
