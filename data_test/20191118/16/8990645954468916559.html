Confirmed Shooting at Duncan Walmart This Morning

18 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Stryker

Reports are coming in about a shooting at the Walmart in Duncan, Oklahoma.

KSWO is reporting that multiple people were shot outside of the local Duncan Walmart this morning, they also say the shooter is among the dead as well. OKC Fox is reporting at least three fatalities at the location. No other information is available at this time. We will update the story as more comes in.
