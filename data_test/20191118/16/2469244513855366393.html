The team in Torquay dedicated to curing back pain

The Mount Stuart Hospital has a new spinal service

18 Nov 2019, 16:12 by Ginette Davies, Charlotte Gosling

One in four of us will suffer back pain, but for those who suffer from persistent pain, a new service in Torquay aims to help by providing a fast and accurate diagnosis.

Mount Stuart Hospital has a network of highly-qualified consultants and physiotherapists allowing patients to easily access the treatment and support they need.

Patients can move up and down a treatment chain at the private hospital, which includes physiotherapy, diagnostics, pain management and surgery, if appropriate.

"At Mount Stuart Hospital you will be kept informed about every stage of your treatment journey," said Jeanette Mercer, hospital director.

Your consultant will talk you through your condition and explain the reasons behind the pain and discomfort that you are experiencing before providing expert advice and recommendations on effective treatments.

“There is no need to go on suffering with back pain. In many cases, much can be done to relieve it and Ramsay Health Care has a broad range of expertise to give sufferers the best possible chance of recovery, " added Ms Mercer. 

“As well as excellent clinical care, Ramsay hospitals offer private patients our Premium Care package which aims to make your stay as comfortable as possible and ensure you get treatment at a time which suits you.”

It offers a range of treatments including injection therapies and spinal surgery, such as decompression and discectomy and spinal fusion.

Procedures involve a stay in hospital and the physiotherapy team may be involved in your rehabilitation, both while you are in hospital and afterwards.

So why choose the Mount Stuart Hospital for spinal care?

  • It has a coordinated team approach
  • It offers a first-class service
  • You will receive rapid access to treatment
  • There is a high level of patient satisfaction
