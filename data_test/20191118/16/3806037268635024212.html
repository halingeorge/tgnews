MEDICOM Toy Presents New BE＠RBRICKs for Marvel's 80th Anniversary

The graphic features several iconic superheroes.

18 Nov 2019, 16:32 by Isaac Rouse

Following the announcement of its Neon Genesis Evangelion-inspired collectible, MEDICOM Toy has presented its upcoming 100％ & 400％ BE＠RBRICK for Marvel‘s 80th anniversary. This Bearbrick features an all-over graphic of some seminal Marvel Comics covers, including Todd McFarland‘s iconic Spider-Man #1 cover and an issue of Punisher 2099. On the back of each piece features a giant ribbon commemorating Marvel’s milestone anniversary, with the numbers featuring key Marvel characters.

Although both Bearbrick’s are launching November 22, advance sales will start at Tokyo Comic-Con on the same day, with purchases limited to 1 piece per person. Grab Marvel’s 80th Anniversary 100% & 400% pieces for ￥12,000 JPY (approximately $110 USD).
