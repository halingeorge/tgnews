Michelle Keegan's latest glam is a look and fans are surprised

18 Nov 2019, 16:01

Michelle Keegan has given herself a super makeover with her latest glam.

The former Coronation Street star has shared a gorgeous photo of herself in a leather corset after a recently glam session. For what, we don’t know. All we do know is that her cute pooch got in on the action too.

Captioning the photo, she said: ‘Make up & hair did.

‘Beauty light.

‘Dog upstaging me.’

Fans were quick to comment on the Our Girl star’s change of appearance.

While agreeing that Michelle, 32, looked ‘beautiful’ as ever, many of them pointed out that she looked different.

An overwhelming number of heart eye and fire emojis were used by friends and fans throughout the comments.

People posted compliments like ‘unreal’, ‘super hot’ and ‘stunning’.

‘How can anyone be this fit?!’ another user said.

‘Michelle upstaging the world,’ was a comment from someone else.

Other shared their total appreciation of Michelle, while still commenting that her look had changed.

One person said: ‘Look how different she looks?’

Another commented: ‘I didn’t even recognise this as you.’

Of course there were also the fans who loved her dog.

Michelle is right in the midst of filming Brassic series 2, a comedy series set in Lancashire, and so getting a moment for herself is probably pretty difficult right now.

The star, who is married to former footballer and TOWIE guy Mark Wright in 2015, admits that these periods of being away from her husband are difficult.

It was particularly hard when she was away in Malaysia for Our Girl filming and Mark was in LA on a presenting job.

Nonetheless, the actress is clearly getting on with things and trying to have a laugh while she does so, pictured recently having fun on the set of Brassic in Manchester.
