BTS’ Jungkook is the first idol to surpass a cumulative of 1 billion views on his solo lives on the V Live platform

18 Nov 2019, 16:44 by dose-nel

Jungkook of BTS breaks yet another record as the only idol to surpass a whopping billion views on his solo V lives. BTS is the most followed artist on the platform and Jungkook's lives alone count for one-third of their total views. 

Six out of the top 10 of the most viewed BTS V lives belong to Jungkook. He also was the recipient of the "Vlive of the year" award. Congrats to Jungkook for breaking yet another record!
