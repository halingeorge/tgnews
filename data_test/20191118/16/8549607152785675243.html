Bybit launches BTC Brawl competition

18 Nov 2019, 16:55 by Eddie Mitchell

Derivatives Exchange Bybit will be launching the inaugural global ‘BTC Brawl’ Trading Competition. The competition will run from 28 November to 13 December 2019. 

The Bybit Games are back, and this time it’s bigger than ever! 

Come 18 November 2019, registration for the latest installment of Bybit’s hugely popular trading competition, dubbed – BTC Brawl, will kick-off. Unlike previous editions, this upcoming one promises to be a real global spectacle of epic proportions. As you can tell from its name, the competition will be fiercely contested among traders who will all be brawling for a prize pool of up to 100BTC! 

The inaugural BTC Brawl will follow a team format with team leaders gunning to recruit the best traders into their respective teams. Bybit believes that trading in teams will foster a greater sense of team-spirit among participants thus motivating them to work even harder whilst also forging a stronger sense of camaraderie in the community.  

The winners will be chosen by calculating the total “Realized Profit and Loss” of every participant. This ensures that there is a level playing field for all participants, regardless of factors like trading volume. 

Bybit is constantly striving to maintain a safe, reputable and reliable marketplace for traders to conduct their trading activities. This global BTC Brawl competition is the ideal platform for Bybit to showcase to the world that our platform will never have overload issues regardless of how many people are trading on it.  

Competition Info 

To qualify, traders must ensure that they have a minimum of 0.05BTC in equity. We strongly encourage participants to ensure they have the sufficient minimum amount in their accounts when the competition starts to avoid any potential immediate negative returns in the competition.  

Upon successful registration, participants must visit the event page to select and join their favorite team. Pick your teams wisely, once a participant has joined a team, they may not switch teams. 

Registration will commence on 18 November to 28 November 2019 and the competition will kick off on 28 November at 9 am (UTC), running up till 9 am (UTC) on 13 December 2019.  

Prize Pool & Bonuses  

The total prize pool (including team and individual awards) of the Bybit Games – BTC Brawl Edition can hit up to 100BTC. As part of Bybit’s commitment to giving back to society, 5% of the total prize pool (including team and individual awards) will be donated to charity.  

Participants who successfully register during the stipulated registration period will receive a one-time participation bonus. Participants will receive a bonus of either $10, $30 or $50 depending on when they sign up. So mark your calendars and sign up fast!  

Knock ‘em dead brawlers!
