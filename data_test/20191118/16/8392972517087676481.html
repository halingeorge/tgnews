Aldi is selling a gorgeous designer-look velvet chair for less than £60!

We predict a sell out…

18 Nov 2019, 16:11 by Nancy Brown

Fans of Aldi's Specialbuys are in for a treat this week after the store revealed details of the latest bargains that'll soon be up for grabs.

Launching on November 21 – but available for pre-order now – is a gorgeous statement velvet chair.

And we predict it'll be a sell out!

The super-stylish accent chair comes in either a deep rich navy or a contemporary grey.
