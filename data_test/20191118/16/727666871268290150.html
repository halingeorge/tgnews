Ludacris Goes Back To School At Georgia State University

18 Nov 2019, 16:00 by AllHipHop Staff

Luda is the subject of class at GSU, so the rap star decided to unexpectedly drop in on the students.

(AllHipHop News) Rapper Ludacris recently surprised students at Georgia State University by stopping by to check out a course dedicated to his career as an entertainer.

The rap star is the subject of a new College of Law class exploring the business contracts that shaped his professional life.

He decided to see what it's all about for himself by heading back to school to pay attorney Mo Ivory and her students a special visit.

Ludacris has since shared video footage of the experience on Instagram and marveled at how far he had come.

"One of the most Humbling Experiences of my Entire Life!!" he exclaimed in the video caption.

"I had the honor of visiting @georgiastateuniversity to drop in on #TheLegalLifeOfLudacris which is unbelievably the college course being taught at @gsulaw on my career by professor @moivoryshow...

"Thank you to all the students I met that wanted to learn more about my journey as an artist, actor, producer, entrepreneur, and philanthropist! I Hope This Inspires not only Artists Worldwide, but future Attorneys as Well. Love!"

The visit was particularly meaningful for Ludacris as he was also a student of GSU during his youth, although he only spent a year there back in 1998 studying music management.
