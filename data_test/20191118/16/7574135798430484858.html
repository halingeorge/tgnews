Anderson .Paak & Mark Ronson share new song from ‘Spies in Disguise’ soundtrack

18 Nov 2019, 16:56 by Andrew Sacher

Mark Ronson is releasing a soundtrack EP for Spies In Disguise, the upcoming animated spy comedy film with Will Smith and Tom Holland (which gets released on Christmas), and it includes a new collab with Anderson .Paak. It finds the two artists offering up their take on classic, ’70s-style soul, and there’s a bit of a Bond Theme vibe to this too which makes sense considering it was written for a spy film. The soundtrack also includes a new Mark Ronson/Dodgr song, the De La Soul collab from the new DJ Shadow album, Rob Base & DJ EZ Rock’s “It Takes Two,” and more.
