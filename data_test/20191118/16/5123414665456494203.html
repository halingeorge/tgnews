Premiere: Asylum Preps Dark, Minimal, 2-Track EP With "Micro Dose" Via Uprise Audio

These articles will focus on specific EP premieres/reviews for certain artists/labels within the 140 scene and beyond. This one features the artist known as Asylum with his 2 track release on Seven's own label Uprise Audio.

18 Nov 2019, 16:01 by Benjamin Oliver Lloyd

Uprise Audio keep up the trend of heavy-weight releases with this 2 track slammer forthcoming from Asylum. Having spent nearly 10+ years engulfed in bass heavy music as part of the renowned London based drum & bass collective Vicious Circle, Asylum was no stranger to the low end vibrations of dubstep when he decided to experiment with the slower tempo in 2011. 

We were lucky enough to premiere the track "Micro Dose" which you can listen too below.
