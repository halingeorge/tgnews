Charmed's Past is the Present in New Promo

18 Nov 2019, 16:11 by Sam Stone

The latest episode of The CW's Charmed reboot takes things back to the past in a new promo for Season 2's seventh episode.

The promo for "Past Is Present" sees the sisters and Harry stumble across several women in a compromising position before learning that the Shadow Demon Parker is poised to make a return.
