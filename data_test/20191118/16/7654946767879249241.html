Dad shares 10-year-old daughter’s ridiculous $10,000 Christmas list 

18 Nov 2019, 16:14 by Hannah Sparks

Santa might have to take out a loan to cover one girl’s exorbitant Christmas wish list.

An over-the-top letter of gift requests has gone viral after a father in Los Angeles shared it with his followers. The list includes 26 presents, including an iPhone 11, a “new” MacBook air, a Chanel purse, Gucci slides, a pet rabbit, Air Pods, a GoPro and $4,000 — without any explanation of intention for the cash.

“My 10-year-old daughter must be out of her mind with this Christmas list,” the father, with the Twitter handle @A_Johnson412, wrote in a tweet with over 124,000 likes so far.

Some on social media were as shocked as dad, and claimed she needed “to be taught the true meaning of Christmas.”

“It’s not about getting presents and being greedy. It’s about loving your fellow man and woman, and giving of your self, and helping the less fortunate,” they wrote.

However many actually praised the young woman’s lavish taste.

“She knows exactly what she wants and how to ask for it. Love it,” wrote one fan.

“An alarm clock, Gucci slides and some laundry detergent? Mama has range and her priorities in check!” said another.

The outlandish list also includes some hilarious spelling errors, such as a request for “asenchal oil” and “julery,” plus other bizarre appeals, including glue and food coloring. With those ingredients in mind, some followers determined she has plans to make slime — a mixture that renders a gooey pudding for kids to play with.

But it was the most mysterious ask of all that seemed to capture social media.

“WTH she gone do with $4,000[?]” one asked.

“I was thinking this wasn’t so bad until I got to the $4,000!!” another added.
