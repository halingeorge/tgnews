'Hazard Was A Lazy Trainer'

18 Nov 2019, 16:13 by SL International

Eden Hazard is widely regarded as one of the world's best footballers, but a former African teammate of his has now claimed that the Real Madrid star was a very lazy trainer at Chelsea.
