CRASH CHARGE

Scots BMW driver charged with dangerous driving after smashing into lamppost having failed to clear frosty windscreen

18 Nov 2019, 16:01 by Blair Meikle

A SCOTS driver has been charged after crashing into a lamppost having failed to clear frost from his windscreen.

Road Policing officers attended in West Lothian after the BMW smashed into traffic lights this morning.

They say the crash was avoidable if they had taken to time to scrape their windscreen.

The driver has been charged with a number of alleged offences, including dangerous driving.

A report will be submitted to the procurator fiscal.

A spokesperson for Police Scotland said: "#WestLothianRP attended a collision this morning which was more than likely avoidable had they taken an extra few minutes to clear their windscreen.

"Driver has been charged with a variety of offences including dangerous driving and will be reported to the Procurator Fiscal."
