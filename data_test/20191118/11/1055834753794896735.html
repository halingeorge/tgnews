Japan: Famitsu hosting Smash Bros Ultimate cross-company tournament featuring Sony Interactive Entertainment

18 Nov 2019, 11:18 by My Nintendo News Admin

Famitsu is organising the Super Smash Bros Ultimate battle to end all battles as they have announced a cross-company tournament which will feature members of Nintendo’s rivals, Sony Interactive Entertainment. This spectacular tournament will kick off on Friday, 22nd November at 8pm Japan time. Here’s the details along with the eight companies who are participating in the “Famitsu Presents Super Smash Bros. Ultimate Cross-Company Game Tournament in Tokyo Odaiba Oedo Onsen Monogatari:”

Employees of the above eight companies will battle it out in team-based matches at the onsen theme park Tokyo Odaiba Oedo Onsen Monogatari to determine which among them are the better Super Smash Bros. Ultimate players.
  • Sun-Star Stationery
  • Sugiko
  • StylingLife Holdings PlazaStyle Company
  • Zoff
  • Taisho Pharmaceutical
  • Tokkyo Chou (Japan Patent Office)
  • Lovegraph
  • Sony Interactive Entertainment
