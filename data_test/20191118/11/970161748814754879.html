Desperate appeal to find boy, 17, who went missing after Liam Gallagher gig

18 Nov 2019, 11:31

A search is underway for a 17-year-old boy who vanished last night shortly after a concert in Newcastle.

Worried family members say Joe Hogg was last seen at around 11.30pm in a car park following former Liam Gallagher’s gig at the Utilita Arena.

They say he had no shirt on at the time and does not have his phone or any money with him.

Now Northumbria Police are asking for the public’s help to find Joe, who is believed to be from the Durham area.

An appeal for help from Joe’s cousin retweeted by former Oasis frontman Gallagher to his 3.2 million followers.

The boy’s relative said: ‘My cousin Joe Hogg has gone missing last night after the Liam Gallagher gig in Newcastle.
