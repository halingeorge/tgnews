The best deals as Amazon starts Black Friday Countdown

The first Amazon Black Friday deals are live and they include Shark vacuum cleaners

18 Nov 2019, 11:27 by Stephen Hurrell

Amazon has started Black Friday early with the launch of discounts on Shark vacuum cleaners, toys and mobile phones.

The Amazon Black Friday Countdown is now live with a series of daily deals and shorter lightning deals as it prepares for the biggest shopping day of the year on 29 November.

The retailer has somewhat confusingly confirmed it will launch Black Friday deals from 22 November but has now released a host of discounts that it is calling a 'countdown' to the sale.

The discounts include a big reduction on popular vacuum cleaner brand Shark, which has been praised by cleaning influencer Mrs Hitch among others as an alternative to Dyson.

The Shark cordless stick vacuum cleaner has been reduced from £359.99 to less than £300 for a day only in the biggest 'daily deal' on Monday 18 November.

Amazon has also cut the prices of Samsung Galaxy 10 handsets , including a 128gb version down from £800 to £614. Samsung smart watches are also reduced from £279 to £179. The watches use Bluetooth and can monitor your sleep pattern and be used as a payment system via Samsung Pay.

Away from electronics Amazon has also reduced the price of Playmobil toys for the day. The deals include discounts on the Hawk Knights' Castle down from £89.99 to £58.99. Other Playmobil box sets have also been reduced for one day only.

The Amazon deals are available early for users of Amazon Prime. The service also includes free delivery on most items and is available for a month completely free using a free trial.

Amazon is not the first retailer to launch early Black Friday deals. Currys PC World has launched an early Black Friday prices sale , which it promises has cut the price of TVs and other items to prices matching those on Black Friday.

Sky TV has also launched an early Black Friday set of deals including half price Sky Sports and Netflix for just £6 a month.
