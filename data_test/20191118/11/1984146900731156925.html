Emirates announce sale on flights from Newcastle Airport to Bangladesh, China and the Philippines

Passengers can head to a number of destinations, including China, Nairobi and Uganda from £498

18 Nov 2019, 11:55 by Kali Lindsay

Looking for your next holiday destination?

Emirates has launched a sale on economy fares from Newcastle International Airport to destinations including Bangladesh, Pakistan, Kenya, China, Uganda and the Philippines.

People can snap up deals to travel between November 21 this year and March 30 next year.

Flights from Newcastle to China or Nairobi start from £489, and to Pakistan from £529.

Passengers can also fly to Dhaka, Bangladesh, from £569 and to Entebbe, Uganda, from £549.

They can also enjoy a 40kg baggage allowance if they book any round-trip economy fare at no extra charge, if they book by November 25.

Full list of flights on sale from Newcastle International Airport

Dhaka, Bangladesh from £569

Karachi, Pakistan from £529

Nairobi, Kenya from £489

Shanghai, China from £489

Entebbe, Uganda from £549

Manila, Philippines from £639

To book your next flight with Emirates visit  www.emirates.com/UK .
