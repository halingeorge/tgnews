I'm A Celebrity Video: Kate Garraway has nightmare in hellish Bushtucker trial

18 Nov 2019, 11:14

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out of Here! kicks off the really grim Bushtucker trials of the series tonight as Caitlyn Jenner and Kate Garraway are trapped in a box full of six-legged critters in Bugged Out.

After almost being blown off a sky scraper before she’d even made it into the jungle last night, Good Morning Britain presenter Kate is having another nightmare as she goes up against the former Olympian.

The rivals are hoping to win stars for their respective camps by dropping balls into moving holes while descending in the air with a swarm of cockroaches to keep them company.

Caitlyn is impressively cool, calm and collected on one end of the spectrum while Kate, however, couldn’t be more hysterical without being actual Gillian McKeith.

But who will come out on top?

Last night ITV launched the 19th series of I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out of Here! with what is without a doubt the best line-up in almost two decades of the show.

Caitlyn has already seen her odds slashed to become Queen of the Jungle after an impressive opening night, while she’s closely followed by Rak-Su singer Myles Stephenson, who is hoping for ‘a second chance’ with the superstar’ supermodel daughter, Kendall Jenner. When was the first?

And fans were delighted by the return of Ant McPartlin as he was reunited with Declan Donnelly after missing last year’s I’m A Celebrity while he continued his recovery following an arrest for drink-driving.

The pair kicked off the night with a timely dig at Prince Andrew and his miraculous powers to swerve heavy sweating, which was just one of the extraordinary revelations to come from the royal’s car crash Newsnight interview on Saturday night.

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out of Here! continues tonight at 9pm on ITV.
