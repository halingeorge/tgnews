Oil price hike, rollback today

18 Nov 2019, 11:03 by Edd Reyes

OIL companies will implement mixed adjustments in pump prices starting today.

Pilipinas Shell announced a P0.85 hike in prices of gasoline but will lower diesel and kerosene prices by P0.25 effective  6 a.m. today.

Petron Philippines also announced the same price adjustment in gasoline products and rollback of P0.30 for diesel and P0.20 for kerosene.

Earlier, Cleanfuel slashed its diesel prices by P0.30 but will implement P0.80 increase in gasoline prices starting tomorrow noon.

Meanwhile, independent oil firm PTT Philippines  announced an increase of P0.85 for  gasoline prices and P0.15 rollback in diesel effective today.
