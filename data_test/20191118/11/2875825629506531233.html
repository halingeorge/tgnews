I'm A Celebrity odds as Myles Stephenson and Caitlyn Jenner impress on the first episode

The Rak-Su singer and reality TV star impressed the most after the first episode

18 Nov 2019, 11:23 by Irishmirror.ie

Myles Stephenson has been the biggest mover in the market to win I’m A Celebrity after the new series started on Sunday.

The Rak-Su member moved dramatically from 20/1 outsider to 6/1 making the singer third favourite with The Pools .

The former X-Factor contestant impressed in the first bushtucker trials after beating James Haskell in the swimming challenge before choosing radio DJ Roman Kemp - also at 6/1 - as his partner.

They partnership paid off as the return of the dreaded plank challenge saw them have a combined time of 1.45 seconds meaning they immediately became a “power pair”.

Former rugby player James Haskell shifted away from being second-favourite at 5/1 to now 8/1 after missing out on the canoe challenge.

The MMA fighter chose Caitlyn Jenner as his partner given her sporting background but failed to beat DJ Adele Roberts ( 21/1 ) and Nadine Coyle who was comforted by Jenner.

The reality TV star impressed the viewers with her calm approach and motivated Coyle to take part in the skydiving challenge with her and saw her odds go from 10/1 to 4/1 with The Pools .

Jenner now joins Good Morning Britain TV presenter Kate Garraway ( 12/1 ) in the first bushtucker trial voted by the public.
