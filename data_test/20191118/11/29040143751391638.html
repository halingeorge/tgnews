Leather cases also tend to be more functional, often with a folio design and built-in card slots for an all-in-one solution for your phone and wallet — but even if you don't dig that style, there are still great leather cases available! Wallet, case: Amovo 2-in-1 wallet case

This PU leather wallet case might just be the perfect case for your Galaxy S9 and S9+, period. It's a folio wallet with three card slots and a cash pocket, with a magnetic closure and a cover that folds into a kickstand. But the inner bumper case detaches from its magnetic hold, so it ends up being just a great leather and TPU rubber case for when you don't need your wallet. Comes in three colors.

Simple and elegant: Shieldon leather wallet case

Shieldon's wallet cases for Galaxy S9 and S9+ have a hidden magnetic closure, giving them a sleek look, and they come in black, red, or a very alluring blue. The inner bumper is made of flexible TPU rubber, which protects your phone from drops and bumps. There are three card slots and a cash pocket inside the front cover.

This unique looking case combines a sturdy TPU shell with perforated PU leather around the bottom half of the case, which holds the card slot that can store up to two cards at a time.

Leather cases add a touch of class to any phone

When you're buying a premium flagship like the Galaxy S9, it just makes sense to go with a premium case — and leather is as posh and luxurious as it gets.

There's a lot of crossover between leather cases and wallet cases, but that's just because it's one of the smartest ways to incorporate leather into a phone case. I'd recommend the Snakehive Folio Wallet Case because it's made with genuine leather and offers sound protection for your phone while also letting you keep your most important cards and some cash with you all in one place. If you want a leather case without the front cover, consider the Poetic Nubuck Credit Card Slot Case.

If, however, you find that a leather case isn't for you, there are still plenty of other cases available for both the Galaxy S9and the S9+. You'll be sure to find something that fits your needs.
