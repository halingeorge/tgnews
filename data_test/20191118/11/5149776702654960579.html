Three Arnold residents win cash prize from Postcode Lottery

The Coppice Road neighbours scooped the windfall

18 Nov 2019, 11:06 by Gemma Toulson

Three people in Arnold are celebrating after winning an £1,000 cash prize from the Postcode Lottery.

The Coppice Road neighbours scooped the windfall when NG5 7HD was announced as a daily prize winner with People’s Postcode Lottery on Monday, November, 18, 2019.

People’s Postcode Lottery ambassador and former X Factor contestant Danyl Johnson sent his congratulations and said: “What a way to start the week. I hope they enjoy their winnings and treat themselves to something nice.”

A spokesman for the Postcode Lottery said: "A minimum of 32 percent of ticket sales goes directly to charities and players of People’s Postcode Lottery have raised £486 million to date for thousands of good causes in Britain and beyond.

"This draw was promoted by Friends of the Earth, which has received over £7.2 million in funding from the players of People’s Postcode Lottery. This support helps it campaign for environmental and social causes around the world."
