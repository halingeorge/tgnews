Inside Edge Season 2 trailer: The Amazon Prime Original looks bigger and better

Inside Edge Season 2 stars Vivek Oberoi, Richa Chadha, Aamir Bashir, Tanuj Virwani, Angad Bedi, Sayani Gupta and Sapna Pabbi among others.

18 Nov 2019, 11:28 by Entertainment Desk

The makers of Inside Edge Season 2 on Monday released the trailer of the Amazon Prime Original.

In the web series, as politics and cricket converge, Aamir Bashir’s character struggles to pull the game into the limelight. Cricketers are seen injecting drugs and getting embroiled in all kinds of scandals. Richa Chadha aka Zarina Malik wants in on the bigger game while we see the characters of Tanuj Virwani and Angad Bedi lock horns.

Sitting and watching all of it unfold is Vivek Oberoi’s cunning and manipulative Vikrant Dhawan. Basically, Inside Edge Season 2 promises to up the ante in all the departments, but only time will tell if it will be able to repeat the success of the first season.
