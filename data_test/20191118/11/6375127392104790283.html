3 drug peddlers busted

18 Nov 2019, 11:20 by Joel dela Torre

POLICE arrested three alleged drug pushers who yielded P1.7 million worth of shabu during a buy-bust at a condominium early morning yesterday in Quezon City.

Acting QCPD director Col. Ronnie Montejo identified the suspects as Emilyn Luna, 35, of Bgy. Tatalon; Zainodin Encio, 45, of Bgy. Sto. Cristo, Diliman, and Mark Mercado, 33, of Bgy. Vasra.

Montejo said a joint operation conducted by the Galas Police Station (PS 11) and the Criminal Investigation and Detection Group (CIDG) of Northern Manila District Field Unit arrested the suspects inside Room 12 at the 21st floor of Grass Residences Condominium Corporation located at the corner of Nueva Ecija Street and Nueva Viscaya in Bgy. Sto. Cristo, at around 3 a.m.

A deal was set up after the operatives completed a drug deal with the suspects.

Seized from the suspects were 250 grams of suspected illegal drugs with estimated value of P1,700,000, the buy-bust money and cell phones used in their drug deals.

Montejo said Encio has been previously arrested for the same offense while Luna was included in the drug watch list of the station.
