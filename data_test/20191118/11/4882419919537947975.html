Ever Banega reveals Lionel Messi's greatest virtue

The Sevilla midfielder spoke about his Argentine captain

18 Nov 2019, 11:46 by Sport EN

Sevilla midfielder Ever Banega revealed Lionel Messi's greatest virtue.

In an interview with Goal, Banega spoke about his Argentina captain, with whom he is good friends.

"Yes, I don't think there's anyone like him," said Banega. "The truth is that for everything he does and the time he's spent among the best, yes I think he's the best."

Banega explained Messi's virtue is that "in humility, Messi never changes. We always greet each other with a hug when we cross paths and never speak about football, instead our families and how we are."
