What time is I'm A Celebrity on tonight?

18 Nov 2019, 11:47

I’m A Celebrity 2019 is underway, kicking off on Sunday night with a cracking series opener.

Last night’s show saw Ant and Dec – reunited on the show after Ant’s year off in 2018 – introducing us to this year’s jungle hopefuls, among them Caitlyn Jenner and Girls’ Aloud’s Nadine Coyle, who got things off to a flying start with a skydive.

The show also saw the camps divided into two as Myles Stephenson, Roman Kemp, Adele Roberts and Nadine Coyle won themselves ‘power pair’ status and got to proceed straight to the main camp, complete with nice food and better conditions – while the others were left to rough it in Snake Rock.

Things are set to get even tougher for the celebs tonight as the first Bushtucker trial of the series – named Bugged Off – rears its critter-filled head, with Caitlyn and Kate Garraway braving the challenge to win meals for camp.

But just what time does it all kick off?

Here’s what you need to know…

What time is I’m A Celebrity on tonight?

I’m A Celebrity is on ITV on Monday night at 9pm.

Sunday night’s series opener was an extended episode to kick the series off, running for one hour and 45 minutes.

However Monday night’s instalment is slightly shorter, running for an hour and a half ahead of the news at 10.30.

The nightly show will get shorter from tomorrow, with hour-long episodes airing at 9pm.

Monday’s show will catch up with the celebs in the main camp and Snake Rock, as well as revealing how Caitlyn and Kate got on with their Bushtucker Trial.

Is I’m A Celebrity on every night?

It is indeed on seven nights a week – although do keep an eye on those schedules in case it’s bumped for any live sporting or other events, as has happened in previous years.
