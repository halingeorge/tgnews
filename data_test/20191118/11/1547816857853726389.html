Coolie No 1: Varun Dhawan Gets Playful On-The-Sets With A Girl And No, She’s Not Sara Ali Khan

Varun Dhawan is been keeping his fans updated with pictures and videos as he is currently shooting for his upcoming film Coolie No. 1. For the first time, Varun has been paired opposite Sara Ali Khan and looks like their chemistry is surely gonna be a hit. Seeing their off-screen Masti in their Instagram posts, we are sure the film will be an out-and-out entertainer. We still remember the first day of shoot where Varun had shared a hilarious testing video of his looks as Raja Babu. He also shared an introductory video of Kunwar Mahendra Pratap.

A while back, Varun took to social media and shared a video of him playing with a girl. But let us tell you the girl is not his leading lady Sara Ali Khan. Sharing the video on his Insta handle, Varun Dhawan captioned it, “Peek a boo on set. A very serious game” As seen in the clip, the actor can be seen in a playful mood with a cute li’l girl who plays peek-a-boo with him. Well, there’s no Bollywood movie complete without a villain. For Coolie No. 1, Vikas Verma has been signed to play the villain in the film. Speaking about his role in David Dhawan’s next, Vikas had told Hindustan Times, “When David sir called me in March and told me he has a part [for me] in a film he was making. I was too excited to ask anything else, and it was enough he had remembered me. Also, I had no idea it was for Coolie No 1 remake. But finally, it’s happening. I had to beef up for the character. David sir wanted me to go bigger and sharper for the role as I play a baddie.”
