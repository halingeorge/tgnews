I'm A Celebrity: Extra Camp fans beg Joe Swash to return

He quit his role on the show earlier this year

18 Nov 2019, 11:04 by Rebecca Carter

The new series of I'm A Celebrity returned last night and fans were delighted to have Ant McPartlin and Dec Donnelly back on their screens.

However, it seems the line-up for spin-off show Extra Camp didn't thrill everyone as many begged Joe Swash to return.

Joe and Scarlett Moffatt stepped down from their roles on the ITV2 show earlier this year and Adam Thomas and Emily Atack joined the show alongside Joel Dommett.
