Things you only know if you live in Coseley

A guide to the weird and wonderful things about living on Coseley

18 Nov 2019, 11:03 by Scott Edwards, Charlotte Regen, https://www.facebook.com/scottedwardsjournalist/, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/charlotte-regen/

Coseley is a place where many people have lived for all of their lives.

Like everywhere there's just some things you only know if you live or are from there.

So Black Country Live have created a list of things you only know if you live or have lived in Coseley!

Here they are:

1. It's Roseville

Who even calls it Rose Village?

2. Doing your weekly shop at Heron Foods/ Cool Trader

There's some right bargains to be had!

3. Scoffs is the best!

Pork stuffing and gravy on top of orange chips? Yes please!

4. When the photography shop was a video shop

Choosing a film to watch with the family was the Saturday night highlight back in the day.

5. Going for a walk down the cut

People actually do this.

 

6. There's no where to park for the commuters

The car parks are for shoppers and library users only!

7. Everyone knows everyone

You're guaranteed to see someone you know when you go into Roseville.

8. The Spar

Gone but never forgotten!

9. There's no shortage of pubs!

And they're all within walking distance!

10. Driving down Central Drive

It's forever double parked, no one knows who has priority and everyone speeds.

Good luck parking outside your own home if you live down there.

11. There's loads of bridges

Are you even human if you don't jump every time a train zooms under them?

12. Jubillee Park aka Jubo

Friday night after school it was the place to be.

13. Mushy Field

This was for the older kids to hang around on - don't tell the year sevens.

14. Coseley Swimming Baths

Going there every day in the six weeks holidays was the highlight of our childhoods.

Not forgetting the yellow slide, which was pretty much always closed.

 

15. The Coseley school was the best!

That was until it closed...

16. Getting a free train ride from Coseley to Wolverhampton.

We know you've done it, don't deny it.

17. Where even is is Coseley?

It ain't in Wolvo and we doe wanna be part of Dudlay!

18. You know you're in Coseley when...

You can see the big green beacon that is the BP Garage and those lovely golden arches at Maccies!

19. BP Garage is your late night saviour

Everyone gets their late night booze stash from there.

20. At least one person owns or knows someone who has a horse...

Nobody mows their front lawn, they've got their pet 'oss to do it!

21. That smell on Darkhouse Lane

You know the one!

22. Cannon Raceway

If you've ever been go karting, you went there!

23. How do you even get to the allotments?

It's a secret, you only know if you've got one.

24. Shout out to the animal hospital!

Roseville vets salute!
