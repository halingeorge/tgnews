Abu Dhabi Culinary Season launches Dine and Win promotion

Win big cash prizes when dining out at restaurants across the capital

18 Nov 2019, 11:28 by Colin Armstrong

Abu Dhabi Culinary Season is here and there’s loads going on.

There’s the exciting Tastemasters series, Pit Stop Menus, the Time Out Abu Dhabi Restaurant Awards and more.

As part of the campaign, The Department of Culture and Tourism - Abu Dhabi has partnered with Zomato to launch the Dine and Win promotion, taking place from December 14.

Prizes up for grabs include Dhs25,000 and Dhs10,000 in mall vouchers, to be spent at locations across Abu Dhabi.

All you have to do is spend Dhs200 at participating restaurants such as Namak by Kunal Kapur, Pachaylen, Bord Eau, Amerigos, Masion Beirut and more to be in with a chance of winning.

You will receive a scratch card every time you spend Dhs200 at the participating venues where you can get the chance to win the prizes.
For the list of all participating restaurants in the promotion, visit www.zomato.com/abudhabi/dine-win-adcs
