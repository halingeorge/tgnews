DNeX 3Q net profit soars 42% boosted by lower income tax

18 Nov 2019, 11:33 by Wong Ee Lin

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 18): Dagang Nexchange Bhd’s (DNeX) net profit grew 42.6% to RM9.48 million in the third quarter ended Sept 30, 2019 (3QFY19), from RM6.65 million in the previous corresponding quarter, thanks to lower income tax.

The company paid lower income tax of RM831,000 in the quarter under review, compared with RM3.41 million last year, due to the over provision in the prior year.

DNeX also posted improved profit before tax from its energy segment of RM6.79 million, 69.8% higher from RM4 million in 3QFY18.

Earnings per share expanded to 0.54 sen, from 0.38 sen.

This was despite a 2.07% decline in quarterly revenue to RM62 million, from RM63.31 million, according to a filing with the local stock exchange today.

For the cumulative nine-month period (9MFY19), its net profit was down 3.65% to RM33.76 million or 1.92 sen per share, from RM35.04 million or 1.99 sen per share last year. Revenue increased 9.45% to RM203.15 million, from RM185.6 million.

Moving forward, DNeX said the group expects to deliver positive results for year 2019, barring any unforeseen circumstances.

“The group continues to aggressively pursue its Group-wide operational cost optimization programme, driving towards achieving improvements in operational efficiency and business profitability based on a sustainable cost structure, includes consolidation of existing business operations,” DNeX said.

Shares of DNeX closed up one sen to 27.5 sen today, valuing the company at RM483.46 million. Some 3.86 million shares were traded.
