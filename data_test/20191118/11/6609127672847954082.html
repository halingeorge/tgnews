DOG DAYS

Woman turns under the stairs cupboard into adorable ‘bedroom’ for her dog

18 Nov 2019, 11:25 by Rebecca Flood

MINIATURE transformations are all the rage now with people turning crawl spaces and cupboards into play houses and hideaways.

Jumping on the bandwagon, one woman decided to turn her cupboard under-the-stairs into a bedroom – for her dog.

Sharing before and after photos to Facebook group to Extreme
Budget DIY & Life Hacks, she revealed her handiwork for her pet Labrador.

She captioned the post: “Wasn’t using my under stairs cupboard so made my Labrador a bedroom.”

The woman had turned an empty white space into a den for her four-legged friend, adding in a dog bed, food bowls, as well as adding marble and tile-effect walls and floors.

She put up a shelf for his doggy treats, and even hung bunting with his name outside and a snap of her pooch in a bone-shaped frame.

Her DIY skills have gone down well with pet owners, with her post racking up more than 500 likes.
