HTET 2019 answer key to be released tomorrow

HTET answer key 2019: Of the 2.83 lakh who applied, 1.77 lakh had appeared for the exam conducted on November 16 and 17. The answer key will be released tomorrow at bseh.org.in or htetonline.com.

18 Nov 2019, 11:24 by Careers Desk

HTET answer key 2019: The Haryana Board of School Education (HBSE) will release the answer key for the Haryana Teacher Eligibility Test (HTET) conducted over the weekend – November 17 and 18 on Tuesday – November 19. The HTET answer key will be available to download at the official websites, bseh.org.in or htetonline.com.

The candidates can raise objections on answer key from November 21 to 25, 2019.

As many as 2,83,878 lakh candidates have applied for the HTET of which 1.77 lakh appeared for the exams. Of the total, 99621 candidates appeared for level 2 of the examination while 78,334 appeared for level 1. The level 2 exam was conducted across 360 centres while level 1 test was held across 292 centres.

A total of 22 exam centres were on high control and were monitored directly through Board head office. A female-only flying squad also visited across centres to curb cheating.

HTET answer key 2019: How to download

Step 1: Visit the official website, htetonline.nic.in
Step 2: On the homepage, click on the link ‘answer key’ (yet to be displayed)
Step 3: A PDF will open, check the answer key

Candidates will be allowed to raise objections against the answer key. All the objections will be considered and a final answer key will be released thereafter. The result will be based on the final answer key.

To qualify for the exam, candidates need to score 60 per cent marks or 90 marks. Those belonging to the reserved category will be eligible with a score of 55 per cent that is 82 marks, as per the rules. Those who clear Paper I of HTET will be eligible to teach in classes 1 to 5 while those who clear the trained graduate teacher (TGT) will be eligible to teach up to class 12.
