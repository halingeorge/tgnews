Kuwait’s Emir Removes Defense, Interior Ministers

18 Nov 2019, 11:15

Kuwait's Emir on Monday ordered the removal of Defense Minister Sheikh Nasser Sabah Al-Ahmad Al-Sabah, and Interior Minister Sheikh Khaled Al-Jarrah Al-Sabah from their posts in the current caretaker cabinet.

Emir Sheikh Sabah Al-Ahmad Al-Sabah also reappointed Sheikh Jaber Al-Mubarak Al-Sabah as prime minister, who declined to be reappointed, state news agency KUNA said.

"I decline this appointment and ask that you accept that," Sheikh Jaber said in a letter to the emir, carried by KUNA.

The Emir will address the nation on Monday evening.

Sheikh Jaber submitted the government's resignation last week.
