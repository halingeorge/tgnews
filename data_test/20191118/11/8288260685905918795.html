Who are the candidates in Birmingham Edgbaston for the 2019 general election?

18 Nov 2019, 11:11 by Jonathan Walker, https://www.facebook.com/JonWalkerPolitics?pnref=story

Birmingham Edgbaston was once considered to be a safe Conservative seat, but it's had a Labour MP since 1997.

The Tories have attempted to win the seat back in a number of elections since then, but they have not been succesful.

Since then, the constituency has changed and it's no longer a Tory target seat. However, it's still marginal enough that anything could happen in this election.

The candidates standing in 2019

Preet Kaur Gill - Labour Party

Colin Green - Liberal Democrats to stop Brexit

Phil Simpson - Green Party

David Wilks - Brexit Party

Alex Yip - Conservative Party
