Burnt man found in rice field

18 Nov 2019, 11:24 by Steve A. Gosuico

STA. ROSA, Nueva Ecija — Police are investigating the death of a man who on Sunday was found burned in a rice field in this municipality.

Town police head Maj. Steven Dela Cruz said the charred body was discovered by Bgy. Rajal Centro Capt. Alex Encarnacion, who immediately reported the gruesome find to the authorities at 7:40 a.m. on the same day.

The corpse was described as about 5’4 in height, 40-45 years old, of medium-built and had a polynesian tattoo on the leg.

Dela Cruz said other evidence  at the crime scene, including CCTV footages, were being gathered to help probers in identifying  the victim and learning the  motive for the killing.
