5 men arrested for gang rape in Bulacan

18 Nov 2019, 11:26 by Zaida Delos Reyes

A MINOR and four others on Sunday were arrested for allegedly raping a 19-year-old girl in Meycauayan City, Bulacan.

Bulacan provincial police director  Col. Chito Bersaluna identified the suspects as Renz Justin Pineda, 19; Jan Fulgencio, 18; Marvin Tulgencio, 20;  Joever Osias, 19, and alias “Marc,” 16.

Members of the Meycauayan police arrested the suspects based on the complaint filed by Danica, 19.

Danica said the rape occurred on November 16 in an abandoned house a few meters away from the suspects’ residence.

Before the alleged gang rape, Danica had drinks with the suspects. When she was already drunk, the suspects brought her to an abandoned house, where they raped her.

The victim managed to escape and reported the molestation to the police, who immediately conducted a follow-up operation that led to the arrest of the five.
