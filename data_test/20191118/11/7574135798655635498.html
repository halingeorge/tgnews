Lunachicks are back, announce first show in over a decade

18 Nov 2019, 11:15 by Bill Pearis

While they never officially broke up, NYC punk rock band Lunachicks went into extended hiatus in 2001 and have only played a couple reunion shows since, the last being the 2004 March for Women’s Lives Benefit in Washington, D.C. The band are now officially back, though, and have announced their first show since then — Webster Hall on April 11. Tickets for that show go on sale Friday, November 22 at 10 AM with an AmEx presale on Wednesday (11/20) at 10 AM.
