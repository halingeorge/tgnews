Fans leave 'lip-synching' Janet Jackson’s concert

18 Nov 2019, 11:47

Cape Town - Janet Jackson’s fans are not having any of it!

During her concert in Brisbane, Australia on Friday 15 November, people stormed out of the venue, accusing the 53-year-old of lip-synching throughout her performance, Fox News reports.

Concert-goer Stuart Watson told the Daily Mail the That’s The Way Love Goes singer’s performance was "terrible". 

"When Janet Jackson came on it was just a disaster," he said.

"Her sound was just terrible. She would be singing and then all of a sudden just do random stuff."

A video taken and posted on social media shows fans walking out just two songs into Jackson’s performance, The Sun reports.

"She’s so obviously lip-synching and barely even dancing. On top of which she looks so out of shape. So unfair to the audience," one fan said.

"If you are going to tour and charge people all that money then do your job @janetjackson."

Sources: Fox News , Daily Mail , The Sun
