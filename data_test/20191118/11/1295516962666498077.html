A Guardians of the Galaxy Star Has Joined the Snyder Cut Movement

18 Nov 2019, 11:11 by John Ridlehoover

DC Extended Universe alums aren't the only ones asking for director Zack Snyder's cut of Justice League. Now, Guardians of the Galaxy star Dave Bautista has joined the #ReleaseTheSnyderCut movement. The actor even hinted he has seen the Snyder Cut himself.

"Yes!! Release the Kracken!!! I mean!! #RealeaseTheSnyderCut!!!" Bautista posted in response to a Tweet from Snyder. "I won’t say that I’ve seen it but I also won’t say that I haven’t! Annnnnnnd... release the damn Zack Snyder cut already!"
