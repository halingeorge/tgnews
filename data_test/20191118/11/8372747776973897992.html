18-year-old Golden Gate Estates man reported missing

18 Nov 2019, 11:31

COLLIER COUNTY, Fla. -- Deputies are asking for help locating a missing Golden Gate Estates 18-year-old.

According to the Collier County Sheriff's Office, Justin Wimmer was last seen around 2:30 a.m. Thursday, November 14 by his roommates at their residence in the 200 block of 15th Street Southwest.

Wimmer is described as 5 feet 11 inches tall and 140 pounds with brown hair and brown eyes.

If you have any information on his whereabouts, please contact the Collier County Sheriff's Office at 239-252-9300.
