Sara Ali Khan On The No Plastic Use On Coolie No. 1 Sets: “There Are Other Changes I’d Like To See”

Sara is not only passionate about acting and fitness but is socially aware

18 Nov 2019, 11:43 by Koimoi.com Team

Bollywood’s ‘Buzz Girl’ Sara Ali Khan is only one year old in the industry and has created a colossal impact post her debut. First seen in Kedarnath, Sara became Bollywood’s sweetheart by winning millions of hearts not only with her excellent acting skills but also her simplistic nature.

Recently, in an interview with a leading magazine, the actress mentioned how she is not only passionate about acting and fitness but is socially aware and wants to do her bit for the society however she can.

Sara shares, “I may not be vocal about a specific political issue, but there are small things I feel I can do to make a difference. For instance, the entire Coolie No. 1 team has pledged to stop using single-use plastic on set. Of course, there are other changes I’d like to see set in motion.”

Winning on both brands and movie front, the actress already has 11 brand endorsements in her kitty where the brands range from sports brands to jewellery brands which only goes on to speak volumes about how versatile ‘Buzz girl of Bollywood’ is!

The actress will be next seen in the recently announced Imtiaz Ali ‘s next alongside Kartik Aaryan and with Varun Dhawan in ‘Coolie No.1‘, respectively. With a trail of promising projects, both the films are slated to release next year.
