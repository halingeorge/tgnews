Breaking: Bello declared winner of Kogi governorship poll

18 Nov 2019, 11:32 by The Eagle Online

The Independent National Electoral Commission has declared Governor Yahaya Bello of Kogi State winner of the governorship election in the state.

Bello, who flew the flag of the All Progressives Congress for the election, was declared winner of the contest on Monday morning.

His closest rival was the candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party, Musa Wada, who on Sunday faulted the conduct of the election.
