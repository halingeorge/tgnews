Days Gone Is PlayStation Game of the Year at This Year's Golden Joystick Awards

Trophy unlocked!

18 Nov 2019, 11:30 by Stephen Tailby

The Golden Joystick Awards 2019 wrapped up over the weekend, and this year's winners have been announced. The annual, public-voted awards ceremony took place on Friday evening, and we can now see which games took home those hefty-looking trophies.

Days Gone was nominated in four categories, and rides away with two wins; Best Storytelling and PlayStation Game of the Year. Also earning itself a duo of awards is Resident Evil 2, which escapes with nods for Best Audio and the top honour of Ultimate Game of the Year. Beat Saber deservedly wins Best VR/AR Game, while Cyberpunk 2077 picks up the award for Most Wanted Game, which seems about right.

Anyway, here are all the winners. What do you think? Are there any winning titles that surprise you? Tell us what you think in the comments below.

  • Best Storytelling - Days Gone
  • Best Multiplayer Game - Apex Legends
  • Still Playing Award - Minecraft
  • Best Visual Design - Devil May Cry 5
  • Best Indie Game - Outer Wilds
  • Esports Game of the Year - Fortnite
  • Best Audio - Resident Evil 2
  • Best Game Expansion - GTA Online: Diamond Casino Update
  • Best VR/AR Game - Beat Saber
  • Best Gaming Hardware - Nvidia 20-Series Super Graphics Card
  • Best Performer - Logan Marshall-Green (Telling Lies)
  • Studio of the Year - Epic Games
  • Best New Streamer/Broadcaster - Ewok
  • Breakthrough Award - House House (Untitled Goose Game)
  • Mobile Game of the Year - BTS World
  • Outstanding Contribution - Life is Strange
  • PC Game of the Year - World of Warcraft Classic
  • PlayStation Game of the Year - Days Gone
  • Xbox Game of the Year - Gears 5
  • Nintendo Game of the Year - Super Smash Bros. Ultimate
  • Most Wanted Game - Cyberpunk 2077
  • Critics' Choice Award - Control
  • Lifetime Achievement - Yu Suzuki
  • Ultimate Game of the Year - Resident Evil 2
