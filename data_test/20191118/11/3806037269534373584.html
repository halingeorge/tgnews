Supreme x Phaidon Vol. 2 Hardcover Monograph

Documenting Supreme history from 2010-2018.

18 Nov 2019, 11:30 by Ambrose Leung

With a decade passing since Supreme’s last monograph with Rizzoli, the long-standing New York street/skate brand is back with Vol.2, this time with Phaidon Publishings.

The 351-page hardcover book documents the brand’s visual history from 2010-2018, stemming from products, collaborations, and game-changing events the brand has been a part of. The likes of Nobuyoshi Araki, David Sims, Kate Moss, and Dash Snow will be featured amongst the number of past collabs highlighted. The book will lead with a poem by Harmony Korine and an essay by Carlo McCormick.

While the monograph only covers archival releases from Supreme, there will be an accompanying red box logo slipcase, poster and box logo sticker.

The Supreme x Phaidon Vol. 2  hardcover monograph will be available at Supreme’s New York, Brooklyn, Los Angeles, San Francisco, London, Paris and online store starting November 21. Japan will see a later release on November 23.
