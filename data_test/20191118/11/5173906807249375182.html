Bank card cloning in Algarve ‘worrying authorities’

18 Nov 2019, 11:27

GNR and PSP police logged over 100 cases of bank card cloning in the Algarve during the month of October.

Of these cases most were registered in Loulé and Albufeira, Faro and Portimão.

According to newspaper Correio da Manhã the PJ police has also registered a very high number of complaints; this month, the number of complaints is lower but reportedly continues to worry the authorities.
