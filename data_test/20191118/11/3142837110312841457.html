Aberdeen Total Dynamic Dividend Fund (NYSE:AOD) to Issue $0.06 Monthly Dividend

18 Nov 2019, 11:43 by Amy Steele

Aberdeen Total Dynamic Dividend Fund (NYSE:AOD) declared a monthly dividend on Monday, November 11th, Zacks reports. Shareholders of record on Thursday, November 21st will be paid a dividend of 0.058 per share by the investment management company on Friday, November 29th. This represents a $0.70 annualized dividend and a yield of 8.06%. The ex-dividend date is Wednesday, November 20th.

AOD traded up $0.01 during trading on Monday, hitting $8.64. The company’s stock had a trading volume of 600 shares, compared to its average volume of 295,410. The firm’s 50 day moving average price is $8.38 and its 200-day moving average price is $8.25. Aberdeen Total Dynamic Dividend Fund has a twelve month low of $6.98 and a twelve month high of $8.66.
