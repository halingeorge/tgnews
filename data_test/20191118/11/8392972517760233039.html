Michelle Visage clashes with James Jordan in online feud after he accuses her of ‘fake tears’ on Strictly

She left the competition last night

18 Nov 2019, 11:59 by Rebecca Carter

Michelle Visage has slammed former Strictly Come Dancing pro James Jordan after he appeared to accuse her of "fake tears" on the show.

The RuPaul's Drag Race star became the latest celebrity to be eliminated from the BBC programme this weekend.

However, Michelle has now found herself locked in an online feud with James, who criticised her routine to Madonna's Vogue on Saturday night.

He tweeted last night: "I actually believed that Michelle was a real contender and had some great performances.

"I was really starting to love their partnership. They took a risk this week with the Vogue dance (If you can call it that) It just didn’t pay off. And people at home obviously felt the same."
