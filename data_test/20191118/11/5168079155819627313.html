Rain over some parts of Oman

18 Nov 2019, 11:40 by Times News Service

Muscat: There is high to medium cloud flow towards the coastal parts of South Al Sharqiyah, Al Wusta and Dhofar governorates and chances of isolated rain over these parts.

Oman Meteorology said in a statement, "Currently, aerial photographs show the presence of clear to partly cloudy skies over the northern Governorates with chance of cloud development over Al Hajar Mountains towards the afternoon. Chances of late night to early morning low level clouds or fog formations.

According to the Public Authority for Civil Aviation (PACA), today's weather forecast includes chance of isolated rain over parts of Al Wusta and Dhofar Governorates.

“Winds will be northeasterly light to moderate over Oman Sea coast, while over rest of the Sultanate it will be easterly to southeasterly light to moderate," PACA added

PACA announced on Sunday that weather analysis at the National Multi Hazard Early Warning Centre has indicated that a trough of low pressure located South of Iran will affect the Sultanate.”
