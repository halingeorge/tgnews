Sky broadband down as thousands report connectivity issues across the UK

The problems are affecting subscribers across the UK

18 Nov 2019, 11:20 by Jillian MacMath

UPDATE: Sky has announced that its services for broadband customers have resumed.

Just before 12.30pm they tweeted: "Our broadband service is back up and running, and we’re sorry for the disruption caused to some of our customers this morning."

Thousands of Sky customers are reporting their broadband service is down this morning.

More than 2,000 customers have reported issues with internet connectivity to outage website Downdetector.co.uk since around 6am, with the majority of problems occurring across the southern half of the UK.

A heat map shows the greatest concentration of outages in London.
