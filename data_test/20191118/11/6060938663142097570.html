Kiara Advani flaunts her svelte figure in a pink one-shoulder outfit in her latest photoshoot

18 Nov 2019, 11:41 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kiara Advani is one of the rising stars of Bollywood, who never misses an opportunity to flaunt her divalicious side by flaunting her stylish side. Not only on the celluloid but even in person, Kiara entertains her fans by sharing mesmerising pictures of herself in beautiful outfits.

Taking to Instagram, the 'Kabir Singh' actress shared a series of pictures from her latest photoshoot right before the trailer launch of her next film 'Good Newwz' which also stars Kareena Kapoor Khan, Akshay Kumar and Diljit Dosanjh.

Dressed in a one-shoulder baby pink outfit with puffy sleeve and overlapping layers, Kiara completed her look with light makeup and nude shade lipstick.

Meanwhile, on the professional front, the 27-year old actress was recently seen in the trailer of 'Good Newwz' which has been receiving a good response from the audience. Apart from that, she also has 'Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2', 'Laxmmi Bomb' and 'Shershaah' in her kitty.
