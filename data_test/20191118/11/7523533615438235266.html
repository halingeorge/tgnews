Kanye & Kim are selling their ‘monastic’ Calabasas condo

18 Nov 2019, 11:58 by The Spaces Team

It seems a month barely goes by without Kanye West and Kim Kardashian making a real estate deal – and their latest move is to offload Kim’s brutalist-inspired Calabasas condo by Vincent Van Duysen.

The Belgian architect designed the 2,260 sq ft California property with clay-coloured concrete walls, neutral tones and wooden floorboards throughout. Its three bedrooms are en-suite, and there’s also a covered deck area off the main living space.

Of course, the condo has attracted attention from the media, with magazines like Cosmopolitan dubbing the Los Angeles bolthole the place ‘where dreams go to die’. Ouch.

That statement seems a bit harsh, but perhaps the photographs don’t do the apartment many favours. However, its new owner will also get bespoke furniture, drapes, chandeliers and lighting fixtures all designed by Van Duysen.

Tomer Fridman of Compass Realty has the listing, which has a guide price of $3.5m.
