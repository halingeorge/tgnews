Huawei nova 6 5G with super wide-angle front camera to be unveiled on 5 December 2019

18 Nov 2019, 22:16

Earlier today, Huawei posted a video on their Weibo account hinting that their upcoming nova 6 5G will be unveiled on 5 December 2019, and it comes with 5G connectivity as well as 105-degree wide-angle selfie camera. But that’s not all, it seems that the selfie camera also comes with a zoom function, which could help in getting that perfect selfie.

Feature-wise, the nova 6 5G comes with new functions in order to help the user take good looking selfies, especially in low-light conditions. In terms of the tech specs, it comes equipped with the HiSilicon Kirin 990 chipset, up to 12GB of RAM, 256GB of ROM, as well as a 6.4-inch display with a pill-shaped notch on the top left.

At the back, the device is expected to come with a 60MP + 16MP + 2MP + 2MP rear camera while the front houses a 32MP + 12MP selfie camera. As for the battery, it should come with a 4000mAh battery which supports 40W fast charging.
