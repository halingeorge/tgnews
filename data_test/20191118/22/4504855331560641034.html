Car catches fire in Northwest Miami-Dade

18 Nov 2019, 22:23 by 7 News WSVN

NORTHWEST MIAMI-DADE, FLA. (WSVN) - Crews have responded to the scene of a car fire in Northwest Miami-Dade.

7SkyForce hovered above the charred four-door sedan in the area of Northwest 52nd Ave and 173rd Drive, Monday afternoon.

A Miami-Dade Fire Rescue firefighter was seen spraying the vehicle with a water hose.

Officials have not specified whether anyone was injured or transported to the hospital, as they continue to investigate.
