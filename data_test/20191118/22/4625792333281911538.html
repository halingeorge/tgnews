‘Utterly shameless’: Nikki Haley and George Conway clash over his comments on Elise Stefanik

18 Nov 2019, 22:46 by Madison Dibble

Nikki Haley and George Conway got into a Twitter spat over his comments about Republican New York Rep. Elise Stefanik.

On Saturday, Conway, 56, referred to Stefanik as “lying trash” and shared a fake photograph of the 35-year-old Republican giving the middle finger during the impeachment hearings. Many found his comments offensive, and the comments earned a response from Haley on Monday.

Haley called Conway’s remarks “pathetic,” adding, “This is absolutely uncalled for and disgusting. What is wrong with people? George Conway is the last person that can call someone ‘trash.’”
