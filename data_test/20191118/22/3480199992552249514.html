Jenner and Garraway brave cockroaches in jungle trial

18 Nov 2019, 22:45

The campers also faced crickets, rats and mealworms.

Caitlyn Jenner and Kate Garraway were strapped into cages filled with 30,000 cockroaches in the first gruelling I’m A Celebrity Bushtucker Trial.

The pair were picked for the Bugged Out challenge by the public.

The stomach-churning task saw the campers strapped on their backs in perspex cages 20 metres in the air. 

Each cage contained coloured balls which the stars had to drop into a moving basket below, with each ball representing a meal for camp. While they struggled with the balls, the cages were filled with jungle critters.
