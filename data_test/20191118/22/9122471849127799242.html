Dallas Cowboys Cornerback Is Now Out For The Season

18 Nov 2019, 22:30 by Matt Hladik

Dallas Cowboys cornerback Anthony Brown will miss the rest of the 2019 season due to a triceps injury, Dallas head coach Jason Garrett announced today.

Brown will undergo season-ending surgery to repair a torn triceps, which he suffered in yesterday’s win over the Detroit Lions. Brown was one of several key Cowboys to exit the game in the fourth quarter with injuries.

Defensive lineman DeMarcus Lawrence and safety Xavier Woods both returned after getting hurt, so they’re likely fine moving forward. Offensive lineman La’El Collins injured his left knee and his status is more uncertain.
