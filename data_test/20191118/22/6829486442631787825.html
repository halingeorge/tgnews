Linda Marigliano And Gen Fricker Are Saying Farewell To Triple J

18 Nov 2019, 22:57 by Joseph Earp

It’s a time of transition at triple j. Yesterday, the station farewelled Tom Tilley, the host of Hack for almost a decade. Now, they’re saying goodbye to two more longstanding and beloved presenters: Linda Marigliano and Gen Fricker.

Marigliano will depart as host of triple j’s Mornings by the end of the year, after almost a decade with the station. Well-known for her instrumental role in triple j’s Good Nights, Marigliano has been a fan favourite for many years.

And hey, if you’re bummed, she’s bummed too. “I feel overwhelmed with joy and genuine love for this station that has been my family for so many years,” Marigliano has written in a statement.

“Thank you for having me. It’s been fucking beautiful.”
