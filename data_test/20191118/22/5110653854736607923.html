What channel is Chiefs vs. Chargers on today? Schedule, time for 'Monday Night Football' game

18 Nov 2019, 22:17 by Tadd Haislop

For the first time this season, Chiefs and Chargers fans will have to scan their TV channels for ESPN in order to watch their teams play. Kansas City vs. Los Angeles, a game scheduled to kick off at 8:15 p.m. ETfromEstadio Azteca in Mexico City, is the "Monday Night Football" feature for Week 11.

The Chiefs, technically the road team for Monday night's game in Mexico, are 4 1/2-point favorites over the Chargers.

Estadio Azteca, which is 7,201 feet above sea level, will host a regular-season NFL game for the fourth time (2005, 2016 and 2017). It was supposed to host a Monday night clash between the Chiefs and Rams last season, but concerns over the quality of the field forced the NFL to move the game to Los Angeles.

There should be no such trouble this season, and NFL fans in Mexico City will finally get their Patrick Mahomes show. The Kansas City quarterback, who is makinghis 26th career start this week, has 8,007 career passing yards, the most by a quarterback in his first 25 starts in NFL history. Mahomes, who has 68 career touchdown passes, also just surpassed Pro Football Hall of Famer Dan Marino (65 touchdown passes) for the most touchdown passes by a player in his first 25 career starts in NFL history.

Below is all the info you need to watch Chiefs vs. Chargers in Mexico Cityon Monday night, including the TV channeland kickoff time.

What channel is Chiefs vs. Chargers on today?

  • TV channel (national): ESPN
  • Live stream: ESPN App

ESPN's "Monday Night Football" presentation this season features Joe Tessitore on the play-by-play call and Booger McFarland providing analysis. Lisa Salters is ESPN's sideline reporter for Monday night games, andJohn Perry is the crew'sofficiating analyst for 2019.

For those who can't get to a TV and wish to find Chiefs vs. Chargers on the radio, the Kansas City call can be heard on Sirius channel 81and XM channel 225, and the Los Angeles call can be heard on Sirius channel 83and XM channel 226.

Chiefs vs. Chargers: What time is kickoff?

  • Date: Monday, Nov. 18
  • Start time: 8:15 p.m. ET

The 8:15 p.m. ET kickoff time for Chiefs vs. Chargers marks the fourth of five prime-time games on Kansas City's schedule this season. The Chiefs also will play the Bears on "Sunday Night Football" in Week 16.

As for the Chargers, Monday night's game in Mexico City marks their third of four prime-time appearances this season. Los Angeles also will host Minnesota on "Sunday Night Football" in Week 15.
