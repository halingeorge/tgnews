Couple want their 4-month-old child reunited with them

18 Nov 2019, 22:01 by Staff Reporter

A couple urged the district administration to take necessary steps to reunite their four-month-old newborn with them who they alleged was taken away by the girl’s parents.

They submitted a petition at the district collectorate on Monday.

The couple Raja and R. Meena, residents of Nainampatti village, got married against the wishes of their parents in 2017. The couple worked at a garments firm in Tiruppur and Meena recently delivered a boy in Tiruppur.

Raja said that post-delivery, Meena became unwell and she was taken for treatment at the Tiruppur Government Hospital.

Better treatment

“For better treatment, she was later referred to the Coimbatore government hospital and I informed her parents regarding Meena’s condition.

They decided to take Meena and newborn to Salem for better treatment at a private hospital here.”

Meena said, “I recovered in a few days.

However, my family lied to me that my son and husband died and they convinced to marry another person.

I was taken back to village and I was locked up in my house.

Later, I managed to escape from the place and returned to my husband in Tiruppur.

Child adoption

When enquired, my parents told that they gave our child in adoption to a family in Chennai.

We have complained with police and Collector.”
