I'm A Celeb stars miserably fail at winning luxury items for other camp in trial

The celebs in Main Camp failed to bag any luxury items for Snake Rock during the gruelling Put Your Oar In It challenge

18 Nov 2019, 22:17 by Brogan-Leigh Hurst

I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! Main Camp stars failed to grab any luxury items during a Bushtucker trial.

Kate Garraway, Adele Roberts, Roman Kemp and Myles Stephenson all took part in the challenge but despite their hard teamwork they failed.

Gathering by a dirty swamp to take part in a night time challenge, Roman read a laminate out to everyone which said: "In front of you are 10 luxury items, one for each of the celebrities in both camps.

"Your job is to get each item from the wheel onto the basket however you can only use the oars. You will win each luxury item that reaches the basket."

Kate gave a stunned response: "Oh no, what if we drop someone elses. Oh the pressure!"

 

The celebrities lined up in a row with their oars balanced above the swamp and they had to pass each luxury item box down the row on the oars without dropping it in the swamp.

They didn't do very well and the only luxury items they managed to successfully pass from wheel to basket were Adele and Kate's.

It left nothing for Snake Rock.

 

Fans of the show were left baffled as to why Rak Su's Myles Stephenson chose to take a disposable camera into the Jungle when the whole thing is being filmed.

One said: "Why would you chose a disposable camera as your luxury item when THE WHOLE THING IS BEING FILMED FOR TV".

Another penned: "A disposable camera? Yeah, like it's not on telly or anything like that! #ImACeleb ".

A fourth wrote: "Miles wants to make memories on a disposable camera on a show that’s being recorded and televised for the world to see".

While others were questioning why the stars were getting luxury items already just a day into the new series.
