Coty to buy majority stake in Kylie Jenner's businesses

18 Nov 2019, 22:11

REUTERS: Cosmetics maker Coty Inc said on Monday it would acquire a majority stake in reality TV star Kylie Jenner's make-up and skincare businesses for US$600 million.

Coty will acquire a 51per cent ownership in the company and the deal is expected to close in the third quarter of fiscal year 2020.
