Spoilers: Louise broken as Lisa spirals out of control in EastEnders

18 Nov 2019, 22:01

Lisa Fowler’s (Lucy Benjamin) accusations against Sharon (Letitia Dean) regarding the death of Mel Owen (Tamzin Outhwaite) lead to a dramatic series of fallouts in EastEnders, leaving Louise (Tilly Keeper) fearing that her fragile mum is suffering from another breakdown.

Having previously suffered from psychosis, Lisa was sectioned some time ago after taking Louise captive while she was injured from a fire at the prom. Since then, Lisa has had her demons under control but after Mel was killed following a road accident, Lisa accused Sharon of killing her.

Viewers saw Mel cross the road to her crashed car after hearing Hunter call to her and in the chaos, she was hit by a lorry before Sharon’s eyes. Lisa knows that Mel was about to blow the secret to Phil (Steve McFadden) that Sharon’s baby isn’t his. Believing that Sharon took murderous steps to silence her, Lisa told Phil what she knew and went on to brandish Sharon a murderer.
