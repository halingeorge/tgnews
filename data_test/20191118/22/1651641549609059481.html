Apple announces new App Store focused event

18 Nov 2019, 22:58 by Michael Allison

Apple has announced a new event scheduled for early December. Unlike previous events, this isn’t launching a new streaming service like Apple Music or TV, it appears to be aimed merely at recognizing interesting and notable apps in the App Store. No doubt developers would be delighted to be featured in this, even if just for the exposure alone.

The firm just launched its new AirPods Pro, MacBook Pro, and Mac Pro, expected to be its last device launches of the year.

CNBC confirms this, nothing that insiders confirm that no devices are expected to be launched this year.

We’ll see exactly what Apple’s new event is about on December 2nd.

Source: CNBC
