Petrol forecourt's roof torn down during attempted ATM robbery in Co Down

18 Nov 2019, 22:20 by Ruairi Scott Byrne

A petrol station's forecourt roof was torn down during an attempted ATM robbery in Co Down this morning.

The failed attempt occurred at Carlisles' on the Belfast Road in Ballynahinch in the early hours of Monday morning.

Those involved in the attempted theft were reported to have fled the scene empty-handed, but having caused substantial damage to the premises, and to the roof of the forecourt.
