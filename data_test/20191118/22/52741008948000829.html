Police looking for missing woman

She was last seen Monday morning

18 Nov 2019, 22:05 by KitchenerToday Staff

Waterloo Regional Police are asking for your help finding a 71-year-old woman.

Elaine Wolley was last seen Monday morning in the area of Westmount and Highland Road in Kitchener.

She is 5 '5", 135 pounds, with short grey hair and glasses.

She was wearing a lime green coat, blue jeans and black shoes.

Police say they are concerned for her wellbeing.

Anyone who has information is asked to call police at 519-570-9777.
