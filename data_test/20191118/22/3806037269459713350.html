Samsung Announces Special Edition Star Wars Galaxy Note10+

Inspired by Kylo Ren in ‘Star Wars: Rise of Skywalker.’

18 Nov 2019, 22:29 by Isaac Rouse

Samsung has announced a special edition Star Wars-themed Galaxy Note10+ inspired by Kylo Ren and the upcoming release of Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker in December. To commemorate the final installment in the Skywalker Saga, this Star Wars-inspired handheld will be bundled with an exclusive case, lightsaber-themed S Pen, metal collector’s badge item and a pair of matching Samsung Galaxy Buds.

Accented with Ren’s signature red hue, the black device is as moody as the iconic antihero from the revamped saga. On the rear, a metallic Star Wars logo appears underneath a rendering of the insignia that belongs to Ren’s First Order. Included with the thematic bundle is exclusive digital content, such as wallpapers, shutdown animations, icons, and sounds that fans of the franchise may find familiar, to further underline the Star Wars influence.

Expect the Galaxy Note10+ Star Wars Edition to be available in limited quantities in an Unlocked by Samsung version online and at limited Best Buy, Microsoft, and Samsung Experience Stores for $1,299.99 USD starting December 13 in the United States.
