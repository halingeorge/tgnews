ONE PUNCH MAN: A HERO NOBODY KNOWS to Release in Late February 2020

18 Nov 2019, 22:27 by Tommy Williams

We finally know when One Punch Man: A Hero Nobody Knows is releasing! I wasn’t able to participate in the closed beta, but I know a couple of people who did (you can read one reaction here), and they really enjoyed it. Well, it looks like on February 28, 2020, I will be able to try the game out for myself. In addition to the announcement of a release date, pre-orders are now available as well. If you pre-order, you’ll get early access to Saitama (Dream Version), plus you’ll get a Saitama mask and additional outfits for everyone’s favorite hero, including his jersey and black suit.

Oh, also, in the new trailer below, it was revealed that Metal Knight, Stinger, and Melzargard are joining the fight.

One Punch Man: A Hero Nobody Knows will be available for PC (via Steam), PS4, and Xbox One on February 28, 2020.
