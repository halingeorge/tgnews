Oskar Rozenberg Teases Two Nike SB Collaborations (UPDATE)

Special SB Dunk Highs and SB Blazers on the way.

18 Nov 2019, 22:00 by Sophie Caraan

UPDATE (November 18, 2019)

After first teasing his SB Blazer collaboration earlier in the month, Swedish skateboarder Oskar Rozenberg has shared more photos of the corduroy-equipped design and unveiled a first look at an SB Dunk High as well. Of note on the Dunk High is the nonexistent midfoot Swoosh — it’s been replaced in whole by an embroidered black and white shark graphic with bloody red teeth, bending just enough to provide familiar design lines. The rest of the silhouette appears to feature a soft black nubuck base, red laces and an eggshell white midsole. Via the caption, Rozenberg announced that the Dunk will release on December 14, the same day as his Blazers. Get a first look here and stay tuned for more detailed info as it’s available.

ORIGINAL STORY (November 6, 2019):Swedish skater Oskar Rozenberg has shared images of what seems to be a forthcoming collaboration with Nike SB. Taking to Instagram to tease the special Blazer iteration, the Bryggeriet graduate made sure to highlight its corduroy upper in rose brown, along with the hits of suede around the eyestay, tongues and panel Swooshes in hues of tan. The tongue tag and insoles are embellished with the Nike SB and OSKi logos, tied together with matching brown laces for a uniform finish.

Stay tuned for more imagery and pricing details. The OSKi x Nike SB Blazer is expected to hit select skate shops on December 14.

In other footwear news, Nike and Kyrie Irving have unveiled the Kyrie 6 preheat collection.
