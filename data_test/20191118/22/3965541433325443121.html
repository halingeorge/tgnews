USPS launching site to adopt letters to Santa from low-income kids

18 Nov 2019, 22:06 by Falycia Campbell

Forget Elf on a Shelf....this year be the Elf yourself and help the jolly old Elf himself with Operation Santa.

All over the country kids will send letters to Santa asking for everything from toys to basics, like coats or shoes.

Starting on Monday, letters to Santa from low-income kids are available for adoption online through the United States Postal Service.

You will be able to pick a letter from any city in the country and it's tax-deductible.

Just remember your gifts need to be mailed by December 20.

If you want your child to be added to the list, beanelf.org has everything you need to know.
