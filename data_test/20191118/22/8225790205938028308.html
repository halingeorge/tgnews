Flames destroy structure, equipment at Maui agricultural park

18 Nov 2019, 22:53 by HNN Staff

HONOLULU, Hawaii (HawaiiNewsNow) - Fire investigators are trying to figure out what cause a massive blaze overnight on Maui.

Flames broke out around 8:40 p.m. Sunday at the Kula Agricultural Park storage building.

The 2,500-square-foot structure housed farm materials and equipment.

It took over four hours for firefighters to extinguish the flames.

A damage estimate has not been released.
