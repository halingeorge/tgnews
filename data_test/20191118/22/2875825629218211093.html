I'm A Celebrity fans demand to know where shamed Kiosk Keith is after sacking

Some I'm A Celebrity viewers questioned where Kiosk Keith had gone - but others reminded them he was accused of turning up drunk to work and behaving inappropriately towards a female colleague

18 Nov 2019, 22:58 by Irishmirror.ie

I'm A Celebrity fans were demanding to know where shamed Kiosk Keith was after noticing he was missing.

Some I'm A Celebrity viewers questioned where Kiosk Keith had gone when Jacqueline Jossa and Andrew Maxwell finished their Dingo Dollar challenge.

The celebrities reached the Outback Shack and were greeted by popcorn, crisps and Kiosk Kev.

Kev started doing shifts at the shack last series after Kiosk Keith was sacked back in 2018.

Keith, real name Raymond Grant, was accused of turning up drunk to work and behaving inappropriately towards a female colleague.

Keitg mysteriously disappeared part way through the 2017 series with hosts Ant McPartlin and Declan Donnelly assuring fans he was under the weather and would be back.

But he didn't return though, and in January 2018, show bosses confirmed Keith had been sacked.

ITV said in a statement: “Ray is no longer under contract on the show. There are no plans for him to return this year.”

The Kiosk Keith Twitter account gave a final sign off was to fans at the end of 2018.

It read: "OK just read up that i’m no longer on the show for reasons i won’t say, you can find out yourself ffs.

"This account will no longer be used so enjoy this last tweet of mine."
