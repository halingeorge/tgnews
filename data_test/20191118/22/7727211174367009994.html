I'm A Celebrity Ian Wright admits he cried for days after not making Euro 96 squad

The former footballer was bitterly disappointed to miss out to Robbie Fowler

18 Nov 2019, 22:02 by Sophie McCoid

I'm A Celebrity star Ian Wright admitted he cried for days after missing out on the England team for Euro 96.

In tonight's show James, Ian and Caitlyn chatted about sport in Snake Rock.

James asked Ian if he’d fulfilled everything he wanted to do.

Ian said: "To be honest I just wanted to win the FA Cup and the League and then in the end I won the FA Cup a couple of times, won the Premier League and played for England.”

But Ian added: “I couldn’t believe I got left out of (Euro) 96, I cried for days. He (Glenn Hoddle) took Robbie Fowler and Les Ferdinand who are obviously top players, unbelievable players but I was devastated.”

Talking in the Bush Telegraph after Ian's revelations, Caitlyn said: “Ian, James and myself all some from a sports background so for me, I’m always intrigued by people who have achieved so much in sport and I want to hear their story.”

James told Ian how he got into rugby as a child but that initially he was more interested in football, athletics and tennis.

Talking in the Bush Telegraph about himself, Ian and Caitlyn, James said: “I think everyone, especially the three of us appreciates what it took to get to the levels we did.  We have definitely got a common ground, it’s nice to share that and hear from two people I really admire.”
