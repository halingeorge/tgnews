Helen Flanagan speaks out over 'vile' I'm A Celebrity as 2019 series starts

The Coronation Street star starred in the ITV reality show in 2012

18 Nov 2019, 22:25 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Helen Flanagan has spoken out over her experience in the I'm A Celebrity jungle as the 2019 series starts - and it doesn't make great hearing.

The Coronation Street star starred in the ITV reality show in 2012.

And Helen, who is in a relationship with ex-Aston Villa star Scott Sinclair, watched as the show returned to our screens on Sunday night.

The Rosie Webster actress admitted she says it brought back some terrifying memories for her.

 Speaking to fans on Instragram Helen said: "Just the thought even when Ant and Dec come on and say 'we've got this challenge' just gives me the fear.

"I remember when they used to try to explain things to me about the trials, I'd panic because I would be so scared.

"I wouldn't even process what they were telling me because I would be so worked up.

"I remember I did this trial, it was vile.

 

"I hate crocodiles.

"I had to go under water and I had to go get these stars, but I forgot I had to get them with my mouth.

"But I was so panicky and worked up, I just didn't think."

Watching the first challenge she said: "I'm so happy right now that I'm in my dressing gown at home and that is not me.

"Honestly though, watching that height challenge literally makes me feel sick."
