I'm a Celeb viewers blast decision to send Kate to main camp over Caitlyn 'a joke'

Viewers were furious that Kate Garraway was moved to the more comfortable camp over Caitlyn Jenner, who was in tears that morning

18 Nov 2019, 22:22 by Irishmirror.ie

Viewers fumed at tonight's I'm a Celebrity after they slammed the decision to send Kate Garraway to the main camp.

Kate and Caitlyn Jenner completed a harrowing Bushtucker trial where they were trapped in a tank with cockroaches, crickets and rats.

Afterwards, they were told that the Snake Rock camp, which includes Jacqueline Jossa, Ian Wright, James Haskell and Andrew Maxwell, had chosen one of them to move into the main camp.

In Snake Rock, life is hard and campmates sleep on the floor with a diet of just rice and beans.

Things are much nicer in the main camp, with the added bonus of not being eligible for Bushtucker trials.

Ant and Dec revealed that the campmate lucky enough to be chosen to move into the main camp was Kate Garraway.

Caitlyn had a difficult morning in the camp, crying and already missing her family.

Plus, she performed much better than Kate in the Bushtucker trial.

Because of this, viewers took to Twitter to call the decision to move her to main camp instead of Caitlyn unfair.

"The way caitlyn deserved to go to the main camp. kate just f****d up that whole thing tbh", said one.

"Kate is a f****n wuss I'd be so annoyed if I was Caitlyn, blaming all the failure on her", ranted another.

"Kate is very annoying, fuming she's in the main camp", said a third.

"Kate literally did s**t all in the trial and gets to go to main camp? What a JOKEEE, Caitlyn Jenner deserves to go", exclaimed a fourth.

Another added: "Kate garraway did not deserve to go to main camp whinging woman".

"But gutted for Caitlyn though to be fair, Kate seemed in much better spirits even before going to main camp", pointed out another.

Kate's wails while she attempted the Bushtucker trial also seemed to get on viewers' nerves.

"Can we not have Kate do the next trial??? Her voice is so irritating", requested one.

"Actually tempted to mute the tv during the trial just so I don’t have to listen to Kate whining", said another.
