Spoilers: Ben vows to change in EastEnders and makes a big gesture

18 Nov 2019, 23:59

Ben Mitchell (Max Bowden) has been going to some pretty dark places in EastEnders as of late, but in the coming episodes, he seemingly attempts to change his ways, as he makes a big gesture.

In an attempt to acquire the wrench Stacey (Lacey Turner) used to bludgeon Phil (Steve McFadden), Martin carried out Ben’s dirty work. However, Ben refused to let him go — even after he’d handed over said wrench — and blackmailed Martin once more, threatening to turn him over to the police for car theft.

Martin made several attempts to get out — and he even had Ben arrested — but Ben refused to budge, and even hung from the railway bridge in an attempt to show him who’s boss.

Martin’s life was hell, but when Kathy found out what Ben was up to, she scolded her son for being so cruel, and warned him to change his ways before it was too late.
