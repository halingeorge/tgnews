I'm A Celeb fans baffled as Adele picks photo of Jane McDonald as luxury item

18 Nov 2019, 23:46

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! fans were baffled on Monday night as Adele Roberts chose a framed photograph of Loose Women’s Jane McDonald as her luxury item to bring into the camp.

During an extra task in the show, the team at Base Camp were given the opportunity to win meaningful luxury items for each of their fellow contestants by passing them along a row on paddles above a swamp. If the item fell in, the chance of winning the prize sank along with it.

With most of the contestants opting for practical necessities, like James Haskell’s orthopedic pillow, or sweet mementos from home, like Roman Kemp’s picture of his family, the only items to sadly make it through were Kate Garraway’s concealer and Adele’s prize.

‘Just to let you know what we’re playing for,’ the Radio 1 DJ warned her campmates with faux seriousness, ‘mine is a picture of somebody really special to me.’

Finding out that the chosen picture was of Loose Women star and gay icon Jane McDonald, the campmates (Roman Kemp in particular) looked pretty flabbergasted by the choice.

‘She’s my favourite Loose Woman, she’s going to get me through the jungle, I love her,’ Adele insisted.
