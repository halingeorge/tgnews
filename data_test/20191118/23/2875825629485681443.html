Dec claims Ian Wright is sure to 'crack' on I'm A Celebrity within hours

The former England and Arsenal footballer won't take well to having to go without food as the stars are already concerned about the lack of meals served up at Snake Rock

18 Nov 2019, 23:48 by Irishmirror.ie

He quickly predicted Ian Wright could be this year's most bad tempered I'm A Celebrity camp-mate.

Now Declan Donnelly thinks we could be just hours away from Ian Wright losing his cool.

The 44-year-old said after the opening show that "he might blow, he might just go" as soon as Ian gets hungry.

Now after chatting on Extra Camp, Dec thinks we're already nearing the former footballer's breaking point.

"I feel sorry for him as we're all waiting for it to happen," teased Emily Atack.

Dec replied: "It's the food situation that is going to get him I think.

"So let's see how they do in the trial, I think it could be a really fun trial actually.

"But I think he might just crack today."

The co-hosts then broke into a chorus of 'he's gonna snap, he's gonna snap!'

The with the 2018 contestant yards away, the Geordie joked: "We were all sat here saying the same thing about John Barrowman last year!"

Dec said on Sunday that's the ex-Arsenal player is one to keep an eye out for.

"I think there could be one or two spiky characters," he said.

"If he gets hungry..... He's also got quite a short attention span."
