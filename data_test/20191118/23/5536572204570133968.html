Panthers Owner Says Team Not Interested In Signing Colin Kaepernick

18 Nov 2019, 23:53 by Darrelle Lincoln

Eric Reid might want his good friend to sign with the Carolina Panthers as he stated after Sunday’s lost, but the team owner does not agree.

Following Carolina’s blowout loss to the Atlanta Falcons on Sunday, Reid went on record campaigning for the quarterback to sign with the Panthers.

24 hours later, Panthers owner David Tepper stated the team did not sent a representative to Kaepernick’s workout because they were not interested in any veteran quarterback, and that includes Colin Kaepernick.
