Joel Dommett accidentally calls Caitlyn Jenner a 'guy' on I'm A Celebrity

18 Nov 2019, 23:24

Joel Dommett has been criticised after referring to Caitlyn Jenner as a ‘guy’ during the first episode of I’m A Celebrity: Extra Camp.

The opening night of I’m A Celeb saw Kylie and Kendall Jenner’s parent, who is transgender, pair up with rugby player James Haskell before losing the challenge and having to spend a night at the uncomfortable Snake Rock.

Speaking about the shock loss for the team of sports players – Caitlyn is a former Olympic decathlete – over on the ITV2 companion show, stand up comedian and Extra Camp host Joel put his foot in his mouth while presenting.

‘How do you think that the two athletes, Caitlyn and James, ended up in Snake Rock,’ he asked panellists Alison Hammond and John Barrowman while sitting with co-host Emily Atack.

He continued: ‘I can’t believe that, the two fittest guys, you know?’

While ‘guys’ can be a gender neutral term, many fans took the context which it was spoken in to mean that Joel had accidentally referred to Caitlyn as male.
