Freeway Shooting Suspect

18 Nov 2019, 23:01 by BRIEF

PHOENIX – A man’s false arrest and imprisonment claims against Arizona may continue, a federal judge in the state ruled. The man was jailed for several months as a suspect in the 2015 I-10 Freeway shootings in Phoenix.
