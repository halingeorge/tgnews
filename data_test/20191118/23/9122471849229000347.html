Jake Fromm, Brother Tyler Share Heartfelt Moment After Game

18 Nov 2019, 23:33 by Tzvi Machlin

Georgia quarterback Jake Fromm led his the Bulldogs to victory once again last weekend. Unfortunately, his team’s win came at the expense of Auburn tight end Tyler Fromm, his younger brother.

But the two Fromm brothers showed no animosity at all after the rivalry game.

They met on the field after the game was over and exchanged jerseys before hugging and returning to their lockers.
