Spoilers: Linda is arrested in EastEnders as her drinking gets out of control

18 Nov 2019, 23:59

Linda Carter (Kellie Bright) has been struggling to cope with the pressures as of late in EastEnders, and as a result she’s sought solace in alcohol. Her reliance on drink increases in the coming episodes, and as a result she ends up in trouble with the law, as she’s arrested on suspicion of drink driving.

With another doctor’s appointment on the horizon for Mick (Danny Dyer), Linda agrees to take Ollie to school — in spite of feeling hungover from the night before. However, as she pulls up to the school gates, she accidentally backs her car into another.

Linda’s unsure of how to proceed, but — as no one’s seen her — she decided to remain silent. However, little does she know that someone has seen the whole thing.

Later, Linda exits the school with Chantelle (Jessica Plummer) by her side, but she’s shocked to find Mieko and Shelley — aka the woman Shirley (Linda Henry) punched several months ago — waiting with two police officers.
