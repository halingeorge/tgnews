 Zimbabwean CLOCKS 350km/hr with Audi RS

This unidentified Zimbabwean ‘speed demon’ recorded himself clocking 350km/hr with an Audi RS, while blasting Reverend T Chivaviro’s Tariro Yangu.

One word, exhilarating!

The driver seems to have performed the daring stunt on the popular German autobahn which generally doesn’t have speed limits, somewhere near the city of Stuttgart.

We are just not sure which ‘home’  he was missing at that particular moment, Zimbabwe or the heavens by driving at such adrenaline-pumping speeds.

The Audi RS which suffered his merciless accelerations is from the family of high-performance range versions manufactured by the German automotive company AUDI AG, a member of the Volkswagen Group.
