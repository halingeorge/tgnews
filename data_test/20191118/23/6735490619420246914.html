Andrea Barber ‘Almost Ran Over’ ‘Big Bang Theory’ Star Mayim Bialik

18 Nov 2019, 23:29 by Shakiel Mahjouri

Mayim Bialik almost did not make it to the set of “Big Bang Theory”.

Andrea Barber appeared on “People Now” and did a little Q&A. Reading questions from a hat, Barber revealed “Big Bang Theory” is her favourite sit-com aside from “Full House” and “Fuller House”.

It was after she praised “Big Bang Theory” that Barber revealed she almost hit Bialik with her car.

“They were our neighbours too, just across stage 25,” Barber shared. “We had different taping schedules. I almost ran over Mayim Bialik one time and I’m dying to meet her and apologize.”

“I was backing up out of my parking space and she was behind me,” she continued. “I was like, ‘oh my God, I almost ran over Mayim Bialik.’ Mortified.”

Barber, 43, also admitted she has told her “Fuller House” co-star Jodie Sweetin that she has a crush on her.
