Kick Off Your Shoes and Watch This A Capella Group Sing Lizzo Like Nobody's Business

18 Nov 2019, 23:03 by Karenna Meredith

My hair is tossed and my nails are sufficiently checked, all thanks to Citizen Queen. The a capella group — featuring Cora Isabel, Nina Nelson, Hannah Mrozak, Kaylah Sharve, and Kaedi Dalley — recently shared their cover of "Good as Hell" by Lizzo with an accompanying nostalgic sleepover music video. The matching silk PJs alone would set my middle school self into an excited spiral. You might recognize Citizen Queen from their viral "Evolution of Girl Groups" video, and their stunning vocals and on-point beat boxing would make the "Truth Hurts" queen proud. Watch the group's badass rendition of the hit song above.
