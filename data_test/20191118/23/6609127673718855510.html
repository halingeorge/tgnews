SQUADDIE HURT

Grandad ex-soldier ‘stabbed in the face’ in ‘attack’ at Irvine home which saw another man plunge from window

18 Nov 2019, 23:14 by Douglas Walker

A FORMER squaddie was allegedly stabbed in the face in a suspected attack which saw another man plunge from a window.

Grandad Lenny Law, 63, also suffered hand and back wounds.

He is in a stable condition in hospital.

The 63-year-old was injured on Sunday morning at the home he shares with wife Mary in Irvine, Ayrshire.

Dale Ferguson, 26, was arrested.

He is said to be in a critical condition.

One neighbour said: “There were police and ambulances everywhere.”

Lenny served with the 4th Royal Tank Regiment.

It is believed he has three daughters.

Cops said officers were called following reports of an intruder.

A spokesperson said: “Enquiries are continuing.”
