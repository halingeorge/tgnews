Joe Dante’s ‘Gremlins’ Returning to Theaters for One Week Run in 4DX This Holiday Season

18 Nov 2019, 23:15 by John Squires

While we wait for the animated prequel series “Gremlins: Secrets of the Mogwai,” we’ve learned tonight that Joe Dante‘s original classic is coming back to theaters for Christmas!

Regal has announced that they are bringing the original Gremlins back to the theaters this holiday season in 4DX for a one week run from December 5th – December 11th.

4DX at Regal is the ultimate experience involving motion chairs and special effects like wind and rain.

Tickets are on sale now, so be sure to check local theater listings.

You can also pick up Gremlins on 4K Ultra HD, released last month!
