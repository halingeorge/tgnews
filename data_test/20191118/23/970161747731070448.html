Chelsea star Willian makes transfer decision as Barcelona plot fresh move

18 Nov 2019, 23:25

Willian wants to renew his contract at Stamford Bridge despite Barcelona plotting a fresh move for the Chelsea star.

Brazilian winger Willian has made more than 300 appearances for Premier League giants Chelsea since signing from Anzhi Makhachkala in 2013.

Helping the Blues win two Premier League titles, the Europa League, FA Cup and EFL Cup, Willian has enjoyed substantial success in west London.

With his current contract set to expire next summer, the 31-year-old will be free to talk to other clubs in January and Barcelona are monitoring his situation.

But Goal say the experienced winger is keen to extend his stay at Chelsea, particularly as he is enjoying a revival under club legend Frank Lampard.

Willian has featured in all but one of Chelsea’s Premier League matches so far this season and Lampard admits he wants the Brazilian to put pen to paper on a contract extension.

‘The word isn’t ‘no-brainer’ because I can’t get involved in the money, that’s up to the club to do,’ Lampard said earlier in the month.

‘But everyone sees how much I’m relying on him and what he’s producing. I love him at this football club, so there’s my answer.’

Willian, meanwhile, recently revealed that he had the chance to sign for Barcelona last year.

He told DAZN: ‘The only real offer I had to leave Chelsea came from Barcelona last year, after the World Cup. Barca presented an offer to Chelsea but it was rejected.

‘That was the only time I ever had to discuss something like that with my family, but we really love it here [in London]. So there were several factors that helped me stay.’

Chelsea, who are currently third in the Premier League, travel to defending champions Manchester City on Saturday.
