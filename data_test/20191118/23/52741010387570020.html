Rangers to be featured in game on Sportsnet

That will happen in early March

18 Nov 2019, 23:15 by KitchenerToday Staff

NEWS RELEASE
ONTARIO HOCKEY LEAGUE
*************************
The stars of the Canadian Hockey League are ready to shine in Sportsnet’s broadcast spotlight with exclusive national coverage of the 2019-20 CHL season.
 
CHL fans across the country can enjoy 19 regular season games on Sportsnet beginning Sunday November 17 through Saturday March 21.  The broadcast schedule also includes the 2020 Kubota CHL/NHL Top Prospects Game on January 16, multi-game playoff coverage, and the entire 2020 Memorial Cup Presented by Kia set for May 21-31 in Kelowna.
     
2019-20 CHL Broadcast Schedule on Sportsnet
