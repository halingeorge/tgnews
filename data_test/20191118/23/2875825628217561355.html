Martin King says he'd like to co-host TV show with photographer wife Jenny McCarthy

“There isn’t a husband and wife team on the TV in Ireland and I think the dynamic would work"

18 Nov 2019, 23:18 by Ciara Phelan

Martin King has said he would like to co-host a TV show with his wife Jenny McCarthy.

Jenny, a celeb photographer, added there’s a gap in the market for an on-screen husband and wife duo.

Martin, who presents the Six O’Clock Show with Muireann O’Connell, told the RTE Guide: “I wouldn’t mind doing a little bit of TV work with this woman.

“I think it would work great, Jenny wouldn’t let me away with anything.”

Jenny, who also appears on Ireland AM, said she loves the adrenlaine rush from appearing on telly.

She added: “There isn’t a husband and wife team on the TV in Ireland and I think the dynamic would work. I just don’t know if I’d have the time.”
