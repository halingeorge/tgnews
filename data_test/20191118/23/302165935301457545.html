Amazon offers ad-supported free music streaming service

18 Nov 2019, 23:33

NEW YORK, Nov 19 — Amazon.com Inc said yesterday it has introduced an ad-supported free version of its music streaming service Amazon Music, taking on rival Spotify Technology which has nearly 140 million users for its free version.

Amazon offers paid plans for its music service starting at US$9.99 (RM41.50) per month and it is also part of its prime bundle. The company launched a free version of its music service for Alexa users on Echo devices in September.

Spotify, the world’s most popular music streaming service, also offers a “freemium” model that includes a free ad-supported version along with a paid ad-free version at US$9.99 per month.

However, Apple Inc’s Apple Music offers only paid subscription service for US$9.99 per month and accounts for nearly a fifth of the paid music streaming market, according to Counterpoint Research.

Amazon shares were up nearly 0.5 per cent, while those of rival Spotify fell about 5 per cent. — Reuters
