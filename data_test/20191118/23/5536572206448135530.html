Lakers’ Danny Green Gets Drug Tested One Day After Monster Dunk Against Atlanta Hawks (VIDEO + TWEET)

18 Nov 2019, 23:38 by Darrelle Lincoln

Danny Green did something on Sunday night that many NBA fans just aren’t used to as he skied high for a put-back dunk in Sunday’s win over the Atlanta Hawks.

That dunk was so crazy and athletic that the league thought something might be going on, so they sent a random test his way.

The Los Angeles Lakers veteran took to Twitter to announce he had been drug tested.
