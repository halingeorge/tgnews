Antonio Brown Hints Eagles Should Sign Him After Terrible Offensive Performance vs. Patriots (PICS)

18 Nov 2019, 23:15 by Darrelle Lincoln

The Philadelphia Eagles had 10 points by the start of the second quarter following a successful review of Dallas Goedert’s catch in the end zone. The team wouldn’t score again for the remainder of the game and lost 17-10.

The team desperately lacks the wide receivers to help with the passing attack, as DeSean Jackson has been out for much of the year due to injury. Alshon Jeffery is battling an ankle injury, while Nelson Agholor continues to drop crucial catches, much like one that could’ve tied the game late in the 4th quarter.

That got Eagles fans to take to social media and implore Antonio Brown to come and save the team.

Brown seemed to agree as he reposted several messages.
