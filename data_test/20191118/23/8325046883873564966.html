Why we selected these particular deals:

1. Up to 30% off Patagonia clothing, jackets, and more

Patagonia stuff is, by and large, not cheap. 30% off puts the brand’s top-notch outerwear that much more within reach, making it an exceptionally good time to buy it.

2. Up to 30% off Smartwool base layers, hats, gloves, and more

Smartwool is great in all seasons, but it really shines in winter, during ski season, which is often when we’re stuck paying full price. This is a great time to stock up on the brand’s merino wool products before heading out onto the slopes this year.

3. Buy one get one free on Nalgene bottles $10.95 (originally $10.95 each) [You save $10.95]

We could all stand to have an extra reusable water bottle lying around, and, probably two for that matter. Being unbreakable, Nalgene is a favorite, and a twofer always comes in handy if you’re buying stocking stuffers or party favors.

4. Up to 33% off select Garmin GPS watches (and handhelds)

Garmin is a leader in the GPS realm and makes some of the most user-friendly handheld devices around. The brand also offers some great GPS watches for hikers and runners, and you can save well over $100 on select models.

5. Save 20% on Smith ski goggles and helmets

In 1965, Dr. Bob Smith brought to life the first prototype of the modern-day ski goggle. The brand as remained at the forefront of the technology ever since. As with any, Smith’s goggles can run upwards of $200, so saving 20% is substantial.

6. REI Co-op Base Camp 4 Tent, $271.99 (originally $389) [You save $117.01]

REI Co-op’s Base Camp tents aren’t the lightest, but they’re built well and much more affordable than others. Shave off an extra $100+? That’s a steal.

7. Save 25% on all Swiss Army Knives

There’s always room for a Swiss Army Knife, be it on a keychain, in a toolbox, or a pocketbook. Another item we don’t see on sale all that much, this is a deal worth jumping on for most anyone on your list (over a certain age, at least).

8. REI Co-op Wallace Lake Down Shirt Jac – Men’s, $63.99 (originally $129) [you save $65.01]

Shirt Jacs, Shackets, or Work Shirts, are all the rage of late, but for good reason. And while most of them are brushed cotton or flannel, this one is made with a light (but formidable) filling of down, and at its sale price is far less than most quality Shirt Jacs on the market.

9. Up to 65% off REI “Peak Deals” items

REI Peak Deals is a roundup of the retailer’s best deals at any given moment and all of which are exclusively at least 50% off. REI-Co-op is healthily covered here, but you’ll see tents from Kelty, ski goggles from Spy, and eventually (we predict) more.

United By Blue makes our favorite winter socks using Bison down, which is also used in its puffer vests and jackets. The brand also pulls one pound of plastic trash out of our waterways for every product it sells.
