EastEnders fans disgusted by Ben Mitchell's sick remark about abuse victims

Furious Kathy Beale slapped her son across the face after discovering he was blackmailing Martin Fowler and had threatened to harm the Slaters on Monday night's EastEnders

18 Nov 2019, 23:45 by Irishmirror.ie

EastEnders fans were disgusted tonight by Ben Mitchell's sickening remark about abuse victims.

Nasty Ben was finally exposed during a family meal when Kathy Beale discovered her son has been blackmailing Martin Fowler into doing risky jobs.

Kathy was horrified when she overheard Ben threatening to harm Jean Slater, Big Mo and little Arthur if he didn't get his way.

But when she confronted a drunken Ben in the kitchen, he turned the tables by slamming his mum for faking her death and "abandoning" her family.

Vile Ben said: "Everything that happened to you. You asked for it. Martin too. The thing with you weak people, you just love being abused."

Seething Kathy slapped her son across the face in a furious rage for his sickening comment.

With tears in her eyes, Kathy added: "I hope to god you never live to be ashamed of your own child."

Crying Kathy stormed out of the room - while guilt-ridden Ben was left to reflect on his actions.

Taking to Twitter , one furious viewer said: "I rarely say this but Ben deserved that & I'm really annoyed at him after saying weak people love being abused you freaking serious right now."

"I love Ben but he was wrong for saying that's however it's not good to be talking about domestic violence then to end on a cliffhanger with violence," added another.

A third said: "OMG Ben , you never learn , It seems you want to be abused , your wounds are too deep"

This came after Ben and Martin clashed during Ian's special meal in memory of the late Mel Owen.

The pair started winding each other up after Martin discovered that Callum wants to join the police force.

Ben's fears grew and he told Callum they may have to split if he is serious about joined the police- leading Callum to storm off.

Later in the week, things will get much worse for Ben when his family and criminal lives collide.

Hungover Ben has no time for his mum when she warns him to change his ways or risk losing everything he loves.

Kathy pleads for Lola’s help with Ben, leading her to plan a weekend away with Lexi. which he reluctantly agrees to.

However, Ben’s good mood is short-lived when Callum comes with a shocking proposition for him.
