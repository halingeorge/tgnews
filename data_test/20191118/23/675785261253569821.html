Inside Wes Nelson and Arabella Chi's love nest as they move in after just three months

The Love Island stars have taken a huge step in their relationship after just three months of dating

18 Nov 2019, 23:33 by Vicki Newman, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/vicki-newman/

Wes Nelson and Arabella Chi have taken their relationship to a new level, moving in together after just three months of dating.

The couple took to Instagram to show off their new love nest as they unpacked their household appliances and unpacked their boxes.

Wes, 21, who took part in Love Island in 2018, and 29-year-old Arabella, who starred in the 2019 series, are officially cohabitating just three months into their romance.

Their new flat is lightly-furnished so far but it's early days.

They've just started moving in their boxes, filling up their wardrobes and stocking up their kitchen.

Wes cracked open a beer as he booted up his laptop and stuck some music on.

The lovers' flat features sleek wooden floors and marble kitchen countertops, as well as built-in wadrobes for a super tidy design layout.

As they gave fans a tour of their new place, Wes pointed out his favourite item - a spice rack.

He joked it would be Tommy Fury and Molly-Mae's worse nightmare after people recently poked fun at them over their plain-looking meal.

Wes laughed: "Here is the kitchen set-up at the minute. Some nice little bits that we got today and this in particular, I imagine is Tommy and Molly's worst nightmare."

So far the kitchen counters are stocked with bottles of champagne and gin, a water filter, a kettle and toaster, and some other bits and bobs.

The hallway is almost empty for the minute, with just a small grey table with a lamp sat on top.

Other rooms are piled high with boxes waiting to be unpacked by the lovebirds.

During a shopping trip, Wes said he'd be documenting the move-in for fans, and encouraged people to offer suggestions about their furnishings.

He said: "We are gonna blog the whole move in and we want you to help designing the apartment."

The move comes after the couple opened up about their age gap, saying it doesn't mean a thing to them.

Wes told OK! magazine : "People tell me I act way older than my age. I'm very mature, and Arabella is the immature one."

Arabella added: "People always think I'm 21 because I don't look, or act my age. Wes has had a lot of life experience because he's been on Love Island and has done a lot, so he has been fast tracked."
