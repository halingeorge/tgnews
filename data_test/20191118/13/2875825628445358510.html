Michelle Keegan looks very different as she poses in leather zip-up corset

The 32-year-old actress was almost unrecognisable from her Coronation Street days as she posed for a glamorous photoshoot

18 Nov 2019, 13:26 by Irishmirror.ie

Michelle Keegan's fans have commented on her looking strikingly different in her latest Instagram photo.

The actress, who shot to fame in Coronation Street , posed for a pouty selfie on Monday wearing thick layers of make-up and and a very sultry expression.

The glamorous look was completed by Michelle, 32, slipping into a cleavage enhancing leather corset that featured a zip-up front and waist cinching belt.

She wore her brunette hair in thick tumbling waves and added a pair of diamante drop earrings to complete her makeover.

Natural beauty Michelle uploaded the photo to her social media using a checklist as the caption: "Make Up & hair did, Beauty light, Dog upstaging me."

Her husband Mark Wright was clearly a fan as he responded with a single flame emoji to show his appreciation.

"How can anyone be this fit?!" another of Michelle's fans gushed in the comments.

Although some of her followers questioned if the Our Girl star was looking a little different.

"Blimey this looks nothing like you!!" exclaimed one.

"What happened?" another wanted to know.

Others compared Michelle's glamorous look to that of X Factor judge Nicole Scherzinger, who often favours a red lip, big hair and false eyelashes.

After growing up in the public eye, Michelle has always strongly denied having any work done to her face.

Though in May this year it was revealed that she had a little help in the dental department to achieve her good looks.

Michelle and hubby Mark were both reported to be fans of ‘invisalign’ treatment, which straightens teeth using an almost invisible brace and costs up to £5000.

The former Corrie star has been rumoured to have undergone Rhinoplasty in the past, which she has always laughed off.

Michelle previously said: "I like the fact people point out they think I’ve had a nose job.

"They think I’ve had new cheeks, a new chin – but I haven’t and I don’t think I ever would, if I’m being honest.

"I’m scared it’ll go wrong!"
