Christmas burger, fries and drink launched at KFC

The menu goes live today

18 Nov 2019, 13:29 by Neil Shaw

KFC has launched its Christmas menu for 2019 and the burgers, meals and drinks go on sale from today - Monday, November 18.

There are three new items added to the menu just for the festive season - the KFC Festive Burger, Festive Fries and a new Mint Choc Krushem Soft Serve.

The KFC Festive Burger is a classic Original Recipe fillet with a hash brown, sage and onion stuffing mayonnaise, a cranberry dressing and a layer of cheese.

Costing £4.59 by itself, you can also get it as a meal for £5.59, or get a Festive Burger Box Meal for £6.89, reports The Mirror .

You can also save on the new festive treats and order through Just Eat here- where you can get a free £15 order- bargain!

The box meals includes a burger, drink with beans and a chicken drumstick.

The new KFC Festive Fries are sprinkled with a smoky seasoning of pepper, toasted onions, garlic and herbs.

The Mint Choc Krushem Soft Serve (£2.79) is soft vanilla ice cream with chocolate and mint sauce, sprinkled with Cookie Crunch, and finished with a glittery chocolate bow tie.

Everything will be available from now until January 1.

Jo Tivers, Top Festive Elf at KFC UK & Ireland said: "A round of santa-plause for this epic festive feast please. We’ll let turkey have its moment on the 25th but until then, we’ve got your back."
