Jennifer Cardini reveals seventh Correspondant compilation

Out December 6th, the 17-track LP features several collaborative works, like one from Krystal Klear and Kodi Najm.

18 Nov 2019, 13:19 by Marissa Cetin

Jennifer Cardini's label Correspondant will release its seventh compilation next month.

The French producer's Berlin-based outlet collects 17 tracks from affiliates old and new for Compilation 07, out on December 6th. It features a handful of collaborations, such as Krystal Klear & Kodi Najm, Terr & Aera, Concret & Cantor, and Mijo, Rodion & Eddie Mercury.

Listen to "Scorcho!" by Bawrut.
