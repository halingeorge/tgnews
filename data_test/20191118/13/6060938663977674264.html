Photos of the day: From ‘Good Newwz’ trailer launch to Manish Malhotra’s father’s funeral to trailer preview of Ajay Devgn’s ‘Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior’, pictures that grabbed headlines

18 Nov 2019, 13:39 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ajay Devgn’s ‘Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior’, pictures that grabbed headlines It was a busy day in B-town today with the much-awaited trailer launch of Akshay Kumar-Kareena Kapoor Khan starrer, ‘Good Newwz’ to the trailer preview of Ajay Devgn’s period drama, ‘Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior’. B-town celebs were also in full attendance at designer Manish Malhotra’s father’s funeral today.

Here are pictures that grabbed headlines today:

Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Diljit Dosanjh and Kiara Advani at ‘Good Newwz’ trailer launch

The much-awaited trailer of ‘Good Newwz’ was unveiled today. Dressed in their best, Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Diljit Dosanjh and Kiara Advani were seen posing for the shutterbugs. Filmmaker Karan Johar also joined the leading cast on stage at the launch where he was seen interacting with the media.

Bollywood celebs attend Manish Malhotra’s father’s funeral
Bollywood designer Manish Malhotra’s father’s funeral was held on Monday morning. The fashion designer's close friends from the film industry dropped by the Malhotra's Mumbai residence to pay their last respects. Malaika Arora, Shilpa Shetty, Karan Johar, Shabana Azmi, Amrita Arora, Farah Khan, Urmila Matondkar and others were seen attended the last rites.

Trailer preview of ‘Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior’ for B-town
Ahead of the trailer release of Ajay Devgn starrer ‘Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior’, the makers had arranged a special preview of the trailer for their Bollywood counterparts. Seen attending the same were Rohit Shetty, Imtiaz Ali, Sanjay Leela Bhansali, Bhushan Kumar, Aanand L Rai and others.
