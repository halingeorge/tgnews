Emmerdale's Lucy Pargeter hints Chas and Paddy will split up

Chas and Paddy recently had a daughter together

18 Nov 2019, 13:10 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Actress Lucy Pargeter, who plays Chas Dingle in Emmerdale, has hinted her alter ego will split from boyfriend Paddy Kirk in the future.

After a terrible time last year losing their baby daughter Grace, the couple were finally brought some happiness as their daughter Eve was born last month.

However, despite their joy at having a newborn, it looks like there could be trouble ahead for the pair.

Speaking to Inside Soap, Lucy hinted Chas and Paddy could be heading for a break-up.

She said: "Once it's seen that an audience likes a couple together, then there's nothing better than to see them possibly split, then rejoin.

"So who knows what the powers that be have planned!"

She added: "At the moment they seem to be doing okay. I think they'll be strong and open."

Lucy then went onto discuss how the arrival of little Eve could bring some turbulence into their relationship.

She said: "A new baby in any relationship is bound to be a test, but I think Chas and Paddy will support each other through it. Hopefully it will go smoothly for them."

Since the birth and death of baby Grace, the couple have managed to form a strong partnership.
