Michy Batshuayi willing to fight for Chelsea place

Michy Batshuayi has suggested that he has no intention of leaving Chelsea in a bid to secure regular first-team football.

18 Nov 2019, 13:26 by Darren Plant

Having been kept at Stamford Bridge by recently-appointed boss Frank Lampard, Batshuayi has contributed five goals from two starts and nine substitute outings in all competitions.

However, despite needing to maintain his sharpness ahead of Euro 2020, the Belgium international has indicated that he is settled at Stamford Bridge.

The 26-year-old is quoted by The Metro as saying: "I have never been a full-fledged starter, but Chelsea is my club, that's where I feel at home. It's a club I love deeply."

Batshuayi will hope to feature when Chelsea take on Manchester City at the Etihad Stadium on Saturday evening.
