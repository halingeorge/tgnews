Hands-on video of Samsung’s upcoming foldable phone leaked online

18 Nov 2019, 13:05 by Rahul

A few days back, we reported that Samsung is working on a cheaper Galaxy Fold smartphone, a hands-on video of which has recently leaked online(via Slashleaks). Surprisingly, according to the leaked video, the upcoming foldable phone from Samsung will be called Samsung W20 and not Galaxy Fold 2 despite the fact that design-wise, both the devices look exactly the same.
