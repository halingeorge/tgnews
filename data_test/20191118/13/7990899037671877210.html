George Conway Erupts After Nikki Haley Defends Rep. Stefanik and Criticizes Him: You Said Trump Was a ‘Truthful’ Man

18 Nov 2019, 13:46 by Matt Naham

Former U.S. Ambassador to the United Nations Nikki Haley, who recently defended President Donald Trump as a “truthful” person, criticized conservative attorney George Conway on Monday for calling Rep. Elise Stefanik (R-N.Y.) “lying trash.”
