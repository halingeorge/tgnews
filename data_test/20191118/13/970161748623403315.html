Spoilers: Trouble for Ben and Callum in EastEnders as they clash over police job

18 Nov 2019, 13:02

Ben Mitchell (Max Bowden) and Callum ‘Halfway’ Highway’s (Tony Clay) road to romance in EastEnders has been anything but smooth but, nonetheless, the pair persevered, and thus they’ve formed a special relationship as a result. However, further problems are set to ensue for the duo’s relationship in tonight’s episode — and Callum’s desire to join the police is seemingly the cause.

Almost two weeks ago, Ben and Callum made a commitment to one another, confirming that they are now boyfriends. However, not long afterwards, Callum dropped something of a bombshell when he revealed to his lover that he wants to join the police force.

It’s safe to say that Ben was devastated, as he pondered what to do for the best. Mind you, considering his penchant for dodgy deals, it’s no wonder he’s conflicted!

The young man’s problems are set to pursue tonight, as Martin (James Bye) discovers Callum’s desire to become a cop, and he wastes little time in winding him up about it.
