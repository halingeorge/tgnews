Joey Essex nearly ate leaves during I’m A Celebrity amid 2.5st weight loss

18 Nov 2019, 13:41

Joey Essex has revealed just how hard it was existing just on one small meal of rice and beans a day on I’m A Celebrity.

As the 2019 cohort got to grips with the Australian jungle after landing in the bush last night, Joey of the jungle opened up on his own time.

The Towie star placed fourth in the 2013 series of the ITV show and admits the hardest part was the lack of food.

In fact, he came close to eating the wild vegetation in order to cure the hunger pangs, as he saw his weight plummet during his time.

‘I’m a big eater, I remember finding myself eating orange skins,’ Joey said on Loose Women on Monday.

‘I was about to start eating leaves. I lost about 2.5st over three weeks.’

While he became super lean over the weeks, he added their meal of rice and beans once a day was the norm as campmates failed to win enough challenges to enjoy the cooked meals the Dingo Dollars provide.

Speaking about this year’s gang of contestants, the panel joked James Haskell would be eating five chickens a day, as Joey said: ‘He’s going to struggle, he looks like a big eater.’

Last night the contestants were tasked with jumping out of a helicopter and scaling a skyscraper to talk along a plank.

Not for the faint of heart.

For the reality star, though, Joey admitted that first challenge goes by in a whirlwind.

‘Everything is so unexpected, from the minute you leave, you look back and you’re on the boat, the next moment you’re jumping out of the helicopter,’ he reminisced.

‘It’s all a bit of a whirlwind, you don’t know what’s going on.’

And he’s pretty proud of Caitlyn Jenner’s guts, who, to Joey, ‘don’t look scared at all jumping out of the helicopter’.

You can brave Keeping Up With The Kardashians, you can jump out of a chopper.

‘Jumping out of the helicopter was something else, I would never do it again…unless I was pushed,’ Joey continued to muse.

‘You’re on TV, million people watching, can’t mug myself off can I?’

Loose Women returns 12.30pm on ITV and I’m A Celebrity continues tonight 9pm on ITV.
