20 pictures capture some of the worst parking found across the UK

It's shocking that drivers thought it was acceptable to leave their car in these places

18 Nov 2019, 13:55 by Carly Odell

Parking is always a controversial issue everywhere you go.

Whether it's fighting for a space at a supermarket or shocking pavement mounting outside your house, bad parking can cause tension.

Particularly outside schools which seems to bring the worst parking out of parents.

Social media can be awash with some of the worst parking possible from parking on pavements to covering two parking space with one car.

If you're one of those people who gets infuriated when you see bad parking online or in public, you might want to avert your eyes.

From parking in four bays to parking in a motorcycle space, here are 20 pictures showing some of the worst parking in the country.
