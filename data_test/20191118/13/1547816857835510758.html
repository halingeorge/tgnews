Day After Internet Sensation Ranu Mondal Is Trolled For OTT Make-Up, A Video Of Her Cat Walking On 'Jalwa' Emerges

18 Nov 2019, 13:12 by Gretel Sequeira

Internet singing sensation Ranu Mondal was recently criticized for her heavy make-up at a fashion show. Now, in a video shared on social media, Mondal had turned model for a fashion show and she walked the runway with a lady on the famous song 'Jalwa'

Internet singing sensation Ranu Mondal rose to fame with a viral video of her singing to a famous Lata Mangeshkar song. While Mondal received a lot of acclaim from the netizens for her melodious voice, Ranu earned a lot of fame and offers from B-Town overnight. As the singing sensation is currently enjoying her spotlight after a few song releases thanks to Himesh Reshammiya, in a video shared on social media, the Mondal has turned model for a fashion show and is snapped walking the runway with a lady on the famous Priyanka Chopra, Kangana Ranaut song 'Jalwa'

Ranu Mondal recently received a lot of flak from the netizens for her overdone make-up. In pictures shared on social media of Ranu from an event, the singing sensation was snapped dressed in a peach-coloured lehenga, while her face was covered with heavy make-up. In a video shared on Instagram from the event, Mondal is seen gracing the ramp with a lady as she moves her ghagra while walking like a model. The just-turned singer has now turned into a model and was spotted walking the ramp with another lady, supposedly the designer.
