Yaad Piya Ki Aane Lagi: Divya Khosla Kumar Rocks Sabyasachi Mukherjee’s Lehenga Choli From His Latest Collection Isfahan

18 Nov 2019, 13:19 by Amruta Patel

Divya Khosla Kumar just rocked a super sexy neck plunging lehenga choli by ace designer Sabyasachi Mukherjee. The actress wore this bridal wear from the designer’s latest collection, Isfahan, for her music video Yaad Piya Ki Aane Lagi

Just as we were just getting over Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh’s first wedding anniversary pictures, where the two got all decked up and dressed in ace designer Sabyasachi Mukherjee’s creation, comes in Divya Khosla Kumar, sporting a lehenga choli designed by this ace designer for her latest music video. Better half of music label, T-series’ head honcho Bhushan Kumar, after trying her hands at direction and production, she has finally gotten back to her first love, i.e. acting with the music video, titled Yaad Piya Ki Aane Lagi.


Mrs Bhushan Kumar who appeared in a music video is seen flaunting Sabyasachi Mukherjee’s black neck plunging lehenga choli, totally slaying it and looking all captivating in the video. On the poster of the music video, Divya has completed her look with heavy neckpiece, bangles and maang theeka.  Inspired from the Mughal era this edition, Isfahan, plays with a lot of hues and motifs. The collection focuses on the fabric, velvet and thrives on the colour palate of pomegranate, patina and Jamun. The collection has been introduced by the designer as his 2019 winter collection targeting the soon-to-be brides. ALSO READ: Bollywood Divas Bedazzle At The Red Carpet Of Femina Women Awards 2017

Though the original outfit has Sabyasachi’s panther logo on the waist, the one chosen by Divya for the music video does not feature it. Divya has flaunted different looks in this music video, which was released online on November 17.
