The Good, Bad And Ugly Of Last Week: Rani Mukerji, Athiya Shetty, Manushi Chhillar, Tabu, Shweta Tiwari

18 Nov 2019, 13:43 by Prajakta Ajgaonkar

Nothing really UGLY about last week. Lots of good things happened but some bad news also came along. That's a part and parcel of life, isn't it? Without further delay, let's begin scrutinising the GOOD, BAD and UGLY incidences that hit Bollywood and Telly land in the week gone by

Athiya Shetty (BAD): Suniel Shetty’s daughter Athiya’s second outing Motichoor Chaknachoor failed to click at the box-office. Even the critics panned the film. It seems like Athiya will have to wait longer to earn a HIT film in her filmography.

 

Jasmin Bhasin (GOOD): After a long wait, Ekta Kapoor finally made the announcement around the female lead in Naagin 4. It is Nia Sharma. Now, she officially announced the second Naagin and it’s going to be Jasmin Bhasin. Indeed, the actress has bagged a plum deal!

Ayushmann Khurrana (GOOD): Ayushmann Khurrana is on a hit-making spree. His latest release Bala grossed over Rs 100 crore worldwide. The actor has even reason to celebrate its victory.

 

Shweta Tiwari (GOOD): Shweta Tiwari’s comeback show Mere Dad Ki Dulhan was embroiled in a legal mess but the producers Tony and Deeya Singh are smiling away to glory because they have won the plagiarism case against a Punjabi producer.

 

Dimple Kapadia (BAD): Reports of senior actress Dimple Kapadia being unwell surfaced on social media. However, the actress cleared the air and stated that it’s not her but her mother Betty Kapadia who is hospitalized. So, while it’s a relief to discover that the actress is doing fine, it’s bad news to know that her mom is unwell. We wish her a speedy recovery.

 

Lata Mangeshkar (BAD): Lata Mangeshkar was admitted to the Breach Candy Hospital on Nov 11 due to breathing difficulty. Since then, there have several sorts of reports around her health, some even scaring us. While, we had reported that the legendary singer is on Ventilator, her family members assured one and all that she is responding well to the treatment and should return home soon. Fingers crossed!

 

Manushi Chhillar (GOOD): Miss World 2017 Manushi Chhillar has been signed by Yash Raj Films Studio for their magnum opus Prithviraj opposite Akshay Kumar. The announcement was just made recently and the lady is on cloud nine.

Tabu (GOOD): We all know what a powerhouse performer Tabu is! Her versatility has been proven time and again. Now, as she has bagged the sequel of Bhool Bhulaiyaa, we think her presence takes the project up a notch.

Rani Mukerji (GOOD): Rani Mukerji’s no-nonsence cop avatar of Shivani Shivaji Roy returns with Mardaani 2 after 5 long years. The trailer was unveiled recently and without a second thought, the actress is ready to kick some serious butt as she takes on a serial rapist. The actress packs in enough promise.

Sushant Singh Rajput (BAD): Sushant who was in the news for moving out of his apartment and shifting in with rumoured girlfriend Rhea Chakraborty is down with dengue. Doctors have advised him complete bed rest.
