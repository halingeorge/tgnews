Hey, remember Babylon's Fall? What do you mean, "no"? Who could forget its dramatic unveiling at... Er... E3 2018, apparently. It got a brief and entirely forgettable trailer at Square Enix's showcase, but to be fair, it is being developed by Platinum Games, so we do have some hope for this one despite all the snark.

Producer Yosuke Saito tells Japanese publication Automaton -- as translated by Gematsu -- that more information on Babylon's Fall is arriving before the year is out. Maybe it'll show up at The Game Awards in December? Hopefully it'll be enough to stick in our heads this time around.
