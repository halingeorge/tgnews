Man arrested on suspicion of two rapes in Shrewsbury

18 Nov 2019, 13:18 by Lucy Todman

A man has been arrested on suspicion of two rapes in Shrewsbury.

The 31-year-old man has been bailed while investigations continue.

A spokeswoman for West Mercia Police said: "A 31-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of two separate counts of rape and has been released on police bail."
