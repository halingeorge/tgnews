Gospel Singer Size 8, DJ Mo Welcome Second Child

18 Nov 2019, 13:53 by Eva Nyambura

Gospel artiste Linet Munyali alias Size 8 and disc jockey Sammy Muraya aka DJ Mo have apparently welcomed their second child.

According to those privy to the details, the couple welcomed their son, last week on Wednesday. The name of the newborn, is still unknown.

The source intimates that the child and mother are well.
