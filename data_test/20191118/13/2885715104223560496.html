Nayanthara spends birthday in New York with Vignesh Shivn

Director Vignesh Shivn took to Instagram to share an adorable picture of himself and girlfriend Nayanthara.

18 Nov 2019, 13:52 by Antara Chakraborthy

Nayanthara is celebrating her 35th birthday today. She is spending the day in New York with her boyfriend Vignesh Shivn.

Vignesh took to Instagram to share an adorable picture of himself and Nayanthara. He captioned the photo, “This Sky & her smile —— surreal!! Bringing in her birthday in #newyorkcity #nayanthara #love. This city is so so so beautiful! #freezing #nofilter #newyork #brooklynbridge #birthdaygirl #birthday #eve.”

In the photo, Nayanthara and Vignesh Shivn are standing on the Brooklyn Bridge, seemingly lost in each other. They are also seen taking a walk in Central Park. Another photo from their trip features filmmaker Boney Kapoor and daughter Khushi Kapoor.

On the work front, Nayanthara will be next seen in AR Murgadoss’ Darbar, opposite superstar Rajinikanth. She is also busy with Milind Rau’s Netrikaan and has signed Mookoothi Amman with RJ Balaji.

Vignesh Shivn, on the other hand, is making a film with Sivakarthikeyan, which is tentatively titled SK17. Anirudh Ravichandran has been roped in to compose music for the film.
