'Humanity over politics': Raveena Tandon hails Pakistani for saving Indian flight from disaster

18 Nov 2019, 13:21 by News Desk

Iconic Indian actress Raveena Tandon hailed a Pakistani air traffic controller on Monday for his heroics in saving a Jaipur flight from disaster.

According to LiveMint, the air traffic controller helped guide the flight from India’s Jaipur to Muscat through an air traffic emergency.

Tandon took to Twitter thanking the Pakistani citizen,”When humanity wins over politics. Pakistani air traffic controller saves a Jaipur to Muscat flight from disaster.”
