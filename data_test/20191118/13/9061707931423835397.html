A38 speed limit to be cut while lane is closed for nearly a month

It could cause major delays

18 Nov 2019, 13:05 by Jenny Moody

One lane of the A38 near Burton is set to be closed for nearly a month.

According to Highways England, work is set to be carried out from 8pm tonight, November 18, until 6am on Friday, December 13, meaning drivers could experience delays on their journeys.

However, motorists have reported the lane closure was already in place last night.

One lane of the A38 will be closed on the northbound carriageway from the Fradley over-bridge to the start of the Alrewas north exit slip road.

Highways England has said that this is due to electrical works being carried out in the area from junction A5127 to junction A513.

A speed restriction to all drivers using the busy carriageway will be in place while the work is being carried out. It is not known what the temporary limit will be.
