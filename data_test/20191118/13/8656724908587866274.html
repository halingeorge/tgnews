The Infinix S5 is rumored to launch with an infinity O-Display – Techjaja

18 Nov 2019, 13:14 by pages/Techjaja/291142381017322

Infinix is set to launch the very first phone model with an Infinity O-Display. The Infinix S5 set to launch on Wednesday 20th November 2019, hoping to woo its biggest fans with the Infinity O-Display and camera features.

The Infinix “S” series is well known for the front camera (otherwise known as the selfie camera). It replaces the Infinix S4 that launched with a waterdrop notch mid this year whose first impressions & review we covered.

Expected Infinix S5 Specs

According to credible sources, the Infinix S5 will come in two major versions, S5 (64GB ROM/4GB RAM) and S5 Lite (32GB ROM &3GB RAM).

The new O-Display 6.6-inch screen is designed to leave more space for a wider display experience housing its front camera at the upper left corner to make it easier to take selfies. As if that is not enough, The Infinix S5is rumored to have Rear Quad Camera taking mobile photography to a whole new level in just a click even in the dark.

The Infinix S5 is said to be the first model that offers Quad Camera at its price range (the source anticipates it to be between UGX600,000/= to UGX700,000/=)

The 3D curved surface designed model is also said to be equipped with a 4000mAh battery that can last 2 days for an average user without charge supported by 2A fast charging technology.

Other specs

  • 6.6″ HD+ Infinity O-Display
  • 32MP FF / 16MP AF + 5MP AF + 2MP FF +AI Lens Cameras
  • 4000 mAh Battery
  • 2G/3G/4G Connectivity

We await on its launch for more information in regards to price, available colors and other key specifications.
