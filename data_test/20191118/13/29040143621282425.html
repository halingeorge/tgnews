The successor

T-Mobile COO Mike Sievert to replace John Legere as CEO next year

Legere will continue to focus on a smooth leadership transition and closing the Sprint acquisition.

18 Nov 2019, 13:54 by Babu Mohan

What you need to know

  • T-Mobile today announced that Mike Sievert will succeed John Legere as T-Mobile CEO on May 1, 2020.
  • John Legere will continue as the CEO of T-Mobile until the end of his contract on April 30, 2020.
  • Legere will also remain a member of the board.

T-Mobile has announced that its current President and COO Mike Sievert will replace John Legere as the company's CEO on May 1, 2020. Legere will continue to serve as T-Mobile CEO until April 30, 2020, which is when his contract is set to come to an end.
