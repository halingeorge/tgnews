Watch: Hrithik Roshan's mum Pinkie Roshan shares a throwback video of the actor dancing

18 Nov 2019, 13:48 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

It's known to all that Hrithik Roshan is an outstanding dancer and the latest songs from his last outing is proof. He can give a tough competition to anyone in the industry. However, it seems like this dancing bug was in him right from his childhood.

Recently, the actor's mother Pinkie Roshan took to her Instagram profile to share a throwback video. In the video, we can see the actor sporting a blue shirt with a pair of trousers and dancing on the stage as if nobody’s watching. His mother shared the video and wrote, "#onecapturedmoments".

Meanwhile, this year proved to be a boon for Hrithik as the saw two big hits at the box office. The recently released action flick co-starring Tiger Shroff and 'Super 30' which was based on the Mathematician scholar Anand Kumar who trained 30 economically-backwards students for IIT-JEE. While talking to media about the movie Anand Kumar said, “I am very grateful for Hrithik Roshan and the whole team of Super 30 for bringing this story to the public. He is as amazing as a person as he is an actor and I wouldn’t have anyone else to play the role.”
