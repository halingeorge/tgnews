Best charity Christmas gifts 2019 for presents that give back

18 Nov 2019, 13:26 by Precious Adesina

While Christmas is a time for giving and sharing with your family and friends, it can also be used as an opportunity to give to charity.

One way to make Christmas go further is by choosing unusual presents that have a charity attached to it in some way. This could be by gifting someone an item that, when bought, a donation is given to a specific UK or international charity. Another option is to donate to a cause in someone's name.

We certainly love the idea of using Christmas gifts to give back to the community. Here, you can find a list of our top charity Christmas gifts, including everything from jewellery to more specific forms of aid.  

You can also get involved in The Daily Telegraph's Christmas Charity Appeal for 2019.
