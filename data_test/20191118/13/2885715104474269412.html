Tata Memorial Centre recruitment 2019: Apply for professor posts, salary above 1 lakh

Tata Memorial Centre recruitment 2019: TMC has invited applications for professor posts, apply till November 22 at the official website-tmc.gov.in

18 Nov 2019, 13:46 by Careers Desk

Tata Memorial centre recruitment 2019: The Tata Memorial Centre has released an official notification inviting applications for the Professor posts. Interested candidates can apply on the official website-tmc.gov.in till November 22, 2019.

The applications are invited for the Surgical Oncology, Radiation Oncology, Aneasthesiology, Radio Diagnosis, Medical Oncology and Pathalogy posts.

Tata Memorial Centre recruitment 2019: Eligibility

-Age: Interested candidates should not be more than 50 years of age. However, SC/ ST/ PwD category candidates will get age relaxation according to the government norms.

-Academic qualification: The candidates must have MD/ DNB or equivalent postgraduate degree in their respective fields with minimum 8 years post MD or 9 years post DNB experience.

However, the Professor in Surgical Oncology should have M.Ch.(Surgical Oncology) or any equivalent degree with 5 years post M.Ch experience or MS (General Surgery / ENT) or equivalent postgraduate degree recognized by MCI with 8 years post M.S. experience in any teaching institute.

Tata Memorial Centre recruitment 2019: Salary

The selected candidates will get a remuneration of Rs 1.23 lakh.

Tata Memorial Centre recruitment 2019: Application fee

-Interested candidates should pay Rs.300 as application fee. However, SC /ST / Female/PwD/Ex-servicemen do not have to pay any fee.

Tata Memorial Centre recruitment 2019: How to apply

Step 1: Visit the official website-tmc.gov.in

Step 2: On the homepage, click on the link that says, ‘Careers’

Step 3: On the page that opens, click on the link of online application form corresponding to the post you want to apply.

Step 4: Fill in the details asked and click on submit.

Step 5: Pay the application fee and take a print out of the form for future reference.

Candidates must take a print out of the application form and submit mail it by marking ‘Post applied for Adv-129/2019’ to the 2nd Floor, Service Block building, H.R.D. Department, Tata Memorial Hospital, Parel, Mumbai – 400012 by November 29, 2019 along with the list of following documents.

-a recent passport size photograph

-Birth Certificate / School leaving certificate / S.S.C passing certificate

-Educational Qualification

-Experience Certificates

– Caste Certificate

-Persons with Disability certificate.
