Chris Hemsworth looks cool while surfing as he reveals yet another reason to lov

18 Nov 2019, 14:40

Chris Hemsworth is already a talented actor, loving husband and devoted family man.

And now pictures reveal he’s excellent at surfing too.

If he wasn’t so awesome we would have to hate him.

The Avengers star was spotted riding some waves in his homeland of Australia and looking very cool about it all.

In an Instagram post he revealed he was at Urban Surf in Melbourne.

Alongside the snaps he wrote: ‘Straight off the plane and into the pool for more waves than I’ve had all year!’

Chris seems calm and collected as he navigates a huge wave. He even somehow managed to pull a silly face for one photo.

If that was us we’d have fallen off the surf board and be looking like a total mess by now.

But you can’t help but adore the Thor actor, especially with all the love he’s been giving out recently.

Only at the weekend he showed his soft side by sharing an old photo of young Chris and his mum, wishing her a happy birthday.

‘Happy birthday mum. True champion of champions. Love ya x’

Absolute angel.

And earlier in the month Chris was spotted cuddling his really cute dog on the beach.

He was out enjoying the sun and fresh air with his whole family – his wife Elsa Pataky and his three children, India Rose, seven and Tristan and Sasha, five.

Honestly can you get any more perfect than that?

To top it all off, the 36-year-old bought a house right next to his brother Liam in Broken Head, near Byron Bay to be there for his brother after the shock split from Miley Cyrus.
