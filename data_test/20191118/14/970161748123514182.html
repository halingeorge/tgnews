Spoilers: Daniel devastated in Corrie tonight as he returns for Sinead's funeral

18 Nov 2019, 14:26

It’s been a difficult few months for Daniel Osbourne (Rob Mallard) in Coronation Street, after wife Sinead Tinker (Katie McGlynn) died as a result of her cancer diagnosis. Understandably, he struggled to adjust to life without his wife, and thus he departed Weatherfield. However, Sinead’s funeral arrives tonight, and he decides to return to the cobbles as a result.

After Ken (William Roache) and Claudia (Rula Lenska) failed to track Daniel down in Scotland, Peter (Chris Gascoyne) discovered that he was staying in Chesire, and thus attempted to make him come home.

He ultimately failed to do so, but his words clearly got through to Daniel, as — with Sinead’s funeral set to take place — the young man returns to the cobbles during tonight’s episode.

With Sinead having been a much-loved resident of the Street, her funeral will no doubt be a highly emotional affair as all those who dwell in Weatherfield gather to bid her farewell.
