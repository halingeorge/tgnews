Take Me Out fans can holiday at Fernando's - but they've got to be quick

Anyone looking for love can grab a special trip to the idyllic isle location featured on the show to celebrate the latest run of the dating programme

18 Nov 2019, 14:34 by Brett Gibbons, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/brett-gibbons/

Fans of TV dating show Take Me Out know that the Isle of Fernando's is the location for would-be couples who want to get to know each other.

The destination, located in real-life at Puerto de la Cruz in Tenerife, is the ideal location for a romantic getaway and anyone looking for love can grab a special trip to the idyllic isle inspired by the show to celebrate the latest run of the dating show.

The limited edition chance to secure a place at the Take Me Out resort is available for one day only on November 18 with bookings through holiday website Travel Republic.

fans of the show can enjoy the sun, sea and glamour of the programme’s famous destination - staying in a 5* hotel and enjoying everything from spa treatments and local excursions.

Frank Rejwan, managing director at Travel Republic, commented: “Working with Take Me Out to bring Fernando’s to life has been a great way to make fans feel part of the show whilst also giving them an incredible holiday experience.

 

"At Travel Republic, we work hard to make sure we can provide everyone with the holidays they want so we’re delighted to bring this never-seen-before bookable package holiday to our customers.”

To book your Take Me Out Fernando’s - inspired trip, visit  https://www.travelrepublic.co.uk/offers/take-me-to-fernandos
