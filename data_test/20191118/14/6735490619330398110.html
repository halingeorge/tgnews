‘America’s Got Talent’ Opera Superstar Emanne Beasha Announces Christmas Album After Signing With Decca Records

18 Nov 2019, 14:41 by Aynslee Darmon

Emanne Beasha is ready to get festive.

The 11-year-old opera superstar who made her mark on the 14th season of “America’s Got Talent” just announced her very own holiday album, A Christmas Wish.

The new album is her first release since her run on the show, which led to her signing a deal with Decca Records.

The mini-EP will be feature Beasha’s soaring vocals on such familiar holiday classics as “Ave Maria” and “O Holy Night”.

“So excited about my first work with Decca Records U.S. at Universal Music Group A Christmas Wish 🎄 holiday set,” the youngster shared on Instagram.

A Christmas Wish will drop Nov. 22.
