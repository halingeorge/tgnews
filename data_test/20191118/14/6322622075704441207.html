India's October oil imports down 3.3% on year — trade data

18 Nov 2019, 14:43 by Reuters

NEW DELHI (Nov 18): India imported about 4.56 million barrels per day (bpd) of oil in October, down nearly 3.3% from the same month a year ago, according to data from shipping and industry sources.

The Middle East's share in India's total oil imports declined to about 57% in October from 58% in September, while the share of African grades declined to 16.5% from 21%. South American grades were about 12% of India's overall purchases compared to about 11% in September, the data showed.
