Cyberpunk FPS ‘Ghostrunner’ to be Produced by 3D Realms

18 Nov 2019, 14:36 by Mike Wilson

The house that Duke Nukem built, aka 3D Realms, have announced that they will be producing and distributing One More Level’s cyberpunk action game Ghostrunner, which was last shown off at this year’s Gamescom. All in! Games will still be publishing the game, which is slated for a 2020 release.
