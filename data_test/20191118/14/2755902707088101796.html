“This Food is for ZANU PF Supporters Only,” WATCH as ZANU PF Official politicises food

18 Nov 2019, 14:51 by Philip Mangwiro

It’s Official, If you are not a ZANU PF supporter you will go hungry. An unnamed ZANU PF official was recorded on video telling party members that the food he had brought was exclusive to ZANU PF supporters. He is heard boldly declaring that any member of the opposition was going hungry even making a reference to the Local MDC Councillor.

“Tikugamuchira chikafu chava Mnangagwa,Muno muna Concillor we MDC apa Haulume,”
