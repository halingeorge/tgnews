Jammu JKBOSE 11th results for private students declared, check direct link

JKBOSE 11th result: The students can check the result through the official website- jkbose.ac.in

18 Nov 2019, 14:34 by Education Desk

JKBOSE 11th result: The Jammu and Kashmir State Board of School Education (JKBOSE) declared the results for Class 11 bi-annual examinations for Jammu division students. The students can check the result through the official website- jkbose.ac.in.

The HS part one result is equivalent to class 11.

JKBOSE 11th Result 2019 Jammu Division: How to download

Step 1: Visit the official website, jkbose.ac.in

Step 2: On the homepage, click on the link ‘part one HS result 2019’

Step 3: You will be redirected to the new page

Step 4: Log-in using roll number, click submit

Step 5: Result will appear on the screen.

Download the result and take a print out, it will act as a provisional mark sheet.

There are over 4,225 affiliated schools affiliated to the Board. It is also the main board of school education of Jammu and Kashmir which conducts the exam for Jammu, Kashmir, Leh and Kargil divisions.
