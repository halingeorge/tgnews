Here’s how Shah Rukh Khan’s noble gesture inspired a biopic on boxing legend Kaur Singh

18 Nov 2019, 14:02 by Filmfare

The lesser-known side to Bollywood superstar, Shah Rukh Khan is that he's a philanthropist. The actor has been involved with a lot of charity and had even donated five lakhs to boxing legend, Kaur Singh, in 2017, when he was out of job and struggling to pay his medical bills. Now, actor Karam Batth is making a film on the boxer's life. Said Batth, “When I first got to know about Kaur Singh and his achievements (in December 2017), he was undergoing post-operation treatment in Mohali. His poor financial condition and inability to meet the medical expenses came as a shock for me. The pitiable condition of the Arjuna awardee also moved actor Shah Rukh Khan so much so that he gave him an amount of Rs 5 lakh through his team Kolkata Knight Riders.”

Karam further added, "After listening to the news, I was sure about my decision of choosing this story for my film. I was confident that people deserve to know about the struggles and life of this legend."

The film will also feature Prabh Grewal, Baninder Bunny and Sukhbir Gill and has been shot in various parts of Punjab. It is expected to release in 2020.
