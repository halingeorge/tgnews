Nsubuga, Ssebuguzi target MX65 crown

18 Nov 2019, 14:50 by Johnson Were

Four points separate the two riders contesting for the crown.
 
Mountain Dew National Motocross Championship
 
Date: November 24
Venue: Garuga
Entry fee: sh10, 000
 
It will be fireworks as Gift Ssebuguzi battles Kreidah Nsubuga for the MX65 title in the Mountain Dew National Motocross Championship in Garuga on Sunday.
 
Four points separate the two riders contesting for the crown.
 
Nsubuga, one of the most improving riders, learned a lot from foreign riders after a training programme abroad.
 
He has on several occasions beaten Ssebuguzi at the start gate but failed to secure victory because of the latter’s speed.
 
However, Nsubuga is determined to achieve a better performance according to his father Peter Ndiwalana.
 
“He is in good shape, has trained on the newly designed track and I believe can do better,” Ndiwalana said.
 
Ten-year-old Nsubuga, a P5 pupil at St. Agnes Primary school has 263 points while 9-year-old Ssebuguzi, a primary three pupil at City Parents primary school has 267.
 
Ssebuguzi’s mother Nulu Nabaweesi said,” Gift has been a bit sick, but he is ready to compete and I believe will achieve victory
