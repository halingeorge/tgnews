Twinkle Khanna visits grandmother Betty Kapadia in the hospital

18 Nov 2019, 14:54 by Poonam Jain

Veteran actor Dimple Kapadia’s mother, Betty Kapadia, was hospitalised on November 14. She was admitted to the ICU at the Hinduja hospital in Mumbai. Earlier in the day there were rumours of Dimple Kapadia being hospitalised after pictures of Twinkle Khanna and Akshay Kumar outside the same hospital were doing the rounds on the internet.

But Dimple refuted the rumours saying,"I am alive and kicking. It's my mother, who has been hospitalised. I don't want to talk about it. My mother is doing fine. She is better now. I need all the prayers and best wishes.”
