Water released for first crop in Palani taluk

18 Nov 2019, 14:38 by Staff Reporter

PALANI

Water for irrigation of first crop in areas of Palani taluk was released from the Palar Porundhalar and Kuthiraiyar dams here on Monday.

Minister for Forests Dindigul C. Srinivasan released 128.43 cusecs of water for 130 days, totalling to a maximum quantum of 1442.50 mcft from the Palar Porundhalar dam.

About 6168 acres of land in villages in Palani taluk like Periyammapatti, Thamaraikulam, A. Kalaiyamputhur, Manoor, Korikadavu, Keeranur and Kottathurai will benefit from the release.

The Minister also released water for 90 days, totalling to a maximum quantum of 56.31 mcft from the Kuthiraiyar dam. About 700 acres of land in Andipatti village of Palani taluk will benefit.

District Revenue Officer B. Balu, Palani Sub-Collector S. Uma, former Mayor V. Marudharaj, and officials from PWD and other departments were present.
