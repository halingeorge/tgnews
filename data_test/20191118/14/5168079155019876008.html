These pictures of Oman National Day celebrations has gone viral

18 Nov 2019, 14:30 by Times News Service

Muscat: For photographers, every national day is a time to exhibit the beauty of Oman through their lenses.

Times of Oman spoke to popular photographers Adnan, Moosa, and Khalfan to know how they feel about capturing the mood of national say celebrations.

Adnan Dhaeesh from Dhofar governorate said: "I am keen to portray the mood of the national celebration and reflect this joy and happiness through pictures and share it with people through my social

media accounts . This occasion means a lot to us and everyone is eager to participate and express their love for Oman in their own way."

Moosa Al Hajri, posted a number of pictures of national day celebration from the Empty Quarter which was attended by adults and children.
