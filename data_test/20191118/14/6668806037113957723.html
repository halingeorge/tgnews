Gauri Khan, Kareena Kapoor Khan and more grieve the loss of Manish Malhotra’s father

18 Nov 2019, 14:29 by Kajol Mathur

Ace fashion designer Manish Malhotra’s father passed away Monday morning after battling ill health for quite sometime. Mr Malhotra was in his early 90s and soon after the news of his demise broke out, various B-town stars rushed to offer their condolences to the grieving family.

Snapped at Manish Malhotra’s Mumbai residence this evening were celebrities Gauri Khan, Jaya Bachchan, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Shweta Bachchan, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Karisma Kapoor, Malaika Arora, Amrita Arora, Raveena Tandon, Shilpa Shetty, Rima Jain, filmmaker Farah Khan, Diana Penty, Kiara Advani, Athiya Shetty among others.
