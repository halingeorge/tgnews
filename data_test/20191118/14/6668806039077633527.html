Boney Kapoor gets emotional as late wife Sridevi awarded with the ANR Award

18 Nov 2019, 14:21 by Filmfare

It’s been over a year since Sridevi left us but she continues to live in everyone’s memories. Recently, the Akkineni Nageswara Rao Award was bestowed on the late actress and husband Boney Kapoor received it on Sridevi’s behalf.

While he did so, he got emotional as he received the ANR Award which was presented to him by South superstars, Chiranjeevi and Nagarjuna. On receiving the prestigious award, Boney Kapoor gave an acceptance speech. While receiving the honour, Boney broke down in tears as he remembered his late wife. He said, "I’m sure she (Sridevi) would have felt humbled to receive this award and I accept this on her behalf with humility." He also thanked Nagarjuna, Chiranjeevi and the ANR Awards committee.

Boney Kapoor took to social media to share the special moment with Sridevi’s fans. He wrote, "A big thank you to #ANRAwards #AkkineniFamily, Chairman ANR awards committee @tsubbaramireddy, Chiranjeevi for honoring @SrideviBKapoor with #ANRNationalAwards. @iamnagarjuna #LegendsLiveOn."

South megastars, Nagarjuna and Chiranjeevi, also remembered Sridevi and praised the late actress for her achievements. Chiranjeevi remembered Sridevi and called her, ‘The Lady Superstar of India', while Nagarjuna said that she waa the Goddess of grace and would live on as long as cinema is alive.

Bollywood actress Rekha also received the ANR Award for 2019. After receiving the award, Rekha said, "Sridevi is my little sister and an actor par excellence." She also said that it is an honour for her to receive the award alongside Sridevi, which is like a homecoming for her.
