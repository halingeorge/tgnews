Chadwick Boseman Wants Nothing To Do with a Marvel/Disney+ Series

18 Nov 2019, 14:11 by Tom Miller

With the buzz around Marvel's upcoming Disney+ series steadily increasing, Black Panther star Chadwick Boseman was asked which of his Avengers co-stars he would want to team up with for an adventure on the small screen.

"I wouldn't, because I don't want to do a TV show," he said, adding that he has other things he's excited about doing and "none of those are franchises."
