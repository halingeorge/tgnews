Kanye West calls himself 'the greatest' artist God has ever created

18 Nov 2019, 14:06

Cape Town – Another week, another claim by Kanye West that we're all blessed to be living in the same time as him. Kanye, who leads a weekly Sunday Service, made a special appearance at Pastor Joel Osteen's service at his Lakewood megachurch in Houston on Sunday.

In a video posted by celebrity news site TMZ, the father-of-four said: "Now, the greatest artist that God has ever exist- uh, created, is now working for Him."

He added: "I know that God has been calling me for a long time and the Devil has been distracting me for a long time."

Earlier the rapper said: "God is using me to show off" when he got a large tax break worth millions of dollars. 

Just a little while ago Kanye also said that he has been with Kim for what feels like 500 years because marriage years are more like dog years.

In between all of those controversial comments the creative has released an album titled Jesus is King, which was released well after it's the due date.
