Bigg Boss 13: Netizens Not Happy With Arhaan Khan’s Eviction; #Whyarhaankhan Trends On Social Media

18 Nov 2019, 14:04 by Amruta Patel

Arhaan Khan’s eviction from Bigg Boss 13 house has not gone done well with netizens and his followers. Post Arhaan bidding goodbye to the show, #Whyarhaankhan started trending on Twitter, supporting him and asking makers to give him one more chance

This weekend saw wild card entrant Arhaan Khan packing his bags and bidding goodbye to the Bigg Boss house. Arhaan was compelled to leave the house, due to less number of votes. Reported to be dating Rashami Desai, viewers were expecting to see some sparks between the rumoured couple, but the two remained in denial. This eviction on the show was quite a shocker to both the housemates and audience. Arhaan’s eviction from the house got the social media trending with the hashtag, #WhyArhaanKhan.

Many social media users, in their post, expressed of Arhaan being one of the most deserving candidates and deserves one more chance for his fair play on the show. Some of Twitter users tweeted, “I think Arhaan khan deserves one more chance. #WhyArhaanKhan,” “Bigg Boss 13 may be full of twists but this eviction is totally shocking! Hard to believe that a deserving contestant like Arhaan Khan was eliminated! This is unfair! #WhyArhaanKhan” and many more tweets came pouring in.
