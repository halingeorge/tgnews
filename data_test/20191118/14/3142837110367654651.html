National Bank Financial Trims NEWMONT GOLD-TS (TSE:NGT) Target Price to C$61.00

18 Nov 2019, 14:28 by Teresa Graham

NEWMONT GOLD-TS (TSE:NGT) had its price target decreased by research analysts at National Bank Financial from C$64.00 to C$61.00 in a note issued to investors on Monday, BayStreet.CA reports. The brokerage presently has an “outperform” rating on the stock. National Bank Financial’s price objective suggests a potential upside of 21.85% from the company’s previous close.

Shares of NGT stock traded up C$0.36 during trading hours on Monday, hitting C$50.06. 72,181 shares of the company’s stock were exchanged, compared to its average volume of 149,144. The company has a debt-to-equity ratio of 33.65, a current ratio of 2.18 and a quick ratio of 1.39. The company has a market cap of $41.29 billion and a price-to-earnings ratio of 14.84. NEWMONT GOLD-TS has a fifty-two week low of C$40.01 and a fifty-two week high of C$54.87.

About NEWMONT GOLD-TS

Newmont Goldcorp Corporation, together with its subsidiaries, operates in the mining industry. The company primarily acquires, develops, explores for, and produces gold, copper, and silver. Its operations and/or assets are located in the United States, Australia, Peru, Ghana, and Suriname. As of December 31, 2018, the company had proven and probable gold reserves of 65.4 million ounces and an aggregate land position of approximately 24,000 square miles.
