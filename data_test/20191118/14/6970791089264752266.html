POWER Pitches Idea Of Tariq + Saxe Wanting Ghost Taken Out: “Did He Take The Law Into His Own Hands”

18 Nov 2019, 14:27 by Rosario Harper

The “Power” marketing team have nearly wrapped up their line-up of possible mid-season-ending shooters line-up. The series has teased the idea of characters Cooper Saxe and Tariq St. Patrick wanting Ghost in a plastic bag.

This past weekend, the “Power” socials shared promo clips making cases for both fictional characters.
