Canadian stunner Danielle Knudson most likely caused a few jaws to drop among her numerous fans when she shared a sizzling bikini look on Sunday afternoon. The gorgeous lingerie model updated her Instagram page with a eye-catching seaside photo that showed her sunning her beautiful bod in a cute little two-piece, and racked up thousands of likes from her followers.

Danielle was wearing a chic teal bikini that perfectly showcased her insane body. The swimsuit was a triangle string design made up of a plunging top and low-waist, v-shaped bottoms. The revealing cut showed plenty of toned, bronzed skin, offering fans a copious view of Danielle’s incredible figure. The fair-haired beauty topped off her look with a trendy beige fedora by Joël Joseph, which beautifully flattered the color of her beach apparel. She accessorized with stylish oval sunglasses, which sported tan-colored frames that matched her hat.

The 30-year-old hottie put her fierce physique on full display in the itty-bitty bikini. Snapped as she soaked up the sun in a stunning tropical setting, the blond bombshell flaunted her toned midriff and washboard abs — striking a coquettish pose that highlighted her fit, muscular body. Danielle cocked a hip to the side and slightly bent the other knee, in a posture that emphasized her chiseled thighs. Likewise, her sculpted hip and supple, muscular calves were also on display, as was her taut waistline and impossibly flat tummy.

The gorgeous Guess girl added a whimsical touch to the shot by grabbing the brim of her hat in a playful gesture, which drew attention to her slender arms. Photographed from the mid-profile, she gazed into the distance with a beaming smile. Her golden tresses were styled with a mid-part and framed her gorgeous face, falling down her back and over her shoulders in loose waves. The soft-blond color of her locks was a perfect match to her glowing tan, both of which were beautifully accentuated by the teal bikini.

Danielle was standing next to a swanky pool, one overlooking a lush garden by the sea. While the model didn’t mention where the photo was taken — her post didn’t have a geotag attached to it — the snap was most likely a throwback photo from her Italian vacation on the Amalfi Coast. The stunning pic offered a mesmerizing view of a picturesque coastline and showed a glimpse of a crystal-clear sea. The Canadian beauty captioned the photo with a single emoji that showed a grinning face with smiling eyes, which truly captured the playful mood of her post.

As expected, the sun-kissed bikini shot immediately caught the eye of her fans, garnering 4,200 likes and a little shy of 50 comments. Plenty of followers were left speechless by the sexy look, expressing their admiration solely through the use of emoji. The Joël Joseph Instagram account followed suit, leaving three fire emoji under the gorgeous snap.

Other Instagrammers proved more loquacious, penning sweet messages of adoration for the Canadian model.
