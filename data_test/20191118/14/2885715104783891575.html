IIM-Kozhikode enters into collaboration with Brunel University on education, research

The MoU, signed recently, will be effective for three years and is intended to promote cooperation and advancement of international understanding between both the Institutes

18 Nov 2019, 14:13 by PTI

The Indian Institute of Management Kozhikode (IIM-K)) has signed a memorandum of understanding with Brunel University London to join forces on Education and Research Collaboration.

The signatories to the MoU are Prof Debashis Chatterjee, Director IIM-K and Prof. Rebecca Lingwood, Provost, Brunel University, a press release said.

The MoU, signed recently, will be effective for three years and is intended to promote cooperation and advancement of international understanding between both the Institutes besides encouraging research collaboration in areas of mutual interest.

Brunel University was represented by a three-member faculty team of Dr Monomita Nandy, Dr David Sarpong and Dr Claire Donavan.
