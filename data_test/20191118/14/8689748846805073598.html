I’d Rather Stay At Home And Pray Than Go To Church Filled With Hypocrites – Xandy Kamel discloses(+Video)

18 Nov 2019, 14:33 by vanessa nordzi

Ghanaian actress and entrepreneur Xandy Kamel has revealed why she has stopped attending church.

Xandy who was originally born a catholic has joined lots of other celebrities who have quit going to church.

In a recent interview with Zionfelix on his uncut show, Xandy Kamel disclosed that  her decision to quit church is because of the high level of hypocrisy in the churches.

According to Xandy, the church is plagued with a “family and friends” syndrome because no one helps you unless they have an affiliation with you.

Xandy believes in praying at home rather than attend church because some pastors look down on and shy away from celebrities who are very outspoken.

“the church is supposed to reform people so if you will just criticise and not straighten me then I’ll just stay in my house and pray”.
