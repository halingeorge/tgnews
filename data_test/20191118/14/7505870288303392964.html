The Live Capture Adapter (JVA02) is exactly what you need for handling video recording and playback, as it is a powerful HDMI™ capture card. It allows you to capture 60 frames per second of uncompressed 1080p video under USB™ 3.0, providing you with a remarkably clear picture and video playback. The JVA02 connects easily to the cold shoe adapter of your camera so you can live stream without any visual hiccups. You’ll be recording full HD video and audio, thanks to the USB-C™ 3.1 connection.

Speaking of HD audio, while you’re consistently broadcasting, you can use three audio mixing modes to produce the sound that you want. There’s a microphone input option that will give your video the levels it needs and you’ll be able to clearly hear exactly what was recorded. There’s also a HDMI™ mode as well as a Mix mode for professional listening and recording.

This adapter is made out of super lightweight aluminum, so it’s built to last and keeping it in your computer or camera bag is perfectly viable. One of the amazing features of the JVA02 is that it consumes a low amount of power but has Power Delivery 3.0, so you can charge your devices while you’re using it to live stream. It’s also fully compatible with OBS™ and other third-party software.

Now if you’re wondering how exactly you get it to hook up to your camera, j5create has you covered! With the purchase of this adapter, you’ll receive a USB-C™ to USB-C™ cable, a USB-C™ to USB™ Type-A cable, a Quick Installation Guide and two screws to attach to the cold shoe adapter. It also comes backed by a two-year limited warranty.
