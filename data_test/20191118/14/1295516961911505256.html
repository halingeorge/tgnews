Marvel's Runaways Season 3 Trailer Reveals Why Cloak & Dagger Show Up

18 Nov 2019, 14:11 by Noah Dominguez

Hulu has officially revealed a full-length trailer for the third season of Marvel's Runaways, which is scheduled to drop on the streaming platform on Dec. 13. The trailer confirms Runaways Season 3 will be the series' last.

The trailer revisits the team of teenage superheroes, who are still reeling from the events of the Season 2 finale. Season 3 joins the Runaways on a quest to locate their comrades Chase, Gert and Karolina, all of whom were captured as Season 2 came to a close.
