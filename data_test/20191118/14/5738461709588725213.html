Service of Hope for Our Lady of Lourdes unit

Oncology Unit

18 Nov 2019, 14:19 by David Lynch

NECRET and the Oncology and Haematology Service in Our Lady of Lourdes Hospital in Drogheda invite those currently attending or have previously attended the Unit, to their Annual Service of Hope on Friday December 6 at 1pm in the Westcourt Hotel, Drogheda. Light refreshments will also be provided afterwards.
