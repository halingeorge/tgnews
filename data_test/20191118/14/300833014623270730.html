Renewed Apple MacBook Air and MacBook Pro Computers on Deals

18 Nov 2019, 14:26 by Review Team

Apple products are considered to be priced on the higher side. The company claims it has a superior technology to offer and the users go with this claim. There may still be a large group of computer users out there who would want to own a MacBook Air or a MacBook Pro if the prices are affordable. There are renewed models of Apple computers available at much lower prices than the new ones. They are usually certified and are good for use for a long time.

Apple 2018 13.3-Inch MacBook Air (Renewed)

This refurbished Apple MacBook Air has a 13.3-inch LED-backlit IPS display. The processor is an Intel Core i5-8210Y CPU. It’s a dual-core processor. The UHD Graphics 617 provides graphics support. The RAM is 8 GB DDR4 SDRAM and the storage memory is 128 GB PCI-E flash. You have three microphones, stereo speakers and a 3.5 mm headphone jack. The product is certified for its reliability. The seller has thrown in a year’s warranty.

This refurbished Apple 13.3-inch MacBook Air is currently sold at $749.99.
