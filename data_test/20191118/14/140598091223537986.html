Christmas gifts for men 2019: Guide to the best present ideas for him this year

18 Nov 2019, 14:01 by Precious Adesina

While you can never go wrong with a good sock or tie when buying Christmas gifts for men, make 2019 the year you surprise him by gifting something a little different. Your present doesn't have to be extravagant (it's the thought that counts after all) just different enough that he'll know you put some thought into it. 

But getting the perfect present is no easy task when it comes to treating your boyfriend, husband, father or son - which is why we're providing you with ample inspiration. This extensive list of Christmas gifts for men includes everything from ethically conscious clothing and wardrobe staples to whiskey and mountain climbing experience days. 

If you're feeling creative this year, you could always try your hand at a homemade gift too?

Though, if he does love receiving typical (but very useful) items, such as socks, briefs or razors, then our round-up of the best Christmas gifts for men has got those options covered. Sometimes practicality is the way forward. Enjoy.
