London Tops EU Incomes League

18 Nov 2019, 14:21 by David Manners

Didposable incomes vary widely across Europe says Eurostat.

Inner London West is the EU region with the highest levels of annual disposable income.

The research measured disposable income in purchasing power consumption standard.

Oberbayern in Bavaria came second, while Outer London West and North West came third.

 

Eastern Europe had the lowest levels of disposable income and four of the lowest five were in Bulgaria.

The lowest ranked region is actually Mayotte, an overseas department of France  in the Mozambique Channel, it is France’s poorest region by a significant margin.
