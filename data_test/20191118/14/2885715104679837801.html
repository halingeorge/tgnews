Bigg Boss 13 evicted contestant Arhaan Khan: Will miss Rashami Desai the most

Arhaan Khan had entered Bigg Boss 13 as a wild card but could not really make a mark in his two-week long stay.

18 Nov 2019, 14:56 by Sana Farzeen

Host Salman Khan on Sunday evicted Arhaan Khan from Bigg Boss 13. The actor had entered the show as a wild card but could not really make a mark in his two-week long stay. Post his eviction, Arhaan spoke exclusively to indianexpress.com and shared his disappointment.

He shared, “Everyone who I am meeting is in shock, just like me. I still can’t comprehend what led to my eviction. I did so many tasks and I think it’s the first time in the history of the show that after winning a challenge, a housemate got eliminated. The journey was good but I wish it was long. There was so much more that I wanted to do in the show.”

The Badho Bahu actor was rumoured to be dating co-contestant Rashami Desai. When asked if the lack of chemistry between them caused fans to not vote for him, Arhaan said, “Whatever feedback I have received is that they were really enjoying my time with Rashami. They were all rooting for us. The only problem is that I got eliminated too soon. Or else fans would have definitely seen a lot of us in the coming days.”

The 31-year-old was recently given the title of Bol Bachchan on the show by fans. Stating that he accepted the title with pride, the actor said, “My game kickstarted the moment I entered the show. There were eight weird tags and everyone had to wear one. Earlier, the housemates had given the ‘Bol Bachchan’ title to Bhau but then it came to me. Honestly, I didn’t take it as an insult rather a compliment. It means someone who talks big, and I do. But I also have the confidence to stick on my words and do complete justice too.”
