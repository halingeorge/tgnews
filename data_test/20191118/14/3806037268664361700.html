Air Jordan 11 Low "Cool Grey" Makes Its Way Onto the Golf Course

Last spotted on the greens in 2015.

18 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Ambrose Leung

Jordan Brand continues its strong release of OG colorways of the AJ11 but this time in golf cleat form. Following up from its past all-white AJ11 cleat, Jumpman has chosen to revisit an iconic “Cool  Grey” colorway from 2001.

The uppers resemble the 1995 silhouette in every way, from the tonal-grey patent leather mixed with pebbled leather and synthetics, down to the recognizable lace supports and icy-blue translucent rubber outsole. Readying this pair of Cool Greys for the green are the set of golf spikes that sit just below the outsole.

Those looking to try their hand at golf, just like Mike, can find these Cool Grey Air Jordan 11 Lows dropping on November 22 at nike.com and select retailers for $220 USD.
