Spoilers: Corrie paedophile Kel takes Bernie hostage

18 Nov 2019, 14:55

Evil sex abuser Kel (Joseph Alessi) is set to bring serious danger to Bernie Winter (Jane Hazlegrove) when he takes her hostage after she confronts him over being a paedophile in Coronation Street.

Paul Foreman (Peter Ash) recently confided in his tortured past as a teenager who was groomed and sexually abused by stepdad Kel. And this week, the truth will come to light to the rest of his family as a spate of burglaries ends up exposing Kel.

Coronation Street has now revealed a fresh look at what’s to come as an explosive showdown rocks the household. When Kel confronts Bernie after he finds himself in trouble with the police, things turn violent and he holds her hostage.
