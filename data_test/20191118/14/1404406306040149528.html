NBA YoungBoy's Mother Says He Kicked Her Out of the House He Bought Her

18 Nov 2019, 14:20 by Jessica Todd

NBA YoungBoy's mother took to Instagram Live to reveal that the Baton Rouge rapper has kicked her out of the house that he bought for her.

She explained in the video that she was tired of being told what to do, including who she can have over to her house. YoungBoy's mother added, "My child is one of the best rappers, but you're not fixing to rule me." She went on to speak about being told not to get on Instagram Live at the house, which you can hear more about above.
