Rajinikanth completes dubbing for Darbar

Besides Rajinikanth, Darbar also stars Nayanthara, Nivetha Thomas, Dalip Tahil, Suniel Shetty, Sriman, Yogi Babu and Prateik Babbar.

18 Nov 2019, 14:09 by Entertainment Desk

Director AR Murugadoss had recently revealed that Rajinikanth started dubbing for Darbar. In addition, he posted a picture of the Superstar seated in a dubbing studio.

Today, Murugadoss tweeted that Rajinikanth had finished his portions. “One of the best dubbing sessions in my life… Thalaivar Darbar dubbing completed,” read his tweet, with a photo of the duo discussing something.
