Michael Jackson's Accusers Reportedly May Get New Trial

18 Nov 2019, 14:26 by VladTV

According to reports, the California Court of Appeals issued a tentative ruling that would allow the dismissal of lawsuits filed by Wade Robson and James Safechuck against Michael Jackson for sexual abuse to be overturned. This move was based on the grounds of a new California law that extends the statute of limitations on child sex abuse cases.

Previously, Jackson accusers Robson and Safechuck filed suit against Jackson and his estate, but both cases were dismissed. Now, the two accusers may be able to present their arguments to a jury.

Oral arguments for the tentative ruling are set to be made this week. Stay tuned for more details.
