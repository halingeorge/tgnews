Netflix reveals pics of Pearl Thusi and cast of 'Queen Sono'

18 Nov 2019, 14:28 by Algoa FM

Netflix posted the first pics of Pearl Thusi and the cast of the African original series, 'Queen Sono'.

Queen Sono is a Netflix South Africa original series. The lead character who is played by Pearl Thusi, is a highly trained South African spy who faces changing relationships in her personal life while taking on the most dangerous missions yet.

Earlier today the netflixsa Instagram account posted the following pics with the following simple caption: "Get ready. She's coming. A first look at the first Netflix African Original #QueenSono."
