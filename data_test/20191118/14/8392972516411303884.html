KFC unveils Christmas box which includes festive burger and fries

A new shake is on the menu!

18 Nov 2019, 14:31 by Rebecca Carter

KFC has unveiled its Christmas menu for this year and it includes a festive burger and fries.

The fast food chain has announced their festive menu has arrived in stores today (November 18).

There are three new items on the menu - the KFC Festive Burger, Festive Fries and a new Mint Choc Krushem Soft Serve drink.

Read more: KFC is selling an 80-piece Popcorn Chicken bucket for just £5.99

The festive burger consists of a classic Original Recipe chicken fillet with a hash brown, sage and onion stuffing, mayo, cranberry dressing and a layer of cheese.

Just wow.

A round of santa-plause for this epic festive feast please.

The burger costs £4.59 by itself or you can get it as a meal with fries for £5.59.

But if you want to really push the boat out, you can buy a Festive Burger Box for £6.89, which includes the burger, a drink, fries, a tub of beans and a chicken drumstick.

The festive fries are seasoned with smokey pepper, toasted onions, garlic and herbs.

You can add a Mint Choc Krushem Soft Serve for £2.79 which includes vanilla ice cream drizzled with chocolate and mint sauce, Cookie Crunch sprinkles and finished with a chocolate bow tie.

The festive menu is available until January 1, 2020, and you can also order it on Deliveroo.

Top Festive Elf at KFC UK & Ireland Jo Tivers said of the menu: "A round of santa-plause for this epic festive feast please.

"We’ll let turkey have its moment on the 25th but until then, we’ve got your back."

Last month it was revealed that KFC was testing out an 80-piece Popcorn Chicken bucket in its Glasgow stores.

And the best part, it was only £5.99.
