Troy Aikman Has A Message For Jerry Jones About Dak Prescott

18 Nov 2019, 14:19 by Andrew Holleran

Dak Prescott continued to light up the stat sheet on Sunday afternoon, as the Dallas Cowboys quarterback put up monster numbers in a win over the Detroit Lions. Prescott threw for 444 yards and 3 touchdowns in a 35-27 victory in Detroit.

It’s been a monster year for Prescott, who’s on pace for a record-setting Cowboys season.

The timing could not be better for the former Mississippi State QB, who’s playing in the final year of his contract. He’s going to get paid in a major way.

Even former Cowboys star Troy Aikman agrees with that.

“I say pay the man,” he wrote on Twitter.
