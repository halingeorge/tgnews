‘Jersey Shore’ Star Mike ‘The Situation’ Sorrentino And Wife Lauren Open Up About ‘Heart-Wrenching’ Miscarriage

18 Nov 2019, 14:37 by Aynslee Darmon

Mike “The Situation” Sorrentino and his wife Lauren are opening up about the heartbreaking miscarriage she suffered seven weeks into her first pregnancy.

The “Jersey Shore” stars stopped by “Good Morning America” talk show “Strahan, Sara & Keke” on Monday, revealing they conceived their first child together the night Sorrentino returned home after serving his eight-month prison sentence to tax evasion.

“The night he came home [from prison] we actually conceived,” Lauren, 34, explained. “And then at about six-and-a-half, seven weeks, I miscarried. It was heart-wrenching. When I found out we were pregnant, I felt like this is why we went through all these challenges for years and that this was our time and it was our blessing.”

She added, “It was hard. It was really difficult.”

And for why Lauren decided to share the news, she said, “I didn’t want to hold this in.”
