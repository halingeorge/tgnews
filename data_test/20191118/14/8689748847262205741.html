Fantana Flaunts Her Gorgeous Mother At The 2019 MTN 4Syte Music Video Awards- WATCH

18 Nov 2019, 14:35 by Ghbase New Writer

Fantana and her mother stole the show at the recently held 4Syte Music Video Awards which went down live at the Kempinski Hotel in Accra on Saturday evening.

For once, you might doubt if the woman is truly Fantana’s mum because of her relatively young age. What’s more, her gargantuan assets do not look like one who has given birth before. Man, she’s damn HOT!

The singer was full of smiles as she took her mum by the hand and strolled with her as they headed towards the part of the auditorium which is strictly demarcated for artists.

The beautiful woman is called Dorcas Nda-Affo Toffey aka Nzema Yaa Asantewaa. She is a businesswoman and a philanthropist.

Recall that recently, she gained widespread prominence when she was elected as NDC’s parliamentary candidate for the Jomoro constituency in the Western Region of Ghana.
