Jack reunion

Phil Parkinson confirms contact between Hibs and Sunderland over John Potter

18 Nov 2019, 14:19 by Alan Galindo

PHIL PARKINSON has confirmed that Hibs have been in contact with Sunderland over John Potter.

The ex-St Mirren and Dunfermline defender followed Jack Ross to the Stadium of Light and looks set to rejoin him at Easter Road.

Black Cats boss Parkinson said: "There has been contact between Hibs and Sunderland about John Potter.

"There is the obvious lure of going back to Scotland for John and if he does go, we wish him all the best."

The new Hibees gaffer is allowed to bring in his own staff after penning a three-and-a-half-year deal.

Former Scotland squad keeper Craig Samson is also likely to follow the pair to Leith as goalkeeper coach.

Ross will take charge of his first game on Saturday against high-flying Motherwell before a visit to his former club St Mirren next Tuesday.

The Hibees go into the fixture full of confidence after a 4-1 win over St Johnstone before the international break.
