Fire Emblem Heroes adds Guardians of Peace in latest update

Don't worry, it's just a name

18 Nov 2019, 14:30 by Chris Moyse

Nintendo has revealed the newest update for its popular mobile battle title Fire Emblem Heroes. The new update, which launches today on iOS and Android devices, welcomes four new heroes to the gigantic roster.

The "Guardians of Peace" update includes a quartet of characters from 2002 Game Boy Advance title Fire Emblem: The Binding Blade. These are cheerful dancer Larum, noble horseman Perceval, brave resistance fighter Echidna, and finally sharpshooting archer Igrene.
