Racing Podcast: Leading analyst Mark Howard joins the team to reflect on the November Meeting at Cheltenham

18 Nov 2019, 14:59 by Sporting Life

Leading analyst Mark Howard joins the team as they look back on the November Meeting at Cheltenham.

Defi Du Seuil’s Shloer Chase success comes under the microscope while novices Thyme Hill and Hang In There are also discussed.

There are also reflects on the recent wins of Douvan and Faugheen, plus Saldier’s emergence as a leading Champion Hurdle hope following his Morgiana triumph.
