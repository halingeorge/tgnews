Two workers from Bihar die in reactor blast at chemical factory in Hyderabad

18 Nov 2019, 14:19 by P Pavan

Hyderabad: Two workers hailing from Bihar died in a reactor blast in a chemical company in Hyderabad industrial area on Monday afternoon. Two others, including a woman, were injured in the mishap at Jeevika Life Sciences Private Limited company.

Police said the deceased were identified as Abarish and Anwar. About 20 persons were working near the reactor when it burst at around 3 in the afternoon. The roof of the company blew into pieces and scattered all around. People in the vicinity of 500 meters heard the deafening blast sound. Smoke engulfed the surroundings. Workers ran out of the premises in panic.

A worker said that the blast happened on the upper floors while they were working in the lower floors.

People gathered outside the factory and expressed their anger. They even demanded for the supervisor to show up and face the people.


State disaster response team and the fire services brought the fire under control after fighting for four hours.

Locals said they had lodged complaints with the Pollution Control Board several times about the pollution emanating from the company, but the complaints were ignored.


Jeedimetla police said they had seized the company following the blast. Reasons for the mishap are being ascertained.
