Cubicolor Announces New Album 'Hardly A Day, Hardly A Night'

Cubicolor has also released the first single "Points Beyond."

18 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Ryan Middleton

Tim Digby-Bell, Peter Kriek and Ariaan Olieroock, better known together as Cubicolor has announced their new album Hardly A Day, Hardly A Night. The album from the Dutch deep house trio will be released via Anjunadeep, where they released their debut album Brain Sugar in 2016.

In addition to the album announcement, they have shared the first single “Points Beyond,” which gives an indication of where this album is headed.
