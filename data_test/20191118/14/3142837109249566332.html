Centene Corp (NYSE:CNC) Expected to Post Quarterly Sales of $18.55 Billion

18 Nov 2019, 14:52 by Scott Moore

Analysts expect that Centene Corp (NYSE:CNC) will report $18.55 billion in sales for the current quarter, according to Zacks. Six analysts have made estimates for Centene’s earnings. The lowest sales estimate is $18.19 billion and the highest is $19.27 billion. Centene posted sales of $16.56 billion in the same quarter last year, which suggests a positive year over year growth rate of 12%. The business is expected to announce its next earnings results on Tuesday, February 4th.

On average, analysts expect that Centene will report full year sales of $74.41 billion for the current year, with estimates ranging from $73.96 billion to $75.05 billion. For the next financial year, analysts forecast that the business will report sales of $80.19 billion, with estimates ranging from $78.39 billion to $81.71 billion. Zacks’ sales averages are a mean average based on a survey of sell-side research analysts that follow Centene.

Centene Company Profile

Centene Corporation operates as a diversified and multi-national healthcare enterprise that provides programs and services to under-insured and uninsured individuals in the United States. The company's Managed Care segment offers health plan coverage to individuals through government subsidized programs, including Medicaid, the State children's health insurance program, long-term services and support, foster care, and medicare-medicaid plans, which covers dually eligible individuals, as well as aged, blind, or disabled programs.
