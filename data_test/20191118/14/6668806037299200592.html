“Success is not something I am consciously chasing, says Sara Ali Khan

18 Nov 2019, 14:05 by Poonam Jain

Sara Ali khan is having a gala time during her New York trip with friends. Her social media is proof of that. From sipping coffee to dining,  Sara seems to be making the most of each moment.

The actress, who made her debut with Abhishek Kapoor’s Kedarnath, has become a hot favourite in Bollywood. She believes that the key to success is self-belief and perseverance. Sara has about 15.2 million people following her on Instagram and fan clubs across social media. Despite huge success, Sara seems to be grounded. “I may probably have a flop. I’ll be honest with you – I don’t think that there will be any amount of success that will be enough. But then, I don’t think that success is something that I’m consciously chasing,” she was recently quoted said. But she also added that she is scared that all the success and stardom might just fade away one day.

Sara is currently on a break before she starts promotions for her upcoming film, #Aaj Kal with Kartik Aaryan.
