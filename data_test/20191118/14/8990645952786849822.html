Crime Stoppers Crime of the Week: Burglary at Mik’s Clothing

18 Nov 2019, 14:38 by Johnny Thrash

Wichita Falls Crime Stoppers is looking for information on a recent burglary of a building.

Unknown suspects broke into Mik’s Clothing at 2302 Brook Avenue at around 4:59 am on Friday, November 1. The suspects stole several clothing items, jewelry and Gucci sunglasses.

If you have any information on this or any other felony crime, call Crime Stoppers twenty-four hours a day at (940)322-9888, or if you’re calling long distance, call (800)322-9888. You never have to give your name, and if your information leads to the arrest and board approval, you could earn up to $1,000.
