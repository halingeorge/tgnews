When are Black Friday deals on Amazon dropping?

18 Nov 2019, 14:06

Amazon announced that 2019 will be their biggest ever Black Friday Sale so far, with thousands of products due to be discounted in the run up to Black Friday.

This year, Black Friday falls on Friday 29 November, which is next Friday.

Despite being two weeks away still, brands have already started offering Black Friday bargains and Amazon have a string of discounts on their website – although these are separate from the eight days of deals that the online retailer is running this year for Black Friday.

So when are the main Black Friday deals dropping on Amazon?

When are Black Friday deals dropping on Amazon?

Amazon first started offering Black Friday bargains to their customers nine years ago in 2010, so to celebrate their 10th Black Friday sale, this year the online retailer is promising: ‘tens of thousands of deals available to customers.’

The Amazon Black Friday sale will begin on Friday 22 November at 00.01 and run until 23:59 on Friday 29 November.

As well as discounting thousands of products, the website will also feature a Deals of the Day section that will round up the best bargains customers can score every day.

There will also be thousands of ‘Lightning Deals’ – products available at a discount, in limited quantities, for a short period of time.

Doug Gurr, VP, UK Country Manager at Amazon.co.uk said: ‘We know how important it is to help our customers save money where they can at this time of year, so we’re excited to be launching our tenth Black Friday Sale to UK customers in the lead-up to the festive season.’

‘With new deals announced every day across eight days, savvy shoppers will be able to discover great deals on a huge range of products and stock up on everything they need to make the most of their celebrations.’
