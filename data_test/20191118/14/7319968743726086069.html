Hot Irish barman singing The Rare Auld Times will make your day

18 Nov 2019, 14:43 by IrishCentral Staff

A pint in the center of Dublin and a gorgeous barman serenading you with a beautiful folk tune! The dream! 

The visitors to Dublin city center's Talbot Street pub, The Celt, got quite the treat when barman, Donncha, took the leap to entertain his punters with a beautiful rendition of The Rare Auld Times one afternoon. We think you'll agree it was worth the price of a pint. 

The punters at Celt certainly agreed and joined in on the chorus of the famous tune. The reaction to Donnacha's performance online was glowing, of course. 

Just some of the responses included "amazing got shivers listening to this,"  "love his voice and this song,"  "brilliant he would get a crowd going," and "he is a beautiful singer."
