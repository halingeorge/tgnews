NO-ASIS

Oasis reunion torpedoed by Liam Gallagher as he blasts ‘moron’ brother Noel for calling Scotland ‘third world country’

18 Nov 2019, 14:41 by Ewan Mowat

LIAM Gallagher appeared to rule out a potential Oasis reunion, hitting back at his brother Noel for his comments about Scotland.

He blasted his older sibling, calling him a "moron" three times in response to Noel using the same insult to describe him.

The High Flying Bird's frontman had previously said his younger brother's tweets were a 'nail in the coffin' of any Oasis reunion, but Liam has rubbished the claim.

And it seems like the Gallaghers' feud will continue to rumble on after Liam slated his "big head" brother.

Noel used an interview with The Big Issue to call out his former bandmate over rumours of a reunion.
