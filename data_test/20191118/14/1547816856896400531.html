Rashami Desai is one very strong contestant on Bigg Boss 13 and after Arhaan Khan’s exit from the show, people are mighty impressed with the way she is playing her game, despite many betrayals. Calling her a fighter and lauding her for her strength, fans trend ‘We Love Rashami Desai’

Bigg Boss 13 is turning out to be quite an interesting season, especially because of the varying personalities on the show this time around. Rashami Desai has had a rollercoaster ride full of ups and downs on the show, especially with her unending fights with Sidharth Shukla.
