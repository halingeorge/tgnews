Detour Gold (TSE:DGC) PT Raised to C$29.00

18 Nov 2019, 14:50 by Doug Wharley

Detour Gold (TSE:DGC) had its price target lifted by investment analysts at TD Securities from C$28.00 to C$29.00 in a research report issued to clients and investors on Monday, BayStreet.CA reports. The firm presently has a “buy” rating on the mining company’s stock. TD Securities’ price objective would indicate a potential upside of 28.04% from the stock’s current price.

Several other equities analysts have also commented on DGC. Barclays increased their price objective on shares of Detour Gold from C$26.00 to C$29.00 in a research report on Friday, August 16th. BMO Capital Markets increased their price objective on shares of Detour Gold from C$25.00 to C$37.00 in a research report on Wednesday, September 11th. Eight Capital lowered shares of Detour Gold from a “buy” rating to a “neutral” rating and increased their price objective for the company from C$16.00 to C$21.00 in a research report on Thursday, August 8th. Raymond James increased their price objective on shares of Detour Gold from C$19.00 to C$22.00 in a research report on Thursday, August 1st. Finally, National Bank Financial lowered their price objective on shares of Detour Gold from C$28.00 to C$27.00 and set an “outperform” rating for the company in a research report on Monday. Two investment analysts have rated the stock with a hold rating and five have assigned a buy rating to the company’s stock. Detour Gold has a consensus rating of “Buy” and a consensus target price of C$26.45.

DGC stock traded up C$1.26 during trading on Monday, hitting C$22.65. 770,624 shares of the company’s stock traded hands, compared to its average volume of 839,942. Detour Gold has a 52 week low of C$9.55 and a 52 week high of C$25.45. The company has a debt-to-equity ratio of 10.42, a quick ratio of 1.91 and a current ratio of 2.71. The business has a fifty day simple moving average of C$20.23 and a 200 day simple moving average of C$18.38. The stock has a market capitalization of $3.77 billion and a price-to-earnings ratio of 118.59.

About Detour Gold

Detour Gold Corporation, a gold mining company, engages in the acquisition, exploration, development, and operation of mineral properties in Canada. It primarily explores for gold. The company's primary asset is the Detour Lake property consisting of a contiguous block of mining claims and leases totaling 646 square kilometers located in the District of Cochrane.
