New gymnasium for Faith Lutheran School and Church expected to be completed by early 2020

18 Nov 2019, 14:36 by Matt Gunn

KIRKSVILLE, Mo. — Construction of the brand-new gymnasium at Faith Lutheran School and Church is now fully underway.

This week workers from Sparks Construction are expected to pour the foundation of the new gym with the goal of being able to start putting up walls for the building next week.

The school needed a new gymnasium because the current facility has been outgrown by the number of students that currently attend the school.

The school started 14 years ago with only 36 students, now the school population has increased to 140 students.

Initially, the gym was set to be completed by this month or next month, however, due to weather delays the completion date for the new gym is February 1, 2020, according to Faith Lutheran School and Church.

Students are excited about the new gym and can’t wait for it to open in the coming months.
