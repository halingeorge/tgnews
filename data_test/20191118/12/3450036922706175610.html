No charges filed against man involved in Ottumwa standoff

18 Nov 2019, 12:22 by Beth Waldon

OTTUMWA, Iowa — No charges have been filed against an armed man who engaged in an hour-long standoff Friday with Ottumwa authorities.

Just before 4 p.m. Friday, Ottumwa police officers received several reports of a man acting strangely near Christ’s Church, just south of Ottumwa.

Investigators say the suspect was holding a sharp object and threatened suicide. Police later determined that the sharp object was a piece of glass.

Officers told him to drop the weapon, but he refused.

The man surrendered about an hour after the incident began. He was later taken to the hospital for evaluation.

Because the man had shown signs of mental illness, Ottumwa Police Lt. Chad Farrington said the department won’t be pursuing charges against him.

His name is not being released.
