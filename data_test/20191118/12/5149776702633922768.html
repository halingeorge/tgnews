Online booze auction is selling off high-end bottles of whisky and gin from as little as £1

It could be a cheaper way to stock up in time for Christmas

18 Nov 2019, 12:23 by John Scheerhout, Hayley Watson

It's about that time of year when people are starting to think about stocking up the bar for Christmas.

If your tipple of choice is whisky or gin, there is a sneaky way you could get your hands on some high-end bottles for less.

An auction house is selling off a huge lot of premium spirits including whisky, vodka and gin - and bidding starts at just £1.

Many of the bottles would usually retail for between £30 and £50, says the MEN .

Stockport-based auction house Simon Charles launched the online booze auction last week.

However some items have attracted no bids at all, meaning they could go for as little as a quid.

The lot has come from a hospitality company which had intended the spirits for Christmas functions, weddings and corporate dos.

Auctioneer Ed Railton said: "It's a buyers' market. Everything will be coming in at much lower than you would expect to pay at retail, which is great for people tightening their belts at this time of year.

"For the price of a normal supermarket midrange, you could get something a little bit more special."

Among the bottles on offer are gins and whiskies from small independent distilleries some of which are limited editions and are no longer being produced, according to Ed.

While bidding has started on many of the items, many are still available for the opening bid of £1.

These include 500ml bottles of Kreator Jin by Mackmyra - which retail at around £30 - and 20cl mini bottles of Darnley's Small Batch London Dry Gin, which typically cost £6.

Across the rest of the lot, bottle of 12-year-old Johnnie Walker Black Label Whisky, which retails at around £30, is available for as little as £3.

While a boxed edition of Jameson's Select Reserve Single Distillery Black Barrel, which is sold for £59 online, is available for just £12.

Premium mixers could also be snapped up for just £1 including 24-packs of 200ml Fever-Tree Premium Ginger Ale, and 8-packs of 500ml Fentiman's Botanically Brewed Connoisseurs Tonic Water.

Branded glassware sets and serving bottles are also available for a pound.

Bids are being taken on the lots until 8pm on Saturday 23 November.
