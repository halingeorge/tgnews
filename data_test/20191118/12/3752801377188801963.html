Police prevent looting at Mahikeng mall following blaze

18 Nov 2019, 12:11 by Ntwaagae Seleka

North West police prevented a crowd of people from looting shops in Mahikeng on Monday morning, following a fire at a mall.

Police spokesperson Brigadier Sabata Mokgwabone said they had received a call that the group wanted to loot after one of the shops at a complex went up in flames around 06:00 on Monday.Â
