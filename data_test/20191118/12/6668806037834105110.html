First poster out: Aamir Khan introduces himself as Laal Singh Chaddha

18 Nov 2019, 12:37 by Vedanshi Pathak

Aamir Khan and Kareena Kapoor Khan have teamed up yet again to captivate the audience together on the big screen. This time around, they star together in Laal Singh Chaddha which is the official Hindi remake of the iconic Tom Hank starrer Forrest Gump. Aamir and Kareena started shooting for the film in Chandigarh a few days back and now the makers have dropped in the first poster from the film. Aamir took to Instagram to reveal his character from the film today. The actor has transformed his looks yet again for his character. Aamir is seen as a Sikh in the poster with a long beard and turban. The actor took to Instagram to share the first look of his character and wrote, “Sat Sri Akaal ji, myself Laal...Laal Singh Chaddha.”

The film hits the theatres on Christmas next year. Keep watching this space for more on Bollywood.
