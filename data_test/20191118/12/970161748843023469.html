Hero schoolgirl, 13, repeatedly stabbed with machete while protecting nephew

18 Nov 2019, 12:08

A teenager was seriously injured when she ‘threw her whole self’ over her 11-month-old nephew after they were attacked by machete-wielding thugs.

The 13-year-old girl is in a critical condition after being stabbed at a house in Lisnaskea, Co Fermanagh, Northern Ireland, at around 9pm on Saturday.

A video posted online is believed to have shown the girl’s injuries, which are said to have included a deep wound down her back.

Family member Elizabeth Joyce, was also treated for stab wounds to her leg but has since been discharged from hospital, said the girl was a hero and she was the reason the boy was unharmed.

Ms Joyce told the BBC: ‘The baby was unharmed because his auntie is a hero.

‘She’s 13 years old and she threw her whole self over that baby, and she saved his life. She is a hero.

‘It is something that we will never get over.’

Ms Joyce said she hit one of the attackers with a photo frame to defend herself.

She added: ‘I had to get off the chair because I knew I’d be sliced.

‘I picked up my frame with my kids’ photo on it. I hit one of them at the door to get him away from her.’

The teenage girl sustained serious stab wounds and was taken to hospital where she remains in a critical but stable condition.

Police have launched an attempted murder investigation.

The girl’s family are from the Travelling community and police believe the attack could be in relation to a dispute.

PSNI Detective Sergeant Keith Monaghan said: ‘This must have been a terrifying ordeal for those involved and we are determined to find the men responsible.

‘We have several lines of enquiry which we are progressing at this time but I would ask anyone with information or who noticed any suspicious activity in the area to contact detectives in Enniskillen and pass that information on.”

Alliance Fermanagh representative Matthew Beaumont said whoever carried out an attack in Lisnaskea needs to be caught quickly.

‘This attack in Lisnaskea, carried out by men carrying machetes, is to be condemned in the strongest possible terms. Whoever was responsible for this horrific attack must be caught quickly and face justice.”

‘I hope the two females injured make a full and speedy recovery. This must have been a terrifying experience for them.’
