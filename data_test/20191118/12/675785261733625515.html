Pep Guardiola's agent responds to Bayern Munich return reports

Manchester City boss Guardiola has been linked with a return to Bayern but agent Josep Maria Orobitg has played down reports he could go back to the Allianz Arena

18 Nov 2019, 12:53 by James Whaling, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/james-whaling/

Pep Guardiola's agent Josep Maria Orobitg has played down reports the Manchester City boss could return to Bayern Munich as Niko Kovac's successor.

Kovac was relieved of his duties after a 5-1 humbling by Eintracht Frankfurt and has been replaced by coach Hansi Flick for the remainder of 2019 as Bayern source a long-term successor.

Reports in Germany had suggested Guardiola could be in line for a return to the Allianz Arena - but Orobitg insists the Spaniard is happy at the Etihad.

"In football, nothing is certain," Orobitg told Goal.

"I spoke with Pep last Wednesday, and everything is the same: He feels good at Manchester City and also in the city of Manchester, and he has a contract until June 30, 2021.

"Nobody but the press contacted me about the future Pep."

Arsene Wenger held talks with Bayern chief Karl-Heinz Rumenigge but the duo could not strike a deal and the Frenchman has now taken on a new role at FIFA.

Erik ten Hag, Thomas Tuchel and Jose Mourinho have also been linked with the post as Bayern look to cut the four-point gap to Bundesliga leaders Borussia Monchengladbach.

Guardiola spent three years as Bayern boss between 2013 and 2016 - winning three Bundesliga titles and two German Cups.

He has claimed two Premier League titles so far at Manchester City along with two League Cups and the FA Cup as the Blues completed the domestic treble last term.

But defeat at Anfield prior to the international break saw them slip nine points off the title pace behind table-topping Liverpool .
