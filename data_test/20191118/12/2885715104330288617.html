Brazil win U17 World Cup title after late comeback against Mexico

Just three days after they recovered from being 2-0 down to beat France 3-2 in the semi-finals, Brazil scored two goals in the last 10 minutes to overcome Mexico to lift their fourth FIFA U17 World Cup.

18 Nov 2019, 12:24 by Reuters

Brazil staged a remarkable comeback for the second time in a week on Sunday to beat Mexico 2-1 in front of their own fans and win the Under-17 World Cup for a fourth time.
