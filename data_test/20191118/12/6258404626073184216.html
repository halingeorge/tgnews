Google Black Friday Deals Include Big Discounts On Pixel 4 And Pixel 3a

18 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Paul Lilly

If going by the calendar, Black Friday is about a week and a half away. The deals have already started to trickle out, and we suspect as Thursday, November 28 gets even closer in view, more bargains will make early debuts. In the meantime, companies have started previewing upcoming Black Friday discounts, including Google, which will be offering a range of gadgets on sale.

One of the highlights is a $200 discount on unlocked Pixel 4 and Pixel 4 XL models. The slashed pricing will actually go into effect on Sunday, November 24, bringing the cost of entry down from $799 to $599 for the Pixel 4, and from $899 to $699 for the Pixel 4 XL. This presumably will apply to all three color options, those being Clearly White, Just Black, and Oh So Orange.

The other early bargain applies to the Pixel Slate. Users will be able to save up to $648 on the Pixel Slate and select accessories. Starting on Sunday, November 24, it looks like the baseline model will drop in price from $799 to $449. That's the version with an 8th generation Intel Core m3-8100Y processor, 8GB of RAM, and 64GB of storage. The more powerful configurations will likely drop in price as well.

As for deals that will go live on Thursday, November 28, here is what Google is previewing...

  • Stadia Premiere Edition + Nest Wifi: $398 (save $80)
  • Nest Hello Doorbell: $149 (save $80)
  • Nest Hub Max: $199 (save $30)
  • Nest Mini (2nd gen): $29 (save $20)
  • Nest Cam Outdoor: $159 (save $40, and up to $522 on packages)
  • Nest Wifi Router: $229 (save $40)
  • Nest Learning Thermostat: $179 (save $70)
  • Pixel 3a: $299 (save $100)
  • Nest Hub: $79 (save $50)
  • Nest Protect: $99 (save $20)

Incidentally, you don't have to wait until Black Friday to pick up a Pixel 3a at a discount. It's available at Amazon for $299 right now, while the Pixel 4 XL is discounted to $399. The sale price applies to all three color options.
