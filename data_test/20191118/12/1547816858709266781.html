Bigg Boss 13: Khesari Lal Yadav's Makeover Prompts Sidharth Shukla And Himanshi Khurrana To Become Bullies

18 Nov 2019, 12:02 by Gretel Sequeira

Bigg Boss 13 contestant Khesari Lal Yadav is playing his game righteously on the show. As the actor gets a new avatar with his latest hairstyle, Yadav gets ragged by contestants Sidharth Shukla, Himanshi Khurrana, and Hindustani Bhau while others enjoy the show having a laugh riot

The entry of the wild card contestants in the Bigg Boss 13 house has added on the fun and entertainment in the controversial house. While some contestants were at loggerheads with each other thanks to the nasty fights and negativity in the house, the contestants are now often seen having a gala time laughing and joking around. As housemate Khesari Lal Yadav gets a new avatar, the actor gets ragged for his look by other contestants in the house, while others have a laugh riot.

Today, sharing a glimpse from the upcoming show tonight, Colors TV took to their Twitter handle to share a promo video featuring contestant Khesari Lal Yadav being ragged by other locked up members of the house. In the video, Khesari Lal Yadav becomes a victim of ragging for his new hair colour and style by Sidharth Shukla, Himanshi Khurrana and Hindustani Bhau. The video shared features Shukla, Hindustani Bhau and Himanshi enjoy ragging Yadav over forgetting his wife as soon as he entered the show, while Shefali, Devoleena and others enjoy the show.
