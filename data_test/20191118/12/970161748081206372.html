Spoilers: Lydia discovers she could be seriously ill in Emmerdale tonight

18 Nov 2019, 12:12

Lydia Hart (Karen Blick) has been through a lot over the past couple of months in Emmerdale, but she’s set for more heartache and devastation in tonight’s episode when she not only comes face-to-face with her long-lost mum Agatha, but she also learns some heart-breaking news.

After all the torment she’s caused Lydia, Mandy (Lisa Riley) is determined to make amends tonight, and thus she sets out to speak to Lydia’s sister, Beth, once more.

However, as Mandy arrives at Beth’s place, she comes face-to-face Agatha —aka Lydia’s mum. Mandy’s confused, as she believed Agatha to be dead — as did Lydia for that matter.

Agatha asks Mandy to take her to see Lydia and, after some persuasion, Mandy agrees to do so. Later, they arrive at the Dingles’ home — and Lydia is shocked when she sets her sights upon her mum.
