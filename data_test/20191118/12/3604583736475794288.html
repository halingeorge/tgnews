Driver, occupants allegedly flee collision in Ballarat East

18 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Alex Ford

Police are investigating after the occupants of one car involved in a serious collision in Ballarat East all allegedly fled the scene.

The incident occurred about 7.45pm on Monday, and left one car, a dark Citroen hatchback that is believed to have been stolen, on its roof.

The other car, a blue Ford sedan towing a trailer, was badly damaged.

No one was injured in the crash, and Ambulance Victoria confirmed no patients were treated.

The intersection at the corner of Stawell Street and Charlesworth Street was blocked by police tape as investigators established a crime scene.
