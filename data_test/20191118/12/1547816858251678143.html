Good Newwz Trailer Celeb Reaction: Varun Dhawan, Taapsee Laud Akshay Kumar-Kareena Kapoor, Diljit Dosanjh-Kiara Advani Starrer

18 Nov 2019, 12:35 by Divya Ramnani

Varun Dhawan, Taapsee Pannu and other Bollywood celebs can’t stop praising the crazy trailer of Good Newwz starring Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor, Diljit Dosanjh and Kiara Advani

After a long wait and some back-to-back quirky posters, the makers of Good Newwz have finally unveiled its trailer and it has sent fans into a tizzy. Well, what else can one expect when there’s Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Diljit Dosanjh and Kiara Advani in one frame? The trailer is a laugh riot with some entertaining performances by the entire cast and, as promised, it looks like they have surely managed to deliver the biggest goof-up of the year. Other than the netizens, a lot of celebrities like Varun Dhawan, Taapsee Pannu among others, too, have approved of this madness. (ALSO READ - Good Newwz Trailer Launch: Karan Johar Reveals, "I Went To Akshay Kumar For Some Other Big Film, He Told Me 'Usko Side Mein Rakhte Hai, Yeh Banaate Hai'")

Varun Dhawan lauded the film's plot and cast by writing, "This looks like a good film. What a superb cast can’t wait. Great job raj and congratulations well done @ShashankKhaitan." An excited Riteish Deshmukh, on the other hand, praised the entire cast. He tweeted, "Outtttttstanding trailer !!!!!!!! sundiii ...@akshaykumar this will end 2019 with a bang & being 2020 with a bigger bang- You are going to deliver a Huge Blockbuster #GoodNewwzTrailer @karanjohar amazing concept - fabulous @diljitdosanjh @advani_kiara #Kareena you guys rocked it." Taapsee Pannu, too, found it funny. "This is so damn funny! I think this is the closest to your real funny self I have seen since years sir! Really looking forward for this one #GoodNewwzTrailer."
