Lucky punter turns £5 into £17,000 after remarkable 18-match bet

One Ladbrokes customer made a fortune off over a £5m punt with 18 selections across the international weekend including Scotland, Wales, Spain and Italy

18 Nov 2019, 12:17 by Sam Meade

One lucky Ladbrokes punter made more than £17,000 off a £5 bet at the weekend.

The customer managed to correctly predict the outcome of every Euro 2020 qualifier on Friday and Saturday.

The £5 18-fold was turned into £12,868.25, but he activated Labrokes Odds Boost feature which thumped his winnings up to an incredible £17,181.19.

Steven Davis will certainly receive plenty of thanks from the punter who backed Northern Ireland to get a draw against the Netherlands at Windsor Park.

The Rangers midfielder had the perfect opportunity to give the hosts the lead from 12 yards but blazed over.

Alex Apati of Ladbrokes said: "Our plucky punter has had a fantastic weekend on the acca front and things couldn't have been easier in truth."

He added: "Friday night's games went according to plan with all eight of his selections winning without conceding a goal between them, while things went almost as swimmingly in the remain 10 fixtures on Saturday for a sensational bet during the international break."

The bet featured 17 win selections with Wales and Scotland backed to win their visits to Azerbaijan and Cyprus respectively.
