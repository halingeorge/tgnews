From staff discount to flexible hours - Jobs being advertised at the Metrocentre right now

You can apply for a range of full-time and part-time jobs at the Gateshead shopping centre

18 Nov 2019, 12:46 by Kali Lindsay

If you are looking for a new job, there are a number of opportunities at the Metrocentre.

From sales assistants, store managers and a head chef, there a various opportunities at the Gateshead shopping centre.

Jobs are available at Paperchase, Toby Carvery, Swarovski and Guess.

These are some of the positions currently being advertised:

Assistant store manager - Ann Summers

You could earn up to £21,000 and receive a 50% staff discount.

The firm is looking for a manager to motivate their team and ensure outstanding levels of customer service.

They are looking for someone who is passionate about the Ann Summers brand, experienced in managing a similar turnover store and who is able to demonstrate results.

You will be given two weeks training with an expert academy development manager who will ensure you will delight customers and achieve the best possible results.

To apply visit here

Supervisor - Paperchase

You will be responsible for leading the team on a part-time basis.

The successful applicant will have previous line management experience, high levels of enthusiasm, the ability to excel in a fast paced environment and be able to work independently.

You will be given training and guidance from your line manage to support your development.

To apply visit here

Supervisor - Inglot Cosmetics

The store is a family run, Irish business that has 21 stores across the Republic of Ireland, Northern Ireland and UK, with more than 200 employees.

On a 25-hour contract, the company is looking for a supervisor to help grow the business at the Metrocentre.

They are looking for someone who can being leadership to the store and inspire all employees to deliver exceptional service that will increase sales.

Head chef - Toby Carvery

You will be responsible for overseeing food ordering, food preparation, managing the team, stock control and ensuring all health and hygiene regulations are met.

As part of the role, you will train, motivate and inspire the team to deliver an efficient food operation and be responsible for the kitchen's financial performance.

The salary is £29,450 per year and you will get 33% off at all Mitchells and Butlers business when you eat.

Seasonal sales consultant - Swarovski

Duties for the seasonal sales consultant role at Swarovski include, but not limited to: support the store at the busiest time of the year, deliver excellent customer services, and to be an ambassador of the brand during peak time.

This role is for a 12-hour contract on a temporary basis.

To apply for this role, hand your CV in to the Swarovski store located in lower green mall.

Sales assistant - Be More Geek!

A part-time vacancy is being advertised at the shop which specialises in comic merchandise, games and props.

You will be greeting customers, be involved in stock control and assist shoppers to find products they are looking for.

As part of the role, you will be stocking shelves, answering queries and reporting discrepancies and problems to a supervisor.

You will have excellent customer service skills, friendly personality, comfortable working with others and members of the public.

To apply please email CV and covering letter to suzanne@bemoregeek.co.uk.

Applicants must be willing to work flexibly during retail hours, with evenings, weekends and holiday hours likely.
