Bollywood stars offer last respects to Manish Malhotra’s father

18 Nov 2019, 12:18 by Kajol Mathur

Bollywood’s favourite designer Manish Malhotra’s father passed away on Monday morning. He was in his early 90s and has been keeping unwell for quite some time. Karan Johar, Shabana Azmi, Sophie Choudry,  Boney Kapoor, Urmila Matondkar, David Dhawan, Punit Malhotra and several other stars attended the funeral and offered their support.
