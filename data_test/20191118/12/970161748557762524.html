Spoilers: Jacob reunites with Maya as she's released from prison in Emmerdale?

18 Nov 2019, 12:14

David Metcalfe (Matthew Wolfenden) and Leyla Harding (Roxy Shahidi) couldn’t be happier in Emmerdale about the fact that Jacob Gallagher (Joe Warren-Plant) has finally begun to move on following his ordeal at the hands of Maya Stepney (Louisa Clein). However, the pair are set to get some incredibly upsetting news tonight — and it’s something that could set the young man’s progress back.

Yes, Maya groomed and sexually abused Jacob for the majority of a year. The truth came out earlier this year when — following a night out with her mates — Priya (Fiona Wade) saw Jacob and Maya kissing.

She wasted little time in informing Leyla and Tracy (Amy Walsh) — both of whom subsequently sought revenge.

After everything that transpired on the night in question, Jacob and Maya fled the village, but David ultimately set a trap and Maya returned, before being apprehended by the police.
