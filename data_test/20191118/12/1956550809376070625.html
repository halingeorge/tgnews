Singer-songwriter Prateek Kuhad coming to Dubai

He’ll be playing at The Rotunda at Caesars Palace Bluewaters Dubai

18 Nov 2019, 12:04 by Amy Mathieson

Fans of singer-songwriter Prateek Kuhad – make a date in your calendar as the talented performer is coming to Dubai in 2020.

The popular Indian artist will be performing in Dubai for the first time, and will be taking to the stage at The Rotunda at Caesars Palace Bluewaters Dubai on Friday January 24, 2020.

Tickets cost Dhs150 and go on sale this Thursday (November 21) on www.ticketmaster.ae and ae.bookmyshow.com.

Kuhad became one of Spotify India’s most-streamed artists when the service launched in 2019, and his latest EP cold/mess came out at number one. The star has also had a fantastic year in America with a run of shows in New York.

The singer is now in the middle of a world tour – playing 30 shows across America, Canada, the UK, France, Germany, Belgium, The Netherlands, Spain and India.

Rock The Rotunda aims to bring a series of live music and comedy shows to the region in 2020, all in an intimate setting. The venue is at Caesars Palace Bluewaters Dubai and has 360-degree views for shows. Previous shows here have included circus spectacular WOW, Tom Odell and Tenors of Rock. It’s also hosted boxing matches.

Growing up in Jaipur, Kuhad started learning guitar at school, but it didn’t go as planned. However, he was determined to make it as a musician and immersed himself in music.

His first full-length album, In Tokens and Charms, was released in 2015 and was an instant hit. It earned him an MTV Europe Music Award, Indie Album of the Year honors from iTunes, and the title of Best Pop Artist at the Radio City Freedom Awards, while the album’s track, “Oh Love,” won the International Songwriting Competition – a prize won by names including Gotye and Passenger in the past.

Fancy checking him out? Make sure to book your tickets this week.
