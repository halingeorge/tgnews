We’re crying over who went home from Dancing With The Stars season 28 this week.

The seventh elimination of the competition happened just moments ago and was determined from the judges’ scores, and the weekly live-viewing audience vote tonight, following a ton of dances from the semi-finalists.

Fans were able to vote online at ABC.com, on the ABC app, and through text.

The five remaining celebs left in the competition are Lauren Alaina, Ally Brooke, Hannah Brown, Kel Mitchell, and James Van Der Beek.
