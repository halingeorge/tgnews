5 snaps of the man in Masasa Mbangeni’s life

18 Nov 2019, 12:29

Phakamani Mvelashe

Cape Town - Actress Masasa Mbangeni is head over heels in love with her man.

The former Scandal! actress is usually private about her personal life but has recently taken to sharing pictures of the man in her life on social media.

Although she hasn’t revealed his name or tagged him in any of her pictures, she has shared some heartfelt messages to him on her social media pages.

Masasa has been in the entertainment industry for a number of years and has acted in shows such as Scandal!, Harvest and Machine Gun Preacher.

She is also a two-time SA Film and Television Award and Royalty Soapie Award winner.
