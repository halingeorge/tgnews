The Walking Dead: Gamma Unmasks, Reveals Name in Mid-Season Finale Clip

18 Nov 2019, 12:11 by Sam Stone

The Walking Dead Season 10 has been rife with intrigue and betrayal as tensions between the rival survivor factions of Alexandria and the Whisperers has come to a head.

Now, AMC has released a pivotal clip from the midseason finale featuring a conversation between Aaron and Gamma. The Whisperer leader Alpha had tasked Gamma with meeting Aaron regularly to gain information about Hilltop and Alexandra. Instead, Aaron helps uncover Alpha's deception and manipulation, leading Gamma to finally remove her flesh mask and show him her true face.
