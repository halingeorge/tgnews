Sky Broadband went down today as huge outage wiped out customer's internet

18 Nov 2019, 12:28

A huge outage brought down Sky Broadband customers’ internet this morning.

Problems started at about 6am this morning and have left huge numbers of people unable to log on.

‘We are aware of an issue affecting some of our customers broadband across parts of the UK, we’re sorry for this,’ Sky said on Twitter.

‘We have the right people working hard to fix this. Keep up to date on the My Sky app or our service status page.’

On the website Down Detactor, a man from Deal, Kent, wrote: ‘I am unable to get on certain sites, or some are taking hours to load.

‘For example, it took me (literally) 30 minutes to log into my email this morning before the “error” pages came up.

‘I’ve been able to get on websites one minute, but then I’m unable to access them the next.

‘Hopefully this issue will be resolved sooner rather than later.’

At abut half past twelve, Sky said the problem was fixed and added: ‘Our broadband service is back up and running, and we’re sorry for the disruption caused to some of our customers this morning.’
