Jay-Z Stunned After Watching Yo Gotti Lose $500,000 in Blackjack

18 Nov 2019, 12:09 by VladTV

Yo Gotti indulged in a high-stakes blackjack game at Jay-Z's Shawn Carter Charity Gala, which didn't turn out in his favor. 

A video quickly spread across social media, which showed Jay-Z looking on in shock as Yo Gotti lost $500,000 in the game after the dealer had 21. Jay exclaimed, "Oh My Goodness," while walking away from the table with his hands in the air.
