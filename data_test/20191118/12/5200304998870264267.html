Box Office: ‘Marjaavaan’ gives Sidharth Malhotra highest opening grosser

18 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Raj Baddhan

Sidharth Malhotra’s recently released ‘Marjaavaan’ has surpassed ‘Student Of The Year’, ‘Kapoor and Sons’ and ‘Jabariya Jodi’ in terms of opening business.

The film, which releases on 15th November 2019, grossed Rs. 24.42 crores in the opening weekend. Produced by Bhushan Kumar’s T-Series, the film has given Malhotra’s highest opening grosser ever.

Directed by Milap Zaveri, the film also stars Tara Sutaria, Riteish Deshmukh and Rakul Preet Singh. The movie has received rave reviews and strong word of mouth has pushed many cine-goers thronging to watch it. The action, songs, and dialogues have been a huge hit amongst people.

Social media is abuzz praising the film, audiences are loving the new avatar which Malhotra has donned. The versatile actor has shed his chocolate boy image and has got an angry young man back into Bollywood.

‘Marjaavaan’ has all the right ingredients which make it the biggest potboiler of 2019.
