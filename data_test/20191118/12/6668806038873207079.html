Bala might enter the Rs 100 crore club this week

18 Nov 2019, 12:25 by Vedanshi Pathak

Directed by Amar Kaushik, the Ayushamann Khurrana starrer Bala has impressed the critics and the audience alike. The film revolves around Bala (Ayushmann Khurrana) and the problems he faces in his love life because of premature balding. The film also stars Yami Gautam and Bhumi Pednekar. The film has been doing well at the box-office and earned well during its second weekend at the cash counters. During its first week, the film made Rs 72.24 crore followed by Rs 3.7 crore on its second Friday, Rs 6.73 on Saturday and Rs 8.01 crore on Sunday making the grand total Rs 90.74 crore. 

The film is expected to cross the Rs 100 crore mark this week and with it Ayushmann Khurrana will have yet another blockbuster added to his long list of hits. Keep watching this space for more.
