Nominations open for Glass Slipper Awards which recognise inspirational women in the region

You still have time to nominate an incredible North East woman for a Glass Slipper Award

18 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Katie Collings

Do you know a woman whose remarkable achievements deserve to be recognised?

Then there is still time to nominate them for a special award - but the deadline is soon.

The annual Glass Slipper Awards, held by the Sunshine Fund, pay tribute to inspirational women from our region in seven different categories such as Woman of the Year, Young Achiever and Outstanding Business Leader.

The glittering ceremony will take place at the Hilton Newcastle Gateshead early next year but the deadline to put someone forward is Friday December 6.

The seven categories are:

Remarkable Woman

Woman in the Community  Sponsored by Reach PLC

Unsung Hero  Sponsored by Ant & Dec

Outstanding Business Leader

North-East Champion Sponsored by Your Homes Newcastle

Young Achiever  Sponsored by Sunderland University

Lifetime Achievement Award

The Woman in the Community award will be a public's choice award and readers can vote for their winner.

Finalist short listing will take place in January 2020, successful finalists and their nominators will be contacted following the selection.

Among the winners in 2019's ceremony was Gemma Lowery who was honoured in the Remarkable Woman category for the work she has done since losing her son Bradley to cancer in 2017.

Professor Alison Murdoch was also honoured with the lifetime achievement award for her work as head of Newcastle Fertility Centre at the Centre for Life where she's been instrumental in helping couples become families for decades.

She is an internationally-recognised scientist and clinician who has been at the forefront of developments in genetics and was among the first people in the UK to be legally allowed to clone human embryos.

Rebecca Jama, from the Sunshine Fund, said: "Every year we are astounded by the calibre of women that are nominated for our Glass Slipper Awards, it makes me so proud to be part of such an inspiring region.

"It's been wonderful to watch the event grow year on year and I can't wait for our 2020 awards!"

Tickets and tables for the event can be bought at £50 per ticket/£500 per table of 10.
