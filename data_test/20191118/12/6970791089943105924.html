50 Cent Reveals Truth Behind His Instagram Page Getting Deleted: “You Can’t Just Make Some S**t Up”

18 Nov 2019, 12:41 by Angie Dare

New York rapper 50 Cent is keeping it 100. The hip-hop veteran has come forward to address his Instagram page suddenly getting deleted.

Last night, 50 denied reports claiming IG shut him down and admitted he intentionally turned off the account.
