(Video) Jurgen Klopp celebrating appears in Jeremy Corbyn’s Labour railways promise

18 Nov 2019, 12:37 by Jordan Chamberlain

Jeremy Corbyn posted a video on Twitter this morning that shows a Labour promise of better railways should his party win the upcoming general election…

In it, a famous video of Jurgen Klopp fist-pumping has been used to show how delight in ‘much cheaper’ fares for all – as Corbyn promises to run the railways ‘for people, not profit’.

Having regularly been delayed on incredibly expensive trains going up and down the country to follow football – this seems like a great idea!

You can vote in the general election on December 12.
