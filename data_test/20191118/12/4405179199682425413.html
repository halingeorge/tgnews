Three's cheap Black Friday iPhone 11 deal: Unlimited data, just £49/mo

Three's offering unlimited data on the iPhone 11 for just £49 per month. It's one of the best plans around for uncapped speeds.

18 Nov 2019, 12:03 by Somrata Sarkar

If you're looking for unlimited data on an iPhone 11 deal it's a good time to check out Three's Black Friday offers, which have launched well ahead of 29 November, the official Black Friday date. The mobile network is offering the iPhone 11 for £49 per month with a £49 upfront cost –  a discount from the usual rate of £55 per month, saving you £144.

It's the best price we're seeing for unlimited data at the moment without any speed restrictions. Comparable unlimited plans are also available from Vodafone, if you go to Phones.co.uk or Mobiles.co.uk, but with either a higher monthly or upfront cost.
