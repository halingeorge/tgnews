Marjaavaan earns fairly well at the box-office during its first weekend

18 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Vedanshi Pathak

Sidharth Malhotra and Tara Sutaria have teamed up for the first time in Marjaavaan.  The film is a massy actioner directed by Milap Zaveri. The film hit the theatres last Friday and had a decent first weekend at the box-office. On its opening day, the film made Rs 7.03 crore, followed by Rs 7.21 crore on Saturday and Rs 10.18 crore on Sunday. It has earned Rs 24.42 crore overall so far.

Though the film has received mixed reviews from the critics, the box-office numbers indicate that the single-screen audience, in particular, has taken to the film.
