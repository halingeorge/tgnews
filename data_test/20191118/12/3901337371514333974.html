You Choose Your Team Of The Week

18 Nov 2019, 12:41 by Soccer Laduma

The international break offered a mixed bag of results for Bafana Bafana. Many fans were cautiously confident that our national side could gain a clean sweep of victories over Ghana and Sudan, considering our impressive display against a strong Mali outfit during the last international break.

Although the boys lost in Ghana, a much-needed 1-0 victory against Sudan followed at the Orlando Stadium on Sunday.

Selections are based on the performances of players in fixtures that have taken place in the past weekend's football action. 

With that brief summary of the weekend’s results, have a go at selecting your Team of the Week and see how close you get to the official Soccer Laduma selection.
