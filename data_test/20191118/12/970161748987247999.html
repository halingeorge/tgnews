When is Frozen 2 released in the UK and do Anna and Elsa return?

18 Nov 2019, 12:41

Stars and their kids proved the cold never bothered them anyway when they turned out for the premiere of Frozen 2 in London last weekend – meaning that it’s not long before the public will get to see the second installment of the frosty, fantasy Disney adventure.

Lily Allen, Holly Willoughby and Stacey Soloman were among the famous faces to grace the icy carpet on Sunday to celebrate the new movie, which features new songs and some new characters.

But when is the much-anticipated movie out in the UK and are the stars of the first move, Anna and Elsa, returning for Frozen 2?

When is Frozen 2 released in the UK?

The wait is almost over for Frozen fans in the UK as the new movie comes out this week.

Frozen 2 hits UK cinemas on 22 November, which is this Friday.

The plot of the new film has been kept a secret, but we know from the trailer that this next installment will see trouble brew in the kingdom of Arendelle, with the characters forced to venture into mysterious new lands to save their home.

Do Anna and Elsa return in Frozen 2?

Although not all of the original cast is returning, Anna and Elsa fans will be happy to hear that both Idina Menzel’s character Elsa, and Kristen Bell’s character Anna, are back for Frozen 2.

The closely-bonded sisters can be seen in the trailer teaming up once again with their sidekick – the enchanted snowman Olaf voiced by Josh Gad – by their side. Actor Jonathan Groff also returns for the new movie, voicing Kristoff once again.

Watch Frozen 2 in UK cinemas from 22 November.
