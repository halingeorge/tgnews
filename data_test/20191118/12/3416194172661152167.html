Ally Brooke Thanks Fans For Keeping Her On 'DWTS' Ahead of Semi-Finals

18 Nov 2019, 12:50

Ally Brooke is 100% ready for the semi-finals on Dancing With The Stars tonight!

Taking to her Instagram Stories ahead of the show, the 26-year-old singer wrote about how lucky and blessed she felt to be part of the show.

“I’m so thankful for every minute on this show. I’m truly having the time of my life and am growing more than I imagined. I can’t wait for tomorrow. SEMI FINALS BABY!!” she wrote.

Ally added, “Can’t believe I made it this far. Thank you to everyone who has supported us and voted for us. I always give my all on that floor. I love u guys more than anything.”

Tune into Dancing With The Stars TONIGHT @ 8PM ET/PT on ABC to watch Ally and partner Sasha Farber perform two dances!
