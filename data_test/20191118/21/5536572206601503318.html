Patriots Got Away With Obvious Missed Offsides Call on Opening Series That Held Eagles To FG (PIC)

18 Nov 2019, 21:04 by Darrelle Lincoln

Some teams seem to really catch all the breaks. Some more than others, like the Patriots…

That was the case during Sunday’s matchup between the Philadelphia Eagles and New England Patriots when the opening drive for the Eagles stalled out after left tackle Jason Peters was called for a false start.

Upon further review, it seems a Patriots player was lined up in the neutral zone, but refs missed it and Peters found himself getting caught instead.
