Dr. Dre Gets The Holy Ghost With Kanye West For New Album "Jesus Is King II"

18 Nov 2019, 21:57 by AllHipHop Staff

According to Kanye West, he is already working on the follow-up to this critically acclaimed album "Jesus Is King."

(AllHipHop News) Kanye West lit up the Internet today, thanks to an upcoming collaboration with Dr. Dre.

Yeezy popped up on his Twitter account today (November 18th) to reveal he was working on a sequel to his newest album Jesus Is King.

According to Kanye, super-producer Dr. Dre is collaborating with Dr. Dre on Jesus Is King II

The pair offered no information on the follow-up to the rapper's faith-based album, which topped the Christian Albums and Top Gospel Albums charts.

The album also made Kanye West the first artist to take over the entire Top 10 on both Hot Christian Songs and Hot Gospel Songs, which launched in 2003 and 2005.

Jesus Is King also took to the top slot on Billboard's Top 200, Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums and Top Rap Albums charts.
