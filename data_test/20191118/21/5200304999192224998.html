Reality show ‘The War Of Talent’ begins streaming on YouTube

18 Nov 2019, 21:58 by Raj Baddhan

A brand new reality show, giving platform for the first time to singers, actors and dancers on stage, has launched on YouTube.

‘The War Of Talent’ consists of 36 episodes and is created and directed by twins Parag and Prayas Chaudhary. It is produced by Rohit and Sohit Maithil along with Asif and Danish Patel.

The show’s celebrity judges include Ismail Darbar (legendary music director who gave music for films like Devdas, Hum Dil De Chuke Sanam), Mugdha Veira Godse (model & Bollywood actress in films like Fashion, Jail, Heroine etc.) and Salman Yusuff Khan (leading dancer & Bollywood choreographer, winner of DID season 1, Jhalak Dikhlaa Ja)

The show is hosted by Bollywood actor Rahul Dev.
