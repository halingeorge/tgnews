Google Stadia vs. Project xCloud: Which is better?

18 Nov 2019, 21:04 by Ash

The Google Stadia launches tomorrow, November 19th, and ahead of its launch we’ve put together a list comparing the two services so you can see which you think would be right for you.

Spoiler alert: To put it simply, the two services aren’t really all that comparable. While we know plenty about the Stadia, xCloud is still shrouded in so much mystery that implying that either service is inherently superior to the other just doesn’t work.

What we can do, though, is compare what we do know with what information is currently available to us and make educated guesses about everything else.

We also have the official hands-on MSPoweruser Stadia review coming at a later point so if you’re into Stadia, keep an eye out for that.

So, without further ado, welcome to MSPoweruser’s Google Stadia vs. Project xCloud comparison that’s based mostly in guesswork and where the points don’t matter!

When is each service available?

The Stadia technically launches tomorrow, November 19th. I say “technically” because, unless you’ve purchased the Founder’s Edition, you’ll have to wait until the Base model releases in 2020 to get your hands on a Stadia.
