Lane Kiffin Posts Heartfelt Message For QB Tua Tagovailoa

18 Nov 2019, 21:19 by Alek Arend

Tua Tagovailoa’s season-ending injury is one of the lone dim moments of what’s been a great college football season. The Alabama quarterback has been such a iconic figure in college football over the past few seasons. The sport won’t be the same without him for the rest of this year.

Tagovailoa’s injury has sparked an outpouring of appreciation and heartfelt messages for the young man. Lane Kiffin, former Alabama offensive coordinator and current FAU head coach, is the latest to offer his love and support for the Alabama quarterback.

Kiffin posted a simple, yet thoughtful message for Tagovailoa on Monday, via Twitter.
