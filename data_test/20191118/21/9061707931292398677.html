This is why police were in a city centre street

Three police vehicles were at the scene

18 Nov 2019, 21:33 by Anna Whittaker

Police were in a Derby street last night after a person died.

A spokesperson at Derbyshire police said there had been a sudden death at a property in Merchant Street, off Ashbourne Road.

They said no crime had taken place, but the force attended so it could report the death to the coroner.

The coroner mainly receives reports about deaths from the police, doctors and medical referees.

Derbyshire Live received messages on Sunday at around 3.45pm about the police presence in the street.

One resident said there were two marked vehicles and an unmarked police van parked up.

The resident, who did not wish to be named, said there were no sirens as police approached the property.

Our reporter who went to the scene said there were two police vehicles in the street when she arrived.
