Born between January 21 and February 19, Aquarius is part of the water element of the Zodiac. Here is the horoscope for Aquarius for November 19, 2019.

Written By Anushka Pathania | Mumbai | Updated On: November 18, 2019 21:43 IST

Aquarians are born between January 21 to February 19, and are ruled by the planet Uranus. They make their own mind up and then stick to it. More than that, Aquarians are very liberal, sensible, and wise. They will never compromise with what they want, and have a dark side to themselves, which they never reveal to anyone. They are very good at keeping secrets and like to keep their lives private. 

Aquarius – What to expect today?

Today might turn out to be a dull day for you overall. Some people might call you up today to cancel the plans that you were looking forward to. But by the end of the day, you will realise that it was for the better. The moon is in your sixth house, making you more imaginative and creating situations in your mind about what could have been and what could not have been. One side of you is willing to rush and take the risk of making a change and the other side wants to sit and not do anything.
