Tomi Lahren thanks Democrats after Trump fundraising surge

18 Nov 2019, 21:28 by Yael Halon

Fox Nation host Tomi Lahren thanked Democrats for their impeachment probe into President Trump after his reelection campaign raised millions in donations following the start of public impeachment hearings last week.

"The Democrats thought this was gonna be their chance to finally get him and well ... sorry to disappoint them but it’s not happening. This latest witch hunt may actually be doing more for Donald Trump’s reelection efforts than the oblivious and blinded-by-bitterness Democrats realize," Lahren said in the latest episode of Fox Nation's "Final Thoughts." 

Last week, Trump's reelection campaign raised $3.1 million from Wednesday's public impeachment hearing, according to campaign manager Brad Parscale.
