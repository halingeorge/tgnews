Bayelsa governor-elect visits Tinubu in Abuja

18 Nov 2019, 21:11 by Olalekan Adetayo

Olalekan Adetayo, Abuja

Bayelsa State Governor-elect, David Lyon, on Monday evening visited the national leader of the All Progressives Congress, Asiwaju Bola Tinubu, in his residence in Asokoro, Abuja.

The visit came a few hours after the Independent National Electoral Commission declared Lyon of the APC the winner of the governorship election held in the state on Saturday.

The governor-elect was accompanied on his visit to Tinubu by the Minister of State, Petroleum Resources, Timipre Sylva.

Sylva is a former governor of the state and also a leader of the party in the state.
