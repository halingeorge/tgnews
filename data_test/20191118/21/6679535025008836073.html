Inter-State sand smuggling gang busted in A.P.

18 Nov 2019, 21:47 by Special Correspondent

The Venkatagiri police busted an inter-State sand smuggling gang with the arrest of nine members, including six from Tamil Nadu, at Kalapadu village, near Venkatagiri on Monday.

On a tip off, the police conducted a surprise raid on late Sunday and nabbed the accused and seized from them ₹5,000 in cash, four mobile phones, one car, two tipper vehicles and two trucks used by them to transport the construction material on the Venkatagiri-Rapur Road, Venkatagiri Circle Inspector Sk. Anwar Basha said.

Cases were booked against them under relevant IPC and Mines and Minerals(Development and Regulation) Act and Prevention of Damages to Public Property Act.

The arrested were Malli Murali(27) from Nellore, D. Sainad(30) from Sullurpet, P. Venu(36) from Somasila, Doraswamy Balakrishnan(44) from R.K.Vedika, Tiruvallur district, J.Venkatesan(32) from Gummidipundi, K.Appan Rajan(23) from Gummadipundi, K Ajit(20) from Ponneri, R.Rajkumar from Kulluru and V. Saravanan(24) from Didar Nagar near Pulamchedu.

Check posts set up

On the directions of Superintendent of Police Aishwarya Rastogi, the district police have set up 17 check posts at stocking points and on inter-State and inter-district borders to curb illegal transportation of sand.

Those in knowledge of any illegal mining and transport of sand could pass on the information on the WhatsApp number 9390777727 or dial police helpline 100 or inform SIs or CIs concerned.

Informants’ identity would be kept confidential, police said.
