Kim Kardashian Released 4 Hilarious '90s-Style Skims Infomercials Starring Kris Jenner

18 Nov 2019, 21:41 by Chanel Vargas

Kim Kardashian's Skims Solutionwear collection will officially be back in stock on Nov. 20 — but wait, there's more! To promote the restock, Kim enlisted the help of her mom, Kris Jenner, to create a series of '90s-style infomercials that are giving me all the late-night TV flashbacks. The video stars Kris as the host — alongside actors Kyle Richards and Lisa Rinna — promoting the Skims Sculpting Bodysuit, Sculpting Bra, Sculpting High Waist Brief, Solution Short, and Sculpting Short available in sizes XXS to 5XL. There's even a 30-day money-back guarantee!

Check out the Skims website to shop the entire restocked Solutionwear collection (shipping and handling included!) on Nov. 20 at 9 a.m. PST/12 p.m. EST, and see the ridiculously funny Skims infomercials featuring Kim herself ahead.
