Larry C. Brown

18 Nov 2019, 21:48

Larry Clifford Brown, age 67, of Wisconsin Dells, Wisconsin, passed away in his sleep at home after a fierce battle with prostate cancer on Friday, November 15, 2019 surrounded by his loving family.

Larry was born February 29, 1952 in Boscobel, Wisconsin the son of Lawrence and Bernadette (Flynn) Brown. On October 9, 1993 he married Shari (Heidenrich) Coon in Wisconsin Dells, Wisconsin. While working at Klein Industries Larry attended MATC, Madison, Wisconsin where he earned his Millwright certification degree. After completing his apprenticeship he took a position with Libby's, Baraboo, Wisconsin now Seneca Foods. He retired from Seneca as plant superintendent after 40 years. Larry was a passionate man who loved fiercely and was loyal to a fault. He was proud of being an all-conference baseball player as an underclassman in high school, yet he only talked about the other players and his coach. He was a founding member of Dells golf league and won eight championships with two different partners. He enjoyed hunting, fishing, and refurbishing cars. In recent years, he found enjoyment with drawing and wood carving.

Survivors include his wife, Shari; his four sons and one stepdaughter, Craig (Stephanie Nolden) Brown of Baraboo, Andrew Brown of Baraboo, Tyler Stanley of Baraboo, Logan Brown of Wisconsin Dells and Ashley (Brenden) Westerfeldt of Spring Green; six grandchildren, Sabrina, Maya, Ella, Lily, Sydnee, Bodhi and Kendall; mother-in-law, Muriel Blaeske; two sisters, Bernadetta "Bernie" (Orville) Aspensen of Holmen and June (John) Jenkins of Arizona; nephew/brother, Paul Arnold; as well as many nieces, nephews, other relatives and friends.

He was preceded in death by his parents; grandparents; father-in-law, Dale Heidenrich; father-in- law, Robert Blaeske (Shari's step father); two sisters, Harriet Arnold and Shirley Brown; and one brother, PFC Albert (Al) Brown.
