Helly Shah to make brief appearance in ‘Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke’

18 Nov 2019, 21:42 by BizAsia Correspondent

After her recent stint in ‘Sufiyana Pyaar Mera’, Helly Shah is set to make a pivotal entry in Star Plus’ popular series ‘Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke’.

It has been reported that Shah will play the role of Neha in the current track where Abeer and Mishti are seen happier after Mehul’s arrest.

Shah confirmed the news but refused to divulge details.
