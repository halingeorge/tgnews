Gardaí investigating body found in burning car in Lucan

Gardaí were alerted to the incident at approximately 8.15pm on Monday

18 Nov 2019, 21:44 by Shauna Bowers

Gardaí are investigating an incident in Co Dublin after a body was found in a burning car on Monday evening.

The discovery was made at Mount Andrew Rise in Lucan at approximately 8.15pm.

Dublin Fire Brigade and An Garda Síochána were called to deal with a car that had been lit on fire and discovered a body in the passenger seat when the fire was extinguished.

Gardaí are currently treating the incident as suspicious.

The scene has been preserved and the State Pathologist and the Garda Forensics Bureau have been informed.
