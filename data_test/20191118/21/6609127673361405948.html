KIT CLASH

YouTuber who did Celtic and Rangers tours in rivals’ shirts got racist abuse and someone ‘wanted him dead’

18 Nov 2019, 21:33 by Aidan Smith

A YOUTUBER received racist abuse and someone 'wanted him dead' after wearing a Celtic top during a Rangers tour before visiting Parkhead in a Light Blues jersey.

Ninh Ly, who runs the Ultimate Bucket List channel, has toured several grounds around Europe dressed in the kit of the home team's rivals for his videos.

On Friday he visited Ibrox wearing a Celtic strip before touring Parkhead with a Gers shirt on.

Ninh went viral for the stadium stunt and admitted he was treated brilliantly at both clubs.

He said: "Both Rangers and Celtic were very very nice to me.

"I concluded that the hatred isn't real and it's grossly exaggerated."

But despite being treated impeccably on both tours, he was hit with a tirade of online abuse when he returned to his car.

After reviewing each ground in his video, the YouTuber said: "I'm sorry to report that most of the comments were overwhelmingly negative.

"People reacted really badly to me turning up to a stadium in a different coloured shirt.
