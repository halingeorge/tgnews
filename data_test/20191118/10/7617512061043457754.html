Search for missing kayaker at Lake Cowichan suspended

18 Nov 2019, 10:50 by Cindy E. Harnett

The search for a missing man at Lake Cowichan has been suspended until further notice, according to Cowichan Search and Rescue.

Lake Cowichan RCMP were at the scene this morning where a missing man’s fishing kayak washed ashore near Little Shaw Creek. Seventy-year-old Colin Court has been missing since at least Friday.

At 10 a.m. Monday, all of the detachment’s officers were at the scene.

On Sunday, Cowichan Search and Rescue was scouring the shores and surface waters of Lake Cowichan after the kayak washed ashore.

Court, who celebrated his birthday on Thursday, told his family he was going flyfishing on Friday and that’s the last they heard from him, according to searchers.

RCMP have reported a motorist found a truck belonging to Court parked at the Little Shaw campground on Cowichan Lake, west of Youbou, on Saturday.

Rain plagued Saturday’s search, but crews returned to the search at daybreak on Sunday.

Lake Cowichan RCMP could not be reached for comment.
