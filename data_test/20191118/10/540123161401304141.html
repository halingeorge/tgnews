Professional Rugby Player, Israel Folau, Blames Brush-fires on Gay-Marriage, Abortion, Triggers Mainstream Media

18 Nov 2019, 10:21 by Video Manager

Israel Folau, a professional rugby player in Australia blamed a recent wave of devastating brush fires Down-Under on “same-sex marriage” and “abortion”. The statements compelled the country’s Prime Minister Scott Morrison to call the comments “appallingly insensitive”.
