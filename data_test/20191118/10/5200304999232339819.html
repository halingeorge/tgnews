Overnights: ‘Ehd-e-Wafa’ on Hum TV tops UK ratings on Sunday

18 Nov 2019, 10:54 by Raj Baddhan

While ‘Ehd-e-Wafa’ topped UK ratings on Sunday, across all Asian TV channels, it was PTC Punjabi that led amongst broadcasters, according to exclusive data obtained by BizAsiaLive.com.

PTC Punjabi delivered a share of 0.13%, followed by Sony Entertainment Television with 0.07%, Star Plus, Star Bharat and Hum TV with 0.05%, Colors, New Vision TV, ZEE TV and ZEE Cinema with 0.04%, Sony MAX and Star Gold with 0.03%, Sony SAB TV, Geo News and Dunya News with 0.02%.

Sony TV’s top attractions on Sunday were ‘Indian Idol 11’ at 20:00 with 39,500 viewers, followed by ‘The Kapil Sharma Show’ at 21:30 with 17,400 viewers and ‘Crime Patrol’ repeat at 11:15 with 10,500 viewers.

On Star Plus, ‘Dance+ 5’ at 20:00 was watched by 16,600 viewers, followed by ‘Divya Drishti’ at 21:30 with 12,500 viewers. ‘Veera’ re-run’ earlier in the day fetched 15,200 viewers at 13:00.

On Hum TV, ‘Ehd-e-Wafa’ topped ratings with 55,400 viewers at 20:00, followed by ‘Gul-e-Rana’ at 21:00 with 19,400 viewers, ‘Mr Shamim’ at 18:15 with 13,600 viewers and ‘Resham Gali Ki Husna’ at 19:00 with 11,900 viewers.

On Colors, ‘Bigg Boss 13 – Weekend Ka Vaar’ pulled in 33,200 viewers at 21:30.

On ZEE TV, ‘Gold Awards 2019’ topped ratings with 14,900 viewers at 19:30.

On ZEE Cinema, ‘1920 London’ at 21:00 fetched 14,700 viewers, followed by ‘Veere Di Wedding’ at 18:00 with 12,400 viewers.

Please note: Above data is overnight figures, which may change slightly after seven days when consolidated data has been accounted for. BizAsiaLive.com publishes consolidated figures every Monday.
