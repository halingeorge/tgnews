TRAGIC DISCOVERY

Man’s body found at Aberdeenshire burn as cops probe unexplained death

18 Nov 2019, 10:32 by Katy Pagan

A POLICE probe has been launched after a man's body was found at an Aberdeenshire burn.

The tragic discovery was made near Haughs park in Turriff at around 9am on Sunday morning.

The area was cordoned off as an investigation got under way.

Cops are now investigating the death which is currently being treated as unexplained.

A police spokesman said: "We can confirm that a body of a man was discovered at the Burn of Turriff, Aberdeenshire around 9.10am on Sunday.

"The death is currently being treated as unexplained and enquiries are ongoing to establish the full circumstances."

Meanwhile, an Aberdeen bridge was locked down this morning after the death of a man.

Officers raced to Diamond Bridge in the north of the city after the alarm was raised at around 7.40am.

It is not thought the man's death is suspicious.
