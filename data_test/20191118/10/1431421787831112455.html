Conway Slams Trump's GOP 'Rising Star' as 'Lying Trash' After Impeachment Hearings

Elise Stefanik complained that Adam Schiff had unfairly 'muzzled' her and other Republicans from asking questions. In fact, each side’s lawmakers had received equal time

18 Nov 2019, 10:02 by The Associated Press and Haaretz

It didn’t take long at Friday’s impeachment hearing before Rep. Elise Stefanik, the House Intelligence Committee’s only Republican woman, was gaveled down by the panel’s Democratic chairman.

Stefanik drew praise from U.S. President Donald Trump on Twitter, writing "a new Republican star is born," while George Conway, husband of Trump senior adviser Kellyanne Conway, called her “lying trash” and donated the maximum amount to her 2020 Democratic opponent Tedra Cobb.
