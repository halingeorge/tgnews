Harry Styles Is Going On A World Tour And Yes, Our Boy Is Making His Way Back To Australia

18 Nov 2019, 10:14 by By PEDESTRIAN.TV

Harry Styles has announced the first round of dates for his upcoming 2020 world tour, ‘Love on Tour’, and I’m pleased to confirm that the most beautiful 1D member is making his way Down Under once again. What a sweet, sweet creature.

‘Love on Tour’ will kick off in the UK mid-April next year before heading to Europe in May, North America in June and Mexico in October.

In terms of confirmed supporting acts, the ever-ethereal King Princess will be joining Haz for the European leg, Jenny Lewis for the US and Canadian portion, and Koffee in Mexico.
