Street Fighter V: Champion Edition announced for February, Gill returns

18 Nov 2019, 10:14 by Aran Suddi

Capcom has made two major Street Fighter announcements today. First off is the reveal of Street Fighter V: Champion Edition which is set to release on February 14th for PS4 and PC. The Champion Edition will contain the majority of content from Street Fighter V and Street Fighter V: Arcade edition which means 40 fighters, 34 stages, and more than 200 costumes. It will not include the Fighting Chance costumes, Capcom Pro Tour DLC, and brand collaboration items.
