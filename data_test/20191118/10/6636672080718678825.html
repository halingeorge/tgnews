Only Connect: On TE O.C.H. Micro Circular connectors

18 Nov 2019, 10:42 by EW Staff

Welcome to another in the series by Nick Locke, of Nicab Ltd, who has more than 20 years experience in the electronics manufacturing industry specialising in interconnection cable assembly. This post looks at a new Micro Circular connector series.

As we continue to see more products developed to meet the demands of small lightweight technology TE have introduced its O.C.H Micro Circular connectors.

Designed with wearable tech

In my previous blog post (see Only Connect: On smart connected textiles) I discussed how this is going to become a massive area for growth in the near future.

Let’s take a closer look at this new connector range. As part of the Net Warrior Program developed by the US Army the idea is to bring together technology to the frontline that is bot ultra-modern and lightweight.

According to TE:

“Our ruggedized O.C.H. micro circular connectors have passed the rigorous performance requirements set by the US Army, including the mechanical and performance requirements of MIL-STD-810G. These circular connectors feature a push-pull coupling and quick disconnect per the US Army’s Nett Warrior specification. The connectors can be ordered against National Stocking Numbers 5935-016595575 and 5935-016594090.”

The technical specifications are as follows:

  • Shell: Aluminium alloy, selectively plated zinc-nickel over electroless nickel overall
  • Inserts: Thermoplastic
  • Contacts: Copper alloy, plated gold over nickel

Electrical:

  • Insulation Resistance: 100 MΩ min. at 500 VDC
  • Contact Current Rating: 5 A (-18°C to +71°C, crimped to 22 AWG wire

Mechanical:

  • Shock: MIL-STD-810G, Method 516.6, Procedure IV
  • Vibration: MIL-STD-810G, Method 514.7, Procedure I, Method 514.6, Category 5, Procedure II
