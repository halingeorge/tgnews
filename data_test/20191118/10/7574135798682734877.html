Paul McCartney headlining Glastonbury 2020

18 Nov 2019, 10:52 by Andrew Sacher

Glastonbury 2020 happens June 24-28, and they just announced a very big headliner: Paul McCartney. He plays on 6/27. “Having Paul McCartney coming back to headline the Pyramid next year is an absolute dream come true. There really was no one that we wanted more for the 50th anniversary,” Glastonbury co-organizer Emily Eavis said. Paul previously headlined the festival in 2004.

The former Beatle joins Diana Ross, who plays the Sunday (6/28) legends slot on the Pyramid Stage.

Tickets for Glastonbury 2020 are currently sold out, but there will be a “resale of any returned coach + ticket packages” on April 16, followed by a generation admission relsaae on April 19. In order to get tickets, you have to register.
