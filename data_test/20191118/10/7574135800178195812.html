Beach Slang announce 2020 tour

18 Nov 2019, 10:22 by Andrew Sacher

Beach Slang are gearing up to release their new album (with The Replacements’ Tommy Stinson on bass) on January 10 via Bridge 9, and they’re currently on tour opening for the Goo Goo Dolls. They’ve now also announced a headlining tour for spring 2020.

The newly-announced tour includes a NYC show on April 16 at Knitting Factory Brooklyn. Tickets for that show go on sale Friday (11/22) at 10 AM with a venue presale starting Thursday (11/21) at 10 AM.

Meanwhile, the Goo Goo Dolls/Beach Slang tour hits the NYC-area on December 11 at The Paramount in Huntington (sold out except resale tickets).

So far, Beach Slang have released two songs from the new album, “Bam Rang Rang” and “Tommy in the 80s” (a tribute to power pop icon Tommy Keene). Listen to both, alongside the list of all tour dates below.
