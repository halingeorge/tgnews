NBA roundup: LeBron James stars as Lakers make their best start in eight seasons

LeBron James led the way once again as he scored 33 points to help the Los Angeles Lakers to a 122-101 win over the Atlanta Hawks.

18 Nov 2019, 10:41

The Lakers have now made their best start to a season since 2010-11 and boast the best record in the NBA at 11-2.

James fell just short of a triple-double, managing 12 rebounds and seven assists.
