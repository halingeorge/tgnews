TRAGIC DEATH

Livingston baby dies aged five months as police seal off house

18 Nov 2019, 10:09 by Justin Bowie

A BABY boy aged five months died in Livingston after police sealed off a house in the town.

It has been confirmed that the tot passed away in St John's Hospital on Friday.

The tragic death is being treated as "unexplained" by cops.

It came amid reports that a property was sealed off on Scott Brae, according to the Edinburgh Evening News.

A Police Scotland spokesperson said: "Police in West Lothian are investigating after a five-month-old baby boy from the Ladywell area passed away in St John's Hospital on Friday, November 15.

"The death is being treated as unexplained pending further enquiries and a report will be submitted to the Procurator Fiscal."
