'Great ability'

Ex-England striker Darren Bent urges Everton to sign Rangers’ Alfredo Morelos

18 Nov 2019, 10:13 by Gary Taylor

DARREN BENT believes Everton should sign Rangers star Alfredo Morelos.

The Colombian hitman has been in stunning form this season netting 22 goals for the Light Blues in all competitions.

Morelos also scored his first goal on the international stage after hitting a last-gasp winner against Peru.

The Toffees have struggled in front of goal this season hitting just 13 goals in 12 Premier League games.

And Bent reckons Morelos could be the man to solve Everton's problems.

He told Football Insider: "I'm surprised Everton don't try to go and sign him.

"He's a bit temperamental, but he can score goals, he's got the great ability and to be fair to Rangers if they can go and get a load of money for him, let's say £20m+ for him, they've still for Jermain Defoe who gets goals week-in-week-out."

SunSport exclusively revealed last month how Crystal Palace were plotting a £20million January swoop for the hitman.

The 23-year-old is also on Aston Villa's radar as they look to add to their striking department.

Light Blues boss Steven Gerrard emphatically declared the striker WON'T be leaving the club during the winter window. 

This was backed up by new sporting director Ross Wilson who said he would 'categorically' not be moving on just yet.

Rangers return to domestic action on Sunday when they travel to face Hamilton Accies.

Gerrard's men are sitting level on points with Celtic at the top of the Premiership with just one goal separating the pair in the league standings.
