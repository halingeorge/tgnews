Men Behaving Tradly bring jazz fun to Uffculme

The band are heading to Gordon’s Jazz Club on Wednesday, November 20

18 Nov 2019, 10:41 by Lewis Clarke

Men Behaving Tradly will be taking to the stage in Uffculme next week.

The band are heading to Gordon’s Jazz Club on Wednesday, November 20.

Jane Mulholland from the club said: “It’s a sure-fire recipe for an evening of fantastic music and good-natured fun.

“Their collective pedigree is extensive including performing with local, national and international jazz bands and soloists, and a wide-ranging repertoire from the early jazz period through to the classics of the great American songbook.”

Gordon's Jazz Club presents traditional and New Orleans Jazz on the third Wednesday of every month from 7:30pm until 10:00pm at the Magelake Pavilion, Uffculme, EX15 3DP. Tickets cost £7.
