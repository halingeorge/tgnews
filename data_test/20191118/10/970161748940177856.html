Call Of Duty: Modern Warfare has 200-player battle royale reveals datamine

18 Nov 2019, 10:37

The rumoured new battle royale mode for Call Of Duty: Modern Warfare has respawn tickets to revive dead teammates, amongst other innovations.

Not for the first time, rumours have emerged that Call Of Duty: Modern Warfare will be getting a 200-player Battle Royale, although this time there seems to be evidence within the game’s own updates.

Well respected dataminer Sensecallo listed his findings on Reddit, which reveal an unusual system for respawning players that relies on ‘Respawn Tokens’.

Tokens can be looted like any other item but can only be used once per match. They work by a teammate dropping a corpse of a comrade near an ambulance and then using a token.

The revived player will then be placed in a temporary holding area, where they’ll wait for their chance to respawn by competing in a 1 vs. 1 battle with another defeated player.
