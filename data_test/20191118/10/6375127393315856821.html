1 killed, 4 injured in stabbing

18 Nov 2019, 10:21 by Jojo C. Magsombol

A drunk man was shot dead after he and his brother went on a stabbing spree that left four people injured in Purok 6, Bgy. Bolbok, Batangas City last Sunday evening.

Suspect Erwin Nuñez, of legal age, was killed after he stabbed P/Staff Sgt. Leovino Mendoza who rushed to the place after the suspects wounded Bgy. Councilor Victorino Lanor, tanod Roderick Macalalad, and an unidentified one, and painter Jayson Royo who helped in pacifying the suspects. Another suspect, Zerwil Nuñez, was arrested.

Police said the stabbing spree started when the suspects who were under the influence of liquor went wild and made trouble that prompted bgy. Councilor Lanor to intervene with his tanods.

Recovered at the scene of the crime were a fan knife and an empty bullet of gun.
