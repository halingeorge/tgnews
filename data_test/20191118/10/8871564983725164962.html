Thief terrorizes 7-Eleven clerk at knifepoint in Thai northeast (Video)

18 Nov 2019, 10:21 by Coconuts Bangkok

A thief who robbed a convenience store clerk by knifepoint in the Thai northeast over the weekend has been taken into custody, police said.

Oomsin Noinarong, 18, has reportedly confessed to robbing a 7-Eleven store in Udon Thani city around 1pm on Saturday, and police said they arrested him later that night for a crime that netted THB4,200 (US$140.)

Footage of the robbery circulating online shows a thief dressed in dark clothing with a tinted motorbike helmet during the robbery.
