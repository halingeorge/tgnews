What happened to Claire Foy in The Crown and who is in the season 3 cast?

18 Nov 2019, 10:12

The much-anticipated third season of The Crown has dropped on Netflix and we are all kinds of excited.

Olivia Colman and Helen Bonham Carter take over the reins from award-wining Claire Foy and Matt Smith.

It’s safe to say it is an action-packed season, covering the turbulent years of the 1970s, which saw the miners’ strikes and the breakdown of Princess Margaret’s marriage as well as Prince Charles and Camilla’s infamous love affair.

That’s not to mention the Aberfan disaster and the assassination of Lord Mountbatten.

But before we get started on all that, let’s take a look at the cast, and why Claire Foy won’t be returning.

What happened to Claire Foy in The Crown?

Claire Foy won countless awards – including multiple BAFTAs – for her portrayal of the young Queen Elizabeth II in the first two seasons of the royal drama.

So while she clearly aced the role, at 35, she’s simply too young to play the Queen, who will pass through her forties over the newest season.

Who is in the season 3 cast of The Crown?

The show feels pretty different from the first two installments, as the cast has been completely overhauled.

Our new favourite red carpet duo, Olivia Colman and Helena Bonham Carter, will play royal sisters Queen Elizabeth II and Princes Margaret and will be accompanied by Tobias Menzies (Outlander) as Prince Philip.

Other new additions to the all-star cast include:

  • Josh O’Conner (God’s Own Country) as the Prince of Wales
  • Emma Corrin (Granchester) as Princess Diana
  • Gillian Anderson (X Files) as Prime Minister Margaret Thatcher
  • Emerald Fennell (Call the Midwife) as Camilla Shand
  • Ben Daniels (The Exorcist) as Lord Snowdon
  • Erin Doherty (Les Misérables) as Princess Anne
  • Marion Bailey (Mr Turner) as the Queen Mother

Also joining them will be John Lithgow as Winston Churchill, Jason Watkins as Harold Wilson, Samuel West as Anthony Blunt, Charles Dance as Lord Mountbatten and Geraldine Chaplin as Wallis Simpson.
