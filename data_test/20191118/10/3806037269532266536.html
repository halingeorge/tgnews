KAPITAL Drops Western Mexican Tuxedo Pants/Jacket Combo

Featuring a skeleton-embroidered trucker jacket.

18 Nov 2019, 10:47 by Ambrose Leung

KAPITAL continues with its skeleton-embroidered apparel in the form of the Satin Western Mexican Tuxedo pants and jacket release.

Available in tan or olive, the drop features a pair of tapered pants with large bone chain-stitch embroidery down the sides of the legs. The pants feature a button fly, a large “KAPITAL” jacron, and a rear cinch. The jacket sees bone embroidery down the arms along with a full ribcage at the back of the jacket. The trucker style jacket also contains stamp-branded buttons and a front pocket label.

Those interested in this new KAPITAL Mexican Tuxedo release can find the jacket and pants over at KAPITAL’s online store for ¥59,180 JPY and ¥37,180 JPY, respectively (approximately $540 USD and $340 USD).
