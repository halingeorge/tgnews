424 & adidas Originals Unveil Minimalistic Luxe Leather SC Premiere & Pro Model Collaboration

Featuring hits of the Fairfax label’s signature red.

18 Nov 2019, 10:33 by Eric Brain

adidas Consortium has enlisted Fairfax imprint 424 for its latest line-up of clean collaborative kicks, introducing two reworked iterations of the SC Premiere and one Pro Model.

The collaborative pairs adopt a minimalist approach with colors such as “Core White” and triple black adorning the uppers, while 424’s signature red graces the tongue and heel tabs. The SC Premiere comes in both colorways, with the “Core White” iteration sporting a “Scarlet” red suede tongue tag debossed with 424 branding in black, black adidas Trefoil branding on the rear, and tonal debossed 424 branding on the rear-quarter of the lateral midsole.

Much like the original, the SC Premiere is sans-Three Stripes, but goes further with the minimalist look with the deletion of its adidas-branded mid-panel window. The triple black iteration sees a similarly clean finish, while the Pro Model sports a variety of new additions.

424 has added a doubled-up sole unit and finishes the sneaker in premium “Core White” leather. The heel tab is offered up in red debossed leather, and rounding off the shoe is its iconic shell-toe cap, tonal laces and zig-zag cut Three Stripes.

Take a look at the 424 x adidas Consortium SC Premiere and Pro Model collection in the gallery above. Prices range from approximately $140 USD to $180 USD, and can be picked up from Sneakersnstuff on November 22.
