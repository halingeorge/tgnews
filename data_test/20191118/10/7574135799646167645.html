mewithoutYou teasing something for this Friday

18 Nov 2019, 10:58 by Andrew Sacher

mewithoutYou recently said that 2020 would be their last year as an active band, and their plans for that year including opening Thrice’s Vheissu tour, doing a Brother, Sister tour in the spring, a farewell tour in the fall, and putting out some reissues. Now, they’ve put out a teaser video with this Friday’s date (11/22/19). What do they have in store? Stay tuned…
