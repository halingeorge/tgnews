Staff at Radford shop 'threatened with a knife' during robbery

The offenders got away with stolen cash

18 Nov 2019, 10:51 by Phoebe Ram

Police were called to reports of a knife point robbery in Radford on Sunday night.

Officers attended the McColl's convenience store on Ilkeston Road, Radford around 11.30pm on Sunday, November 17.

Staff were threatened with a knife, police confirmed and offenders stole cash.

Nottinghamshire Police remained at the scene overnight and continue to investigate local CCTV footage.

If you have any information, any dash cam footage of the area between 11-11:59 on Sunday night or you witnessed the incident call 101, quoting incident number 847 of 17 November 2019.
