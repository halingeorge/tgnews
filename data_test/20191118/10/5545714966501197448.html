Guilty Gear 20th Anniversary Pack headed to North America on Switch

Heaven or Hell?

18 Nov 2019, 10:30 by Chris Moyse

Following on from the fresh reveal of Guilty Gear Strive's new moniker, Arc System Works has also announced that Guilty Gear 20th Anniversary Pack is headed to Nintendo Switch in North America. The double pack was previously only made available in other territories such as Europe and Japan.

The Guilty Gear 20th Anniversary Pack includes a physical copy of the game - comprised of the original Guilty Gear and Guilty Gear XX Accent Core Plus R - a steelbook casing, a commemorative coin and, best of all a Sol Badguy headband. Imagine wearing that? Ehh, I'm not your dad, do what you will. Guilty Gear 20th Anniversary Pack includes a revamped multiplayer system, Joy-Con support, and the option to check out the rare Korean soundtrack.

No release date was announced, but at least North American fans, (or those who didn't import a European edition), can take comfort that some classic GG action will be headed their way in the future.
