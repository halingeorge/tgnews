Ayushmann Khurrana wishes brother Aparshakti with the sweetest message

18 Nov 2019, 10:26 by Filmfare

Actor Aparshakti Khurana who turns a year older today is spending his birthday while shooting for his upcoming film. Talking about his birthday plans, he said, "I believe that birthdays should be spent doing what you love and I love working. My wife Aakriti will be coming to Ludhiana on the shoot location, so that makes my birthday perfect. I will be doing what I love and being with people who I love. I cannot ask for more.”

While away from the birthday boy, brother Ayushmann Khurrana still managed to find a special way to wish the actor with a picture from his childhood with a sweet message that read, “You’re the world’s best brother. And I wanted to show this picture of yours to the world, where you are trying to mimic Raj Kapoor saab of Shri 420. (Mera joota hai jaapaani)”

On the work front, Aparshakti will be seen in Pati Patni Aur Woh directed by Mudassar Aziz. The film which is a remake of the 1978 film with the same name is scheduled to release on December 6.
