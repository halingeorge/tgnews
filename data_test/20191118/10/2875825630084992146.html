Andi Peters reacts to Ant and Dec mocking his presenting skills on I'm A Celebrity

It's fair to say Andi Peters saw the funny side when I'm A Celebrity hosts Ant McPartlin and Dec Donnelly mocked him on the show

18 Nov 2019, 10:29 by Irishmirror.ie

I'm A Celebrity fans were in hysterics during the launch on Sunday night as Ant McPartlin and Dec Donnelly took no prisoners.

One moment saw Ant and Dec mock fellow TV presenter Andi Peters, making a dig at the ITV star.

Dec also took the opportunity to mock his competition segment presenting skills before he was heard asking who Andi was.

The presenter had said: "I was thinking, isn't it nice to see a daytime TV presenter standing on a luxury yacht without hearing the words, 'you and a friend could win £50,000 for life'."

He then pointed his finger in the air and did a twirl on the spot.

Ant quipped: "Good Andi Peters impression," only for his pal to ask: "Who?"

Speaking on Lorraine on Monday morning, host Lorraine Kelly brought up the mention of her co-star.

Andi seemed pretty excited by it, completely seeing the funny side of the mockery before mentioning Dec.

He told her: "I know, I was so excited Lorraine!

"Let's be honest cos I don’t think anyone notices what I do, except Dec Donnelly does!"

Andi had responded to the hilarious moment shortly after on Twitter too, claiming Dec had made a mistake.

He wrote: "I think you’ll find it’s £100k, in time for Christmas! @antanddec @itv @imacelebrity #ImACeleb #youvegottobeinittowinit."

Dec apologised in response, commenting: "I stand corrected! Soz Andi! D x."

Fans began asking the TV legend to sign up to the show himself, wanting to see him in the jungle.

Others found the light-hearted exchange between presenters hilarious, glad to have the jungle series back on TV.

Lorraine airs weekdays at 8:30am on ITV.
