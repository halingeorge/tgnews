Dabangg 3: Kannada Version Of This Salman Khan Led Franchise To Release In 250 Screens In Karnataka

Dabangg 3 is been helmed by choreographer turned director Prabhu Deva and it is been produced under Salman's home production, Salman Khan Films.

18 Nov 2019, 10:25 by Koimoi.com Team

The makers of Bollywood superstar Salman Khan starrer Dabangg 3 are leaving no stone unturned to promote the action drama. Post releasing the trailer, the Dabangg 3 team recently revealed the music videos from the film followed by the jukebox.

The latest news related to the film is, the Kannada version of the magnum opus will be released in 250 screens across Karnataka.

Manjunath Gowda, who has bought the rights of the film’s Kannada version in an interview to cinemaexpress.com said, “Dabangg 3 will be the first dubbed film to be released in so many theatres. Several buyers from Mysuru, Shivamogga, and Davanagere want the Kannada version to be released in their areas.”

The Salman Khan starrer is one of the most anticipated releases of the year. The action drama venture has Kannada superstar Kiccha Sudeep as the lead antagonist following which the buzz and the demand for the film among the Kannada speaking viewers are way too high in Karnataka.

Apart from Hindi and Kannada, the Salma starrer action drama will also release in Telugu and Tamil languages.

Dabangg 3 also stars Sonakshi Sinha and debutant actress Saiee Manjrekar in lead.

The film also has a strong supporting cast of actors like Amol Gupte, Mahesh Manjrekar, Milind Gunaji, Nawab Shah, Arbaaz Khan along with others.

Dabangg 3 is been helmed by choreographer turned director Prabhu Deva and it is been produced under Salman’s home production, Salman Khan Films.

The music for the magnum opus is been composed of composer duo Sajid-Wajid.

Dabangg 3 will release on 20th December.
