Ace Designer Manish Malhotra’s Father Passes Away, Bollywood Offers Condolence

With the news of his demise, several friends from the B-Town poured in to offer their condolences to Manish Malhotra and family.

18 Nov 2019, 10:45 by Koimoi.com Team

Ace Bollywood designer Manish Malhotra lost his father today. According to reports, his father who was in his 90s had not been keeping well for quite some time now. With the news of his demise, several friends from the B-Town poured in to offer their condolences to Manish and family.

Amongst the very first from Bollywood who rushed to Manish was Shabana Azmi and Sophie Chaudhary. There were several pictures of stars viral over the internet. We also saw Karan Johar visiting his dear friend Manish Malhotra. Both the filmmaker and designer have collaborated on several projects and have hit designs to their credit.

Manish Malhotra’s nephew director Puneet Malhotra was also spotted. In the pictures, Puneet can be seen performing the last rituals of his late grandfather. Puneet made his directorial debut with Karan Johar produced Student of The Year 2.

Manish has been a prominent name in Bollywood for almost two decades now. The designer who made his entry in 1990s has reached peaks of success. Recently, he made his debut on stage where he designed clothes for the Feroz Abbas Khan’s adaptation of K. Asif’s yesteryear classic Mughal-e-Azam in 2016.

We offer our deepest condolences to Manish Malhotra and his family.
