Republic of Ireland team to play Denmark: Squad for the Euro 2020 qualifier

Shane Duffy will captain the side for the crucial Euro 2020 qualifier,

18 Nov 2019, 10:32 by Robert Hynes

Jack Byrne and Troy Parrott have both been named in the Republic of Ireland 23-man squad to face Denmark.

Scott Hogan and Callum O'Dowda are the two men to miss out, having been used from the bench away to Switzerland.

Shane Duffy will captain the side for the crucial Euro 2020 qualifier, which gets underway at 7.45pm on Monday.
