Best early Black Friday iPhone XS deals

Here's where to get the best price on the iPhone XS, whether you're looking to buy on contract or SIM-free, as the Black Friday deals start to appear

18 Nov 2019, 10:15 by Ashleigh Macro

Apple updated its iPhone lineup in September 2019, and one of the results of this reshuffle was the discontinuation of the beloved iPhone XS. The company no longer sells this model, having replaced it with the iPhone 11 Pro - and if budget allows, you could do a lot worse than buying the newer model.

However, the iPhone XS is still on sale at other vendors and networks, and the launch of the 11-series handsets means you can find good deals. You could spend a few hours scouring dozens of retailers to find the best price, but our experts have done the hard work for you - below we present the best contracts and SIM-free offers to suit you.

The iPhone XS has a 5.8in display, water resistance, a still-powerful A12 Bionic chip, a superb camera and good battery life. It's available in gold, silver and Space Grey. If you're looking for deals on the XS's (also officially discontinued) bigger sibling, check out our iPhone XS Max deals roundup.

iPhone XS price

Even 14 months on from launch, the iPhone XS remains a premium handset, and you should expect a price tag to match. Still, it's no longer brand-new, so some sort of discount would be appropriate.

When it came out the XS cost £999/$999 for the 64GB model, £1,149/$1,149 for the 256GB model and £1,349/$1,349 for 512GB when bought SIM-free. If your vendor of choice doesn't knock a hundred or two off that to acknowledge the passage of time, get the new iPhone 11 Pro instead.
