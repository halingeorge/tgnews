Kundali Bhagya Actress Anjum Fakih Goes Missing From The Show, Here's Why?

18 Nov 2019, 10:51 by Neeki Singh

Anjum Fakih who is known to be chirpy, bubbly and full of life is currently unwell and hasn't been shooting for almost two weeks now. She has been missing from the sets of the popular TV show, Kundali Bhagya

Actress Anjum Fakih is missing from her TV show, Kundali Bhagya since many days now and her fans have been wondering why. The actress, who is known to be chirpy, bubbly and full of life is currently unwell and hasn't been shooting for almost two weeks now. She said, "I caught the viral infection a few days back and have been suffering from severe weakness and fever for days. The makers have been kind to give me a sick leave so that i can recuperate well."

The actress added, "I know my fans must be thinking where have I disappeared but want to tell them that I will be soon back, with a great dose of entertainment. Hopefully i will start shooting next week, if the doctor allows me." Anjum plays the sister and parallel lead role of Srishti in the show Kundali Bhagya.

She has earlier done many TV shows like Tere Sheher Mein and Ek Tha Raja and Ek Thi Rani. She is a model-turned-actress and is a feisty, ambitious, independent girl, who wants to do many kind of roles.

A few days back she had revealed that her journey into the TV world has not been an easy one. It has been fraught with many roadblocks and hardships. Her family also was not on the same page as her's about her decision to make a dent in the telly world. She had also added that she comes from an extremely orthodox muslim family, where even watching TV was seen as a taboo.

In 2009, the actress decided to pack her bags and leave for Mumbai to make a career in showbiz. She initially started with modelling assignments and later made her TV debut after many misses. The rest as they say is history.
