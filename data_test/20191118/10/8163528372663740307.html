Green Day to Celebrate Dookie’s 25th Anniversary With American Music Awards Performance

18 Nov 2019, 10:37 by Anna Chan

Green Day is set to rock the American Music Awards on Sunday, November 24, Billboard has exclusively announced. The band, who are celebrating the 25th anniversary of their 1994 album Dookie, will perform the record’s hit “Basket Case,” as well as “Father of All,” the title  track off their upcoming album.
