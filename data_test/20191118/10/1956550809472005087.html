Dubai's Breeze Beach Grill to launch ladies’ night

And there’s a deal for gents too at the Palm Jumeirah bar

18 Nov 2019, 10:21 by Reema Rahman

If sipping on grape-based mixed drinks, watching the Dubai sunset and salsaing the night away sounds like your kind of night out, ladies, then this is for you.

Palm Jumeirah’s popular beach bar, Breeze Beach Grill, is launching a ladies’ night. And gents – there’s something for you too.

From Tuesday November 26 onwards, grab your best mates and get ready to enjoy two hours of unlimited red and white grape-based mixed drinks and three tapas dishes for Dhs100, while gents can sip on three hops and chomp away on three dishes from the tapas menu for Dhs200.

There are also going to be DJs on the decks and salsa dancing lessons, so get ready to dance the night away on the beach overlooking the Dubai skyline.

The brand-spanking new ladies’ night, named Miss Havana, kicks off at 7pm and runs until late so you can choose whatever time you fancy heading down for the two-hour package.
