Deputies investigate after man crashes pickup truck into Vancouver consignment store

18 Nov 2019, 10:58 by KATU News

VANCOUVER, Wash. – A driver crashed a pickup truck through the front window of a consignment store in Vancouver Monday morning, the Clark County Sheriff’s Office said.

Deputies responded to a report of the crash at Once and Again consignment at 7903 NE St. Johns Rd. at around 8:15 a.m.

When they arrived, deputies found a man inside a Toyota pickup truck and determined he had driven through the front window of the shop.

Witnesses said the driver was traveling south on St. Johns Road when he rear ended another vehicle, then took a sharp left turn while accelerating, and collided with the business.

The consignment shop was not open at the time of the crash.

The driver of the Toyota was the only person injured. The sheriff’s office said it appears he will survive.

The cause of the crash is under investigation.
