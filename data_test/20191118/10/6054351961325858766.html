Tay-K Reportedly Indicted For Shooting & Killing Man In Chick-Fil-A Parking Lot

18 Nov 2019, 10:43 by Kyle Eustice

Bexar County, TX – Incarcerated rapper Tay-K was reportedly indicted on a capital murder charge in Bexar County last week.

According to ABC 12 San Antonio, the Bexar County District Attorney’s Office has accused Tay-K — real name Taymor McIntyre — of shooting and killing Mark Anthony Saldivar during an April 2017 robbery.

Tay-K, who was only 16 at the time of the shooting, was named as a triggerman in Saldivar’s murder as part of a wrongful death lawsuit filed by the victim’s mother Lucia “Lucy” Saldivar last year.
