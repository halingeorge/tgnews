Two people die in Bwabwata National Park road accident

18 Nov 2019, 10:30

A gruesome car accident on the Trans-Zambezi Highway in the Bwabwata National Park in the Kavango-East Region claimed the lives of two people Monday morning.
The accident occurred about 40 kilometres north of Divindu via Kongola, where a driver of a foreign registered seven-seater minibus lost control and overturned around 07h00.
Two female passengers died on the spot and had to be pulled out of the wreckage.
Eyewitness assisted in removing five passengers, including the driver, from the wreck.
They suffered slight to serious injuries and are treated at the Divundu Hospital.
Although the Namibian Police could not confirm, those at the scene says the vehicle's occupants are all Zimbabwean nationals.
