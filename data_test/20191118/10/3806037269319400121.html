Watch Chun-Li Morph Into a Power Ranger in 'Power Rangers: Legacy Wars'

Check out her new moves.

18 Nov 2019, 10:50 by Mallory Chin

Originally released in 2017 for iOS and Android devices, Power Rangers: Legacy Wars has revamped its mobile game with an updated crossover event with Street Fighter.

In the short promo ad shared by Power Rangers: Legacy Wars and its developer nWay Games, famed fighter Chun-Li can be seen morphing into a Power Rangers getup. As Chun-Li Ranger, the powerful fighter can set her foes alight with blue Phoenix fire using the Volcano Kick, Phoenix Dance and her super, the Thousand Phoenix Kicks. Her character has been in the game since 2018, alongside Ryu, Guile, Akuma, Cammy, and M. Bison. Check out the trailer above which shows off her new moves.

Power Rangers: Legacy Wars is currently available for all Android and iOS devices.
