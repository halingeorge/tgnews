Salman Khan's Dabangg 3 all set to become the widest release of Bollywood

18 Nov 2019, 10:09 by Filmfare

The fans have become the countdown for Dabangg 3. The film Salman Khan in his Chulbul Pandey avatar. The trailer and the title track of the film have already created a stir among Bhai’s fans and we are expecting it to have a mind-boggling opening at the ticket windows this December. According to the latest reports Dabangg 3 is expected to have the widest release in Bollywood till date. Before Dabangg 3, the widest Bollywood release in India was Aamir Khan and Amitabh Bachchan starrer Thugs of Hindostan, which had played in 4950 screens in Hindi, Tamil, and Telugu versions. Dabangg 3 is all set to release across the nation in a whopping 5400 screens in four different languages - Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada. There are plans for having early shows in place, and the makers are looking at getting five shows per single screen, unlike four shows a day.

Apart from Salman Khan, Dabangg 3 also stars Sonakshi Sinha, Arbaaz Khan, debutante Saiee Manjrekar and Kannada superstar Kichcha Sudeepa in crucial roles. Dabangg 3 is set to hit the screens on December 20, 2019.
