African Artist Creates Mane Painting In The Most Astounding Way

18 Nov 2019, 10:46 by SL Social

With a growing reputation for creating artwork in an unorthodox fashion, an African artist recently created an astonishing portrait of Sadio Mane. Did you see it?

A quick glance at the artist's Instagram account, and it’s safe to say he is no ordinary man of his craft.

Known as "Bou Bou", the African artist uses random objects such as boxing gloves, machetes and hats in his painting process, with the end product being nothing short of spectacular.

Earlier this year, the Senegalese man painted a portrait of Paris Saint-Germain star Neymar by simply using his foot. As expected, his work went viral and had social media in awe.
