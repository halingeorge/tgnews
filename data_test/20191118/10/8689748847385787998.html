We reported that Stonebwoy subtly threw new shots at Kelvynboy during his performance at the 4syteTv Music Video Awards last Saturday. In case you missed that article, read that below:

Stonebwoy Subtly Throws New Shots At Kelvynboy During His Performance At The 2019 4Syte Music Video Awards

It seems Kelvynboy does not agree with his former boss Stonebwoy on what he said thus took to Twitter to speak his mind.

In Kelvynboy’s post, he addressed the part where Stonebwoy called him an underground artiste during the performance. He said;

Lol you dey call me underground out of pain
you see underground wey dey play shows every weekend before
fanfoolers zalaaa

Kelvynboy continued to say that some people always want to be good in the eyes of others; painting others black forgetting they’re black people too.
