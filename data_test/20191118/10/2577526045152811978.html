Capcom Has Announced Street Fighter V: Champion Edition

18 Nov 2019, 10:30 by Luke Plunkett

Capcom has announced Street Fighter V: Champion Edition, a bundle that includes almost everything released as part of both Street Fighter V and its Arcade Edition. That’s “40 characters, 34 stages and over 200 costumes”. It’s out in February 2020 for PS4 and Steam.
