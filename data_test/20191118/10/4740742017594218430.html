Aldi is launching Friends range for Christmas including Central Perk mugs

There will also be cushions, duvets and more

18 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Simon Duke, Emma Grimshaw

Just as rumours swirl about a potential  Friends reunion being on the cards, Aldi launches a new range dedicated to the classic American comedy.

Central Park mugs, cushions and even duvet sets are all being sold at the bargain supermarket just in time for Christmas.

You might not believe it but 25 years have now passed since the series made its debut, reports Chronicle Live.

And, after Jennifer Aniston sent Instagram into overdrive by joining up and posting THAT selfie of her and her co-stars, and classic episodes of Friends are about to hit cinemas, Aldi has more excitement for Friends fans with its new range.

Some of the highlights include pyjama sets emblazoned with 'How You Doin' and 'I'd Rather Be Watching Friends' for £8.99.

And you can enjoy a cosy sleep wrapped up in the ultimate Friends duvet which comes in three different styles and are being sold for £14.99.

But arguably the top must-have for any  fan has to be the Central Perk mug and you can pick up one of those for just £4.99 along with a travel cup featuring classic slogans from the show or a 'How You Doin' mug.

And if you need some Friends paraphernalia around you while you binge your favourite episodes you can furnish your sofa with a variety of Friends cushions, each priced £5.99.

*Aldi's Friends range will be available online and in store from Thursday, November 21.
