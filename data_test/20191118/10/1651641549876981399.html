Samsung’s upcoming flagship the Galaxy S11 won’t feature a waterfall screen design, but will be a very attractive smartphone

18 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Rahul

2020 is going to be a very busy year for Samsung as the company has plenty of product lineups to launch. The first product that the Korean manufacturer is expected to launch in early 2020 is the Galaxy S11 series of smartphones and while we’re still a few months away from the official launch, rumors have already started doing the rounds that Galaxy S11 is going to set a new benchmark for other major players in the smartphone industry.

Not only the Galaxy S11 is going to have a great camera but also according to the famous leaker Ice Universe, the upcoming Galaxy S11 will have bezel narrower than the Note 10 series of smartphones and, therefore, we can expect the upcoming Galaxy to be more pleasing to look at. It also means that the rumor of the Galaxy S11 getting a waterfall screen design is false — it won’t look like the Vivo Nex 3.
