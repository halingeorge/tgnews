PUMA Revamps RS-X3 Puzzle With "Char Rose" & "Limestone" Iterations

Multi-panel mosaics.

18 Nov 2019, 10:50 by Charlie Zhang

Following its BEAMS collaboration, PUMA has returned with two contrasting iterations of its RS-X3 Puzzle, rendered in a brightly colored “Char Rose” and a muted greyscale “Limestone.”

This silhouette features a bevy of geometric panels that have been layered over one another for a seamless, visually-rich three-dimensional look. The top of the tongue is stamped with “RS-X” branding alongside PUMA’s logo, with cream-colored laces covering the rest of the tongue. The uppers come in a mix of mesh, suede and smooth leather textiles, starting with mesh at the collars, followed by suede midbody and smooth leather underneath. Along the body are PUMA’s side stripes that extend all the way back to the heel. Suede mudguards, a protective leather cap and a breathable toe box round up the details of the uppers. All this sits atop a chunky white midsole, equipped with a bevy of EVA plugs for added support and cushioning.

The PUMA RS-X3 Puzzle “Char Rose” and “Limestone” are currently available at atmos-tokyo.com for $119 USD.
