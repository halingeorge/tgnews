Grenade attack close to military training facility in Kabul leaves 5 wounded

18 Nov 2019, 10:13 by Khaama Press

Unknown gunmen hurled hand grenades close to a military training facility in Kabul city earlier today which left at least five people wounded.

Nasrat Rahimi, a spokesman for the Ministry of Interior confirmed that the gunmen hurled two hand grenade close to Kabul Military Training Center at around 7:30 am local time.

Rahimi further added that the attack wounded five people including Afghan National Army personnel and a civilian.

No group individual or group including Taliban militants has so far claimed responsibility for the attack.
