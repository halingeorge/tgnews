Fabolous Announces 'Summertime Shoutout 3: The Coldest Summer Ever' Release Date

18 Nov 2019, 10:14 by Trent Clark

It appears fans won’t have to wait until next year for Fabolous’ next project.

After teasing the masses with the single “B.O.M.B.S.” and “Choosy,” a tropical single featuring Jeremih and Davido, Loso has officially unveiled the release date for Summertime Shoutout 3: The Coldest Summer Ever to coincide with Black Friday 2019.

The news of the upcoming mixtape also coincides with the Brooklyn rap star’s 42nd birthday.
Summertime Shoutout 3: The Coldest Summer Ever marks Fab’s first major release since Friday On Elm Street, his 2017-closing, star-studded collaborative album with Jadakiss.
