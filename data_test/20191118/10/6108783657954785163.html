Animal Crossing Pocket Camp subscription mirrors Mario Kart Tour

18 Nov 2019, 10:32 by Steve Wright

The service is reportedly starting on 21 November.

A new Animal Crossing Pocket Camp subscription will allegedly mirror that offered inside Mario Kart Tour, a new report suggests.

ResetEra user ZeoVGM reports that a new Pocket Camp Club subscription will be offered from 21 November, providing two tiers of service to those who wish to sign up. The first tier will offer an NPC caretaker to maintain a camp, while the second tier will provide access to fortune cookies (aka, randomised loot boxes) alongside increased storage capabilities.

Pricing details weren’t provided, but Mario Kart Tour‘s Gold Pass subscription service is priced at $4.99 USD/£4.99/$7.99 AUD.

We’ll have more on Pocket Camp Club as information is made available.

Animal Crossing Pocket Camp and Mario Kart Tour are currently available on iOS and Android devices.
