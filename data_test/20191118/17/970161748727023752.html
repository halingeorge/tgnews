Stranger Things' Joe Keery and Maika Monroe spend quality time with their dogs

18 Nov 2019, 17:09

Stranger Things star Joe Keery looked like he was really enjoying his burger while having lunch with girlfriend Maika Monroe, some friends and two adorable little pups.

So much so that he seems to have gotten some to take home.

The 27-year-old actor – who plays Steve Harrington on the hit-Netflix show – was seen waiting for his burger and chips at a restaurant in West Hollywood, while his actress girlfriend played on her phone and their friends laughed with each other.

Two adorable little dogs sit under the table… probably keeping their eyes peeled for any scraps.

When the foursome get their meals, Joe waits no time before taking a massive bite out of his burger.

Then, clearly satisfied, Joe and Maika walk off the calories they’ve just consumed with a box of leftovers in hand and the furry little friends in tow.

What a cute little lunch date.

Joe and Maika started dating back in 2017 and were spotted posing away alongside Miley Cyrus and ex-husband Liam Hemsworth back in June.

They were all attending the Saint Laurent runway show in Malibu.

More recently, Joe made headlines after he debuted a bold new look that divided fans across the world.

Joe rocked up to a Chanel event in Los Angeles, with Maika, to celebrate Margot Robbie’s new launch.

However, all eyes were on his locks as he showed off his new look – going from long sweeping hair to a bit of a front curtains part look.

To be honest, we’re glad to see he’s grown back his locks since then.
