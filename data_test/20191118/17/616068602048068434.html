Release the spikes!

18 Nov 2019, 17:00 by John Lawless

At about 8 a.m. this morning, Armstrong RCMP were notified of a stolen vehicle that was parked on Six Mile Creek Road.

The officers found the vehicle with a person inside it, but the suspect drove off. He didn't get very far, as the police threw down a spike belt and left the vehicle not driveable.

The thief then took off on foot into the woods. Using the help of police dogs, the RCMP were able to locate the man and apprehended him.

"We also had a helicopter deployed to help with our search," says Sgt. Justin Thiessen. "Our officers did an outstanding job to track down the suspect."

The call took a total of approximately 3 and a half hours, as the RCMP were leaving the scene before noon.
