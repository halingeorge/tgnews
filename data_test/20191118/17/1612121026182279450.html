Sony confirms its Android 10 roadmap for Xperia devices; did yours make the list?

18 Nov 2019, 17:05 by Jared Peters

If you have a Sony device you’re probably itching to get a taste of Android 10. While Sony is usually pretty good about pushing out updates quickly, we still haven’t seen the latest Android OS on Xperia phones yet.

The company has released a roadmap of what phones will be getting Android 10, and when that update is happening. Check if yours is on the list.

  • From December 2019

    • Xperia 1
    • Xperia 5

  • From early 2020

    • Xperia 10
    • Xperia 10 Plus
    • Xperia XZ2
    • Xperia XZ2 Compact
    • Xperia XZ2 Premium
    • Xperia XZ3

Their newest devices, like the Xperia 1 and Xperia 5, should see the update next month. Older devices are set to follow at the beginning of next year, which is only a couple months away. The only disappointment here is the lack of many mid-range devices, aside from the Xperia 10 phones. Some of those will still likely get Android 10, we just don’t have an official roadmap for it yet.
