Shooting outside Oklahoma Walmart leaves several dead: police

18 Nov 2019, 17:16

Duncan: At least three people have been shot and killed outside a Walmart store in Oklahoma, according to police.

The shooting happened on Monday morning, local time, at the store in Duncan, Oklahoma.

Duncan Police Chief Danny Ford said two men and a woman were fatally shot.

Two victims were shot inside a car and the third was in the parking lot outside the store, he said.

Police are looking for witnesses to the shooting, Ford said.

A dispatcher said "everyone is at the scene."

Duncan is about 130 kilometres south of Oklahoma City.
