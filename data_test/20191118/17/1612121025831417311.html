Xiaomi will flood the market with 5G phones in 2020

18 Nov 2019, 17:19 by Jared Peters

Xiaomi is known for its aggressive pricing strategies, but sometimes that kind of strategy doesn’t mesh well with brand new technology, like 5G. But if anyone is going to combine the two, it’s a pretty safe bet that it’ll be Xiaomi.

Their CEO has doubled down on Xiaomi’s commitment to launching at least 10 new smartphones with 5G connectivity, and now he’s clarified that any phone over €250 will have 5G support on release.

Compare that to the first slew of 5G phones released earlier this year, like the Galaxy S10 5G or Galaxy Note 10 5G, which cost over $1000. Xiaomi’s going to do the same at a quarter of the price. That’s going to rapidly increase 5G adoption, especially in China, and should put pressure on other companies to keep their 5G phones priced a little more modestly.

These new phones will join Xiaomi’s current lineup of the Mi Mix 3 5G, Mi 9 Pro 5G, and the mythical Mi Mix Alpha. Like many of those, these phones will also probably have a 4G-only option for consumers that just don’t care about 5G and want to save some cash. Expect to start seeing these new phones in 2020 and beyond.
