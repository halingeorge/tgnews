Star Wars: The Mandalorian's Baby Yoda Brought Werner Herzog to Tears

18 Nov 2019, 17:11 by Renaldo Matadeen

Werner Herzog has given his seal of approval to Baby Yoda, aka "the Asset," admitting it brought tears to his eyes on the set of the Disney+ Star Wars series The Mandalorian.

Herzog plays the Client, the person who hires the titular bounty hunter to steal the child for him, and he revealed the emotional gravity of the baby impacted on him even though he's yet to see it on screen. "I have seen it on the set. I've seen it on the set," he told Variety. "And it's heartbreaking! It's heartbreakingly beautiful."
