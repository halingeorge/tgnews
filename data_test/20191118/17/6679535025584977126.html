Milk producers demand appointments to vacancies in Aavin

18 Nov 2019, 17:56 by Staff Reporter

Members of the Tamil Nadu Milk Producers’ Welfare Association petitioned Chief Minister Edappadi K. Palaniswami to take necessary steps appoint people in various vacancies in Aavin.

The petitioners demanded that the State government should make these appointments before the announcement of local body elections. the The milk producers said that absence of authorities in important positions is affecting the quality of products.

The milk producers said that there are nearly 2,500 vacancies in Aavin and it has put to question quality of Aavin products. They also said in their petition that over 200 managerial positions are lying vacant for over two years and due to this, Aavin was not able to maintain FSSAI standards. They also said that the vacancies have put additional burden on existing staff.

The petitioners demanded that the State government must take necessary steps to appoint individuals to vacant positions before an announcement regarding local body elections are made.
