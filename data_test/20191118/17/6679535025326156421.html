Man gets life for murder

18 Nov 2019, 17:38 by Special Correspondent

The principal district and sessions court on Monday convicted a man for murdering his friend in 2017 and sentenced him to life imprisonment. Chief judge P. Dhanabal pronounced the judgment and sentenced Arumugam, 34, a resident of Kathavarayan on Karasur Main Road to life term. The Sederapet police had registered a case against Arumugam for murdering Iyyappan, a resident of Karasur on Mettu Street in 2017.
