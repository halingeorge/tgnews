Bro Sammy speaks on sleeping with Akuapem Poloo in Kumasi rumors

18 Nov 2019, 17:57 by ‌ RASHAD

For the first time, Brother Sammy has reacted to rumors that he slept with Akuapem Poloo in Kumasi during the shoot of the ‘Baby Mama’

According to Brother in an interview on Brunch2Lunch Entertainment on Radio Universe, he never slept with Akuapem Poloo as rumored.

Brother Sammy wandered why Ghanaians will accuse him of sleeping with only Akuapem Poloo when there were many women on set during the movie shoot.

He denied ever sleeping with Akuapem Poloo his entire life and stated boldly it such accusations that force people to sin. Meaning he was tempted to curse people who made the allegation.

Brother Sammy said:

“Everybody wake up and say whatever they want in Ghana. There were many women on the set so why will they make this allegation of me sleeping with only Akuapem Poloo.” he quizzed.

” Somebody is making this up and if you don’t take care, you will commit a sin. There is no truth in that,” Brother Sammy lamented.

Few days after the shoot of Baby Mama produced by Tracey Boakye, Xandy Kamel who was on set alleged that Akuapem Poloo slept with Brother Sammy.

She claimed that Akuapem Poloo sneaked into Brother Sammy’s house to sleep with him.

Quickly, Poloo came out to denied and explained she was invited to shoot a commercial for Brother Sammy.
