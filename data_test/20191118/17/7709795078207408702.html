Darts rankings: Gerwyn Price insists Michael van Gerwen is now scared of him as he targets world number one spot

18 Nov 2019, 17:06 by Sporting Life

Gerwyn Price insists Michael van Gerwen is "scared if me now" as he targets World Championship glory and top spot in the darts rankings.

The Welshman has now climbed to number three in the PDC Order of Merit after retaining his Boylesports Grand Slam of Darts title in blockbusting fashion at the weekend while he's now been slashed to 7/1 second favourite for the Ally Pally crown next month and 5/1 to win this weekend's Players Championship Finals.

Peter Wright labelled Price as "unplayable" during Sunday's final in Wolverhampton, which the Iceman won 16-6 with a sparkling average of 107.86 just hours after claiming his first ever victory over MVG at the 20th attempt, and these kind of performances are starting to become the norm rather than just a hot streak of form.
