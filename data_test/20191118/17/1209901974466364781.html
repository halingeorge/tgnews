PHOTO: Car Club presents donation to the Stettler Hospice Foundation

The $1,000 donation came from the Drag Race event this past July

18 Nov 2019, 17:30

Stettler Car Club President Scott Roenspies presented $1,000 to the Stettler Hospice Foundation, which was represented by Dr. Drummond. These funds were raised during the Drag Race event in July.
