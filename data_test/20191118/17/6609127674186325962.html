Teenage boy arrested over ‘rape’ of 14-year-old girl in Glasgow

18 Nov 2019, 17:05 by Connor Boylan

A TEENAGE boy has been arrested following the alleged rape of a 14-year-old girl in Glasgow.

Cops launched an investigation into the alleged serious sexual assault on the schoolgirl, which is said to have taken place in the Royston area on Saturday.

And officers have now confirmed a 16-year-old boy has been arrested in connection with the alleged incident.

A Police Scotland spokeswoman said: "Officers are carrying out enquiries after receiving a report of a 14-year-old girl having been seriously sexually assaulted in the Royston area of Glasgow on Saturday, November 16.

"A 16-year-old male youth has been arrested in connection with the incident and enquiries are ongoing."
