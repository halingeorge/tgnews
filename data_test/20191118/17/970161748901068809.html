Teresa Giudice proud mum as daughter, 14, works hard to lose over 2.5 stone

18 Nov 2019, 17:23

Real Housewives Of New Jersey star, Teresa Giudice, has opened up about how proud she feels of her daughter after 14-year-old Milania asked for a nutritionist.

Teresa is really into fitness and nutrition herself so you can only imagine how happy she was when her daughter followed suit.

Milania has worked really hard to lose 40 pounds by changing her nutritional and exercise habits recently.

Teresa revealed to HollywoodLife: ‘She told me, she’s like, “Mommy, can you get me a nutritionist?”’

She continued: ‘I said to her, “You want me to get you a nutritionist?” And then at first she said no, but then when she was ready, she told me. And then I got her.’

A mixture of factors has helped Milania to look and feel healthier.

‘Just eating right’ played a major part in it according to Teresa.

‘I was getting her meals. I had a nutritionist come to the house and they did her macros and what she should be eating and we teamed up with this meal prep company, Brielle.

‘That was amazing.’

The 47-year-old added that Milania had also been exercising with her.

‘I’m making her meals and exercising. She said she’ll go to the gym with me and she did it.’

In an Instagram post just a few days ago, Teresa called Milania her ‘shining star’.

Teresa has four children, Gia, Gabriella, Milania, and Audriana, and it’s been a tough time for the whole family as they navigate life alone while father and husband Joe Giudice served his 41-month prison sentence.

On 4 March, 2014, both Teresa and Joe entered a guilty plea covering 41 counts of fraud. They were allowed to serve their sentences separately so one parent could always be with the children.

With all of the difficulty the family has faced, Teresa says she’s just glad to see her daughter doing so well.

‘She would work, she would do cardio morning and night and weights.

‘Yeah, she’s amazing.’
