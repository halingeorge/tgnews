GQ's 'Men of the Year' 2019 are absolute legends

18 Nov 2019, 17:26

GQ’s Men of the Year include Jennifer Lopez, Tyler, the Creator and Al Pacino and Robert De Niro.

The popular have just put out their ‘Men of the Year’ issue and the four winners are powerful, inspirational and downright cool.

Describing each one as at ‘dramatically different junctures in their careers’ and yet all worthy of this recognition, the magazine went on to explain why they have won this award.

They began with singer and actress Jennifer Lopez, 50, who they called ‘The Icon’.

In particular, they drew attention to her unreal appearance in 2019’s Hustlers, suggesting it may have been the best film of the year.

Ultimately she is praised for all the incredible work she has done, being open about her age, potentially being a ‘bad bitch’.

Secondly there is Tyler, The Creator, 28, who GQ calls The Provocateur, probably because 2019 was ‘his most ambitious year yet’.

The rapper, singer and fashion designer not only smashed his musical career but also showed the world his true entrepreneurial spirit.

‘He sold out arena shows, went platinum for the first time with “IGOR,” and expanded his sphere of creative influence in fashion, footwear, and ice cream.’

And finally, Hollywood heroes Al Pacino, 79 and Robert De Niro, 76, appeared together as GQ’s third winners.

The duo are about to appear in another movie together, the Irishman, which the magazine describes as potentially ‘one final coronation’ for the two actors.

In their Men of the Year interviews, the ‘two legends talk earnestly and honestly about their latest film, Martin Scorsese, The Godfather, and five decades of Hollywood fame’.

What absolute legends.
