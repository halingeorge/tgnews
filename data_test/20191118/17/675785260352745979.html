Best foundations for combination skin 2019 - including full coverage options

High-performing foundations that deliver the perfect balancing act for oily, dry and combination skin

18 Nov 2019, 17:06 by Ruth Doherty, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/ruth-doherty/

Anybody with combination skin knows that the struggle to find the perfect foundation is real.

Common problems include an oily T-zone, including forehead nose and chin, that can be prone to breakouts, while cheeks can sometimes be dry. So your base needs to have, well... all bases covered.

Your foundation should be non-greasy yet moisturising enough to smoothly cover dry patches. Thankfully, new-gen formulas have caught up with all our complex skin needs, and there's a host of balancing bases that target more than one specific problem.

Keep scrolling to discover our roundup of the best foundation for combo skin below.

Best foundations for combination skin

Clever technology means this skincare-meets-foundation nourishes and covers for a natural-looking finish.

Infused with hydrating and plumping hyaluronic acid and Aloe Vera, it gives your complexion a moisturising radiance boost, yet boasts a non-comedogenic formula that means it won't clog your pores and cause blemishes. With 40 shades to choose from, there's a hue that's perfect for you, too.

2. Charlotte Tilbury Magic Foundation

It's also filled with concentrated mushroom extract that controls shine, firms and tightens large pores; and it gives a full-coverage yet non-cakey finish that's brilliant for anyone suffering with rosacea or acne, often associated with combination skin. Go, go, go.

3. Clinique Superbalanced

As the name suggests, it's adept at balancing oily and dry skin types, offering medium coverage with a breathable finish. What's more, it's enhanced with anti-irritants and antioxidants that will offer long-term benefits and target pesky breakouts.
