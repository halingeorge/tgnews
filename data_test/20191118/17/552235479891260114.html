Girl rushed to hospital after being hit by car outside Bearsden school

The female pedestrian was knocked down outside St Nicholas' Primary around 4.10pm today.

18 Nov 2019, 17:46 by Lynn Love

A girl has been rushed to hospital after being hit by a car near to a Bearsden school .

The female pedestrian was knocked down outside St Nicholas' Primary around 4.10pm today.

Emergency services rushed to the scene on Duntocher Road.

The girl was taken to hospital.

Her injuries are currently unknown.
