Amitabh Bachchan begins the countdown for 2020 with an interesting fact!

18 Nov 2019, 17:55 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Just a month and the year will come to an end and the whole world will step into the new year! Megastar Amitabh Bachchan who is quite active on social media took to his Twitter to start the countdown for the year 2020.

However, along with that, he also shared the fact that we won't be saying 'teen' again to announce a year for a century to come. The post read, "T 3553 - Just 43 days left for the Year 'two thousand nineteen' to end .. AND not till we are alive will we be saying 'teen' again to announce a Year .. for a very long time .. !!"
