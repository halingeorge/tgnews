Sephora Black Friday 2019 FAQs:

What should you buy?

Last year, Sephora dropped a huge 50% discount on a ton of items from Benefit, Tarte, Urban Decay, Becca, and of course, Sephora Collection. There were also a lot of stocking stuffers under $15 across makeup, skin-care, hair-care, and more. We’re expecting the same level of discounts this year, and on new-to-Sephora brands like Tower28, Huda Beauty, and Beachwaver too.

Sephora app users will get an exclusive preview of the $15-and-under Black Friday deals starting November 21, so we’ll be updating this post with all the information and best deals as we learn about them.

Is Sephora open on Thanksgiving?

No. Sephora will not be open on Thanksgiving.

Instead, it will be open the day before and after Thanksgiving, with stores operating at different hours. Check your local Sephora store’s hours here to see specific times.

When does Sephora’s Black Friday sale start?

This year, Black Friday officially falls on November 29, but Sephora’s deals will likely be available earlier than that. We’ll update this post as soon as we get an official date.

Sephora often drops exclusive information on its free app, so you may want to download that soon. In the past, app users were the first to get a sneak peek at Black Friday deals, like stocking stuffers $15 and under.

Does Sephora have free shipping?

This depends on your Beauty Insider status.

Sephora Beauty Insiders with Rouge status automatically qualify for free standard shipping. Historically, there have been free shipping promo codes with low minimums that everyone can use, regardless of their Beauty Insider status. Currently, as of this post’s publishing time, Sephora is running a promotion for free 3-day shipping on orders over $50.

If you don’t want to gamble with free shipping, Sephora also has a subscription to a years-worth of free 2-day shipping for just $15. This applies to any and all orders; there’s no order minimum.

What is Sephora’s return policy?

In-store purchases can be returned to any Sephora store within 30 days for a full refund to the method of payment as long as you have your receipt.

Online orders can be returned to Sephora stores within 30 days or sent back with the pre-paid label that came with your order.

Finally, if you sign up for a Sephora or Sephora Visa credit card, you’ll save 15% off your purchase and earn 4% cash back on in-store and online Sephora purchases.
