Notts County given under 1,000 tickets for FA Cup clash at Northampton

But number could rise as Magpies hope for big following

18 Nov 2019, 17:00 by Leigh Curtis

Notts County have been given an initial allocation of 954 tickets for their FA Cup clash at Northampton Town.

The Magpies head to Sixfields on Sunday, December 1, with a 2pm kick-off with the game set to feature on the BBC’s Final Score programme.

Tickets have now gone on priority sale to season ticket holders, and members of Lifeline and the Official Supporters’ Association.

Prices are £15 for adults, seniors are £12, juniors are £5 and under-7s are free.

Tickets can be purchased online by clicking here, or by calling 0115 955 7210 or in person at the Meadow Lane ticket office.

Tickets to the value of £1.50 and above will be subject to a 60p per ticket admin fee when purchased online or by phone.

The Magpies will hope to take a sizable following to Sixfields where Notts were held to a goalless draw last term under previous manager Harry Kewell.

They then hosted the Cobblers at Meadow Lane in April and again the sides could not be separated as the game finished 2-2.

Northampton are now managed by Keith Curle, who is a former manager of Notts and they are currently seventh in League Two.
