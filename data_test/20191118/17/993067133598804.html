Police investigate arson attack and assault in east Belfast

18 Nov 2019, 17:35

POLICE in east Belfast are investigating a link between an arson attack on a house and vehicle, and an incident in which a man was dragged from his car before being assaulted.

The man was in his car in the Templemore Avenue/Albertbridge Road area at around 9.45pm last night when two men approached and attacked the vehicle before dragging him from it and assaulting him.

Later, at around 3.45am today, officers discovered a property and a car on fire at Albertbridge Road.

A number of householders in the area were evacuated from their homes while firefighters tackled the blaze. No injuries were reported.

A PSNI spokeswoman said officers are investigating a possible link between the incidents and have urged witnesses to come forward.
