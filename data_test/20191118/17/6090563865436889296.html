Zimbabwe opposition leader dies

18 Nov 2019, 17:03

Leader of the opposition party, Zimbabwe Development Party (ZDP), Kisinoti Mukwazhe has died, at the age of 49.

Mukwazhe died on Sunday night at Karanda Mission Hospital after a short illness.

He died after being admitted complaining of a sore throat.

Mukwazhe is survived by his wife Shaderah and seven children.

Mourners are gathered at 11 Ceres, Hatfield, Harare burial arrangements will be announced in due course.
