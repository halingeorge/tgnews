Announcing The Genius Community’s Lyricist Of The Year 2019 Bracket Challenge

18 Nov 2019, 17:14 by Genius

As 2019 comes to a close, Genius is looking back at the year’s best lyricists with help from the Genius Community. We’ve narrowed down a list of 16 rappers, one of whom will be crowned Genius Community’s Lyricist of the Year through a fan-voted online bracket challenge.

Only rappers who released a project in 2019 are eligible for the competition, eliminating superstars like Eminem, Kendrick Lamar, Drake, and Pusha-T right off the bat. Logic, J. Cole, Young Thug, and Chance the Rapper lead the list of contenders, which also includes DaBaby, Megan Thee Stallion, Offset, Denzel Curry, and more. The full bracket will be announced on an episode of Genius' original video series For The Record on December 2.

Fans can cast votes in the Lyricist of the Year bracket over the next month via Genius' Instagram Stories. The first round of voting begins on December 2, followed by a second round on December 4, with the semi-finals on December 9. Final round voting will take place on December 11-12, with the winner being announced on December 13.

Follow Genius' Instagram Stories to watch the votes roll in and check out all the lyrics to all the artists featured on Genius.
