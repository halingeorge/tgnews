Two arrested after man killed in crash on A5 near Lichfield

The road at Muckley Corner was closed off after the fatal crash

18 Nov 2019, 17:09 by Jordan Coussins, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/jordan-coussins/

A man and woman have been arrested after a 25-year-old was killed in crash on one of the Midlands' busy dual carriageways.

Detectives from Staffordshire Police confirmed a 41-year-old man and a 37-year-old woman, both from Walsall, were detained after the crash on the A5 on Saturday, November 16.

The man, who has not been named, was pronounced dead at the scene at around 12.30am and specialist officers were supporting his family.

 

An investigation into the circumstances of the collision is under way by officers from CID.

Anyone who witnessed the collision, has dashcam footage or may have seen anyone walking on the A5 Wall to Muckley Corner (eastbound) is asked to call 101 quoting incident number 19 of November 19.

A spokesman for Staffordshire Police said: “A 41-year-old man, from Walsall, has been arrested on suspicion of death by dangerous driving and a 37-year-old woman, also from Walsall, has been arrested on suspicion of perverting the course of justice. Both have been released under investigation pending further enquiries.”

 

A spokesman for West Midlands Ambulance Service added: "We were called to the eastbound carriageway of the A5 at 12.28am on Saturday, November 16. A paramedic officer attended. Sadly, nothing could be done to save the man and he was confirmed dead at the scene."
