Hyperdub and Adult Swim's joint compilation features new tracks by Burial, Laurel Halo, Dean Blunt

Ikonika, Fatima Al Qadiri, Kode9 and more contribute to HyperSwim, premiering on November 22nd.

18 Nov 2019, 17:00 by Steph Lee

Hyperdub and Adult Swim are releasing a joint compilation called HyperSwim.

19 Hyperdub affiliates have contributed new tracks to the record, including Burial, Ikonika, Laurel Halo, Angel-Ho, Lee Gamble, Dean Blunt, Doon Kanda, DJ Haram and Fatima Al Qadiri.

HyperSwim, part of Hyperdub's 15th anniversary celebration, will be streamed in its entirety on November 22nd. The following day, the compilation will be available as a digital-only release on all platforms.
