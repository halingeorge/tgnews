Ranch it up! Hidden Valley launches ranch-filled stockings for the holiday season

18 Nov 2019, 17:07 by STEFANIE DYGA | WSBT Staff

SOUTH BEND, Ind. (WSBT) -- Deck the halls with bottles of ranch this Christmas.

Hidden Valley has officially launched ranch-filled stockings so you can appropriately dress the house for the holidays (and your taste buds). You can also buy other merchandise including a festive throw pillow, sweaters, backpacks, flasks and onesies!
