At University of the Arts London (UAL), we provide thousands of short courses that help people work out the best way to develop their career, get inspired and learn new skills. We know that deciding to reinvent yourself can be an overwhelming decision and navigating the array of upskilling options can be daunting, but there have never been so many ways to evolve your creative focus.

Whatever your reason for wanting to head in a new direction, you can make small changes happen without having to completely start from scratch. We are big believers that one small step can be all that it takes to get you started moving in the right direction.

At UAL Short Courses you can immerse yourself in our studios, get your hands dirty, learn how to use new equipment or develop a specific software skill by joining a one-day workshop in our digital suites. Whatever you choose, all of our short courses are designed for students that are interested in learning something new, developing their skill, making new friends and just giving something a go.

Best of all, short courses are delivered throughout the year, so you can create a schedule that suits you and remain focused on your career while venturing into new creative territory in your free time. Many of our short courses are the perfect add-on that can help keep you motivated and get your career heading in the direction that you want to go.

Boost Your Career

Service Design and Innovation Intensive

This 3-day immersive course explores service design as a human-centred problem-solving process. If you are in a role with innovation, strategy, design or customer service as part of your remit, you will gain valuable skills and knowledge that you can apply directly to your work.

Explore Wellbeing Through Art Making

Health and Wellbeing Through Art Making Short Course

The benefits to health and wellbeing through the creation of art are well documented. On this course, you will learn about your own ability to balance yourself by immersing yourself in art therapy and getting hands on in the studios at Central Saint Martins. This course has been developed for anyone interested in making art and understanding how and why art making can have a positive impact on health and wellbeing.

Essential Guide to the Fashion Business
