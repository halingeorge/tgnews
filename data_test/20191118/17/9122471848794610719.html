Game Time, TV Channel Set For Oklahoma-Oklahoma State

18 Nov 2019, 17:57 by Tzvi Machlin

Oklahoma is riding high and can now cruise into the Big 12 Championship Game after beating Baylor this past week. And if they win out, they’ll have a strong case for a spot in the College Football Playoff.

But a national title push will require a strong win over one of their biggest rivals: Oklahoma State in the Bedlam Series.

On Monday, the Big 12 Conference announced the television details for rivalry week. There will be five games spread over Friday and Saturday, but the Oklahoma-Oklahoma State game will get the primetime treatment.

Kickoff will be at 8:00 p.m. EST on FOX.
