3 Dead in Shooting at Oklahoma Walmart Store in US, Investigation Underway

The injured have been rushed to the nearby hospital. An investigation is underway.

18 Nov 2019, 17:01

New Delhi: At least three people have died in a shooting at the Walmart stores in Duncan, Oklahoma in the United States, the police reported.

While two people were shot inside a vehicle, one person was gunned down outside the vehicle. A few media reports indicate that the shooter is dead, however, there is no confirmation yet.

The injured have been rushed to the nearby hospital. An investigation is underway.
