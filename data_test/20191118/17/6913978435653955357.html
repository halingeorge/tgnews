King to address Shoura on Wednesday

18 Nov 2019, 17:08

Saudi Gazette report

RIYADH — Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques King Salman will deliver his annual speech at the Shoura (Consultative) Council on Wednesday at the beginning of the fourth year of the Council's seventh session.

Speaker, Abdullah Al-Ashaikh, expressed pride over the King's honoring the Council and said the King's speech will outline the Kingdom's policy and define the work of the Council.

He said in the third year of its seventh session, the Council held 65 meetings and issued 262 recommendations which were forwarded to the King.

King Salman’s annual address will outline the Kingdom's stance vis-a-vis world issues and internal achievements which were according to the 2030 Vision.

The royal speech will define the political and economic work during the coming phase and will pinpoint the Kingdom's attitude regarding the regional and international developments.
