Call Of Duty: Modern Warfare finishing moves and how to do them

18 Nov 2019, 17:08

If you’ve heard about finishing moves in Call Of Duty: Modern Warfare but aren’t sure how to do them then follow these handy tips.

It may sound like something that’s more likely to crop up in Mortal Kombat but finishing moves are one of the coolest new features in Call Of Duty: Modern Warfare.

They’re pretty easy to pull off too, if you know the trick to them. They work like stealth kills in other games and to get them to work you need to sneak up to an enemy without being seen and then press the melee button prompt when it appears on-screen.

That may sound easy but in a hectic game of Call Of Duty it’s anything but. The important thing is that you have to make sure you approach the enemy from behind, as it won’t work from the front or the sides.
