Ubisoft turns Assassin’s Creed into an audio drama with Audible

18 Nov 2019, 17:08 by Rachel Kaser

Ubisoft today revealed it’s collaborating with Audible on a new audio drama, set in the Assassin’s Creed series. This new venture, called Assassin’s Creed Gold, is the company’s latest effort to branch out into new ventures using the Assassin’s Creed series.
