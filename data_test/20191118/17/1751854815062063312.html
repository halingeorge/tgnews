Samsung is launching the Galaxy Fold 5G in China but under a different name entirely. The foldable device will be sold in the country as the Samsung W20 5G, and ahead of its release tomorrow, a hands-on video has been posted on the web.

The short video shows the device when it is opened and closed. The phone will be available in white when it launches.
