Bihar Board 10th, 12th exam date sheet 2020 released, check schedule

Bihar Board BSEB 10th, 12th Exam Date Sheet 2020: The Madhyamik (Class 10) examinations will commence on February 17, 2020, while Intermediate (Class 12) is scheduled to be held from February 3 to 13, 2020

18 Nov 2019, 17:06 by Education Desk

Bihar Board BSEB 10th, 12th Exam Date Sheet 2020: The Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has released the date sheet for matric or class 10 and class 12 exams on Monday, November 18, 2019. The Madhyamik (Class 10) examination will be commenced on February 17, 2020, while Intermediate (Class 12) examination is scheduled to be held from February 3 to 13, 2020.

Both the examinations will be held in two shifts — first shift (9.30 am to 12.45 pm), and the second shift from 1:45 pm to 5 pm.
