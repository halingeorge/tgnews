Stacey Solomon shows off bargain Primark gifts for her family in sponsored post

Stacey Solomon showed her bargain buys off to her followers, revealing she spent a lot less than you might imagine on her family members

18 Nov 2019, 17:03 by Irishmirror.ie

Stacey Solomon seems to be having a thrifty Christmas as she's revealed she did her shopping in a bargain store.

The 30-year-old TV personality took to Instagram to show her followers her favourite buys from the budget shop.

But a small hashtag in some of the videos said #iworkwithprimark, meaning she was being paid to advertise their products to her followers.

Mum of three Stacey explained she started her Christmas shopping early because she "can't face shopping in December".

"I need to do it now or I'll never get it done", she added.

"The first shop I went into was Primark", she said.

"I am going to brand partnership these ones but that doesn't take away from the fact I genuinely shop in there and that's where I get most of my stuff."

Stacey is mum to Zachary, 11, Leighton, seven, and Rex, six months.

She explained that the older two prefer getting vouchers for Christmas, but she bought them some small stocking fillers and a Playstation controller alarm clock.

"So I won't have to wake them up any more!", she laughed.

Stacey bought some £3 Christmassy candle holders and a decorative wooden frame worth £6.

She also got matching Christmas pyjamas for the family including fluffy boot slippers that she lined up in a row for the photo.

For her nieces who are "Frozen obsessed", she got an Elsa jumper and some Frozen stamps, and some pyjamas.

Stacey also revealed she had bought her sisters Jemma and Samantha earrings from budget jewellers Warren James.

"They're all real silver but it's just so cheap", she said, having spent £9.99 altogether on her sisters.

She added that the shop charges for boxes, so she bought the gifts without a box and will recycle boxes she already owns.

The organised TV star also posted a photo of her to do list for Christmas gifts where she ticked off the people she had bought gifts for.

She credited the idea to Mrs Hinch, who advises her followers to keep an activity journal.

Stacey tuned into I'm a Celebrity last night and lamented her partner Joe Swash no longer presenting Extra Camp.

Instead of seeing her boyfriend's smiling face, Joel Dommett was joined by new presenters Emily Atack and Adam Thomas.

Taking to Twitter during the episode, she wrote with crying emojis: "Soooo wierd watching #ExtraCamp without you @realjoeswash."

Ever since he won I'm A Celebrity in 2008, Joe has presented the ITV2 spin-off show for the main programme.

But Joe is busy training for his appearance on Dancing On Ice ahead of its launch in January.
