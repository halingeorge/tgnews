Is Maya Stepney returning to Emmerdale?

18 Nov 2019, 17:00

Emmerdale is set to be rocked by some upsetting news on Monday after it’s revealed that Maya Stepney (Louisa Clein) has been released from prison.

The teacher was at the centre of one of the soap’s most controversial storylines as she groomed and sexually abused Jacob Gallagher (Joe Warren-Plant) for almost a year before finally getting her comeuppance.

So with Jacob having finally started to move on from what happened, her release is naturally concerning to David (Matthew Wolfenden) and Leyla (Roxy Shahidi).

Could Maya be about to make a return to the Dales? Here’s what you need to know…

Is Maya returning to Emmerdale for good?

As it stands there are no current plans to bring the character back to Emmerdale.

However it’s no surprise that David and Leyla are worried that Maya might attempt to contact Jacob now that she is a free woman.

Monday night’s episode will see them deliberating over whether or not to tell the teenager about Maya’s release – and in the end decide that they should inform him.

Jacob appears oddly composed about the news but later disappears – leaving the pair worried about whether he has gone to meet his abuser.

And while there might currently be no plans for Maya to return to the Dales, that doesn’t mean she couldn’t make a return in the future.

It’s a worrying time for sure.
