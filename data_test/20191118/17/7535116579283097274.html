If you have never taken off your shoes to get into shops, then you’ve never truly experienced Malaysia. That said, it’s pretty common knowledge that taking them off comes with a risk!

A CCTV recording shared on Twitter by a user that goes by the name @ShazzsAriel997 helped victims debunk the mystery of a pair of missing shoes that occurred at a vape store near Seri Kembangan.

“Salam dear Twitter users, please make this video viral. My friend’s shoes were stolen at Vaporhub, Seri Kembangan. He had just taken them off. It would be really helpful if you could cooperate. Even if it’s just a pair of shoes, a thief is a thief. Just in case anyone knows or has seen this boy.”
