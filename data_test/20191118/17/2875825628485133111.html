Scarlett Moffatt is unrecognisable with full fringe in throwback from eight years ago

Telly favourite Scarlett Moffatt amazed her fans by sharing a snap of herself from 2011

18 Nov 2019, 17:54 by Irishmirror.ie

Scarlett Moffatt has shared an incredible throwback snap from eight years ago showing her looking totally different.

In the picture, the Geordie lass, 29, is seen sporting a full fringe cut into her long dark locks.

Scarlett admitted she found the throwback pretty hilarious, writing alongside the Instagram Story: "8 years ago today," with a line of laughing emojis.

She's changed dramatically over the years since rising to fame on Channel 4's Gogglebox.

The Bishop Auckland-born star went on to win I'm A Celebrity in 2017 and swiftly became a woman in demand.

She joined Ant and Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway and hosted I'm A Celeb Extra Camp until this year.

As she threw herself into her new career as a TV presenter, Scarlett embraced a glam new image - a far cry from her casual look on Gogglebox.

Earlier this month, Scarlett opened up about her body confidence issues, revealing she's finally learned to love her curves.

She shared a sexy selfie told fans she's finally got the confidence to "show a little bit more skin".

The telly sensation wore a plunging black dress to attend a cancer charity ball in London with her boyfriend Scott Dobinson.

She wrote: "Learning to show a bit more skin is becoming very liberating. Go on ladies flash a bit more & own it."

Scarlett went on to thank her glam squad for giving her the confidence to feel good in a revealing dress.

Scarlett, who has been targeted for her changing shape over the years, previously discussed how she is now "really happy" and "embracing her body."

In an aim to shut down trolls, the bubbly star explained she now shows-off her figure instead of covering up.

"I'm not pretending that I'm the perfect body shape. I've got boobs and hips and rather than try and cover them up, I'm embracing them.

"I'm trying to show them off a bit more,"she said.

Scarlett has been bombarded with sickening abuse from trolls over the years, and last month she shared a video showing her crying over a nasty letter.

The Googlebox star sobbed as she read out a mean hand-written note that called her "fat" with a "mouth that looks like a pork chop cut in half".
