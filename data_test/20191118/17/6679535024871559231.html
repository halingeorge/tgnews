India-A wins the under-19 BCCI Challenger Trophy

18 Nov 2019, 17:04 by Special Correspondent

India-A scored a 43-run win over India-B in the final of the BCCI Challenger Trophy (under-19) one-day cricket tournament here.

Batting first, India-A scored 304/8 with Sai Sudarshan (74) and Arjun Murthy (47) being the main contributors.

In reply, India-B was bowled out for 261 in 46.2 overs.

The scores:

Final: India-A 304/8 in 50 overs (Sai Sudharshan 74, Jay Gohil 30, Sameer Rizwi 38, Arjun Murthy 47, Nitish Kumar Reddy 30, Vivek Kumar 3/48) bt India-B 261 in 46.2 overs (Sagar Dahiya 45, Priyam Garg 50, Divyansh Joshi 36, A. Atharva 48, Nitish Kumar Reddy 3/50).

Third place: India-C 258/6 in 50 overs (Arya Sethi 31, Vikrant Singh 50, Saurav Kumar 58 no, H. Shubhang 36 n.o., D. Sagar 3/37) bt Nepal 103 in 32.5 overs (H. Shubhang 3/24).
