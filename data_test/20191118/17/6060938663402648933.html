Photos: Salman Khan, Shilpa Shetty, Karisma Kapoor and other Bollywood celebs attend Arpita Khan and Aayush Sharma's wedding anniversary bash

18 Nov 2019, 17:35 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Salman Khan's sister Arpita Khan Sharma and Aayush Sharma tied the knot on November 18, 2014. The duo has been giving major couple goals for a long time now.

Today, on their wedding anniversary, the duo hosted a bash for their friends from the fraternity. Celebs like Salman Khan, Sohail Khan, Shilpa Shetty with husband Raj Kundra, Daisy Shah, Sanjay Kapoor, Waluscha D'Souza, Ramesh Taurani, Mukesh Chhabra, Karisma Kapoor, Arbaaz Khan with his girlfriend Giorgia Andriani, Salman's designer Ashely Rebello, Salim Khan and Salma Khan, Helen, Amrita Arora with her parents, Atul Agnihotri with Alvira Khan Agnihotri and more. They were all smiles as paparazzi clicked their pictures.

It was double celebrations for the family as it was Aayush and Arpita are celebrating their 5th wedding anniversary while Salim Khan and Salma Khan completed years of marriage!
