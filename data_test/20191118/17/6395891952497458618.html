Funeral services start for Massachusetts firefighter

18 Nov 2019, 17:48 by EndPlay

WORCESTER, Mass.Â (AP) - Mourners have filled a Massachusetts church for the funeral of a fire lieutenant who died after saving two colleagues.

Funeral services for Worcester Fire Lt. Jason Menard started Monday at St. John's Catholic Church in Worcester. They will be followed by a private burial service.

While hundreds of Worcester firefighters filled the church, hundreds more from departments across the region lined the streets near the church.

Menard died Wednesday after he and his crew went into a city home to look for a baby reportedly trapped on the third floor. He helped two colleagues escape but was unable to get out himself.

The 39-year-old Menard left behind a wife and three children.

A fund for Menard's children has been set up at the Worcester Fire Department Credit Union.
