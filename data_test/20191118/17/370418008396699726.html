Ozzy Osbourne Will Perform With Post Malone And Travis Scott At The American Music Awards

The Prince Of Darkness will perform Take What You Want, the track he did with rapper Post Malone.

18 Nov 2019, 17:30

Earlier this year, heavy metal godfather Ozzy Osbourne performed guest vocals on Take What You Want, a new track by rap sensation Post Malone which The Prince Of Darkness later said was his “favorite since Black Sabbath.” Now, it appears that Ozzy will take the song to the stage, as it’s been announced that he will perform the track live with Post and rapper Travis Scott later this year.

Ozzy will join the two rappers at the American Music Awards on Sunday, November 24, at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles. The show will air on ABC, and is also set to feature performances by Green Day, Lizzo, Cristina Aguilera, and more.

All of this is leading up to the release of Ordinary Man, Ozzy’s 11th studio album (not including 2005’s covers collection Under Cover), which is scheduled to drop in early 2020 on Epic Records.
